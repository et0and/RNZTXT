Big Sur live in the RNZ studio

Local band Big Sur arrived in the RNZ studio with a banjo, double bass, and saxophone to play songs from their new EP Black Mountain. Audio

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

Big Sur are a Tāmaki Makaurau-based alternative-folk collective and they are a band we’ve been following here on Music 101. 

Big Sur arrived in the RNZ studio with a banjo, double bass, and saxophone to play songs from their new EP Black Mountain.

Song played: Lands Tir na n Og (Oisin) Sleeping Dogs

Big Sur EP Tour dates: November 16th Auckland at Nice Goblins November 30th Dunedin at Pearl Diver December 1st Lyttelton at Wunderbar. December 6th Wellington at Underworld Tavern
