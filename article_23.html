Cheeseburger Hill Choir (RNZ Auckland studio 2024) Photo: RNZ

Cheeseburger Hill, a choir of young voices from Thailand conducted by Parichart Chanthai, is in New Zealand to take part in the World Choir Games.

The choir performs live in the RNZ Auckland studio, and Three to Seven host Bryan Crump talks with choir spokesperson, Ka John Zak.
