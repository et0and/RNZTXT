Fit for a king: the multi-talented Roderick Williams

Baritone Roderick William talks about mixing work with travel, and also singing with composing. Video, Audio

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

Singer and composer Roderick Williams Photo: Theo Williams

When not wowing listeners with his baritone voice, Roderick Williams is writing music for the Royal Family.

Touring New Zealand for the first time, Williams spoke with RNZ Concert's Bryan Crump about singing, travelling and writing music for the coronation of King Charles III.

While in Aotearoa, Williams will perform a mix of song recitals and masterclasses, as well as singing the baritone solo role in Faure's Requiem with the Christchurch Symphony Orchestra.
