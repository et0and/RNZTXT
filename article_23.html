Dave Wilson: Ephemeral

On Inside Out this week, Nick Tipping features Ephemeral. The new album from Wellingtonian Dave Wilson deals with the nature and shape of relationships. Audio

Dave Wilson Photo: Nick George Creative

 

Wellingtonian Dave Wilson's new album Ephemeral deals with the nature and shape of relationships: those that uplift, those that dissipate, and those that require extra work. Dave's band is comprised of jazz quintet and string quartet, and the instruments work together as an ensemble and separately as improvisers, creating a new and intricate sound.

Also this hour: Lucien Johnson's 'Awa' from Wax Wane, and Austrian bass player Gina Schwarz' band Pannonica plays her tune 'Toronto'.
