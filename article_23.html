Backstage at the Taite Music Awards

Charlotte Ryan speaks to the Taite nominees about why being independent is important to their music. Audio

The Taite Music Awards celebrate the independent spirit and independently signed artists.

The top prize went to Vera Ellen for her album Ideal Home Noise. Look Blue Go Purple received the Independent Music NZ Classic Record award for their 1991 compilation album.

Charlotte Ryan spoke to the iconic Dunedin band and other nominated musicians and winners at the event.
