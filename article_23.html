'It was chaos, the songs were just suggestions'

Ahead of their NZ tour Jon Theodore talks to Music 101 about getting the call to drum for Queens of the Stone Age, and what it’s like to go from the “free for all” shows with The Mars Volta to the controlled chaos of QOTSA. Audio   Audio

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

Before becoming the drummer for Queens of the Stone Age, Jon Theodore was an early fan, first seeing them play at a tiny Baltimore club that held 12 people.  

Ahead of their NZ tour Jon talks to Charlotte about getting the call from Josh to drum for the band, and what it’s like to go from the “free-for-all” shows with The Mars Volta to the controlled chaos of Queens of the Stone Age. 

Queens of the Stone Age NZ tour dates: Thursday 29th Auckland’s Spark Arena Friday 1st March Wellington’s TSB Arena Sunday 3rd March Christchurch Arena
