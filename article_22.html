Jean McAllister - 20 years in Red Mole

This experimental theatre troupe shocked conservative New Zealand but took the younger generation by storm in the 1970s. Performer Jean McAllister talks about music, kiwi counter-culture and the new documentary in cinemas now. Audio

RED MOLE was an experimental theatre troupe that shocked conservative New Zealand but took the younger generation by storm in the 1970s. 

A new documentary Red Mole: A Romance explores the origins, performances, personalities and fate of Red Mole. 

Jean McAllister performed in Red Mole for over 20 years. She joins Charlotte to talk music, kiwi counter culture the new documentary in cinemas now.
