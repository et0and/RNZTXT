Inside Out: Ella Dunbar-Wilcox

Ella Dunbar-Wilcox is a NZ singer who works and studies in Los Angeles. Her first EP 'Almost Love' shows a vocalist with a very bright future. You can hear tracks from the EP on Inside Out this week. Audio

Photo: Craig Allyn Cochrane

 

Ella Dunbar-Wilcox is a NZ singer who works and studies in Los Angeles. Her first EP 'Almost Love' is a fabulous first outing from a singer with a bright future.

Also this hour: Freddie Hubbard plays two tracks from his album 'Ready For Freddie', Joe Henderson and Herbie Hancock play a Jobim ballad, and Nick Tipping remembers gigs in Seoul with Korean pianist Hey Rim Jeon.
