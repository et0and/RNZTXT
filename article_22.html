Sounds of the Sanctuary: getting close to nature with the NZSQ

New Zealand String Quartet violist Gillian Ansell talks with RNZ Concert's Andrew Clark about Sounds of the Sanctuary, the quartet's flagship programme in their 2024 season. It's coming to the Nelson Arts Festival at the end of October. Audio

New Zealand String Quartet (l to r: Gillian Ansell, Rolf Gjelsten, Peter Clark, Helene Pohl) Photo: Latitude Creative

Sounds of the Sanctuary, the flagship programme in the New Zealand String Quartet's 2024 season, makes its way to Brook Waimārama Sanctuary on 28 October as part of the Nelson Arts Festival.

The quartet's violist and founding member Gillian Ansell talks with RNZ Concert's Andrew Clark about the programme, which evokes the beauty of nature and birdsong and includes music by Gareth Farr, Gillian Whitehead, Joseph Haydn and Antonin Dvořák.
