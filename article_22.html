Mana Moana: Live at Spark Arena

The 80-strong Signature Choir and the New Zealand Symphony Orchestra celebrate music of the Pacific Islands with an exuberant audience live at Auckland's Spark Arena.

Signature Choir and the NZSO on stage at Spark Arena, Auckland. Photo: Penina Momoisea, Coconet TV

Mana Moana celebrates the Pacific Islands through music. The Auckland concert features the Signature Choir from Wellington. But this time they are joined by their Auckland friends to swell the ranks of the choir to eighty voices. They sing with the New Zealand Symphony Orchestra under conductor Brent Stewart. Together they make a huge sound that fills the Spark Arena in Auckland. 

Mana Moana was created by the Signature Choir's leader Fepulea'i Helen Tupa’i. The orchestrations were written by Thomas Goss. The concert is introduced by Tofiga Fepulea'i.
