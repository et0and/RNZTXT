NZ's DJ Messie heads to Glastonbury

The Wellington-based DJ jumps on Music 101 before catching the plane to London  Audio

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

Wellington-based DJ Messie, aka Tessa Hills, has just been offered a spot on the stage at Glastonbury. 

Messie, who came to NZ fame when picked by Fred Again to support his massive New Zealand shows, speaks to Kara Rickard about playing their dream festival. 

A film crew are raising funds to document DJ Messie's Glastonbury experience. Their boosted campaign page is here: https://boosted.org.nz/projects/messie
