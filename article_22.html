Inside Out: Mike Nock retrospective

Ahead of Mike Nock's induction into the NZ Music Hall of Fame, Nick Tipping checks out some of the highlights of his career. Audio

Jazz pianist Mike Nock is being inducted into the NZ Music Hall of Fame. Photo: Karen Steains Photography

 

Ahead of Mike Nock's induction into the NZ Music Hall of Fame, Nick Tipping checks out some of the highlights of his career. Tracks range from jazz fusion with The Fourth Way and Almanac, and his landmark ECM album Ondas, to his acoustic and solo recordings from his more recent time in Australia.
