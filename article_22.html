Three to Seven host Bryan Crump chats with conductor Stéphane Denève ahead of the first of two concerts with the New Zealand Symphony Orchestra.

Denève is Music Director of the St Louis Symphony Orchestra and Artistic Director of the New World Symphony, an American orchestral academy for young musicians in preparation for professional careers in classical music.
