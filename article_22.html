New Horizons: Cowboy Carter

Beyoncé's glorious new Cowboy Carter album may well be this year's most startling and most discussed release. William Dart checks out Queen Bey's take on country music, which has already caused tremors in more conservative Nashville circles, with reference back to some of her earlier releases. Audio
