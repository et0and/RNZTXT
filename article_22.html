New Horizons: 'Splash of Anarchic'

With four programmes to go in the Countdown to the end of New Horizons, William Dart can't resist the spurring on of Daniel Beban's terrific new book, Future Jaw-Clap, to investigate the wild and wonderful musical world of Wellington's Primitive Art Group and the Braille Collective. Audio

('Splash of Anarchic' is quoted from Don McGlashan's blurb for the new book)
