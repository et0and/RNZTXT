Inside Out: Blue Note in my Suitcase

Nick Tipping features the recently released album from New Caledonian Hammond organist, Michel Bénébig. Audio

Photo: Michel Bénébig

 

New Caledonian Hammond organist Michel Bénébig has a new album out, called 'Blue Note in my Suitcase'. Also this hour, Freddie Hubbard and Gary Burton play classics, and NZ pianist Anita Schwabe presents a track from her album 'Eat Your Greens'.
