​This weekend, 40,000 people will converge at Auckland Domain for Synthony, a sold-out spectacle that has become New Zealand's largest one-day festival. 

Dance music icons Basement Jaxx are a headliner, and Kara Rickard tracked them down in Auckland’s Domain on the eve of the sold out show.
