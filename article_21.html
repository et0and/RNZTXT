RNZ Concert presents:

Here is how you can win tickets for an exclusive cinema screening of 12,000 Miles, a new documentary film following the journey of New Zealand teams competing at the World Pipe Band Championships in Glasgow, Scotland in 2023.

12,000 Miles is a spellbinding look into the journey New Zealanders took to compete at the World Pipe Band Championships 2023 in Glasgow, Scotland.

RNZ Concert is hosting preview cinema screenings of 12,000 Miles in Auckland, Wellington, and Christchurch in November.

At a screening you will see the documentary before its release and sit in on a live Q+A hosted by RNZ Concert Mornings host, and our very own Scotsman, Andrew Clark with special guests from the film.
