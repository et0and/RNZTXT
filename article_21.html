Congratulations to music scholar Professor Samantha Owens, who's just been awarded the Lilburn Research Fellowship for 2025. She talks with RNZ Concert's David Morriss about her research topic for the year: the history of brass bands in Aotearoa New Zealand from 1842 to 1920. Audio
