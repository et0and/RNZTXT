New video series: Imposter

Imposter is an insight into the journey of Māori musicians who are growing in their confidence, music, culture, and identity. With music as the vessel, this series aims to inspire more rangatahi Māori in their journey of overcoming self-doubt.

Series Classification: PG (Parental Guidance) | Watch the series here

Imposter is an insight into the journey of Māori musicians who are growing in their confidence, music, culture, and identity. Imposter syndrome is prominent all through Aotearoa. With music as the vessel, this series aims to inspire other rangatahi Māori in their journey of overcoming self-doubt.
