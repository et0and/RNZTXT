Concert for Animals

On Guy Fawkes night RNZ Concert offers a radio show of music specially chosen to be gentle for pets and their people during fireworks, in collaboration with the SPCA..

Concert for Animals will be broadcast nationwide on 5 November to comfort and entertain companion animals and their humans from 6pm until midnight on Guy Fawkes night.

Thank you for the great idea to counter the world's madness out there. Love RNZ Concert. Kindest, Sophie (the cat) & Alta. Photo: RNZ Concert listeners

In collaboration with SPCA, RNZ Concert is offering a radio show of music chosen to be gentle for pets during fireworks. 

Both organisations are keen to help improve the wellbeing of their communities. We know music is a positive part of many people’s lives, and we know many RNZ Concert listeners have beloved pets. Now we’re completing that circle with the SPCA’s expertise to provide an enjoyable listening experience for people and their furry friends on a night we know can be challenging for both.

Tanya's dogs Latke and Lox prefer baroque music. Photo: RNZ Concert listeners

We hope thousands of New Zealanders – fur and skin – will settle down and tune in to RNZ Concert on Guy Fawkes night! 

Listen to RNZ Concert on Tuesday 5 November for Concert for Animals between 6pm and midnight.

Tune in on the radio, or stream us from rnz.co.nz, the RNZ App, SKY TV channel 422, or Freeview Channel 51.
