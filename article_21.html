Singalongs and play alongs for all

Miriam McCombe, the Auckland Philharmonia's Learn & Participate Programme Manager, talks with Bryan Crump about what her job involves and some free community events coming up. Audio

Auckland Philharmonia’s Learn & Participate Programme Manager Miriam McCombe Photo: Thomas Hamill

Miriam McCombe, the Auckland Philharmonia's Learn & Participate Programme Manager, talks with Bryan Crump about what her job involves, and some free community events coming up.

For people who love to sing, there's an opportunity this Sunday to learn the famous Wedding March from Wagner’s Lohengrin.

And in September, instrumentalists and singers of all ages and abilities can be part of a huge orchestra in September to play movements from Holst's "The Planets" and Karl Jenkins' "The Armed Man".

Sunday Singalong with Wagner Sunday 4 August, 10.30am Supper Room, Auckland Town Hall

Register on the Auckland Philharmonia website

Community Bash Sunday 8 September, 2pm Great Hall, Auckland Town Hall

Register on the Auckland Philharmonia website
