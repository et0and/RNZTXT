An oratorio, a requiem and a psalm

Bay of Plenty choirs The Edgecumbe Choir and Scholars Baroque Aotearoa are preparing for performances of some big choral works over the next couple of weekends. RNZ Concert's Bryan Crump talks with the man in charge, Chalium Poppy. Audio

The Edgecumbe Choir Photo: Supplied / The Edgecumbe Choir

If you’re in the Bay of Plenty during the first couple of weekends in September, you’re in for some sonic treats.

On Saturday 7 and Sunday 8, The Edgecumbe Choir will be performing Haydn’s masterwork The Creation in Whakatane.

And the following weekend, chamber choir Scholars Baroque Aotearoa presents Schumann’s Requiem and Mendelssohn’s Psalm 42 (Mount Maunganui and Whakatane).

RNZ Concert’s Bryan Crump caught up with music director of both ensembles, Chalium Poppy, to talk about the concerts.
