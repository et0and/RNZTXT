Music Alive: Te Heke Waiata

LISTEN: This concert is a fusion of jazz songs and te reo Māori, featuring singers Whirimako Black, Allana Goldsmith and Leon Wharekura performing during the 2023 Auckland Arts Festival.

Join us at the Auckland Town Hall to listen to some beautiful jazz tunes at the 2023 Auckland Arts Festival.

Whirimako Black, Allana Goldsmith, Leon Wharekura and Dixon Nacey Photo: AAF

Multi-award-winner Whirimako Black (MNZM) (Ngāi Tūhoe) brings her dynamic performance energy and dulcet tones, performing original material as well as jazz standards in both te reo Māori and English.

Joining this legend onstage are bilingual vocal stars Allana Goldsmith (Ngāti Porou, Ngāi Tai) and Leon Wharekura (Ngaati Mahuta, Ngaati Whaawhaakia, Waikato) with Dixon Nacey (Mangaia, Rarotonga), musical director extraordinaire.
