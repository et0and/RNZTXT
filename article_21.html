In 2023, The Phoenix Foundation celebrated the 20th anniversary of their heralded debut album, Horsepower. The band marked the occasion by releasing the album on vinyl and embarking on a series of concerts across the motu, performing the album in its entirety along with crowd favorites from their back catalog. RNZ Music was there to capture the band's performance at Avondale's Hollywood Cinema in Auckland.
