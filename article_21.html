This special NZ Music Month Mixtape celebrates the hero guitars played by some of our most legendary musicians.  

Country singer/songwriter Glen Moffatt wrote a definitive list of the ten hero guitars of NZ music on Audioculture.

Of course once that list was published there was an outcry about which guitars had been left off the list…..so he’s just published another one.  Born in Napier, Glen became a well known performer in the clubs, town halls, theatres, and festivals of New Zealand before moving to Brisbane. He’s now a music historian at Audioculture, sharing his knowledge and appreciation for Kiwi music.
