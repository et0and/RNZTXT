New Horizons: Everyone's Getting Involved

William Dart reviews Everyone's Getting Involved, a new album which pays tribute to the 1984 Talking Heads concert film Stop Making Sense. Startling cover versions of the songs from Lorde, Miley Cyrus, The National, the Nigerian high life band The Cavemen, and others. Audio
