American alternative rock band Blonde Redhead are about to bring their new album to New Zealand with three shows planned.  

Kazu Makino and twin brothers Simone and Amedeo Pace formed the band in New York City in 1993. Thirty years on, their new album Sit Down for Dinner has been described as their finest work. 

Maggie Tweedie catches up with Simone ahead of their New Zealand shows. 

Blonde Redhead New Zealand Tour 2024

Saturday 22 June - Powerstation, Auckland Sunday 23 June - Meow, Wellington Monday 24 June - Loons, Lyttelton
