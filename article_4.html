Six arrested for serious assault that left victim's jaw broken

The assault took place in Pukekohe in 2023.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Six people have been arrested for a serious assault which took place in Pukekohe in September 2023.

The victim was allegedly involved in a disorder outside The Longkeeper bar and sustained a broken jaw in two places, which required surgery, Detective Senior Sergeant Simon Taylor said.

"At around 12.20am on 2 September, an officer was driving along King Street when he noticed a person on the ground surrounded by a group of people," Taylor said.

"It quickly became clear a fight had taken place and a person had been seriously injured."

Police have spent the past six months investigating.

The six people arrested are aged between 19-23.

They were charged with wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm and will reappear in Pukekohe District Court later this month.
