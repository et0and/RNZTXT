Police asking for help to find man missing for almost three months

Police and Joshua's family have serious concerns for his wellbeing.

Joshua, 54, has been missing for almost three months. Photo: Supplied/Police

Police are asking for the public's help to find Joshua, 54, who has been missing for almost three months.

Police and Joshua's family have serious concerns for his wellbeing.

They said he usually resided in Christchurch, was last seen in central Dunedin on Wednesday 11 December 2024.

Police said Joshua has not been in touch with his family since.

They said his vehicle was located in a café carpark on Camp Road in Dunedin.

Police are looking for anyone with infomation or sightings of anyone matching Joshua's photo, likely in Southland, Otago, or Canterbury districts.

Contact Police via 105, either online or over the phone, and reference the file number: 250228/6634.
