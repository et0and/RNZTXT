Country's most prolific stalker jailed for four years two months

Antonio Glen Castillano, AKA Glenn Green, admitted nine charges of criminal harassment, one of posting harmful digital communications and one charge of obtaining by deception.

Antonio Glen Castillano also known as Glenn Green appearing in Auckland District Court. Photo: Supplied / Stuff

The country's most prolific stalker Antonio Glen Castillano also known as Glenn Green has been sentenced to four years and two months imprisonment.

Antonio Castillano pleaded guilty to nine charges of criminal harassment, one charge of posting harmful digital communications and one charge of obtaining by deception.

He was sentenced in the Auckland District Court on Wednesday afternoon.
