Five seriously hurt, SH1 closes in Waikato after crash

Police said two vehicles were involved in the crash at Piarere.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Five people are injured and State Highway 1 closed in South Waikato after a serious crash this morning.

Police said two vehicles were involved in the crash at Piarere at about 5.30am.

Five people were seriously injured but three trapped in the wreckage had since been freed.

SH1 was closed between Horahora Road and Paparamu Road.

Traffic management was being arranged but people should expect delays, police said.
