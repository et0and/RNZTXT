Motorcyclist killed in Gisborne crash

A motorcyclist has died in a collision in Gisborne this afternoon.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A motorcyclist has died in a collision in Gisborne this afternoon.

Police say they were called to Gladstone Road just after 4pm, where a motorcycle had crashed into another vehicle.

They said the rider died at the scene.

Gladstone Road is closed between Campion Road and Aberdeen Road while the Serious Crash Unit investigates.
