One person dead following Ardmore crash

One person has died after a vehicle crashed on a rural Auckland road on Saturday night.

Photo: 123rf.com

One person has died after a vehicle crashed on a rural Auckland road on Saturday night.

Emergency services were called to the scene on Alfriston-Ardmore Road at about 8.50pm, police said.

One person died at the scene.

The Serious Crash Unit has examined the scene and enquiries are ongoing.
