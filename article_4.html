Person hit by car after disorder incident in Christchurch

A person has been taken to Christchurch Hospital after being hit by a car following a disorder incident in the city.

(file image) Photo: Supplied / Te Whatu Ora

A person has been taken to Christchurch Hospital after being hit by a car following a disorder incident in the city.

Police said the person was hit just before 3pm on Richards Ave in the suburb of Papanui.

The collision was linked to a disorder incident on nearby Greers Road, police said.
