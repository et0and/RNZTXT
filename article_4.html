KiwiRail to cut jobs in Palmerston North and Napier

It has to lower its costs to meet changing demands for freight, KiwiRail says.

Freight volumes to Napier Port have fallen. Photo: Supplied / Napier Port

KiwiRail has confirmed it plans to cut roles in Palmerston North and Napier as part of a restructure.

Chief operations officer Paul Ashton says is has to lower its costs to meet changing demands for freight -- including in Hawke's Bay where freight volumes to Napier Port have fallen.

The changes will affect staff and it is in talks with workers in Palmerston North and Napier, he said.

KiwiRail will not comment on the number of roles it was cutting back until after the consultation process is complete.
