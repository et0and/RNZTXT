Crews remain at scene of Auckland house fire

A large house fire in West Auckland has been put out but firefighters remain at the scene.

A two-storey property was found alight by fire crews. Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

Firefighters remain at the scene following a large house fire in West Auckland.

Crews were called to a two-storey property in the suburb of Massey shortly before 8.30pm on Thursday and found it well alight.

The blaze was estimated at 15x10 metres.

The fire was later extinguished but a sole crew remained at the house overnight.

Fire investigators were also called to the scene last night but have since left.
