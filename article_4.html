Farm shed fire in Tasman

Firefighters remain at the scene of a large shed fire in Tasman early on Saturday morning.

Fire and Emergency said at its peak, the fire was about 30 by 30 metres. Photo: Marika Khabazi / RNZ

Firefighters remain at the scene of a large shed fire in Tasman early on Saturday morning.

Six crews were called to a farm shed in Marahau about 3.30am.

Fire and Emergency said at its peak, the fire was about 30 by 30 metres.

The blaze was extinguished and crews were dampening hot spots.
