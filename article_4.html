The Auckland Santa Parade, in photos

Tens of thousands of people lined Auckland's CBD for the annual parade. Here are some of the best pictures.

Santa makes an appearance on Auckland's Queen Street. Photo: RNZ/ Kaye Albyt

Tens of thousands of people lined Auckland's CBD for Sunday's annual Santa Parade.

Crowd favourite Bluey the blue heeler pup took the lead, followed by a giant Shrek, a dragon with steam billowing from its mouth, dancers, and a brass band.

The parade finished with the other stars of the show, Santa and his reindeer.

It was followed by a party at Aotea Square until 5pm.
