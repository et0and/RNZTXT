State Highway 2 closed in Wairarapa after crash

State Highway 2 is closed at Mount Bruce between Masterton and Eketāhuna in Wairarapa after a serious crash.

Photo:

State Highway 2 is closed at Mount Bruce between Masterton and Eketāhuna in Wairarapa after a serious crash.

Waka Kotahi said the single vehicle crash was reported about 12.45pm.

The highway is blocked at the intersection of Hiwitoru Road.

Drivers should expect delays until the crash scene is cleared.

Police and Wellington Free Ambulance have been approached for comment.
