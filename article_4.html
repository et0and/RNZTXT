Man rescued from tree after getting stuck during police pursuit

The man swam away from officers across a river, but ran into trouble back on dry land, police say.

Photo: 123RF

A police chase in Taupō has ended up with an alleged offender having to be rescued from a tree by emergency services.

Police said they spotted a man tampering with boats at the Taupō marina on Sunday, who then dove into the water and swam to the Nukaka side of the Waikato River.

He swam back with officers in pursuit and then climbed up a tree where he got stuck, they said.

Firefighters were called to rescue him.

The man is now in custody.

Police asked boaties in the area to get in contact if they noticed any interference to their vessels.
