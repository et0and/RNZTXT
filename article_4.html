Person dies in workplace incident after quad bike rolls in Tauranga

A person has died in a workplace incident where a quad bike rolled in Tauranga, Bay of Plenty.

(file image) Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

A person has died in a workplace incident where a quad bike rolled in Tauranga, Bay of Plenty.

Police said emergency services responded to the incident on Upper Ohauiti Road, Ohauiti about 9.20am.

They said inquiries were ongoing into the circumstances of the incident.

WorkSafe has been advised.
