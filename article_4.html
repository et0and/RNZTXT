Severe thunderstorm warning for areas of Gisborne

MetService has issued a severe thunderstorm warning for the Gisborne area.

The severe thunderstorms were moving towards the east-northeast, and were expected to lie near Tolaga Bay, Waimata, Kanakanaia and Tauwhareparae.

These thunderstorms were expected to be accompanied by torrential rain and hail, MetService said.

The severe thunderstorms were expected to lie near Tolaga Bay, Waimata, Kanakanaia and Tauwhareparae. Photo: MetService

It warned that torrential rain could cause surface and flash flooding by streams, in gullies and in urban areas, and make driving conditions extremely hazardous.

The warning is due to be updated or lifted by 4.52pm today.

A severe thunderstorm watch is also in place for Gisborne and Hawke's Bay until 10pm Saturday.
