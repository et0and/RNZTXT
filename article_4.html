Search for person missing from boat on Waitaki River

The person was reported missing on Monday night.

The Waitaki River. Photo: 123rf.com

A search is underway for a person who went missing from a boat on the Waitaki River last night.

Police say a report of the water incident came through at 7pm.

The river in the South Island is a large braided river that runs 209km long.

Coastguard and police were carrying out a search.
