One rescued from house fire in Whakatāne

A person was rescued from a house fire in Whakatāne this afternoon, and has been treated by ambulance staff.

Five fire trucks and about 20 firefighters were called out to tackle the blaze. File photo. Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

A person was rescued from a house fire in Whakatāne this afternoon, and has been treated by ambulance staff.

Fire and Emergency (FENZ) was called to the fire at a property on Arawa Street shortly after 4pm.

Five fire trucks and about 20 firefighters were tackling the blaze at its peak.

FENZ said a person was rescued from the house and was being treated by ambulance staff.

St John ambulance said one ambulance and one helicopter is in attendance, but could not confirm the patient's status.

FENZ said the fire was extinguished before 5pm.
