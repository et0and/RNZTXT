Car crashes into side of apartment building

One person has been transferred to Auckland City Hospital in a serious condition.

Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

One person has been seriously injured after a car crashed into an apartment building in the Auckland suburb of Grey Lynn on Sunday.

Hato Hone St John said it responded to a traffic related incident at the intersection of Williamson Avenue and Pollen Street at 3.50pm.

An ambulance, rapid response unit and operations manager were dispatched and transported one patient in a serious condition to Auckland City Hospital, it said.

Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

Police said the road is partially blocked while emergency services attend.
