One dead in Auckland crash

A person has died following a two-vehicle crash in the Auckland suburb of Grey Lynn.

File photo. Photo: 123RF

A person has died following a two-vehicle crash in the Auckland suburb of Grey Lynn on Saturday morning.

Emergency services were called to Great North Road about 3:35am.

The person died at the scene.

The serious crash unit is carrying out a scene examination.
