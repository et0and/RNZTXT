Man found dead in middle of Auckland road

Police are investigating a death in Point England.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

A man has been found dead in the middle of Point England Road, Auckland.

Police say he was found at 11.15pm and they are working to determine the circumstances of his death.

They are asking anyone with information to contact them and and quote file number 241006/5254.
