Fog delays and cancels flights in Auckland

One domestic regional flight has been cancelled and 12 have been delayed due to the heavy fog.

Fog restrictions have been in place at Auckland Airport since 7am (file image). Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

Flights were cancelled and delayed in Auckland after fog enveloped the city.

Fog restrictions have lifted since being put in place at Auckland Airport from 7am on Saturday.

Four domestic regional flights were cancelled and 11 were delayed due to the heavy fog.

Auckland Airport said no international flights had been affected.
