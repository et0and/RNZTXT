Person dead after being burned by coal range

They were flown to Hutt Hospital in Wellington after falling onto the range on Tuesday afternoon.

Boyce Street in Renwick. Photo: Anthony Phelps / RNZ

A person has died in hospital after being badly burnt by a coal range at a house in Renwick, near Blenheim.

They were flown to Hutt Hospital in Wellington on Tuesday afternoon after falling onto the range on Boyce Street.

Police referred the death to the coroner.
