Christchurch rattled by shallow quake

The quake was centred 5km south-east of Christchurch.

A 2.4 magnitude quake was centred 5km south-east of Christchurch. Photo: GeoNet

Christchurch has been rattled by a small earthquake.

GeoNet said the shallow, sharp jolt happened at 6.20am on Tuesday.

The 2.4 magnitude quake was centred 5km south-east of Christchurch and was at a depth of 4km.

More than 600 people reported feeling the quake within minutes of it happening.
