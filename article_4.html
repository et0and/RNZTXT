Man arrested for sexual conduct with child, unlawful recordings

The 40-year-old Auckland man is facing 42 charges for his alleged crimes, spanning nearly a decade.

Photo: 123rf.com

An Auckland man has been arrested this week over unlawful video recordings of women and young girls over nearly a decade.

The police say the 40 year-old is facing 42 charges, related to covertly making and possessing intimate visual recordings and objectionable material.

He has also been charged with sexual conduct with a child under 12.

More charges in Operation Frost were possible.

Detective Sergeant Rick Veacock said the investigation, led by Auckland City's Child Exploitation team, has so far identified 22 victims over a period of eight years.
