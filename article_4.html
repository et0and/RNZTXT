Two critically hurt men arrested after Auckland incident

The injured men are facing charges after "a disorder incident' in west Auckland overnight..

Photo: 123rf

Two men are facing charges after an assault in the Auckland suburb of Glendene earlier this morning.

Police say they responded to a disorder on Scowns Way, where two people had been injured, shortly before 2am.

The two people were taken to hospital with critical injuries, however, their condition is now listed as stable.

Police say they the men, aged 25 and 37, have been charged with wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm.
