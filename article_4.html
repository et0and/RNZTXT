One dead after Northland crash

A person has died after a single vehicle car crash in Whangārei this afternoon.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A person has died after a single vehicle car crash in Whangārei this afternoon.

Police were called to the scene on Church Road in Te Kamo about 3.15pm.

They said one person died at the scene.

The serious crash unit was advised and an investigation is under way.
