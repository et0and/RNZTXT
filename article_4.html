Road closed following serious two-vehicle crash near Whanganui

Parihauhau Road is closed at Parikino following a two-vehicle crash off SH4 this afternoon.

Photo: RNZ / Patrice Allen

Parihauhau Road is closed at Parikino following a two-vehicle crash off SH4 this afternoon.

Police said emergency services responded to the crash near Whanganui around 1:40pm.

The Serious Crash Unit has been advised.

Parihauhau Road is closed while emergency services respond, and a scene examination is conducted.

Police are advising motorists to take an alternate route.
