Firearm presented during Wellington car theft

Police say the Strathmore Park theft is believed to be related to a separate incident in the central city.

Police were called to Ngatiapa Street in Strathmore Park shortly after 4am. Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Police are investigating a car theft in a Wellington suburb where a firearm was presented Sunday morning.

Police were called to Ngatiapa Street in Strathmore Park shortly after 4am.

A police spokesperson said there were no injuries and they were working to determine what happened.

They said another incident on Webb Street in the central city was believed to be related.
