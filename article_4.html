Police hunt for armed man after petrol station robbery

A man entered the store with a gun, demanding money overnight.

(file station) Photo: RNZ / Alexander Robertson

Police are on the hunt for an armed man after a robbery at a petrol station in Miramar, Wellington.

Officers were called to the scene at the Miramar Z station about 3.45am, after a man entered the store with a gun, demanding money.

A scene guard is in place.

Police said anyone with information to get in contact.
