Incident in Parliament grounds leaves one critically injured

Police were called to the scene about 7.30am and a person has been taken to hospital.

Photo: RNZ / REECE BAKER

A person has been taken to Wellington Hospital in a critical condition, after an incident in the Parliament grounds this morning.

Police say they were called to Parliament about 7.30am, after a report of a person being injured.

Wellington Free Ambulance took a patient in a critical condition to Wellington Hospital.

Enquiries into the circumstances are ongoing.
