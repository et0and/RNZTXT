Police appeal for sightings of man missing for a month

Brett Jason McKenzie was reported missing on 16 November and could be in the Christchurch or Nelson/Tasman area, say police.

Brett Jason McKenzie, 40, was reported missing on 16 November, 2023. Photo: NZ Police / Supplied

Police are appealing for the public's help to find Brett Jason McKenzie, 40, who has been missing for a month.

Brett was reported missing on 16 November 2023 and police and his family are concerned for his wellbeing.

Brett could be in Christchurch or the Nelson / Tasman area, said police.

Anyone who has seen Brett or has information on his whereabouts is urged to contact Police on 105, quoting file number 231116/5317.
