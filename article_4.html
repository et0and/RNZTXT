Two critical, highway closed in Otago crash

SH8 near Alexandra has shut after two cars collided, hurting three people.

Two people are critically hurt after the collision. Photo: RNZ / Patrice Allen

Two people are critically hurt and a section of State Highway in Otago is closed after two cars collided.

Police were called to the scene at Fruitlands, near Alexandra, about noon on Wednesday.

A third person had moderate injuries, police said.

Drivers are warned to expect delays to avoid travelling in the area if possible.
