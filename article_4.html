Crash closes lanes on Auckland's Southern Motorway

Motorists should expect delays or find another way to travel home with all but one southbound lane shut, police say.

Photo: SUPPLIED

Three vehicles have crashed heading south on Auckland's Southern Motorway.

Police said that crash in Penrose was reported around 3.30pm on Tuesday.

One person has received minor injuries as a result.

Police said all but one southbound lane have been closed.

"Motorists are advised to expect delays, delay their commute home, or find an alternate route."
