Boy, 15, missing in South Auckland

Police have described him as being about 180cm tall with black hair and green eyes.

Giovanni, 15, was last seen in the Mangere area on Sunday evening. Photo: Supplied / NZ Police

Police are looking for a 15-year-old boy who has been missing in South Auckland since Sunday night.

Police have described Giovanni as being about 180cm tall with black hair and green eyes. He was last seen in the Māngere area on Sunday evening.

His family have concerns for his wellbeing, police said.

If you have any information regarding his whereabouts, please contact police online or call 105 and quote file number 250310/3086.
