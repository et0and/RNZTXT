Teen missing from Auckland's St Johns since Friday

Leanah's family and police have concerns for her wellbeing.

Leanah has been missing since 27 July. Photo: Supplied / Police

There are concerns for the wellbeing of a teenager reported missing from the Auckland suburb of St Johns.

Leanah, 15, was last seen on Friday 27 July.

She is described by police as of a medium build, about 165cm tall with brown eyes and long straight black hair.

Leanah was last seen wearing blue jeans, a brown top, black puffer jacket and white crocs.

Both her family and police have concerns for her wellbeing.

Anyone with information on her whereabouts was asked to call 111 and reference file number 240727/7285.

Further information can be given on 105, or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
