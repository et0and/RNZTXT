Man dead after quad bike crash in South Canterbury

One person is dead after a quad bike crash north of Timaru on Saturday night.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

One person is dead after a quad bike crash north of Timaru on Saturday night.

Emergency services were called to the intersection of Prattley Road and Macaulay Road in Milford around 11.40pm.

A quad bike had left the road and the male rider died at the scene, police said.

A scene examination was completed by the Serious Crash Unit, and the circumstances remain under investigation.
