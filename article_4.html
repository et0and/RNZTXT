Houghton Bay residents may spend night without water

Residents in the Wellington suburb of Houghton Bay could be without water into the early hours of the morning following a burst pipe.

Generic water into glass Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

Residents in the Wellington suburb of Houghton Bay could be without water into the early hours of the morning following a burst pipe.

Wellington Water says crews are working on a fixing the problem, but properties on View Road, Twomey Grove and Hungerford Road could lose their water service.

It was a complex repair and the crew will be working on it late into the evening and early hours of tomorrow morning, Wellington Water said.
