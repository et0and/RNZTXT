'Serious injuries' in North Island crash

The two-vehicle crash near Takapau, central Hawke's Bay, closed State Highway 2.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

A two-vehicle crash in central Hawkes Bay has closed State Highway 2 north of Takapau.

The crash was reported to emergency services about 8.10am on Sunday.

Police said initial indications were that there were serious injuries.

SH2 was closed north of Takapau and was expected to say closed for most of the day, police said.

A detour was in place via SH50.

Motorists were advised to avoid the area and expect ongoing delays.
