Shots fired at Auckland house overnight

No injuries were reported but an increased police presence in Māngere could be expected.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Police are investigating after shots were fired a South Auckland house overnight.

Officers were called to the scene on Plumley Crescent in Māngere just after 8.30pm on Monday.

No injuries have been reported.

Residents could expect an increased presence in the area, police said, as they urged those with information to come forward.
