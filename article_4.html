Two dead in crash near Taupō

Two people have died in a crash in Taupō this afternoon.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Two people have died in a crash in Taupō this afternoon.

Another person was critically hurt in the two-vehicle crash on Broadlands Road reported about 4.10pm.

The road is expected to remain closed for some time.
