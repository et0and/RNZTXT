Busy Auckland mall evacuated in false alarm

Newmarket's Westfield Mall was cleared out during busy Boxing Day shopping after a fog cannon was accidentally set off.

Westfield Newmarket shopping mall. Photo: Google Maps

A busy Auckland shopping mall has been evacuated after a fog cannon was accidentally set off.

Fire and Emergency sent two fire trucks to the Westfield Mall at Newmarket on Tuesday morning.

Hundreds of waiting shoppers have now been let back in.
