Woman missing in Hawke's Bay since Sunday

Police are appealing for information on the whereabouts of Jessica Kim, who was reported missing on Sunday and last seen getting into a ute in Whakatū in Hawke's Bay.

Jessica Kim was reported missing on Sunday, 14 April. Photo: Supplied / NZ Police

Police are appealing for information on the whereabouts of Jessica Kim, who was reported missing on Sunday and last seen getting into a ute in Whakatū in Hawke's Bay.

Police said Kim, who has waist-length dreadlocks and is in her 20s, was last seen between 10.20am and 10.50am on 14 April getting into a single-cab ute towing a trailer.

Two men appeared to be in the ute, they said.

Police want to hear from anyone who can assist in locating her, including the people who picked her up or anyone who was travelling on Te Ara Kahikatea at the time and could be of assistance.

Anyone with information is asked to call 105 or report online at 105.police.govt.nz using reference number 240415/8098, or to call 111 if they have urgent information to report.
