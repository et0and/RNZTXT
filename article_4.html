Gas leak closes roads on Auckland's North Shore

Police are asking people to avoid the Milford area.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police are asking people to avoid the Milford area on Auckland's North Shore because of a gas leak.

Road closures are in place along Shakespeare and Alma Roads and East Coast and Shakespeare Roads.

Emergency services are on Shakespeare Road.
