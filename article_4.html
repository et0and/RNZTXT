Man critically hurt after being hit by two vehicles in Whakatāne

Police are appealing for the driver of a blue SUV to come forward.

A male pedestrian was injured on White Pine Bush Road, just outside Whakatāne. Photo:

A man is in a critical condition in hospital after being hit by at least two vehicles near Whakatāne early on Sunday morning.

One of the drivers was assisting police with their investigation, but police appealed for the driver of a second car - described as a blue SUV - to come forward.

A male pedestrian was seriously injured on White Pine Bush Road, Whakatāne, shortly after midnight on 18 August. Photo: Google Maps

The man was walking on White Pine Bush Road, Whakatāne, when he was hit shortly after midnight on Sunday, police said.

It was believed the injured man was hit by at least two vehicles.

The Serious Crash Unit attended and an investigation was underway.

Anyone in the area around the time of the crash should contact 105 - the police non-emergency service - or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
