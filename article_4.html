Three injured after logging truck and car collide on Northland highway

Three people - including two children - have been injured after a logging truck and a car collided at a one-way bridge in Northland today.

SH15 in Northland has been blocked in both directions. File photo. Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Three people - including two children - have been injured after a logging truck and a car collided at a one-way bridge in Northland today.

Emergency services are responding the serious crash on State Highway 15 - or Mangakahia Road - in Northland.

The collision, which occurred just after 3pm, has blocked the highway in both directions near Huka Road. There are no diversions at this stage.

It left the male driver of the car with multiple fractures.

Two children in the car were also injured. Police said two people had been taken to hospital with serious injuries.

Police, fire-fighters, St John Ambulance and the Northland Rescue Helicopter are responding.
