Police investigate unexplained death in Auckland's Morningside

The woman's body was discovered after police were called to the Don Croot Street property around 9.15pm on Friday.

Photo: RNZ

Police are investigating after a woman was found dead at a house in Auckland's Morningside.

The woman's body was discovered after officers were called to the Don Croot Street property around 9.15pm on Friday.

CPR was performed, but the woman was unable to be revived.

The woman's death is currently being treated as unexplained and a police investigation has been launched.

A scene examination will be carried out at the property on Saturday.

Police are asking anyone with information about the incident to contact them.
