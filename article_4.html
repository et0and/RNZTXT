Incident at Plant Food and Research facility

The incident is believed to involve chemicals, FENZ says.

Generic fire truck. Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

Fire and Emergency (FENZ) are responding to an incident at the Plant and Food Research facility in the Auckland suburb of Mt Albert.

Staff have been evacuated from the site.

Police say they are assisting FENZ and the incident was reported about 12.45pm.

The facility, on Mt Albert Road, is a government-owned Crown Research Institute.

Hone Hato St John are also at the scene.
