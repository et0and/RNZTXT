Evacuations after gas main ruptures on Auckland's North Shore - report

The gas main has burst on Birkenhead Avenue - affecting traffic, including all buses.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

A gas main has burst on Birkenhead Avenue on Auckland's North Shore on Wednesday afternoon.

The News Zealand Herald is reporting some nearby properties had been evacuated as a precaution.

Auckland Transport said a section of the road between Pupuke Road and Onewa Road was closed, and motorists were being asked to avoid the area.

It said all traffic had been affected, including buses.

Diversions had been put in place.
