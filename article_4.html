Watch live: Luxon speaks during visit to Port Hills

Fire and Emergency crews have been at the scene on Thursday dampening down hot spots.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon has visited the Port Hills fireground in Canterbury as crews work on dampening hot spots.

Fire and Emergency Incident Commander Dave Key said crews put in a big effort this week to eliminate most of the hot spots.

The state of emergency, which was in place since the wildfire tore through hundreds of hectares of forest and scrub last week, was lifted on Wednesday.
