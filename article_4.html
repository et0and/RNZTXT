In pictures: NZers gather to celebrate Māori New Year

From Treble Cone to Bastion Point see how Aotearoa celebrated the Māori New Year.

Sunrise over Lake Wānaka. Photo: Pokere Paewai/RNZ

Matariki commemorations began before dawn around the country this morning with the national observance held at Treble Cone near Wānaka this morning.

Hosted by Ngāi Tahu, Prime Minister Christopher Luxon was among those who attended.

The holiday has been celebrated with karakia and hautapu ceremony.
