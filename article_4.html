Person in critical condition after being hit by vehicle

Brockville Road in Dunedin is closed and motorists are advised to take an alternative route.

The person is being taken to hospital. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police say a person is in critical condition after being hit by a vehicle in Dunedin.

They said the person was being taken to hospital.

The crash on Brockville Road was reported at 4.15pm.

The road remains closed and motorists are advised to take an alternative route.
