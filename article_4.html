Extensive damage after Paraparaumu house fire

Nine fire crews were called to the blaze.

File photo. Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

A home in Paraparaumu, near Wellington, is seriously damaged after a fire.

Nine fire crews were called to a house on Tuesday night, where the entire property was ablaze.

Fire and Emergency said crews were able to extinguish the fire, but the damage was extensive.
