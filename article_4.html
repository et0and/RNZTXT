Two arrested after death of Napier homeless man

Two men have been arrested and charged following the death of a Napier homeless man.

Photo:

Two men have been arrested and charged following the death of a Napier homeless man.

Boy Taylor, 58, who has been described as living on the streets, died following an incident in Napier on Wednesday.

Two men, aged 21 and 33, have been charged with wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm and are expected to appear in Hastings District Court on Monday.

Police cannot rule out the possibility of further arrests and charges.
