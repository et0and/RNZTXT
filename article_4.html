Homicide investigation launched after death of homeless man

Police have launched a homicide investigation after the death of a 58-year-old Napier man.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police have launched a homicide investigation after the death of a 58-year-old Napier man.

Boy Taylor was found dead on Emerson Street in the central city early on Wednesday morning.

Detective Inspector David De Lange said a post mortem has confirmed Taylor suffered injuries consistent with assault.

He said a large team of investigators is working on this investigation, and they would like to hear from anyone with information about any incident in the area from around 2am.

Comments on social media paint a picture of a friendly, harmless man who lived on the streets.
