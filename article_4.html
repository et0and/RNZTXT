One dead in Northland crash

One person has died in a single vehicle crash in Hikurangi, north of Whāngarei.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

One person has died in a single vehicle crash in Hikurangi, north of Whāngarei.

Police said the crash on Marua Road was reported about 7.20pm

An investigation is underway and motorists are asked to avoid the area.
