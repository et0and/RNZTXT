A woman has died after quad bike accident in Marokopa this afternoon.

In a statement, police said emergency services were called to Rauparaha Street in the south Waikato township shortly before 5pm, after witnesses reported seeing a person come off a quad bike.

Enquiries are ongoing and the Serious Crash Unit is investigating.
