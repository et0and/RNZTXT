Puketaha Primary School students evacuated after fire in roof

The fire in the school hall's roof had been brought under control using water from the pool.

Puketaha Primary School. Photo: Supplied / Google Maps

Children at a Waikato primary school have been evacuated due to a fire in the roof of the school's hall.

Fire and Emergency northern fire communications shift manager Ryan Geen said the fire at Puketaha Primary School, on the outskirts of Hamilton, was reported at midday on Wednesday.

Geen said four fire trucks, two water tankers, two volunteer brigades and a ladder truck from Manukau were at the scene.

He said the fire was brought under control just after 1pm using water from the school pool.

Geen said there were no injuries to report.
