Family of teen killed in crash express forgiveness for killer

Adam Michael Rapson was behind the wheel of an unsafe car that smashed into a house near Rangiora at high speed after fleeing police.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

The family of Zara Mitchell have expressed their forgiveness for the teenager who caused her death in September last year.

Adam Michael Rapson was behind the wheel of an unsafe car that smashed into a house near Rangiora at high speed on 17 September after fleeing police.

Mitchell, an 18-year-old, was killed by the crash, while two other passengers were injured.

Rapson was today jailed for three and a half years.
