Homicide investigation launched after man's death, arrests made

The man was found unconscious at a Te Awamutu address, but later died.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

A homicide investigation is underway after the death of a man in Te Awamutu.

In a statement, police said it received a call to an incident on Te Rahu Road about 11.45pm on Tuesday.

A man was found unconscious and despite medical intervention, he died at the scene.

Police have since arrested a 27-year-old man and a 34-year-old woman who were at the scene.

"They are now assisting police with our investigation and charges are being considered.

"Police are very early in our investigation and our focus is on establishing the circumstances leading into the incident and to support the victim's family.

"Police cannot rule out the possibility of further arrests as the investigation progresses."

Police said a scene examination was being carried out and traffic management was in place.

People could expect to see an increased presence of police in the area.

Anyone with information about the man's death was asked to contact police on 105 or online, quoting reference number 241016/0455.
