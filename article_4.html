Some Auckland train services cancelled

All Eastern Line services between Ōtāhuhu and Manukau have been cancelled this evening.

Photo: RNZ / Nick Monro

All Eastern Line services between Ōtāhuhu and Manukau have been cancelled this evening in Auckland.

Auckland Transport says a track fault was the cause.

Buses will accept train tickets to and from all stations affected.

Earlier a KiwiRail track issue near Britomart meant all Western Line services were cancelled between Britomart and Newmarket.

Minor delays were expected as the Western Line services returned to normal.
