Bryan Adams to tour NZ in 2025

Award-winning Canadian singer-songwriter Bryan Adams has announced two concerts in Aotearoa.

Canadian singer-songwriter Bryan Adams will play two concerts in Aotearoa in February 2025. Photo: Supplied

Award-winning Canadian singer-songwriter Bryan Adams has announced two New Zealand concerts for February 2025.

Adams, 64, has amassed a large collection of hits, accolades and awards over the past 40 years.

His 1991 song, ('Everything I Do) I Do It For You' has sold more than 15 million copies worldwide and is one of the best-selling singles of all time.

Adams last played in Aotearoa in 2019, although his Christchurch concert was cancelled following the 15 March mosque attacks.

Adams will play two New Zealand concerts in 2025: 1 February at Wolfbrook Arena, Christchurch, and 4 February at Auckland's Spark Arena.

English singer-songwriter James Arthur, one of the world's biggest streaming artists, will play support.

Pre-sale tickets are available from 2 September.
