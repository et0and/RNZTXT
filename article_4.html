Public asked to help find missing 12-year-old thought to be in West Auckland

Kassius left her home in Glendene on 12 January.

Twelve-year-old Kassius. Photo: Supplied / Police

Police are asking for the public's help to find a missing 12-year-old in Auckland.

Kassius was reported missing by her family after leaving her home in Glendene on 12 January.

Police say they believe Kassius is in the Sunnyvale or New Lynn areas.

Because of her age, her family and police are concerned, a police spokesperson says.

Anyone with information is encouraged to contact police.
