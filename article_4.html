Man dies after motorcycle crash in Chatham Islands

A man has died after a motorbike crash in the Chatham Islands.

(File photo) Photo: RNZ / Patrice Allen

A man has died after a motorbike crash in the Chatham Islands.

Police responded to the incident on Port Hutt Road at about 7pm on Saturday 2 November, and the single motorcycle rider was transported to hospital.

However, he had since died, police said in a statement.

Officers are investigating what happened.
