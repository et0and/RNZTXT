Driver dead after crashing into building

A driver whose vehicle hit a building in Howick on Friday has died.

The intersection of Cook and Picton streets in Howick, Auckland. Photo: Google Maps

A driver whose vehicle hit a building in Auckland's Howick on Friday has died.

The car collided with a building near the intersection with Cook and Picton Streets, police said, just before 6.30pm.

The driver was the only person in the vehicle.

Police said they were making inquiries on behalf of the coroner.
