State Highway 2 at Eskdale blocked after serious crash

Police say a person is reported to have critical injuries following a serious crash in Eskdale, Hawke's Bay.

One person is reported to have critical injuries in the crash. Photo: RNZ / Diego Opatowski

Police say a person is reported to have critical injuries following a serious crash in Eskdale, Hawke's Bay.

The crash, involving a truck and a car, happened at the intersection of State Highway 2 and Taits Road.

It was reported to police just before 3pm.

The road is blocked and traffic management is in place.
