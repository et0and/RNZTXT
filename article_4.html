Businesses in the dark as power outages hit Auckland's Ponsonby

Businesses in the Auckland suburb, and parts of Freemans Bay, say they've faced intermittent cuts this morning.

Power cuts in Auckland on Friday morning. Photo: Screenshot

Businesses on Auckland's Ponsonby Road are facing intermittent power cuts on Friday morning.

Vector Energy was investigating a fault impacting most of Ponsonby and parts of Freemans Bay, but hasn't confirmed how many customers were impacted.

Ponsonby Road cafe Sol's Place said it lost power between 6-7am, and then again from 7.45-8.15am.

Fire and Emergency said it was called to a fire at a power plinth in Mt Eden at half-past-seven, but couldn't confirm if it was related to the outage.
