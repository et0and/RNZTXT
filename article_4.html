Car drives through shop-front in Wellington CBD

Emergency services were called to a central Wellington street after a car drove through a shop-front on Monday afternoon.

Emergency services were called to a central Wellington street after a car drove through a shop-front on Monday afternoon.

A bystander said they had been upstairs completing renovations when they heard the commotion at about 1.45pm on Brandon Street.

They said the driver - an older woman - was shaken, but appeared uninjured.

The shop was empty at the time, and was not in use.

The place is being cleaned up and secured until repairs can be done.
