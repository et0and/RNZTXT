Concerns for woman missing in Waikato

Hamilton people are being asked to check sheds and outbuildings for a missing woman.

Photo: Supplied / NZ police

Police are appealing for any sightings of Cassandra Clarkin, who has been reported missing from the Hamilton suburb of Glenview.

Clarkin was last seen at a residence on Ohaupo Road, Glenview about 4.30pm on Monday.

Police and her family have concerns for her wellbeing.

Police asked residents in the area to check any sheds and outbuildings on their properties in case Clarkin sought shelter overnight.

She was wearing a cream and pink short-sleeved top and a black and brown knee length skirt when she went missing.

Anyone with information on her whereabouts is asked to call 111, using the reference file number 240514/8398.
