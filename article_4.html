Boat carrying 500L of diesel capsizes off the Port of Napier

Potential pollution from the site of a capsize off the Port of Napier breakwater will be monitored this morning.

File photo. Photo: Supplied / Napier Port

Potential pollution from the site of a capsize off the Port of Napier breakwater will be monitored this morning.

Four people were rescued from the recreational boat on Friday afternoon.

Hawke's Bay Regional Council said the 10.5 metre wooden recreational craft hit a log.

No one was injured, but the boat was carrying 500 litres of diesel at the time of the collision.

The vessel has been towed to port and will be lifted out by crane, before being taken to a nearby sailing club at the owner's expense.
