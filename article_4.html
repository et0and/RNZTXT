Name released for woman found dead in Blenheim

A 55-year-old man has been charged with her murder.

Police tape blocks off a US crime scene. Photo: AFP or licensors

Police can now release the name of the woman who was found dead at a property in Blenheim earlier this week.

Jennifer Phyllis Sheehan, a 78-year-old woman, was found dead at the Muller Road property on Monday.

Police were called to the property about 8.30am.

The 55-year-old man charged with her murder has been remanded in custody ahead of his next appearance in the High Court at Wellington on 13 December.
