Concerns for missing Auckland teen

The 16-year-old was last seen leaving his home in Weymouth on Tuesday to go fishing.

Photo: Supplied / Police

There are safety concerns for a missing teen who was last seen preparing to go fishing in south Auckland.

Paitele, 16, was last seen on Tuesday, leaving his Weymouth home.

He was wearing a black jersey with 'FRESH' written in red on the front, grey shorts and black shoes.

He was carrying a white bucket of fishing bait and a small, yellow fishing rod.

Police and his family had concerns for his safety given his age, police said.

Anyone who saw Paitele was asked to call police on 111.

Any further information could be provided on 105, quoting quoting file number 240522/9408.
