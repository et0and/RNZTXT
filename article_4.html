Serious injuries, SH2 blocked following two vehicle crash near Waihi

Initial indications suggest there are serious injuries. SH2 is blocked and diversions are in place.

Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

Emergency services are responding to a two-vehicle crash on State Highway 2 (SH2) near Tauranga Road, Waihi, Hauraki District.

Police said emergency services received reports of the crash about 5.40pm.

Initial indications suggest there are serious injuries.

SH2 is blocked and diversions are in place at the intersections of SH2, Trig Road South and Waihi Beach Road.

Motorists are advised to take the alternative route.
