Motorcycle rider dead in Auckland crash

A motorcycle rider has died after a crash with a vehicle in Auckland Saturday evening.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

A motorcycle rider has died after a crash with a vehicle in Auckland Saturday evening.

Emergency services were called to the scene at 5.30pm on Postman Road in Dairy Flat, but the motorcycle rider died at the scene.

Police said they were providing support to the man's family and the driver of the other vehicle.

An investigation into the crash is ongoing.
