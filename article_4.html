Sudden death in West Auckland under investigation

An increased police presence can be expected in Rānui following the unexplained death.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Police are investigating a sudden death in West Auckland.

A police spokesperson said emergency services responded to reports of a death at a property in Rānui shortly before 8am on Tuesday.

The death is being treated as unexplained.

A scene examination is underway and the public can expect to see an increased police presence in the area.
