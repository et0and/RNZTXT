One person dead from 'medical event' in Auckland crash

Several roads are closed in Mt Albert and drivers asked to avoid the area.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

One person has died at the scene of a crash in Auckland's Mount Albert on Friday morning.

Police said the single-vehicle crash happened on Mount Albert Road, near the intersection with Scout Avenue.

A spokesperson said it appeared one person died from a medical event.

Traffic management was being put in place and drivers were advised to expect delays.

Diversions were at Frost Avenue, Bremner Avenue and McCullough Avenue.
