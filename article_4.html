Person in critical condition after two-vehicle crash in Paeroa

Emergency services were called to Thames Road just before 7am.

(file image) Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

A person is in critical condition after a two-vehicle crash in the Waikato town of Paeroa on Sunday morning.

Emergency services were called to Thames Road just before 7am.

A helicopter, two ambulances and a rapid response unit were at the scene.

Police said the Serious Crash Unit had been notified and diversions would be in place.
