West Auckland hit-and-run victim in critical condition

Police are hunting a driver who hit a person in Henderson shortly after midnight and did not stop.

The incident happened on Railside Ave, Henderson, at around 12.25am. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police are hunting a driver who hit a person in West Auckland in the early hours of Saturday morning and did not stop.

The victim is in hospital in a critical condition.

The incident happened on Railside Ave, Henderson, at around 12.25am.

"Police would like to hear from anyone who witnessed this incident and has not yet spoken to us," police said on Saturday.

"We would also like to hear from anyone who has CCTV or dashcam footage that may assist our enquiries."

Anyone with information was urged to call police on 105 and quote file number 241214/7801.
