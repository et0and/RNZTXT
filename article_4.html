Fire crews fight blaze in Onehunga commercial building

A dozen firefighters remain at the scene.

Fire crews are responding to fire at a commercial building in Auckland.

Fire and Emergency says 15 trucks and 60 firefighters were initially sent to fight the blaze at the single level building in the suburb of Onehunga.

A dozen firefighters remain at the scene.

The fire was estimated at 20 by 30 metres.
