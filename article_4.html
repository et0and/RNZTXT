SH1 northbound closed after boat falls from trailer

All northbound lanes of the motorway after Oteha Valley Rd off-ramp have closed.

Traffic building on SH1 after a boat fell from a trailer. Photo: Screenshot / Waka Kotahi NZTA

State Highway 1 northbound after Oteha Valley Rd off-ramp on Auckland's north shore is closed.

Waka Kotahi NZTA advises people to follow the directions of emergency services and consider using Oteha Valley off-ramp to Oteha Valley on-ramp.

The motorway is closed until further notice.
