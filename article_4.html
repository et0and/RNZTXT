Watch live: Hipkins speaks about scaled-back warmer homes scheme

Labour's leader is speaking to the media after visiting the Sustainability Trust, which offered the Warmer Kiwi Homes programme.

Labour Party leader Chris Hipkins is speaking to the media after visiting the Sustainability Trust, which offered the Warmer Kiwi Homes programme in the Wellington region.

The programme, which was put in place in 2018 by the former Labour government, has been scaled back by the current government.

The scheme covers up to 90 percent of the costs to purchase and install insulation and an efficient heater for homeowners.
