Two people injured by falling marble at industrial property

RNZ understands they were hired labourers from a housing company and were hurt inside the container.

Photo: ST JOHN NZ

Two people have been injured by falling marble while trying to unload a shipping container in South Auckland on Tuesday.

Emergency services said they were called to the industrial property on Chonny Crescent in Manurewa at about midday.

RNZ understands they were hired labourers from a housing company and were hurt inside the container.

A spokesperson for the freight logistics company where the incident happened said worksafe is being made aware.
