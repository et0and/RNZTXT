Blenheim man pleads guilty to murdering his mother

A man has pleaded guilty to murdering his mother in November, in Blenheim.

Jennifer Phyllis Sheehan was found dead at her property in Blenheim on 25 November, her son was arrested shortly afterward. Photo: Supplied / Facebook

A fifty-five year old man has pleaded guilty to the murder of his mother in Blenheim.

A homicide investigation was launched after Jennifer Phyllis Sheehan was found dead at a property in the tow, in November.

Her son, Paul Thomas Armon, was arrested shortly after and charged with her murder.

He entered a guilty plea to the charge in the High Court at Blenheim on Friday morning.

He will be sentenced in June.
