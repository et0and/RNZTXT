Crash blocks State Highway 2 in Waioeka

Motorists are advised to expect delays after a truck rolled on State Highway 2 in Waioeka this evening.

Photo:

Motorists are advised to expect delays after a truck rolled on State Highway 2 in Waioeka this evening.

Police said emergency services were called to a crash between Waiata Road and Waioeka Pa Road in in the Bay of Plenty at about 5.30pm.

One person sustained minor injuries.

The road is blocked and has been closed.

Anyone travelling through the area is advised to take an alternate route and to expect delays.
