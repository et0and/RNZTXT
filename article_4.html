SH1 closed following Umawera crash

The crash also brought down powerlines, which has cut power to a handful of properties.

Photo: RNZ / Robin Martin

State Highway One remains closed at Umawera in Northland after a car crash, which left two people with minor injuries.

Emergency services responded to the single-vehicle crash around 6.30am.

The crash also brought down powerlines, which has cut power to a handful of properties.

Top Energy estimates power to be restored by midday.
