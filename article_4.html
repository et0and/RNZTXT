One dead after fatal crash in Northland

A person has died following a crash in Waimate North on Saturday night.

Photo:

A person has died following a crash in Waimate North on Saturday night.

The single-vehicle crash on Te Ahu Ahu Road was reported to police just after 8pm.

The Serious Crash Unit attended and enquiries into the circumstances of the crash are continuing.

It was the third fatality on the roads yesterday.

One person was killed in an accident around 1.30pm on State Highway 32, on Whakamaru Road in the Bay of Plenty.
