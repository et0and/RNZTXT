State Highway 2 closed at Karangahake Gorge after crash

A person has been seriously injured after a crash on State Highway 2 in Waikino in the Karangahake Gorge.

Photo: RNZ / Patrice Allen

A person has been seriously injured after a crash on State Highway 2 in Waikino in the Karangahake Gorge.

Emergency services were called to the single vehicle crash near the Victoria Battery Reserve about 5pm.

Police said the driver of the vehicle has serious injuries.

The road will be closed for some time.
