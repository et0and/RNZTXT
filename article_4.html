Person injured in Kaikohe house fire

The injured person and was referred to paramedics, Fire and Emergency said.

Three fire trucks and one tanker took just under an hour to put out fire, which was contained to a single house. Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

One person has been injured in a Northland house fire this morning.

Fire and Emergency said they were called to a house on Thorpe Road, Kaikohe, shortly after 1.30am on Monday.

A spokesperson said one person was injured and was referred to paramedics.

Three fire trucks and one tanker took just under an hour to put out fire, which was contained to a single house.

A fire investigator will be on site later today to try find the cause.
