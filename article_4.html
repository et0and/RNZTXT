Police looking for missing 60-year-old Auckland man

Ronny Okeke was last seen in the Mt Roskill area at the end of April.

Missing 60-year-old Ronny Okeke. Photo: Supplied / Police

The police are asking the public for help to locate a 60-year-old Auckland man who was reported missing earlier this month.

Sandringham local Ronny Okeke was last seen in the Mt Roskill area on the afternoon of 27 April.

He was reported missing on 3 May.

Missing 60-year-old Ronny Okeke. Photo: Supplied / Police

This was out of character for Ronny and police, along with his family and friends were concerned for his well being.

Anyone who has seen Ronny or has information on his whereabouts was urged to contact police.
