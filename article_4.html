Serious crash closes Palmerston North intersection

Motorists are advised to take an alternative route and expect delays.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Emergency services were responding to a two-vehicle crash in Palmerston North on Friday night.

Police were called to the Clevely Line and Railway Road intersection around 8:35pm.

Initial indications suggest there were serious injuries, police said.

Motorists were advised to take an alternative route and expect delays.
