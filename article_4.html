Fire crews battle workshop blaze in Ōtaki

Firefighters are battling a large blaze at a rural workshop in Ōtaki, north of Wellington.

Smoke from a storage shed fire on a chicken farm near Te Horo Beach, north of Wellington. Photo: TheTrafficFox.co.nz

Firefighters are battling a large blaze at a rural workshop in Ōtaki, north of Wellington.

Crews discovered the fire in the single-storey complex was well involved when they arrived at Swamp Road after 1pm this afternoon.

Fire and Emergency NZ shift manager Andrew Tollison said extra crews from around the region were being called in.

Photo: Google Maps

One issue they are facing is getting water to the site, he said.

Heavy machinery will be used to dismantle parts of the building, to help firefighters get to the flames.

No one has been injured.
