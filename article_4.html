Artist Vallé on Papua New Guinea, barbering and lyrics

Vallé's exploration of thise roots has taken him on a journey of many sorts. In this episode of Here Now, he talks to Kadambari Raghukumar about the impact that's had on his life.

FOLLOW Here Now on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, iHeart Radio or wherever you listen to your podcasts.

Christchurch-based artist Vallé talks to Kadambari Raghukumar about rediscovering his connection to Papua New Guinea, his music and running his barbershop alongside everything else.

After a brief hiatus since his EP, Caged Birds, was released in 2022, he is back with a single, 'Not 4 Sale'.

In this episode of Here Now, Vallé reflects on how reconnecting with his birthplace continues to shape numerous aspects of his thought process and his goals as an artist.
