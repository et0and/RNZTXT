Man dies after being struck by car in West Auckland

Emergency services were called to the crash in Massey just before 7pm.

Photo: St John

A man has died after he was struck by a car in Massey, West Auckland on Tuesday evening.

Emergency services were called to the incident on Lanham Lane just before 7pm.

A police spokesperson confirmed a man has died at the scene.

People are being asked to avoid the area as police make enquiries.
