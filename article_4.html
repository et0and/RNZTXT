Police find body in Piha Beach area

Police believe the body belongs to a man who was unaccounted for after he went fishing at Piha Beach.

Police and Victim Support are providing support to the man's family during this difficult time. Photo: 123rf

Police have found a body in the Piha Beach area they believe to be a missing fisherman.

They were notified of the body on the shoreline on Tuesday afternoon.

While the formal identification process is yet to be completed, it is believed to be a fisherman who was reported missing on Thursday 9 January.

Police and Victim Support are providing support to the man's family during this difficult time.

The death will be referred to the Coroner.
