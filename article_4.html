DJ Fred Again rumoured for show in New Zealand

People are reportedly buying tickets to Wellington in anticipation of the rumoured announcement.

Photo: Supplied

There are suggestions popular UK disc jockey DJ Fred Again could be playing a gig in Wellington.

Wellington City Council did not respond directly to RNZ's inquiry whether the artist was going to play a set.

It said it could not confirm anything until the artist-led announcement.

RNZ was told people have begun buying tickets to Wellington in anticipation of the announcement.

The spokesperson for the Homegrown festival, which was held over the weekend, told RNZ they were not allowed to comment.

It is rumoured that the while the other stages from the Homegrown festival last weekend have been taken down, the Park Stage has been left up for Fred Again to do a pop-up show this Friday or Saturday.

Fred Again posted a video of himself watching an old video of Jonah Lomu playing in an All Blacks game on his Instagram Story at 4pm on Tuesday.
