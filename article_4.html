Truck crash delays traffic on SH1 and SH2 near Wellington

A truck has hit a wall and a blocked lane has led to a buildup of traffic on both SH1 and SH2, NZTA says.

The scene of the truck crash on State Highway 2. Photo: NZTA

A truck has hit a wall and a blocked lane has led to a buildup of traffic on both SH1 and SH2, the NZ Transport Agency says.

Emergency services were called to the crash scene around 12.25pm.

The driver had been taken to hospital in a moderate condition and the road is down to one lane, police said.

"There are significant delays and police advise motorists to avoid travelling in the area if possible.

"It is going to take time to clear the area and a heavy haulage vehicle will be required to remove the truck."

Waka Kotahi said the crash was also affecting traffic flows on State Highway 1.

It was also warning of delays as traffic was quickly queuing back to the split between SH1 and SH2 on the Urban Motorway.

In an update just before 1.30pm, it said: "Traffic is continuing to back up on SH1 on both the Urban Mwy and Ngauranga Gorge. Please expect delays of up to 15 minutes when travelling towards Petone."
