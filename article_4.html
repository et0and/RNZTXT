Child falls from window in Queenstown

A child is seriously hurt and has been flown to hospital after falling from a window,

Emergency services were called to the scene about 9pm. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A child has been seriously injured after falling from a window in Queenstown.

Emergency service, including a St John helicopter, were called to the scene about 9pm on Thursday.

The child has been taken to the emergency department at Lakes District Hospital.
