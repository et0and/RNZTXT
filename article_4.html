Woman missing after visiting Hamilton cemetery

Police are searching for a 77-year-old woman who went missing after visiting a Hamilton cemetery.

Karen, 77. Photo: Supplied / NZ police

Police are searching for a 77-year-old woman who went missing after visiting a Hamilton cemetery on Tuesday.

A police spokesperson said Karen was last seen at Newstead Cemetery about 11am.

They said she was wearing a multi-coloured tunic top, black leggings, Nike slip-on shoes and drove a grey 2017 Citreon C4 hatchback, registration NFE683.

Police said they and her family have concerns for her health and wellbeing.

Anyone with information can contact police on 111, reference P057204330.
