Woman pleads not guilty to throwing rocks through Grey Lynn windows

The incidents took place over almost a year.

[x Woman pleads not guilty to throwing rocks through Grey Lynn windows

A glass door smashed on Sackville Street. Photo: Supplied

The woman accused of throwing rocks through windows in a leafy Auckland suburb has pleaded not guilty in Auckland District Court.

A number of houses on Sackville Street in Grey Lynn had their windows smashed in January, though incidents date back to February last year.

On one of the days, six windows were smashed.

The accused, who has been granted interim name suppression, was remanded in custody before Judge von Keisenberg and is expected to reappear in April.
