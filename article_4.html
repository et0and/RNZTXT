Police release name of man who died after running into sea

Police have released the name of the man who died after running into the sea at Mt Maunganui on 15 April.

The death has been referred to the coroner. Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Police have released the name of the man who died after running into the sea at Mt Maunganui on 15 April.

He was John Rawiri Piripi Pitihira, aged 37, from Mount Maunganui.

Pitihira was one of three men who fled police after an alleged burglary and ran into the sea to avoid arrest. His body was found on Omanu Beach about 5am on 15 April.
