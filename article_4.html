Serious crash closes road in Clutha District

Police and emergency crews at at the scene of a crash in Owaka in the Clutha District.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police and emergency crews at at the scene of a crash in Owaka in the Clutha District.

Crews were called to the serious single-vehicle crash on Owaka Valley Road at about 2.45pm.

Hato Hone St John said that its crews assessed and treated one patient who was airlifted to Dunedin hospital in a moderate condition.

The road is closed, and the Serious Crash Unit will examine the scene.

Motorists should avoid the area if possible.
