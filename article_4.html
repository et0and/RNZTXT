Power lines down on Auckland motorway after crash

Auckland's arterial Great South Road has been closed after power lines were brought down onto the road by a crash, police said.

(File photo) Photo:

Auckland's arterial Great South Road has been closed after power lines were brought down onto the road by a crash, police said.

A vehicle had collided with a power pole in a single vehicle crash, that was reported just before 5pm on Waitangi Day, a police statement said.

No injuries were reported, but drivers were warned to expect delays and have been asked to avoid the area.

The incident happened near Mcannalley Street, in Manurewa.
