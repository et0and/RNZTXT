One dead after two-vehicle crash in South Taranaki

One person has died following a crash on Skeet Road near Manaia Road, in Kapuni, this morning.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

One person has died following a crash on Skeet Road near Manaia Road, in Kapuni in South Taranaki, this morning.

The two-vehicle crash happened just before 11.30am.

As well as the person who died, a second person suffered moderate injuries and was taken to hospital.

The road is still closed and motorists are advised to take an alternative route.

Members of the Serious Crash Unit are attending.
