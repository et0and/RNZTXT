One dead after motorbike and mobility scooter collide

The crash happened in Rotorua yesterday.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

One person has died following a collision between a motorbike and a mobility scooter in Rotorua on Tuesday.

A police spokesperson said the crash happened at the intersection of Amohau Street and Tutanekai Street.

The driver of the mobility scooter died at the scene.

Enquiries into the circumstances of the crash are continuing.
