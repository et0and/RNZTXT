At least two people killed in fiery crash in Taranaki

A section of State Highway 3 is closed after one of the cars caught fire in the crash.

Diversions are in place at State Highway 3 and Rotokare Road following the crash. Photo: RNZ / Robin Martin

Two people are dead after a fiery two-vehicle crash in Taranaki.

The crash at the intersection of Mountain Road and Boylan Road on State Highway 3 in Eltham was reported to police at 5.50am on Wednesday.

The senior sergeant at the scene, Andrew Russ, said there was one person in each car.

He said police were still working on getting the victims' bodies out of the cars, and would then be able to confirm there had been no further deaths.

In a statement earlier on Wednesday, police said "multiple people" had died, but officers were still working out how many people had been in the car that caught fire.

"Inquiries to determine the occupancy may take some time and an update will be issued when possible.

The car which set alight was already "fully involved" when firefighters arrived on scene, Fire and Emergency NZ said.
