Auckland household threatened by two men with firearm

An Auckland household has been threatened by two men with a firearm on Monday.

Two men with a firearm have threatened a household in Ōtāhuhu. Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

An Auckland household has been threatened by two men with a firearm on Monday.

Police say they were altered to the incident in Ōtāhuhu at about 1pm this afternoon.

The two men threatened people at the address, with one man holding a firearm.

The men fled the scene before police arrived, and are being pursued by the Police Eagle helicopter.

Police said no one was injured, and enquires were ongoing.
