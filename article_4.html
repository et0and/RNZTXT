Man arrested after family fight, assaulting police

A person is in a critical but stable condition after a fight between two family members in Auckland.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

A person is in a critical but stable condition after a fight between two family members in Auckland.

The police said they were called to a house on Moncrieff Avenue in Clendon at 2.40am Sunday.

They said a man "was extremely agitated, assaulting responding officers".

The officers were not hurt.

An injured person was taken to hospital where they are now in a stable condition.

A 23-year-old man is due to appear in the Manukau District Court Monday on charges of assaulting and resisting police and wounding with intent.
