Seven rescued after boat overturns in Waikato

Seven men have been rescued after their boat overturned while crossing the Kawhia Bar in Waikato today.

Seven men were rescued after a boat overturned in Waikato. (File photo) Photo: Supplied/ 123rf

Seven men have been rescued after their boat overturned while crossing the Kawhia Bar in Waikato today.

The Coastguard launched a search and rescue operation involving Raglan Coastguard and the Auckland Coastguard's patrol plane when the vessel failed to file a report saying it had successfully crossed the sand bar.

The boaties made it to shore near Opapaka Point.

One had a minor head injury and another was taken to hospital by helicopter with leg injuries.

The police said the men were well-prepared for an emergency and activated their locator beacon.
