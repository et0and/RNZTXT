One killed, two in hospital after Feilding crash

A person has been killed and another two were seriously injured after crashing into a stationary truck in Manawatū on Friday.

(File photo) Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A person has been killed and another two were seriously injured after crashing into a stationary truck in Manawatū on Friday.

Police said a car crashed into a parked truck-trailer on Lethbridge Street in Feilding at about 10.15pm.

One person died at the scene, and two others were taken to Palmerston North hospital.

A police investigation of the crash is underway.
