20 firefighters battle blaze at Auckland home

No one was believed to have been in the building at the time.

Fire teams were called to the home about midday on Monday. (file image) Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Five fire trucks and 20 fire fighters are responding to a house fire in Auckland.

Fire Emergency said it was alerted to a house ablaze in the suburb of Lynfield just after midday on Monday.

A spokesperson said it had been extinguished, and no one was believed to have been in the building.
