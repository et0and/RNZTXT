Covid-19 update: 1693 new cases, 28 further deaths

The number of new cases are higher, and there were more deaths than last week.

Photo: AFP / NOBEASTSOFIERCE / SCIENCE PHOTO LI

There were 1693 new cases of Covid-19 reported in New Zealand over the week to Sunday, and 28 further deaths attributed to the virus.

Of the new cases, 1058 were reinfections.

There were 102 cases in hospital and data on the number in intensive care was not provided.
