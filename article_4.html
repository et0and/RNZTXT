Two Manurewa blazes treated as suspicious

Two separate fires in Manurewa in Auckland overnight are being treated as suspicious.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Two separate fires near Manurewa in Auckland overnight are being treated as suspicious.

Fire and Emergency (FENZ) said crews were called to a two-storey apartment block on Lincoln Road about 4.30am on Sunday.

All persons are accounted for, and four fire trucks remained dampening down hotspots.

And firefighters responded to a blaze at a house on Great South Road about 9.45pm on Saturday, which FENZ was also treating as suspicious.

A fire investigator was at the scene.
