Selwyn rail line closed after accident involving trailer

The two-vehicle crash happened on Main South Road in Selwyn on Friday afternoon.

Kiwirail said the crash involved a trailer rolling over a level crossing and hitting a train track. Photo: 123rf.com

Police are at the scene of a serious crash in Canterbury that saw parts of the train track in the area closed off and a train stopped.

The two-vehicle crash happened on Main South Road in Selwyn at 5.40pm on Friday.

Kiwirail said the crash involved a trailer rolling over a level crossing and hitting a train track. The track was closed.

A train was stopped until the scene could be made safe. Kiwirail said it was a freight train scheduled to go through the area.

It was not immediately known if anyone had been injured.

Police said traffic management was in place at the scene, advising motorists to avoid the area.
