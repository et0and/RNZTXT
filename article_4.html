Serious crash in Te Puke

Police say it appears a person has critical injuries after a vehicle crashed in Te Puke.

The crash occurred on Number 3 Road in Te Puke. Photo: 123rf.com

Police say it appears a person has critical injuries after a vehicle crashed in Te Puke.

Emergency services were alerted at 7.30pm to the crash on Number 3 Road, between Number 4 Road and Bayliss Road.

Traffic management is in place and the Serious Crash Unit has been notified.
