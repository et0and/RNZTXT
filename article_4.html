15-year-old teen missing for almost 2 weeks

Nohokotahi, also known as Bob, was last seen at his family home on 19 February.

Nohokotahi, also known as Bob, was last seen 19 February at his home in Papakura. Photo: Supplied / Police

A Papakura teen has been reported missing and was last seen by family at home in the early hours of 19 February.

Nohokotahi, also known as Bob, is 180-185cm tall and described by police as "solidly built".

Police say they are wanting the public's help to find him and he is known to spend time in the Counties Manukau area.

Anyone who sees Nohokotahi is asked to call police as soon as possible on 111.

Other information about his whereabouts can be given to police on 105 quoting the file number 240221/3740, or anonymously via Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
