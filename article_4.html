Two children in hospital after driveway incident

One of the children remains in a critical condition after being hurt on a Te Kuiti driveway.

Two young children were injured on a Te Kuiti driveway on Wednesday. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

One child remains in a critical condition after being injured in a Te Kuiti driveway.

A police spokesperson says emergency services responded to the incident that involved a vehicle in Alisa Street, at 4.50pm on Wednesday.

Two young children were injured and transported to hospital, one in a critical condition and another in a serious condition.

The child in a serious condition has since stabilised and has minor injuries while the other remains critical.

A scene examination has taken place and the Serious Crash Unit is investigating.
