Motorcyclist dies in hospital after crash in Christchurch

A motorcyclist has died after being injured in a crash in Christchurch on Thursday.

File photo. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A motorcyclist has died after being injured in a crash in Christchurch on 7 March.

The crash on Highsted Road, Casebrook was reported to police at 6.50pm on Thursday.

The motorcyclist was transported to hospital in a critical condition and died on Friday.

Police said inquiries into the circumstances of the crash were ongoing.
