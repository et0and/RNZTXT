One killed in Waikato motorcycle crash

Police say a person has died after a crash near Te Awamutu.

(File photo) Photo:

Police say a person has died after a crash near Te Awamutu in Waikato.

The crash on Flat Road in Kihikihi was reported about 7:45pm on Saturday, police said in a statement.

A single motorcycle was involved, and one person died at the scene.

Police are investigating the cause.
