Person dies after Ōtara single-vehicle crash

Drivers are asked to avoid Antrim Crescent in the Auckland suburb of Ōtara.

Photo: 123RF

The Serious Crash Unit is at the scene of a crash in the Auckland suburb of Ōtara.

One person received critical injuries and died at the scene, police said.

The single car crash was reported to police about 4.40pm.

A section of Antrim Road is blocked while emergency services are at the scene.

Drivers should avoid the area, police say.
