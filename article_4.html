Person's death in Auckland harbour being treated as unexplained

Emergency services found a person unresponsive in the water.

Photo: RNZ

Police are investigating an unexplained death on Auckland's harbour.

Emergency services were called to the Westhaven Marina at around 12.10pm on Tuesday.

A person was found unresponsive in the water.

Police say a post mortem will be carried out.

The death is being treated as unexplained.

A spokesperson said enquiries were continuing to determine what has happened.

A body was found in the ocean south of Dunedin this morning.
