Fire crews battle blaze at boat manufacturer in Auckland

The overnight fire was contained but one truck remains at the scene.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Fire crews from across Auckland have battled a blaze at a boat manufacturer in East Tāmaki overnight.

Fire and Emergency was alerted to the fire shortly after midnight, Thursday morning.

A spokesperson said nine fire trucks, three ladder trucks and a specialist fire team were sent.

He said the fire was contained within about an hour, but one truck remains at the scene.
