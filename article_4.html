Wellbeing concerns for missing 24-year-old man

Joseph Grattan was last seen in late February.

Photo: Supplied/police

Police say they now have serious concerns for the wellbeing of a man they believe was injured in a car crash in Waikato earlier this week.

Joseph Grattan, 24, was last seen on 28 February at Patikura Place, Tūrangi.

Police said they believed he was in a crash on State Highway 32 south of Whakamaru last Wednesday afternoon.

They said they were urgently seeking to locate Grattan and wanted anyone with information to contact them.

Police can be contacted on 105 - reference file number 250311/5175.
