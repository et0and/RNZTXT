Police have 'serious concerns' for missing Canterbury woman

Police say they have serious concerns for the well-being of a woman who has been reported missing in Canterbury.

Henriette, 54, has been reported missing. Photo: Supplied / NZ Police

Police say they have serious concerns for the well-being of a woman who has been reported missing in Canterbury.

They said 54-year-old Henriette was last seen in Woodend Beach yesterday morning.

Police want to hear from anyone who has information that may help. Call 105 using the reference number P060872682.
