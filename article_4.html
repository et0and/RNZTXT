Hundreds without power in west Auckland

Suburbs in both the west and north-west parts of the city are without electricity

The outage about 11am on Monday. Photo:

Hundreds of people in Auckland are without power.

Vector's outage map shows suburbs in both West, and North West of the city are affected, including Coatesville, Dairy Flat, Huia, Parau and Laingholm.

Vector is yet to respond to a request for more details.
