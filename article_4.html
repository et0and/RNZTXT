One dead in crash near Palmerston North

One person has died in a crash just north of Palmerston North this afternoon.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

One person has died in a crash just north of Palmerston North this afternoon.

Emergency services were called to the scene at the intersection of Colyton Road and Watershed Road just before 3pm.

The accident involved a car and a motorbike.

The road is closed and diversions are in place.

Motorists have been advised to avoid the area and expect delays.
