Truck left hanging over edge of road after jack-knifing on highway

Police say the vehicle blocked State Highway 6 at Kawarau Gorge. The road has since reopened.

Police have warned drivers to avoid the area. Photo: RNZ / Tom Kitchin

A truck was left hanging over the edge of a South Island highway after jack-knifing on the road.

Police say State Highway 6 at Kawarau Gorge closed about 8am on Monday after the crash.

It had reopened by about 1pm.

No one was hurt in the crash, but drivers were being warned to avoid the area during the clean-up.
