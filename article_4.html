Marist Saints Rugby League clubrooms destroyed by fire

Fire trucks were called to the property at 3.30am.

Generic fire truck. Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

The clubrooms of a famous Auckland rugby league club have been destroyed by a fire.

Four fire trucks and a ladder truck were called to the Marist Saints Rugby League clubrooms in Mt Albert at 3.30am.

An investigator is at the scene this morning.
