Shepherds Reign lead nominations for 2024 Pacific Music Awards

Polynesian heavy metal group Shepherds Reign are leading nominations for this year's Pacific Music Awards.

Shepherds Reign are nominated for six awards at this year's Pacific Music Awards. Photo:

Polynesian heavy metal group Shepherds Reign are leading nominations for this year's Pacific Music Awards.

The five-piece band, who are based in Auckland, have been nominated across six categories in the annual awards, which take place in Manukau on 29 August.

Aaradhna at the Aotearoa Music Awards red carpet event on 30 May, 2024. Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

Samoan-Indian R&B artist Aaradhna is nominated for five categories and Niuean Auckland-based rapper Diggy Dup√© is nominated for four awards.

Onehunga rap crew SWIDT is up for three awards, while Tokelauan singer Olivia Foa'i has received three award nominations.

2024 marks the 20th anniversary of the annual awards. A total of 27 finalists - 12 of them first-timers - are competing for awards across 13 categories.

Trustee and event producer Petrina Togi-Sa'ena said reaching the 20th anniversary of the awards "signals the importance and growth of the Pacific music community".

"We are grateful to all the artists, families and communities that have shared the journey and have celebrated with us each year."
