One person critical after three cars and truck crash in Waikato

State Highway 39 at Tihiroa is closed.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A person has been airlifted to hospital with critical injuries after a crash between three cars and a truck in Waikato.

The collision happened on Ormsby Road, State Highway 39 at Tihiroa at about 9.45pm.

A second person has moderate injuries.

The road is likely to be closed for some time and motorists in the area should expect delays.
