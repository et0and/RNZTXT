Ram-raid of store in central Auckland overnight

A group of people ram-raided the store in Remuera after failing to smash their way in on foot, police say.

(File image). Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A group of people ram-raided a store in Auckland after failing to smash their way in on foot, police say.

Police said they received reports of a small group attempting to enter the store on Upland Road in Remuera about 1.30am on Saturday.

"After attempts to smash their way in, a vehicle was used to gain entry. Items were taken, and the group left in the vehicle."

Police located the vehicle a short distance away, and said inquiries were ongoing to locate those involved.
