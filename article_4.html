Body found in Lake Wanaka identified

A body recovered from Lake Wānaka, near Waterfall Creek, this morning has been identified.

Photo: RNZ / Tess Brunton

A body recovered from Lake Wānaka, near Waterfall Creek, this morning has been identified.

Police earlier asked for help to identify the body, described as a Caucasian male likely to be in his 20s.

They were wearing a light blue sweatshirt, dark grey chino jeans and white tennis shoes.

Police said they would like to thank the public for their aid.
