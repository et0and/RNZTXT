Fire crews respond to three Northland scrub blazes

Northland crews have been working to contain three scrub fires across the region.

Northland fire crews have been kept busy battling three separate scrub fires across the region since Friday. File photo. Photo: RNZ / Amber Woolf

Northland crews have been working to contain three scrub fires across the region.

A blaze west of Dargaville broke out on Friday and has burned through 15 hectares.

Fire-fighters are extending the fire break line to prevent it spreading.

Further north, three fire trucks, a water tanker and a helicopter are being used to contain a scrub fire at Ahipara.

The one-hectare blaze is on the side of a hill at the Kaitaia-Awaroa Road.

East of Kaitaia, at Hihi, fire crews have also extinguished a vegetation blaze.
