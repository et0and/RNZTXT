Person's death in Tawa unexplained, police at scene

Emergency services were called to a property in the Wellington suburb shortly before midnight on Tuesday.

File photo. Police were carrying out a scene examination on Wednesday morning. Photo: 123RF

Police are at the scene of an unexplained death in the Wellington suburb of Tawa.

Emergency services were called to an address in Tremewan Street shortly before midnight on Tuesday.

A guard was at the property overnight and CIB were to carry out a scene examination at the property on Wednesday morning.

Police gave no further details.
