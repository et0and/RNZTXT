Person in critical condition following paragliding incident near Muriwai

A person was rescued by helicopter and flown to Auckland Hospital.

Muriwai Beach. (File photo) Photo: RNZ / Tom Taylor

One person is in critical condition following a paragliding incident at Maukatia-Maori Bay near Muriwai.

St John said one ambulance and two rapid response units attended the incident around 2pm.

A person with critical injuries was rescued by helicopter and flown to Auckland Hospital.
