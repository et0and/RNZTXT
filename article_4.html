Police appeal for Auckland cyclist missing in Masterton

Christopher Lott has not returned since going on a bike ride on Thursday morning.

Christopher Lott, who is visiting the Wairarapa from Auckland, has been reported missing. Photo: Supplied / NZ Police

Police are appealing for information to locate Christopher Lott, who has been reported missing in Masterton.

Lott is visiting Wairarapa from Auckland and has not returned home since he left Grant Crescent for a bike ride at 10am on Thursday 19 December.

He is wearing black padded motorcycle pants, long sleeved tie-dyed green, white top, white motorcycle boots, and a purple bike helmet. He also has a small backpack and is riding the bike he is pictured in.

Christopher Lott, who is visiting the Wairarapa from Auckland, has been reported missing. Photo: Supplied / NZ Police

If you have seen Christopher, or have any information that may assist in locating him please make a report at 105 online or call 105, referencing event number 241219/2025.
