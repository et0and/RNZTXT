Eftpos outage hits customers

Efpos says a technical issue is preventing some of its transactions from being processed.

An EFTPOS card being swiped at a terminal. Photo: 123RF

Efpos says a technical problem is preventing some of its transactions from being processed.

The electronic payment company did not specify the location or scale of the problem and RNZ has requested more details.

It says its support team are currently investigating.

People calling for support may experience delays, it said.
