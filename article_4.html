Person found dead in West Auckland

Officers were called to Royal Reserve park in the suburb of Massey just after midnight.

Police were called to Royal Reserve park following reports of a person found unresponsive. Photo: Google Maps

A person has been found dead in a West Auckland Park.

Officers were called to Royal Reserve park in the suburb of Massey just after midnight.

They were responding to reports of a person found unresponsive, who was later pronounced dead at the scene by ambulance staff.

Police said they were treating the death as unexplained and a scene guard was at the site.
