Arrest in Porirua after man shot - police

A man has been arrested in Porirua after another turned up at a hospital with gunshot wounds, police say.

A man was shot and another man arrested after a firearms incident in Porirua on Thursday, police say. (File photo) Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

A man has been arrested in Porirua after another turned up at a hospital with gunshot wounds, police say.

The wounded man turned up at an unnamed hospital on Thursday, where he underwent surgery and is now in a stable condition, Detective Inspector Haley Ryan said.

It is believed the firearms incident happened in Porirua and "the victim and the offender are known to each other," Ryan said.

Officers searched a Porirua address on Friday, and arrested a 55-year-old man. Charges are expected to follow.

"The investigation is ongoing and the Porirua community should expect to see an increased Police presence in the area," Ryan said.

Anyone with information about the incident was asked to call Police on 105, or to visit their Update Report page online at 105.police.govt.nz and quote file number 240111/9979. Information could also be provided anonymously through Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111, or on their website.
