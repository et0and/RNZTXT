Three injured after car crashes into building

A vehicle has gone into a building and three people are injured after a crash on Auckland's North Shore this afternoon.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A vehicle has gone into a building and three people are injured after a crash on Auckland's North Shore this afternoon.

Two people have been taken to Auckland hospital, one in a serious condition, and another taken to North Shore hospital.

Emergency services were called just after 1pm to the scene on Beach Road in Mairangi Bay.

Police are in attendance and the serious crash unit has been alerted.

A St John spokesperson said three ambulances, a rapid response unit, and an operations manager responded to the crash.
