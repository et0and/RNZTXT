Central Taupō closed due to gas leak

Those in buildings in the area are being told to stay inside with the windows closed.

Taupō. Photo: 123rf

People are being asked to avoid central Taupō after a gas leak.

The police said cordons were in place in a 800-metre radius around Horomatangi Street and south to the lakefront.

They included the intersections of Horomatangi Street, Ruapehu, Tamamutu, and Titiraupenga Streets, and the lakefront.

Those in buildings in that area were being told to stay inside with the windows closed and air conditioning off.

Two fire and emergency crews were called to the scene at 9.30am. One has since left.
