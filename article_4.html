Fire destroys beach house in Coromandel

Fire has destroyed a water-front house in Cooks Beach in the Coromandel Peninsula and has spread to a neighbouring property.

Photo: 123RF

Fire has destroyed a water-front house in Cooks Beach in the Coromandel Peninsula and has spread to a neighbouring property.

Fire and Emergency was alerted at around 12.30pm Saturday.

A spokesperson said the original blaze in the two-storey house is under control.

Hotspots are being dampened down at the second property, which is also two storeys high.

A third nearby house escaped any damage.
