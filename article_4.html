Body found in the water at Mt Wellington - police

A man has died after what police described as a water-related incident, at Mt Wellington, Auckland.

(File photo) Photo: RNZ

A man has died after what police described as a water-related incident, at Mt Wellington, Auckland.

Police received reports of a body floating in the water in the area, about 3:45pm Saturday, a police spokesperson said.

"The deceased has since been recovered from the water," they said.

The death would be be referred to the coroner.
