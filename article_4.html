Two arrests after men critically hurt in Auckland assault

The arrests were made after an assault in the Auckland suburb of Glendene in the early hours.

Photo: 123rf

Two men have been arrested after an assault in the Auckland suburb of Glendene earlier this morning.

Police say they responded to a disorder on Scowns Way, where two people had been injured, shortly before 2am.

The two people were taken to hospital with critical injuries, however, their condition is now listed as stable.

Police say a 25-year-old man and a 37-year-old man have been charged with wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm.
