Three arrests after series of fires on Māngere Mountain in Auckland

Three people have been arrested following multiple fires on Māngere Mountain this afternoon.

File pic Photo: Auckland Council

Three people have been arrested following multiple fires on Auckland's Māngere Mountain this afternoon.

Fire and Emergency said the scrub fire covered an area of 60 metres by 50 metres at its peak.

About 20 firefighters had been tackling the blaze which had been extinguished by around 6pm.

The firefighters remain at the scene, dampening down hot spots on the south-western side of the mountain, facing Wallace Road which remains closed.

Police were alerted about 4.30pm, and assisted Fire and Emergency at the scene.

A police spokesperson said there were at least three fires on the hillside, and it was reported to police that three males had been seen deliberately starting the fires.

Three people were taken into custody at an address on Kupua Road.

The spokesperson said motorists could expect delays in the immediate area.
