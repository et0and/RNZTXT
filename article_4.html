Attempted murder charge after woman seriously injured in crash

A man allegedly intentionally crashed a vehicle he was driving, with a female passenger who was known to him.

Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

A man has been charged with attempted murder, in relation to a crash in the Auckland suburb of Manurewa that left a woman with serious injuries.

On 3 August, the man allegedly intentionally crashed a vehicle he was driving, with a female passenger who was known to him.

Detective Inspector Shaun Vickers said the victim suffered multiple serious injuries after the vehicle ploughed into a parked truck on Adams Road.

The woman was still recovering from those injuries.

The 24-year-old man is due to appear in Manukau District Court on Wednesday.
