One dead after 'incident' with motorbike in Auckland park

Cordons have been put in place by police at Mountfort Park in Weymouth on Tuesday evening.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A person has died following an "incident" involving a motorcycle in a South Auckland park on Tuesday evening.

Police said they were called to Mountfort Park in Weymouth at 7.40pm.

Cordons were in place within the park, a police spokesperson said.

They asked the public to avoid the area.
