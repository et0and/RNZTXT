One killed in Fiordland motorbike accident

A person is dead following a crash involving three motorbikes in Fiordland this morning.

The Homer Tunnel at Milford Sound. Photo: Mike Dux, public domain.

A section of State Highway 94 has reopened after a fatal crash involving three motorbikes in Fiordland.

The crash happened about 10.50am south of the Homer Tunnel.

UPDATE 1:40PMSH94 remains CLOSED between Monkey Creek and Homer Tunnel. SCU are on-site doing an investigation. An update is expected late afternoon Please avoid the area or delay your journey if possible. ^SG https://t.co/FnG7yWTZSX

Police confirmed one of the motorcyclists died at the scene while two others suffered moderate-to-serious injuries.

A section of State Highway 94 has now reopened after investigators checked the scene.

People travelling to or from Milford Sound are asked to expect delays.
