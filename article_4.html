Police following 'strong lines of enquiry' in Khandallah death investigation

Police investigating the violent death of a Wellington grandmother last week say they are following "strong lines of enquiry".

A 79-year-old woman was found dead at a property on Baroda Street in Khandallah. Photo: RNZ / Ashleigh McCaull

Police investigating the violent death of a Wellington grandmother last week say they are following "strong lines of enquiry".

The body of 79-year-old Helen Gregory was found at her Khandallah home on 24 January.

A police spokesperson said the scene examination would be completed over the next couple of days.

She said the investigation was making good progress and there was a positive response from the community.
