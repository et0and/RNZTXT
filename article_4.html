Man missing on Remarkables hike found safe

The 25-year-old, identified only as Sanjeev, was dropped off at the start of Wye Creek track on Wednesday morning.

Sanjeev. Photo: Police / Supplied

Queenstown police have found a man who failed to returned from a hike to the Remarkables ski field.

The 25-year-old, identified only as Sanjeev, was dropped off at the start of Wye Creek track off State Highway 6 (Kingston Road) on Wednesday morning.

He was planning to walk to the Remarkables, where he was expected to arrive at about 8pm.

His friends reported him missing.

Police said the man was of Nepalese descent, about 150cm tall, with black, shoulder-length hair.

He was believed to be wearing a black/grey jacket, hiking shoes, a beanie and beige cargo pants.

He was carrying a black backpack, one hiking pole and a black DSLR camera bag.

Police urged anyone who had seen someone matching his description in the Wye Creek track area since Wednesday morning to call police on 111 and quote event number P060682376.
