Police not treating Tauranga death as suspicious

Police are not treating the death of a woman in Tauranga as suspicious.

Police are not treating the death as suspicious. Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

Police are not treating the death of a woman in Tauranga as suspicious.

Police say the woman was found dead at a property in Bethlehem about 6.20pm on Sunday evening.

Her death will be referred to the coroner.
