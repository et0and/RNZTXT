Auckland woman missing from home for three weeks

The 63-year-old was last seen on 22 June but reported missing on 13 July.

Ramona Rudolph, 63. Photo: Supplied / Police

Police are appealing for the public's help to find an Auckland woman who has been missing for three weeks.

Ramona Rudolph, 63. Photo: Supplied / Police

Avondale woman Ramona Rudolph, 63, was last seen on 22 June but reported missing on 13 July.

Police believed she could be anywhere in the wider Auckland area.

They have concerns for her safety and asked anyone who sighted her to call police as soon as possible on 111.

Anyone who has further information on her whereabouts can also call police on 105, quoting file number 240713/0243, or anonymously via Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
