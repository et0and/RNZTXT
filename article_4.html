Motorcyclist dead in West Coast crash

A motorcyclist has died after a crash in Greymouth on Saturday morning.

Photo:

A motorcyclist has died after a crash in Greymouth on Saturday morning.

The crash between the motorcyclist and another vehicle happened just after 10am on Omoto Road / State Highway 6.

The highway remains closed just east of the Cobden Bridge and is expected to remain closed for another few hours, police said.

Motorists will need to divert through Stillwater.
