Renewed appeal for man whose burnt out vehicle was found in Waikato

Police are continuing to search for 24-year-old Travis, who has been missing since 17 January.

Travis has not been seen for almost two weeks. Photo: Supplied / NZ police

Police are continuing to search for 24-year-old Travis, who has been missing since 17 January.

He travelled from Wellington to the Waikato region, where his vehicle was found burnt out on a Tolley Road farm in Ngaroma on 18 January, police said.

Ngaroma is a farming district southeast of Te Awamutu.

Officers coordinated a search of the area where his vehicle was located, with the help of Search and Rescue staff.

Private searches have also been carried out by family and friends.

Some personal items belonging to the missing man were found during the search, but there have been no sightings of him since 17 January.

Police would like to hear from anyone who has seen or heard from Travis in that time.

They were also asking anyone in the wider Ngaroma area to check farm buildings, huts and any CCTV footage.

If anyone has any information that could help, it can be provided online or by calling 105, using the reference number 250119/4439.
