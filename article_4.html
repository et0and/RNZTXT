Motorbike rider dies in hospital following crash

A motorbike rider who was critically injured in a crash in Hastings last month has died in hospital.

File photo. Photo: 123rf

A motorbike rider who was critically injured in a crash in Hastings on 28 January has died in hospital.

The man crashed at the intersection of Colin White Road and State Highway 2 in Te Kauke.

Police received a report of the incident at 9.27am.

Police said their thoughts were with his loved ones and enquiries into the circumstances of the crash were ongoing.
