Pedestrian hit by car in Christchurch

A pedestrian is in a critical condition and Riccarton Rd is closed to motorists between Deans and Mona Vale avenues.

File photo. Photo: RNZ

A pedestrian is in a critical condition after being hit by a car in Christchurch.

Riccarton Rd is closed to motorists between Deans and Mona Vale avenues.

Emergency Services were called about the incident just before 3.30pm on Tuesday.

A St John spokesperson said one person was taken to Christchurch Hospital in a critical condition.

An ambulance and two rapid response vehicles were sent to the collision.

Police were at the scene investigating the crash and said the road could be closed for a few hours.

"Police are making further enquiries to determine the circumstances of the crash," a statement said.
