Long delays after Auckland motorway crash

Police are searching for two vehicles that fled the scene of a multi-vehicle crash on Auckland's motorway this afternoon.

A crash slowed traffic. (File photo) Photo: RNZ/Sophia Duckor-Jones

Police are searching for two vehicles that fled the scene of a multi-vehicle crash on Auckland's motorway this afternoon.

The police were called the North Western Motorway around Grey Lynn just before 2pm.

No one was injured but the crash caused long delays.
