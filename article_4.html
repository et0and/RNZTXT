Incident closes portion of Panmure road

Delays were expected for all traffic including bus services, says Auckland Transport.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

An emergency services incident has closed a section of Queens Road Panmure.

The road is closed between Domain Road and Pilkington Road in both directions until the incident is cleared, Auckland Transport says.

Delays were expected for all traffic including bus services, it said.

Affected bus routes were 744, 751, 712, 352 and stops 7525 at Jellicoe Road, 8661 at Sunset View Road and 7527 at Panmure Town Centre.
