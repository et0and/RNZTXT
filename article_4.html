Man charged after Christchurch assault

Two people were taken to Christchurch Hospital after the assault.

A 22-year-old man was due to appear in Christchurch District Court on Monday. Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Police have arrested a man in connection to a serious assault in central Christchurch.

An assault was reported to police on Hereford Street shortly after 4am on Sunday, near the Oxford Terrace entertainment precinct.

One person was taken to Christchurch Hospital in a critical condition and a second was taken in a serious condition.

Police said a 22-year-old man had been charged with wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm.

He was due to appear in Christchurch District Court on Monday.

Court documents indicate the assault was a stabbing.
