Car crashes kill two in in Dannevirke, Waipatu; SH6 closed in Central Otago

A section of highway has been closed in central Otago after a serious crash.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A section of highway has been closed in central Otago after a serious crash.

The two-vehicle collision happened on State Highway 6 - the Gibbston Highway this morning.

The road is expected to be closed for some time.

Meanwhile, a person died in a crash at Waipatu in Hastings overnight and another was killed in a crash on State Highway 2 in Dannevirke Friday afternoon.
