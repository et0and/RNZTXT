Fire destroys buildings in Opōtiki

The large blaze gutted two commercial buildings in the Bay of Plenty town.

File photo. Fire crews were called to the buildings on Church Street on Sunday night. Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Two commercial buildings in Opōtiki have been destroyed in a large fire overnight.

Fire crews were called to the buildings on Church Street in the Bay of Plenty town shortly before 11.30 on Sunday.

The fire covered 15 metres by 8 metres at its peak.

Eight fire trucks were at the scene.

The fire was at risk of spreading to other buildings, but was under control by about 3am on Monday.

A fire investigator was looking into the cause of the blaze.
