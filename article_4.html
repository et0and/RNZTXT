Concerns for missing Ōtara teenager

Police are appealing for sightings of a teenager missing from the Auckland suburb of Ōtara.

Police have concerns for missing man Reo Yorke, who was last seen in Otahūhū on 24 February. Photo: Supplied/ Police

Police are appealing for sightings of 19-year-old Reo Yorke.

Reo Yorke, 19, was last seen in Otahūhū at about 8.30pm on Monday, police said in a statement.

Both Reo's family and police had concerns for Yorke's well-being, and anyone who knows anything about where he might be was asked to call 111.

Yorke is about 180cms tall with black hair. He was wearing dark pants, a dark t-shirt and black sneakers.

Information about Yorke can be reported to police on 111 or 105, by quoting file number P061726679, or anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
