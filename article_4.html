Person critically injured by truck in Auckland

Emergency services were called to The Strand in Parnell on Wednesday afternoon.

Emergency services at the incident on Parnell Road, on Wednesday afternoon. Photo: RNZ/ Marika Khabazi

A person has been taken to hospital in a critical condition after being hit by a truck in Auckland's Parnell on Wednesday afternoon.

St John responded just after 2pm and sent two ambulances and a rapid response vehicle to the scene on The Strand in Parnell.

A spokesperson said one person was taken to Auckland City Hospital with critical injuries.

Police said there would be traffic disruptions. Photo: RNZ/ Marika Khabazi

Police said a section of The Strand is closed following the crash between the intersections at St George's Bay and Tamaki Drive.

A police spokesperson said this would cause disruption to afternoon traffic in the busy area.

The Serious Crash Unit will go to the scene.
