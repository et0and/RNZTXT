Forty firefighters battle blaze in central Auckland

Fire and Emergency says a generator caught fire in the CBD.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Forty firefighters are battling a blaze in central Auckland, after a generator caught fire.

The New Zealand Herald reported that the blaze broke out at Port of Auckland, although Fire and Emergency will only say that it is in the CBD.
