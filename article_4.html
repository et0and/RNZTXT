Two killed in crash near Queenstown

Emergency services found both people dead at the scene.

File photo. Photo: 123RF

Two people were killed in a crash on Malaghans Road in Dalefield near Queenstown.

Police said the bodies were found at the scene, after emergency services were called in about 10pm on Thursday.

An investigation into the crash is under way and the road is now open.
