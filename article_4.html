Person critically injured in Waikato crash

Diversions were in place and drivers should avoid the area, police said.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

A person has critical injuries following a crash involving a car and a motorcycle just south of Morrinsville in Waikato.

Emergency services were called to the crash on the outskirts of town near the intersection of Morrinsville-Walton Road, Eynon Road and Kuranui Road about 7:50pm Thursday.

St John Ambulance said it treated one person at the scene, and took them to Waikato Hospital in a critical condition.

Police said the serious crash unit would attend, and diversions were in place.

Drivers should avoid the area and expect delays.
