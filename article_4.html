Man charged after assault outside pub in central Auckland

A man will appear in court after an assault outside a pub in central Auckland on Saturday night.

A man will appear in court after an assault outside a pub in central Auckland on Saturday night.

A police spokesperson said the victim at the pub on Albert Street was taken to hospital in a critical condition.

The man has been charged with intent to injure and will appear in Auckland District Court on Monday.
