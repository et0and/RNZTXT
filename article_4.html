Man to appear in court after stand-off with police in Christchurch

A man in his 30s will appear in the Christchurch District Court today after an eight-hour standoff with police in the Avonhead suburb late on Sunday night.

The incident took place in Beatrice Place in the Christchurch suburb of Avonhead. Photo: RNZ / Adam Burns

A man in his 30s will appear in the Christchurch District Court today after an eight-hour standoff with police in the Avonhead suburb late on Sunday night.

The man is charged with unlawfully possessing a firearm.

Yesterday armed officers were called to Beatrice Place where a single person was armed inside a house.

Earlier, neighbouring residents were evacuated as a precaution.

No one was injured in the incident, police said yesterday.
