Whales stranded at Farewell Spit

Project Jonah says it is responding to a mass stranding of 30 to 40 pilot whales at Farewell Spit.

Farewell Spit. Photo:

Project Jonah is responding to a mass stranding of 30 to 40 pilot whales at Farewell Spit.

Spokeswoman Louisa Hawkes says a senior team is arriving with specialist equipment at the stranding location in the next half hour.

Low tide is at 5.30pm so the focus will be on keeping the whales comfortable until dark and efforts to refloat the whales will resume again at first light.

High tide is not until midnight so some of the senior team will stay with the whales as long as they can.
