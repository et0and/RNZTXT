One dead, two injured in Taranaki crash

One person has died and two people were injured in a crash in Taranaki on Friday night.

One person has died at the scene of a crash in Urenui, Taranaki, and two people are injured. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

One person has died and two people were injured in a crash in Taranaki on Friday night.

Police were called to State Highway 3 west of Urenui just before 11.30pm.

They say one person died at the scene and two others were taken to hospital with moderate injuries.

Enquiries into the circumstances were ongoing.
