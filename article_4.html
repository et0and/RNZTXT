A scene examination is continuing at a property where a person died in a fire in Paraparaumu Beach on Friday night.

Police were called to assist Fire and Emergency NZ crews at the Manly Street property shortly after 6pm.

A person at the property was found with critical injuries.

Despite the efforts of medical staff, the person died at the scene, police said in a statement.

Officers will be working alongside FENZ investigators to work out the cause of the fire.
