Concerns for welfare of missing 15-year-old

Jadeleigh was last seen in Helensville on Tuesday.

Jadeleigh, 15, was last seen on 26 January. Photo: Supplied / Police

A 15-year-old has been reported missing and police are seeking the public's help to find her.

Jadeleigh was last seen in Helensville about 6pm on Tuesday, 23 January.

Police have concerns for her welfare and were urgently trying to find her.

She was likely in the Auckland city area, police said.

Anyone who sees Jadeleigh was asked to call police on 111 immediately or information can be provided on 105, quoting file number 240123/6721, or anonymously on 0800 555 111.
