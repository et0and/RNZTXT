Police name missing teenager, search at Auckland beach continues

Police have confirmed that Hiran Joseph, 19, went missing at Karioitahi Beach in Auckland yesterday.

Police have named the missing swimmer as Hiran Joseph, 19, from Auckland. Photo: Supplied / Surf Life Saving New Zealand

The search has continued today for a missing swimmer at Karioitahi Beach in Auckland.

Police have named the teenager as Hiran Joseph, 19, from Auckland.

He was swimming with two others on the evening of 2 January when they got into difficulty, said police.

Two made it back to shore, but Joseph remained missing.

Search efforts have been ongoing today with support from Police Land SAR and Surf Life Saving NZ, said police.

They were continuing to provide support to Joseph's family.
