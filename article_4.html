Whanganui Hospital building evacuated after chemical leak

A building on the Whanganui Hospital campus was evacuated on Wednesday afternoon after a chemical leak.

A Whanganui Hospital building was evacuated this afternoon due to a chemical leak. Photo: RNZ / Robin Martin

A building on the Whanganui Hospital campus was evacuated on Wednesday afternoon after a chemical leak.

One building was evacuated, but Health New Zealand says it was quickly contained by the local fire brigade and there were no reports of injuries.

In a post on social media Health NZ - Whanganui said they were "grateful for the speedy actions of all involved".

"We are pleased to report, due to excellent planning and procedures for this type of incident, the site was swiftly evacuated with the spill quickly contained by our local Fire Brigade," it said.

All entrances remain open, with operations at the main hospital unaffected, it says.
