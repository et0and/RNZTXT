Flight delays and cancellations as fog hits

Fog restrictions have been lifted at Auckland Airport.

Almost a dozen flights have been cancelled because of fog at Auckland Airport (file photo). Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

Fog restrictions have been lifted at Auckland Airport.

Eleven domestic regional flights were cancelled and three regional flights delayed on Tuesday morning.

A flight due to arrive from Wellington was also cancelled.

It follows fog disruption on Monday.

Auckland Airport said international flights were not affected and passengers should check with their airlines for more information.
