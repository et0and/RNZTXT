Pipe bursts, pavement closes on central Wellington road

Wellington Water said crews were still investigating what caused the burst.

Pipe that burst on Brandon Street, Wellington. Photo: RNZ/Jazlyn Whales

A burst pipe has left a pool of dirty silty water on a central Wellington footpath.

Wellington Water said crews were investigating what caused the problem on Brandon Street - which had closed a pavement section.

It said it was not sewage water.

The city had ongoing problems with its water infrastructure.

Wellington Water said the burst pipe was not leaking sewage water. Photo: RNZ/Jazlyn Whales
