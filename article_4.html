Pedestrian dies after being hit by ute Canterbury

The crash happened in Sheffield early on Tuesday.

The crash in Sheffield closed a section of SH73. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A pedestrian has died after being hit by a ute in Canterbury.

The crash happened between Malvern Hills Road and Duke Street in Sheffield.

It was reported to police about 6.40am on Tuesday.

State Highway 73 was closed at the crash site and and police asked the public to avoid the area.

The police serious crash unit was examining the scene.
