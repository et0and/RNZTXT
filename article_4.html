Police 'urgently' seeking help to find missing Auckland woman

The 38-year-old's car was found near a park in West Auckland's Glen Eden, but she has yet to be found.

Alyssa Jouk, 38. Photo: Supplied / Police

Police are "urgently" asking to public to help them find a missing Auckland woman.

Alyssa Jouk, 38, was last seen earlier on Friday wearing a short white jacket and white trackpants. Her hair was tied up in a short ponytail.

Police said Jouk's car was found at 1.30pm at an address by Ceramco Park on Glendale Road in West Auckland's Glen Eden.

"Police and her family have serious concerns for her wellbeing.

"Police ask the public across Tamaki Makaurau to please let us know if you have any information on her whereabouts."

People are asked to call police on 111 immediately if they have seen her, referencing P058906192.

Information can also be provided anonymously via Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
