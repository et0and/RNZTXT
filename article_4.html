Shop blaze in Royal Oak quickly dowsed

Four fire trucks attended the blaze and it was out within half an hour.

Four fire trucks attended the blaze and it was out within half-an-hour. Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Firefighters were called to a blaze in a shop in Auckland's Royal Oak on Wednesday morning.

Fire and Emergency said the fire was in one shop on Manukau Road, near Trafalgar Street, at 2.45am.

Four fire trucks attended the blaze and it was out within half-an-hour.

No one was inside at the time.
