Smouldering vegetation fire in Wellington

A helicopter is now working to dampen the fire.

Photo: Supplied / Facebook / David Jensen

A helicopter is working to dampen a smouldering vegetation fire in the southern Wellington suburb of Makara on Sunday morning.

Fire and Emergency said it received a call about the blaze from a vessel in Cook Strait at about 8pm.

While crews were unable to access the location due to the terrain, it monitored the fire overnight until a helicopter could be brought in to assist.

Fire and Emergency said the helicopter is now working to dampen the fire which is currently a smouldering patch.
