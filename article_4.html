Second man charged with murder of Damon O'Rourke

The 21-year-old is also facing a charge of unlawful possession of a firearm.

Police at the scene of the homicide on 20 January 2024. Photo: RNZ / Nick James

A 21-year-old man is the second person to be charged with the murder of Palmerston North man Damon O'Rourke.

He has also been charged with unlawful possession of a firearm.

O'Rourke was found with gunshot injuries at a property on Coromandel Court on 20 January. He died in hospital shortly after.
