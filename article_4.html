Staff revived men found unconscious at bottom of Auckland swimming pool

Two men who got into trouble in the water at an Auckland pool on Saturday night had to be resuscitated by staff and visitors.

Two men who got into trouble in the water at an Auckland pool on Saturday night had to be resuscitated by staff and visitors.

Emergency services were called to the Olympic Pools and Fitness Centre in Newmarket at 7.30pm.

A witness old Newshub he noticed two men in their 20s or 30s at the bottom of a pool at the Olympic Pools and Fitness Centre. He dove down to check on them and saw that they were unconscious.

Pool director Peter Rust said staff - with the assistance of some pool users - resuscitated the pair, who were taken to hospital in a critical condition.

"Emergency services responded quickly and the police have finished their examination."

He said they would have an update later on Sunday morning.
