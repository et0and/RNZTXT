Video: Large fire burst out at scrap metal yard

Firefighters are battling a blaze in Auckland.

Firefighters are battling a large blaze at a scrap metal yard in Takanini, South Auckland.

Fire and Emergency said they were called to the fire just before 10.30am this morning and sent three fire trucks.

The fire is about 5 sq m.

A fire in South Auckland could be seen from the road. Photo: Amy Williams / RNZ

Auckland resident Iain Hudson drove past the fire a short time ago and said it is in a yard right next to the southern motorway.

"We noticed quite large amounts of black smoke even heading through Manukau...really thick black smoke going across the motorway and quite a few people slowing down to have a look."

He said it appeared a pile of metal rubbish was on fire.
