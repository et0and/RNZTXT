Driver killed in Ashburton highway crash

[picture id="4MYWOCU_copyright_image_220966" crop="16x10" layout="thumbnail"] Police were called to the crash overnight.

Police were called to the crash overnight. Photo: RNZ / Patrice Allen

A driver has been killed in a single-vehicle crash on the Methven Highway in Ashburton.

Police say the sole-occupant of the vehicle died at the scene of the crash about 1am on Monday.

The road has since reopened, while police continue to investigage.
