Crash closes SH2 near Whakatāne

The collision was reported about 2pm on Saturday, near Dunlop Road, in Waiotahe.

Emergency services are working at the scene of a serious crash on State Highway 2, north of Ōpōtiki. Photo: Supplied/ NZTA

Part of State Highway 2 is closed after a serious crash east of Whakatāne.

Emergency services were working at the scene of the two-vehicle crash, police said.

The collision was reported about 2pm on Saturday, near Dunlop Road, Waiotahe, and State Highway 2 was blocked, they said.

The Transport Agency Waka Kotahi said the highway was closed between Woodlands Road and Paerata Ridge Road.

Police said diversion were being set up.
