Cyclone-hit Great Walk to reopen in new year

The storm washed out parts of the track, flooded huts and wrecked a cable bridge.

Photo: 123rf.com

The Waikaremoana Great Walk, on the East Coast, will reopen in the new year, after being severely damaged in Cyclone Gabrielle.
