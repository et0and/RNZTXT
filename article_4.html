Five in custody after Mākara armed incident

Five people have been arrested following an incident at a property in the small settlement of Mākara, near Wellington.

The Police Armed Offenders Squad and armed police have responded to a report of a person acting threateningly with a gun in a small Wellington community. (File photo) Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Five people have been arrested following an incident at a property in the small settlement of Mākara, near Wellington.

Police were called to a house just before 2pm, after reports of a person being threatened with a firearm.

The Armed Offenders Squad responded and cordons were put in place.

Police said three people were arrested as they left the property in a vehicle, while two others who had fled on foot were found nearby and arrested.

Investigations are continuing and no charges have yet been laid.
