Lindis Pass closed following serious crash

The Lindis Pass north of Cromwell in Otago is blocked following a serious crash.

File photo. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

The Lindis Pass north of Cromwell in Otago is blocked following a serious crash.

The pass on State Highway 8 is the main inland route between Canterbury and Otago.

Police said the crash took place about 15 kilometres from the summit of the pass on State Highway 8, and involved two vehicles.

SH8 TARRAS TO OMARAMA (LINDIS PASS) - SERIOUS CRASH - 9:45AMDue to a serious crash, SH8 Lindis Pass is now CLOSED. Avoid the area, use an alternative route or delay your travel through the Pass this morning. ^CS pic.twitter.com/g9QPhFPuRC

Waka Kotahi said on Facebook that the serious crash unit was likely to attend the scene.

Motorists were advised to take an alternate route, as the road was expected to be closed for some time.

Waka Kotahi said southbound traffic could detour via SH83, SH1, SH85 and back onto SH8, with the reverse available for northbound traffic - with advice to allow up to an additional 2.5 hours for this route.
