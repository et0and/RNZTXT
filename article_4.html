One dead in Rotorua crash on SH5

The Easter road toll has increased to four after a person died in a crash on State Highway 5 in Rotorua this morning.

This brings the road toll for the Easter period to four. Photo: 123RF

One person has died following a crash on State Highway 5, Whakarewarewa, south of Rotorua early Sunday morning.

The two-vehicle crash was reported to police just after 5am.

Another person sustained serious injuries.

The road would be closed for some time so motorists should use alternative routes, police said.

This brings the road toll for the Easter period to four.

On Saturday, three people died in a crash on State Highway 8 near Pukaki.

The crash involved two vehicles and a motorcycle, and happened between Hayman Road and Tekapo Canal Road.

In 2023, there was one death reported on the roads during the Easter break.
