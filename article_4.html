Seven people hurt as hot air balloon crashes into fence

The balloon crashed into fences before landing in a paddock near Wanaka.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Emergency services have responded to a hot air balloon crash Otago.

Police say they were alerted to the crash at Queensberry near Wanaka shortly after 8.30am on Wednesday.

St John treated seven people, six with minor injuries and another who was in a moderate condition.

Fire and Emergency (FENZ) said the balloon crashed into fences before landing in a paddock.

A spokesperson said a crew from Luggate responded to what it described as "a rough landing".
