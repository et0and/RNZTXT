Fire breaks out at Avondale Jockey Club

Emergency services remain at the scene of the Auckland-based club after a fire broke out after midnight, with crews dampening down hot spots.

Avondale racecourse file photo. Photo: Flickr / Public domain

Emergency services remain at the scene of a large blaze at Avondale Jockey Club in Auckland overnight.

The fire broke out just after midnight, and at it's peak there were 19 fire trucks in attendance.

A FENZ spokesperson said the fire was brought under control by 1am, but crews were still dampening down hot spots.
