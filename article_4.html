Student charged over Wellington Girls' College assault

One person was seriously injured in the incident.

Photo: RNZ/Nick James

A student has been charged with wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm after an incident at Wellington Girls' College last week.

On Friday, a student was seriously hurt and taken to hospital after an assault at the school.

The college confirmed at the time two students were involved.

Police said emergency services were called to the school on Pipitea Street in Thorndon just after 10am.

Police said the student would appear before the Youth Court this week.
