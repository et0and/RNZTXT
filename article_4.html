Seven people rescued after boat fire in Auckland

Seven people have been rescued by police after a boat caught on fire at Waiheke Island this evening.

File photo: Waiheke Island. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Seven people have been rescued by police after a boat caught on fire in Auckland this evening.

Emergency services were notified that a boat was on fire in ÅŒneroa Bay at Waiheke Island just before 6pm.

Fire and Emergency crews used the Maritime Unit boats to access the vessel and extinguish the blaze.

A police spokesperson said all seven occupants were safely removed from the boat, with some suffering from smoke inhalation.

An investigation was underway for the cause of the fire.
