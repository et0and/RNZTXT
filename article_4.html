One dead after fatal crash in Christchurch

One person has died after a two-vehicle crash in the Christchurch suburb Hei Hei this afternoon.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

One person has died after a two-vehicle crash in the Christchurch suburb Hei Hei this afternoon.

Police said they were called to Buchanans Road about 1.40pm, but the person died at the scene.

The New Zealand Herald has reported the crash victim was a motorcyclist killed in a collision with a truck.
