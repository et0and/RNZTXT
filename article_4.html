Magnitude 5.1 earthquake jolts Canterbury

About an hour later a 4.7 tremor struck near Haast on the West Coast.

The quake that struck early on Thursday. Photo: Geonet

A magnitude 5.1 earthquake has woken Cantabrians.

People reported feeling the 5km-deep quake, centred 35km west of Culverden, as far as the West Coast, GeoNet says.

It struck about 6.45am on Thursday.

About an hour later a 4.7 earthquake struck on the West Coast.

GeoNet said the shallow 6km quake was centered about 10km from Haast.

It hit at 7.49am.
