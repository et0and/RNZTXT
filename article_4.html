Police hunt for group who forced person into vehicle and assaulted them in Northland

The person was not injured during the incident.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Police are on the hunt for a group of people who forced someone into a vehicle and assaulted them in Northland.

A police spokesperson says the incident was reported about 3.25pm in the Ohaeawai area.

The person says they were forced into the vehicle, were assaulted and had something stolen from them before they were let out.

They were not injured.

Police are now looking for the vehicle and the people involved.
