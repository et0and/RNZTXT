14-year-old autistic girl reported missing in Whangārei

Hayden, who is autistic was last seen at her Tikipunga home on Sunday night.

Hayden was last seen at her Tikipunga home on Sunday night. Photo: Police / Supplied

A 14-year-old autistic girl has been reported missing in Whangārei.

Hayden was last seen at her Tikipunga home on Sunday night.

Police said she was of slim build with brown eyes and hair. She was likely to be wearing a pink and white tie-dye hoodie with black pants and tan Ugg boots.

"Police and Hayden's family have concerns for her wellbeing, and urge anyone with information on her whereabouts to call 111 and reference job number 240812/0324," police said in a statement.

Anyone with further information should call police on 105 or contact Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
