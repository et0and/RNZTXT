Body found in Tauranga walkway

Tauranga police have launched a homicide investigation after a man was discovered dead in Gate Pa.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Tauranga police have launched a homicide investigation after a body was found.

They were called at 8.45am on Sunday to a walkway in Gate Pa, near George Street, after the discovery of a dead man.

"Currently one person is assisting with our enquiries," police said in a release.

Anyone with information was urged to call 105 and cite the reference number P060196188, or call Crime Stoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
