New Zealand residents escape Gaza, Peters says

Five New Zealand permanent residents have escaped Gaza through the Rafah crossing overnight, Minister of Foreign Affairs Winston Peters says.

The Rafah crossing, between Gaza and Egypt (file photo) Photo: AFP / Said Khatib

Five New Zealand permanent residents have escaped Gaza through the Rafah crossing overnight.

Minister of Foreign Affairs Winston Peters shared the development on X (formerly Twitter).

Last month at least 11 New Zealanders and three permanent residents made it out of Gaza.
