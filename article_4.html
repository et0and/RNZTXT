Murder charge after death of 22-year-old in Christchurch

A 67-year-old man has been charged with murder after the death at a Christchurch property.

File photo. Photo: 123RF

A man has been charged with murder after the death of a 22-year-old at a Christchurch property.

Police were called to a property on Hereford Street just after 10pm on Sunday after reports of a man being stabbed.

Detective Senior Sergeant Craig Johnson said the 22-year-old died a short time later.

He said the accused, 67, is scheduled to appear in Christchurch District Court on Monday morning.
