Armed police in Auckland suburb, cordons in place

Armed officers are standing guard in Waterview as a police operation unfolds.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

An area in the Auckland suburb of Waterview has been cordoned off by police on Wednesday afternoon.

Armed officers were standing guard around Tutuki St as a police operation unfolded.

Police said officers were armed as a precaution.

A spokesperson asked members of the public to stay clear of the cordons.

Nearby Great North Rd remained open for traffic.
