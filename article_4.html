Two people in critical condition after serious crash near Te Anau

Only one vehicle was involved in the crash which closed the road for most of early Monday morning.

SH95 has reopened after a serious crash overnight. Photo: Waka Kotahi

Two people are in a critical condition after a crash near Te Anau overnight.

The single vehicle crash happened around 12am on the Manapouri to Te Anau highway (SH95).

One other person received moderate injuries.

Waka Kotahi said the road was closed following the crash but had since reopened.

"This section of SH95 has now fully reopened. Take extra care when travelling along this route."
