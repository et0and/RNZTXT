Forty firefighters battle blaze in central Auckland

Fire and Emergency says a generator caught fire in the CBD.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Forty firefighters have been battling a blaze in central Auckland, after a generator caught fire.

The blaze was reported about 11pm Thursday and firefighters were still at the scene in the early hours of Friday morning.

The New Zealand Herald reported that the blaze broke out at Port of Auckland.

However, Fire and Emergency would only say that it was in the CBD.
