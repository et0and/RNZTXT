Missing man's car found near Wairarapa bush walk

Police, Land Search and Rescue and a helicopter have been looking for Ben Fox, also known as Sam.

Ben Fox, 56, was last seen at his Masterton home on Wednesday morning. Photo: Supplied / Police

A search has been launched for a man who could have gone missing in the Wairarapa bush.

Ben Fox, also known as Sam, was last seen at his Masterton home on Wednesday morning.

His vehicle has since been found on Mangatere Valley Road near the Mangatere Valley walking track.

Fox is of a medium build, 172cm tall, and has short hair.

Police and Land, Search and Rescue had been searching the area and were working to determine whether he entered the track.

A helicopter was also deployed today in the search.

Ben Fox's car, a white 2013 Subaru Impreza. Photo: Supplied / Police

Officers were appealing for information from anyone who may have seen Fox's white 2013 Subaru Impreza on Mangatere Road between Wednesday morning and Thursday afternoon, police said.

They also wanted to hear from anyone who saw him on the Mangatere track, or who had any information that could assist in locating him.
