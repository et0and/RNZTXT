Large fire in Te Rapa prompts toxic smoke warning

A large fire in the Hamilton suburb of Te Rapa has prompted a toxic smoke warning for neighbouring areas.

Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

A large fire in the Hamilton suburb of Te Rapa has prompted a toxic smoke warning for neighbouring areas.

Fire and Emergency crews were called to a building,, which was being used as used as boat storage, on fire about 5.30pm

Fire and Emergency advised residents in Chartwell and Rototuna to stay inside with their windows and doors closed as they tried to put out the fire.

A mobile alert was sent to residents in those areas about 7pm, advising them do so until further notice.

The public have been asked to avoid the Te Rapa area until further notice.
