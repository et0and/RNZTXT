Quake felt in lower North Island

A 4.2 magnitude earthquake was felt in the lower North Island on Monday morning.

The moderate earthquake on Monday morning was centred at 15km west of Pongaroa. Photo: GeoNet

A 4.2 magnitude earthquake was felt in the lower North Island on Monday morning.

It was centred 15km west of Pongaroa at a depth of 8km, and struck at 7.51am.

Geonet described the tremor as a moderate quake.
