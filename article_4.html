4 magnitude quake rattles North Canterbury

According to GeoNet the quake was centred 15km south-east of Amberley.

Photo: 123RF

A magnitude 4 earthquake has caused light shaking near Amberley in North Canterbury.

GeoNet said the quake was 15 kilometres south-east of the town, at a depth of 22kms.

More than 2000 people have reported feeling the shake, including in Christchurch.
