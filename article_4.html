Crews fight large house fire on Auckland's North Shore

Fire and Emergency were called to the blaze in Castor Bay just before 5am.

Fire crews were called about 5am on Monday to the blaze on the North Shore. Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Ten fire appliances and crews have been working to extinguish a house fire in Castor Bay on the North Shore of Auckland

Fire and Emergency were called to Beach Road just before 5am on Monday and found the house well ablaze.

No-one was hurt, and all people in the house are accounted for.

People were asked to avoid the area.
