Hamilton woman missing for more than a month

Police are asking for help locating a Hamilton woman who has been missing for more than a month.

Police say Megan was last seen 21 December. Photo: Supplied / Waikato Police

Police are asking for help locating a Hamilton woman who has been missing for more than a month.

Megan, 43, was last seen at her home in Frankton on 21 December.

It is believed she may be travelling in a Silver Toyota Blade, registration JLM996, police said.

Megan's family are concerned for her wellbeing and would like to see her return home, police said.

Anyone with information on Megan's whereabouts, can contact police at 105.police.govt.nz, clicking "Update Report" or by calling 105. Please use the reference number 250125/7910.
