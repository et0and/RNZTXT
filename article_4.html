Search on after unexplained fire guts South Island home

Police say it's not known how many people were in the Manapouri house.

Fire crews responded to the fire at a single-storey house in Manapouri about 3.30am on Thursday. Photo: Southland App

An overnight fire has gutted a house in Manapouri and investigators are at the scene.

Crews responded to the fire at a single-storey house in Manapouri about 3.30am.

Police and Fire and Emergency are at the scene along with a fire investigator.

Southland Area Commander Inspector Mike Bowman says its unknown how many people were at the address when the fire started.

Police are working with Fire and emergency to account for them.

Police say the cause of the blaze is unexplained.
