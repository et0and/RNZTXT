Bay of Plenty scrub fire blocks SH2

Police say there were reports of the fire near Te Puna stream received shortly before 2pm.

Emergency services are at the scene of the fire. Photo: Pretoria Gordon / RNZ

Emergency services are responding to a scrub fire in the Whakamārama area in the Bay of Plenty, which has blocked State Highway 2.

Police have said there were reports of the fire near Te Puna stream received shortly before 2pm.

State Highway 2 and some nearby roads are blocked, and police are urging people to delay any travel between Tauranga and Katikati.
