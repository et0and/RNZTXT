Person dies after water-related incident in Gisborne

Police were called to an address on Te Araroa Road in Hicks Bay shortly before 4pm.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

A person has died in a water related incident in Gisborne on Thursday afternoon.

Police said they were called to an address on Te Araroa Road in Hicks Bay shortly before 4pm.

It said the death will be referred to the coroner.
