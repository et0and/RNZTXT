Gas leak closes roads on Auckland's North Shore

Police say a gas leak in Auckland's Milford area has been fixed.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police say a gas leak in Auckland's Milford area has been fixed.

They were notified of the leak on Shakespeare Road about 11am and responded alongside Fire and Emergency.

Cordons have been removed and the road is open to traffic.
