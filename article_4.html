Multiple car crash causes commuter chaos on SH1 in Wellington

Commuter chaos has ensued in Wellington after a three-vehicle crash on State Highway 1.

The crash was reported about 6.44am, and motorists should expect delays. Photo: NZTA

Commuter chaos ensued in Wellington after a three-vehicle crash on the Johnsonville-Porirua Motorway at 6.44am.

All the cars have been towed and all lanes on SH1 Linden have reopened, the Transport Authority said.

Police said there were no injuries.

Motorists are being warned of congestion as traffic starts to flow.

Earlier on Wednesday, the right southbound lane at Linden south of the Kenepuru interchange was blocked for nearly two hours.

Queues were backed up to the Mungavin Interchange.

Six cars appeared to be involved in the crash, an RNZ reporter at the scene said.

UPDATE 8AMTow trucks are now on-site. The right lane remains BLOCKED on SH1 Linden southbound. Please keep using the left lanes and expect DELAYS. ^SG https://t.co/IK3g9AJV4q pic.twitter.com/VhaDna4TIl

Meanwhile, police said there had been another single vehicle crash just north of the Johnsonville turn-off.

There were also no injuries in that crash and it was now clearing.
