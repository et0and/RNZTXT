Police stand-off continues into second day in Clutha

Officers are attempting to engage with the person who is being sought in relation to suspected firearms offending.

Photo: RNZ

Police remain at a Clutha residence and are attempting to engage with a person who is being sought in relation to suspected firearms offending.

Officers said they had talked to the person on Tuesday, who was believed to be alone, at a rural Clutha property.

The situation is contained and cordons are in place to ensure public safety, police said.
