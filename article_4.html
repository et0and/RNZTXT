Fire at South Auckland shops treated as suspicious

Three fire trucks were called to the blaze in Manurewa.

Generic fire truck. Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

A blaze at a block of shops in South Auckland morning is being treated as suspicious.

Three fire trucks were called to the fire in Manurewa about 4am.

Fire and Emergency says the blaze has been extinguished and a fire investigator will be on site on Tuesday.
