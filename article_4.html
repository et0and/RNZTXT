SH35 Whāngārā blocked after crash

Two people have been injured in the crash.

Photo: RNZ / Tim Brown

Two people have been injured in a one vehicle crash on State Highway 35, near Waiomoko Road, Whāngārā, Gisborne.

One person is in a serious condition, the other has suffered minor injuries, police said in a statement.

The road is blocked and police are advising motorists to take State Highway 2.

The crash was reported about 9.50am.

The Serious Crash Unit has been advised.
