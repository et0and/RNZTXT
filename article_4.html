Woman wanted by police believed to be in Wellington area

Police are asking the public to help find a woman who has breached her bail conditions.

Breeze Hunt-Weston. Photo: Supplied / NZ Police

Police are asking the public to help find a woman who has breached her bail conditions.

Breeze Hunt-Weston, 31, has a warrant for her arrest, and is believed to be in the Wellington district.

Anyone who sees her, or has any information on her whereabouts, is asked to call 111, or Crimestoppers - anonymously - on 0800 555 111, and quote case 201220/8063.
