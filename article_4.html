Gas leak causes major traffic delays in Manurewa

A gas leak from a property on Hill Road is causing significant disruption to traffic flows in the area, police say.

Messages written on an Auckland fire engine protesting firefighters' working conditions. Photo: RNZ / Rayssa Almeida

A gas leak from a property is causing significant disruption to traffic flows in the area of Hill Road in Manurewa, police say.

Emergency services were alerted just before 3pm.

Police are assisting Fire and Emergency New Zealand with the incident.

Diversions are in place but are causing traffic to back up prior to the Southern Motorway (SH 1) off-ramp to Hill Road.

Motorists are advised to avoid the area and find an alternative route.
