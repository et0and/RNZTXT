'Dangerous' man on the run, public asked to stay away

Police are looking for Norton Kinnaird, 30, who has "several active charges" against him.

Norton Kinnaird. Photo: NZ Police

Police are on the hunt for Norton Kinnaird, who is currently on several active charges and is considered "dangerous".

The public is being asked not to approach the 30 year old who has "several active charges" against him, and a warrant for his arrest.

Any sightings of him or information on his whereabouts can be reported to police on 111, quoting case number 240501/4148.
