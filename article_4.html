One dead in Arthur's Pass crash

They were found dead at the scene.

The victim was found dead at the scene. Photo: RNZ / Patrice Allen

One person is dead following a crash in Arthur's Pass.

The single-vehicle accident on State Highway 73 was reported to police at 8.20am on Sunday morning.

The victim was found dead at the scene.

Police said motorists should expect delays and avoid the area, if possible.
