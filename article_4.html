Body of missing woman found in Hamilton Gardens

A body has been found in the search for a woman missing in Hamilton.

Hamilton Gardens. Photo: supplied

A body has been found in the search for a woman missing in Hamilton.

Earlier on Friday, police appealed for sightings of the missing 77-year-old after she was reported as missing on Thursday.

Her body was found near Hamilton Gardens on Friday afternoon.

The death has been referred to the Coroner.
