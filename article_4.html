Bus fire at Westgate

Fire and Emergency crews have responded to a bus that caught fire in West Auckland earlier this evening.

Fire and Emergency crews responded to a bus that caught fire in West Auckland on Monday evening.

Crews were called to the scene of the blaze in Westgate about 7.15pm.

The bus fire was "well involved" when firefighters arrived but has since been extinguished.

No injuries had been reported.

Auckland Transport has been contact for comment.
