Man critically injured in reported Auckland stabbing

Emergency services were called just after 9pm to a reported stabbing in Papatoetoe.

Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

Armed police, supported by the Eagle helicopter, descended on a home in Auckland after a man was found critically injured on Saturday night.

A police spokesperson said they were called to an address on Wallace Road in Papatoetoe just after 9pm.

They said police were armed in precaution, as initial reports did not indicate how the man was hurt. The New Zealand Herald reported he was stabbed.

No charges have been, laid but another man - known to the victim - was assisting police.
