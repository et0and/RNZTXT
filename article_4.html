Police respond to unexplained death in Christchurch

A police spokesperson said the death was reported just before 11am on Tuesday.

(file image) Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police in Christchurch are responding to a death at a house in Linwood.

A police spokesperson said the death was reported just before 11am on Tuesday.

The death was being treated as unexplained, police said.
