SH6 partially closed after fatal crash near Greymouth

The two vehicle crash in Paroa was reported just after 4pm on Thursday and the person died at the scene, police said.

(File photo) Photo: 123rf.com

One person has been killed in a crash south of Greymouth.

The two vehicle crash in Paroa was reported just after 4pm on Thursday, a police spokesperson said.

"Despite the best efforts of emergency services, the person died at the scene," they said.

Part of State Highway 6 at Paroa was closed for the Serious Crash Unit to carry out a scene examination.

NZTA said the highway was closed near the intersection of Clough Road.

A detour was operating for both north and south-bound traffic, but they asked motorists to plan for delays.
