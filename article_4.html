Two people injured after Wellington crash

Two vehicles collided on State Highway One, leaving two people in moderate and serious conditions.

(File image). Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Two people have been injured after a two vehicle crash in Wellington.

Emergency services were responding to the crash on State Highway One in Ranui, Porirua that was reported at 12.10pm.

One person was in a moderate condition while the other was in a serious condition.

Police said the southbound lane was blocked just before the Porirua-Titahi Bay on-ramp and traffic was congested.

Motorists were advised to expect delays and use an alternative route where possible.
