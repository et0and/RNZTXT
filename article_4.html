State Highway 3 in north Taranaki closed after crash

Drivers should expect delays going in and out of north Taranaki after a crash closed part of State Highway 3, NZTA says.

Google maps screenshot showing SH3 closed between Ohanga Road and Kaipikari Road near Urenui. Photo: Google Maps

Drivers should expect delays going in and out of north Taranaki after a crash closed part of State Highway 3, says NZTA.

Main North Road/State Highway 3 was closed between Ohanga Road and Kaipikari Road near Urenui following a crash.

SH3 ONAERO, TARANAKI - CRASH - 11:20AMThe road is now CLOSED near the intersection of Onaero River Rd. Emergency services are on-site, please follow their directions. There is no detour available, please delay your journey if possible. ^SG pic.twitter.com/koUknrAwYI

Police were called to the three-vehicle crash just before 11am.

According to initial reports, some of those involved had been seriously injured, police said.

Motorists were advised to avoid the area and expect delays.

Emergency services were at the scene of the crash, the NZ Transport Agency said.

"There is no detour available. Please delay your journey if possible."
