Man believed to be involved in Tūrangi car crash found safe

Before being found he had last been seen 28 February.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

A man missing for more than two weeks in Tūrangi has been found safe.

The man was thought to be involved in a car crash on SH32 on 12 March, police said.

Before being found he had last been seen 28 February.
