12 infringement notices after funeral procession with gang members

Police say there have been no significant issues from a funeral which involved large numbers of Mongrel Mob members in Porirua.

(file image) Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

Police say there have been no significant issues from a funeral which involved large numbers of Mongrel Mob members in Porirua.

The procession started on Thursday morning, going from Cannons Creek, through Tawa, and stopping at Whenua Tapu Cemetery for the service.

The gang members were attending the funeral of a man who died in Tawa last week.
