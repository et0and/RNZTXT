Delays expected after crash in Lower Hutt

Three people were injured, one seriously, after the two-vehicle crash.

Emergency services were alerted to the two-vehicle crash on High Street just before 10.30am. Photo:

High Street is closed following a serious crash in Lower Hutt on Saturday morning.

Emergency services were alerted to the two-vehicle crash on High Street just before 10.30am.

One person was seriously injured and two others received minor to moderate injuries.

The closure is between the Eastern Hutt Road roundabout and Waitara Street.

Motorists are advised to follow diversions and expect delays.

The Serious Crash Unit has been notified.
