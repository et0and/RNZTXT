Two injured in crash on SH1 south of Timaru

Hato Hone St John were alerted at 11.30am and sent an ambulance and a rapid response vehicle to the crash site.

(file photo) Photo: Davina Zimmer

Emergency services have attended a crash on State Highway 1, south of Timaru.

Hato Hone St John said they were notified at 11.30am and responded with one ambulance and one rapid response vehicle.

Staff treated two people at the scene - one in a moderate condition and one with minor injuries. No one was transported to hospital, it said.
