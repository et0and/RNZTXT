Light plane crash in Tauranga

Two people have been transported to hospital with minor injuries.

Two people were taken to hospital with minor injuries after a light plane crash in Tauranga. Photo: St John

Two people have received minor injuries after reports of a light plane crash in Tauranga.

Emergency services were called to Tauranga Airport just before 5pm on Monday.

Police said the pair have been transported to hospital.
