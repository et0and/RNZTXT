Where were the winning Lotto tickets sold?

Five of the Powerball players who won a share of the $50 million jackpot were from one city.

There were no Powerball First Division winners, so the jackpot rolled down to the second division. Photo: RNZ

Five of the Powerball players who won a $7.18 million share of the $50 million jackpot are from Auckland; the other two are from Hamilton and Hastings.

There were no Powerball first division winners, so the jackpot rolled down to the second division.

The winning tickets were sold at:

Strike Four was won last night by a player from Auckland, who took home $600,000. The winning ticket was sold at Jalaram Dairy in Auckland.

Each $7.18m prize was made up of $7.155,649 from the first division Powerball, and the second division prize of $7,180,041.
