Person critically injured at Christchurch ice-skating rink

The person was taken to Christchurch Hospital after an incident at the sports centre.

Alpine Ice Sports Centre on Brougham Street, Christchurch. Photo: Google Maps

A person has been taken to hospital in a critical condition after an incident at a Christchurch ice-skating rink.

St John said it was called to the Alpine Ice Sports Centre on Brougham Street at 11.03am.

Two ambulances and a rapid response vehicle were sent to the sports centre and transported one patient to Christchurch Hospital in a critical condition.

The centre said it was closed for the rest of the day.
