Motorcyclist dies after Palmerston North crash

The person was taken to hospital in a serious condition, but succumbed to their injuries.

Photo: 123RF

A motorcyclist has died following a crash in Palmerston North.

Police said the crash, between a motorcycle and a car, was reported about 7pm Thursday.

It happened at the intersection of Milson Line and Ruahine Street in Roslyn.

"The motorcyclist was taken to hospital in a serious condition, where they have later died," police said about 9.40pm.

"The Serious Crash Unit was notified, and the road is expected to fully reopen shortly."
