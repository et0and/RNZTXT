Auckland Southern Motorway traffic improves after earlier incident

Emergency services were called to the East Tāmaki overbridge at 7.45am.

Traffic at 8.30am Monday on Auckland's SH1 and SH20. Photo: Google Maps

Traffic on Auckland's Southern Motorway has improved after an earlier incident.

Emergency services were called to the incident on the East Tāmaki overbridge at 7.45am on Monday.

Just before 8am two right northbound lanes were temporarily blocked, causing delays.
