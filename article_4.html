Car fire blocks SH20A lanes to Auckland Airport

Waka Kotahi warns commuters to delay their journey or use an alternate route.

A car on fire has blocked two lanes of SH20, Auckland. Photo: Twitter / Waka Kotahi

Traffic is congested on Auckland's State Highway 20A Tuesday morning due to a car fire.

There are delays on both SH20A and Puhinui Road.

One southbound lane towards Auckland Airport is blocked, just before the Landing Drive intersection.

Two fire trucks attended and there was no injuries.

In a statement, police said officers were attending after the fire was reported shortly before 6am.

The incident was being cleaned up, Waka Kotahi said.
