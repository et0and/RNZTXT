Police in standoff with person refusing to leave house

Christchurch police are in a standoff with a person who has locked themselves in a house.

Photo:

Christchurch police are in a standoff with a person who has locked themselves in a house.

The armed officers have been at the Beatrice Place property in the suburb of Avonhead since earlier this afternoon, where they have been encouraging the person to come outside.

Local residents are being advised to avoid the area while police resolve the matter.
