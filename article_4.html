Missing Tokoroa teenager found safe

Police said that a missing Tokoroa 15-year-old has been found safe after being reported missing Friday night.

Tiwani may be with a man, police said. Photo: Supplied / Police

Police said that a missing Tokoroa 15-year-old has been found safe after being reported missing Friday night.

Police thanked everyone who aided in the search.

In a statement earlier today, police said Tiwani was last seen by family at her Tokoroa home around 7pm on Friday.

"There are concerns for her welfare and police and her loved ones want to locate her and know she is okay."
