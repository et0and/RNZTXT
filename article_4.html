Bus driver suffers concussion in crash

An Auckland bus driver is one of the two people injured in a crash in the suburb of Epsom earlier today.

Photo: RNZ

An Auckland bus driver is one of the two people injured in a crash in the suburb of Epsom earlier today.

Auckland Transport told RNZ there were passengers onboard the bus, but none were injured.

An AT spokesperson said the driver suffered a concussion.

Police were called to the scene at the intersection of Manukau Road and King George Avenue at about 1.50pm.

St John said two people were taken to Auckland Hospital, one in a serious condition and the other in a moderate condition.

The road, which had been partly blocked, is now clear.
