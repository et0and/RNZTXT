Firefighters battle blaze on Remuera property in Auckland

Firefighters are battling a blaze on a residential property in the Auckland suburb Remuera.

Fire and Emergency said it was a vegetation fire and it sent four fire trucks to the scene just before 2.30pm on Monday.
