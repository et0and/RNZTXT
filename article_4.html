Person and dog killed in house fire in Porirua

A second person was in hospital in a stable condition, and a dog also died in the blaze in Whitby.

Photo: Ashleigh McCaull/RNZ

A person has died in a house fire in Whitby, Porirua, and a second person was in hospital in a stable condition.

A dog also died in the blaze, police said.

Emergency services were called to the Lanyon Place address about 2.40am on Thursday.

Police said they were providing support to the family of those involved.

Fire and Emergency investigators and police will carry out a scene examination to determine the cause of the fire.
