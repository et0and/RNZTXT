Crews at scene of forest fire on Matakana Island in Bay of Plenty

Fire crews are responding to a forestry fire on the south of Matakana Island, near Tauranga.

File pic Photo: RNZ / Robin Martin

Fire crews are responding to a forestry fire on the south of Matakana Island, near Tauranga.

Fire and Emergency says it was was alerted to the blaze around 7.30pm on Saturday and sent several crews to the island.

Late last night shift manager Garreth Lewis described the fire as approximately 300 metres by 300 metres, and "very visible from the mainland".

Crews have been monitoring it overnight, and a barge and helicopter were due to be used to help fight it at first light.

The island has a small resident population and people are being asked to stay away from the fire.

Civil Defence said there have been no evacuations so far.
