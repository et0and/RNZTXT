Glitches in BNZ online banking for second time this weekend

BNZ says its online banking service is back online after an outage on Sunday morning.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

BNZ says its online banking service is back online after an outage on Sunday morning.

It is the second outage this weekend, following another on Saturday afternoon.

More than 300 people reported that they couldn't access BNZ between 9am and 12.30pm today.

Customers expressed their frustration on social media, saying the recurring outages were not good enough.

Kia ora Jjengar, we apologise for any inconvenience caused by this. Although issues were resolved as of 19:12 yesterday we are now dealing with further outages. We do not have an ETA for a fix at this time. To keep up to date please view our status pagehttps://t.co/ZdX4Vf0yoQ

BNZ said some customers might still have issues logging in.

In a statement to 1News earlier on Sunday, a BNZ spokesperson said the bank was affected by an outage as a result of an issue experienced by a network provider.

It apologised to customers for any inconvenience.
