Live: Slow start to Black Friday sales in Auckland

So far this year looks like it won't be a repeat of 2023's hours-long queues. Audio

It's been a slow start to Black Friday for some retailers in Auckland, despite heavily advertised discounts in stores.

The annual Black Friday sales officially started today, though many stores have been running sales in the lead-up to today.

In 2023, cars were stuck in hours-long queues in the Westfield Newmarket Mall carpark, the weekend before Black Friday.
