Inflation drops to 3-year low

The price of goods rose just 0.4% in the 3 months to June

Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

Inflation has slowed to a three year low, with consumer prices rising 0.4 percent in the three months ended June.

The annual rate slowed to 3.3 percent from 4 percent, the lowest since June 2021.

It also eased to its lowest level in nearly three years in the three months ended March.
