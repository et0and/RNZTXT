Fruit mince pies recalled after complaints and potential metal wire inside

Batches of Woolworths branded fruit mince pies have been recalled because they may contain metal wire.

Woolworths branded Summer Berry Mince Pies. Photo: Supplied / MPI

Batches of Woolworths branded fruit mince pies have been recalled because they may contain metal wire.

NZ Food Safety deputy director-general Vincent Arbuckle said some fine wire might have gone in the Summer Berry Mince Pies due to a manufacturing problem.

"Two consumer complaints have been received but fortunately no injuries have occurred," Arbuckle said.

"As is our usual practice, NZFS will be working with Woolworths to understand how the contamination occurred and prevent its recurrence."

The recall affects two batches of mince pies sold at Woolworths, Countdown, SuperValue and FreshChoice stores nationwide:

The batches under recall have already been removed from store shelves and have not been exported.

Anyone who has bought any of these mince pies, should not eat them, NZFS said. They can be returned to the place of purchase for a refund.

If you have consumed any of this product and are concerned for your health, contact your health professional, or call Healthline on 0800 611 116 for free advice.

For the latest information about batch details, visit New Zealand Food Safety's recall page.
