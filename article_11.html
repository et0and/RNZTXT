A postcard from Berlin's musical melting pot

Life in the German capital "often feels like living in a music festival", says Wellington expat Kava McIntyre Audio

DJ Kava: Postcards from Berlin Photo: supplied

On this episode of Postcards, Wellington expat Kava McIntyre reports back from the musical melting pot of Berlin.

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

"It often feels like living in a music festival", says Kava McIntyre, speaking to Music 101 about his home in Berlin. Based there with his family since 2011, he says he had an impression of "techno music in dark dungeons", but realised the city has "a lot of space, geographically, and also in the music".
