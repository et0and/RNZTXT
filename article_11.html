The Mixtape: Best of 2023 - part one

Notable New Zealanders share the songs which have meaning in their lives. Audio

As Music 101 begins to wrap up the year, we celebrating some of our favourite Mixtapes of 2023.

The Mixtape: Best of 2023 Pt.1 guests Photo: Supplied

Listen as Mikey Havoc, Hayley Sproull, Ariana Tikao, Gareth Farr, Hera Lindsay Bird, and Cleve Cameron share the songs which have meaning in their lives.
