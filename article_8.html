NZ Live: Love Square

Love Square – a musical venture led by Nick Atkinson – blends blues and jazz, reggae and dub, soul and funk. Video, Audio

Love Square Photo: supplied

Love Square is a musical venture put together by Nick Atkinson, former RNZ presenter, founding member of Supergroove and one-half of Hopetoun Brown.

The band blends blues and jazz, reggae and dub, soul and funk, with a revolving door of incredible musicians joining them for gigs here and there and in the recording studio.
