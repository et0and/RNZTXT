WATCH - Māori BMX rider Tyrell on passion, mentorship and happiness

Step inside the life of Tyrell Taylor in the new video series No Limits.

Series Classification: PG (Parental Guidance) | Watch the series here

In this episode, we are invited into the life of Tyrell Taylor, who has found a community of like-minded BMX riders, a space where rangatahi are given a chance to expand their skill set, be mentored, and be seen as equals. Tyrell shares what support looks like, how he puts his passion into perspective, and reminds us not to forget what truly makes you happy.
