2024 Silver Scroll Award winner Anna Coddington wore a sleek all white look, which featured pleated trousers, statement earrings, and a black belt with gold buckles.

Mermaidens' Abe Hollingsworth brightened up the room with a yellow shirt and white mesh top look. But the star of the show were his statement earrings, which featured photos of his fellow band members Gussie Larkin and Lily West.

Maioha Award winner Jordyn With A Why wore a monochromatic all black ensemble, which featured a relaxed blazer, platform boots, and a stylish pair of sunglasses.

SKILAA's Chelsea Prastiti wore a vibrant blue dress which featured tiger prints on it. Her mum found it at an opshop and it even has Tiger in the River written on it - the same name as SKILAA's debut album.

Jazz legend Mike Nock was inducted in the New Zealand Music Hall of Fame at the APRA Silver Scroll Awards on 8 October, 2024. Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Jazz legend and New Zealand Music Hall of Fame inductee Mike Nock wore a dapper brown blazer and a pounamu he found on the West Coast.
