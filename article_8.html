The Barber Shop Sessions: Finale

Watch: In the finale, we celebrate the artists of The Barber Shop Sessions in an uplifting group performance of Dave Para’s 'You’re Magic' inside the barber shop.

Series Classification: G (General Audiences) | Watch the series here

In the finale episode, we celebrate the artists of The Barber Shop Sessions in an uplifting group performance of Dave Para’s "You’re Magic." Supported by Kawiti Waetford, the series music director, our artists come together in a fun and uplifting session inside the barber shop. Through the power of music, we unite, laugh, and share the love with this legendary waiata.
