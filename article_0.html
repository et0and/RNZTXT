Search for person missing from boat in Auckland's Manukau Harbour

Two people were on board, Auckland's Coastguard says. One swam to shore and raised the alarm, while the second is missing.

Coastguard volunteers on three boats from Titirangi and Papakura were called to the harbour shortly after 6pm on Tuesday (file image). Photo: Supplied/ Coastguard

A search is underway for a person missing from a boat in Auckland's Manukau Harbour.

Auckland Coastguard said there were two people on a boat that got into difficulty near Puketutu Island on Tuesday.

One person was able to swim back to shore and alerted police, but the other was still missing.

A RNZ employee said they could see two helicopters circling the area shortly after 7.30pm on Tuesday. Photo: Screenshot / adsbexchange.com
