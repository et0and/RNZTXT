Sir Collin Tukuitonga resigns as chairperson of Te Whatu Ora Pacific Senate

Sir Collin Tukuitonga has resigned as chairperson of Te Whatu Ora Pacific Senate, saying he has no confidence in the new coalition government.

Sir Collin Tukuitonga Photo: RYAN ANDERSON/STUFF

Sir Collin Tukuitonga has resigned as chairperson of Te Whatu Ora National Pacific Health Senate, telling Checkpoint he has no confidence in the new coalition government.

He cited his opposition to the scrapping of the smoking legislation and also a lack of Pasifika representation in new coalition government.
