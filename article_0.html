Call for PM to step in over Peters' comments about Green MP

The Green Party says MP Benjamin Doyle is being sent an "immense" numbers of death threats and Chistopher Luxon needs to intervene.

Winston Peters Photo: RNZ / REECE BAKER

The Greens are demanding the prime minister step in over Winston Peters' statements, saying they have led to "immense" numbers of death threats against Green MP Benjamin Doyle.

Peters in social media posts said questions needed be asked about Doyle's social media accounts and pictures, symbols and language included.

Doyle had posted a series of messages and images on the private account before they were an MP, including an image of their child.

Green Party co-leader Chloe Swarbrick said a term Benjamin Doyle used had been co-opted by rainbow communities for use with irreverence and absurdity.

She said the party had been screening "immense numbers" of death threats after Peters' comments.
