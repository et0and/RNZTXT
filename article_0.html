Live rugby: All Blacks dominate Pumas in first half

Follow all the action from Eden Park, Auckland as the All Blacks attempt a comeback against Argentina.

All Blacks winger Caleb Clarke scores a try. Photo: Brett Phibbs / www.photosport.nz

The All Blacks are seeking redemption this evening at Eden Park after a shock 38-30 loss in their Rugby Championship opener last weekend. The fact of the matter is that the Pumas were smarter, more clinical and the physically more dominant side at Sky Stadium, so how will Scott Robertson's side respond?

Kick-off is at 7:05pm.
