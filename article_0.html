Watch live: Luxon faces questions at post-Cabinet briefing

Follow as the Prime Minister holds his weekly Beehive preference conference ahead of a busy week in Parliament.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon is facing questions after the weekly meeting of the government's top ministers.

Luxon is set to speak at the weekly post-Cabinet press conference in the Beehive from about 4pm on Monday, ahead of a busy week in Parliament.

The coalition is pushing on with its quarter-two action plan announced a week ago.

Not contained in the plan was a range of changes to the Accredited Employer Work Visa (AEWV), announced by Immigration Minister Erica Stanford on Sunday. They aimed to address "unsustainable" levels of migration and better protect incoming workers against exploitation.

Cuts to the public sector are also continuing at pace.
