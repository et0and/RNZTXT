Live: Rowers going for gold at Paris Olympics

We could be in for another magic hour on the water on the eighth day of the Games. Follow our live updates here.

Emma Twigg is the reigning champion in the women's single sculls. Photo: photosport

Rowers are going for gold on Saturday night in hopes of adding to New Zealand's medal haul.

Reigning single sculls champion Emma Twigg is defending the gold she won at Tokyo 2020 at 8.18pm, with Tom Mackintosh up in the men's single scull finals shortly afterward.

Read more: Athletes to watch on the eighth day of the Paris Games

Earlier in the week, New Zealand rowers won three medals - a gold, silver and bronze - in quick succession in a magic hour on the water.

New Zealand shot putters and windsurfers, as well as swimmer Erika Fairweather, are also in with medal hopes overnight.
