Live rugby updates: All Blacks v Springboks

Follow all the action from Ellis Park, Johannesburg.

Scott Barrett is tackled by Springboks Pieter-Steph du Toit and Faf de Klerk Photo: Bob Martin/ActionPress

The All Blacks face their biggest challenge of the season so far when they meet their greatest opponents at ground zero of their monumental rivalry. The world champions Springboks are lying in wait as 62,000 descend on Ellis Park for rugby's biggest rivalry.

Kick-off is at 3am NZT. Grab some coffee and follow RNZ's live coverage!
