Watch live: Aotearoa Music Awards - all the music, glam and winners

Follow all the glitz and the glamour as some of New Zealand's best artists are celebrated at the Aotearoa Music Awards.

The Aotearoa Music Awards is taking place, where New Zealand's biggest names in the music industry are gathering to celebrate their success.

The awards, organised by Recorded Music NZ, have been presented annually since 1965.

They recognise the biggest musical successes of the year, promote emerging artists and highlight the mahi of those who work behind the scenes.

Some of the biggest names in New Zealand music are attending.

Artists Stan Walker, Aaradhna, Marlon Williams, Home Brew, Unknown Mortal Orchestra, L.A.B, and The Beths are some of the finalists who are there.

Broadcasters Kara Rickard and Jesse Mulligan are hosting the awards ceremony.
