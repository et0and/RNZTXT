Homicide inquiry launched after man dies at a Waikanae Beach property

Another man who was known to the victim is helping police with their investigation.

Police tape blocks off a US crime scene. Photo: AFP or licensors

Police have launched a homicide investigation after a man died at a house in Waikanae Beach this evening.

Detective Senior Sergeant Pete Middlemiss said about 4.20pm emergency services were alerted that the man had been seriously injured after "an altercation".

"The man received medical attention at the scene, but sadly died," Middlemiss said.

Another man who was at the house, and knew the victim, was speaking to officers and assisting with their enquiries.

Cordons have been put in place and a scene examination will be conducted over the next few days.

Police did not believe there was any ongoing risk to the public, however, they would be carrying out more inquireis into the circumstances of the death.

Waikanae Beach is around 60km north of the Wellington CBD.
