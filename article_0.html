Man charged in double shooting named as pig hoarder

Coromandel's Stuart Edmondson is known to have kept more than 100 pigs and was the subject of an RNZ documentary. Video

The man charged with murder and attempted murder over a double shooting in Coromandel has pleaded not guilty in the High Court at Hamilton.

Stuart Edmondson appeared before Justice Muir on Tuesday and was supported by about a dozen people in the public gallery.

Kevin Mark Mabbott died about 2pm at Waiau on The 309 Road, and Aaron Buckland was airlifted to Auckland City Hospital with a gunshot wound.

Edmondson was the subject of an RNZ documentary in 2018 Photo: Supplied: Amy Taylor / James Muir

Edmondson is known to have kept more than 100 pigs and was the subject of a RNZ documentary in 2018.

Judge Muir ordered Mr Edmondson to remain in custody until his next appearance in February.
