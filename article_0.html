Watch live: Labour leader Chris Hipkins reveals new opposition line-up

Labour leader Chris Hipkins is announcing the party's new shadow Cabinet following its election defeat.

Labour leader Chris Hipkins is announcing the party's new shadow Cabinet following its crushing election defeat.

The party endorsed Hipkins as leader and voted Carmel Sepuloni the new deputy earlier this month.

Hipkins has confirmed Grant Robertson will stay on as Labour's finance spokesperson and Ayesha Verrall will keep the health portfolio.

He has indicated every MP in his caucus will be assigned a portfolio.
