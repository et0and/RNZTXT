Live: Rowers aiming to add to NZ's Olympic medal haul

It's all go for the women's lightweight double sculls final. Follow our liveblog for the latest updates.

Double sculls rowers Jackie Kiddle and Shannon Cox. Photo: AFP/BERTRAND GUAY

New Zealand athletes are back in action after a magic hour on Thursday saw our rowers bag three medals.

Aotearoa's fastest-ever female 100m sprinter Zoe Hobbs made her Olympic Games debut at the heats on Friday night, coming second to advance to the next round.

Meanwhile, rowers Jackie Kiddle and Shannon Cox are aiming to add to New Zealand's medal haul in the women's lightweight double sculls final.

Later, trampolinist Dylan Schmidt will be aiming for back-to-back medals after bagging bronze at Tokyo 2020.
