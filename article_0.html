Live: Damage from Hurricane Milton becomes apparent as day breaks in Florida

Four people are confirmed dead, roads are underwater and power is out to millions after the hurricane barrelled into the state overnight. Video

Hurricane Milton has moved off Florida's east coast after making landfall on the west on Thursday afternoon (NZ time).

Four people have been confirmed dead and there is widespread devastation from the storm. The full extent of the damage is not yet clear as some areas are not yet accessible due to weather conditions, flooding and debris.

Meanwhile, another storm in the Atlantic, Leslie, has strengthened into a category 2 hurricane. However, it has "likely peaked in strength", the US National Hurricane Centre says.
