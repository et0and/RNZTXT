Olympics live: Fairweather finishes 7th in 200m freestyle

New Zealand's Sevens side are into the semis after a massive win, while swimmer Erika Fairweather has been battling for a medal in the 200m freestyle.

Photo: AFP / SEBASTIEN BOZON

New Zealand have beaten Fiji 38-7 overnight in their final round-robin match while Erika Fairweather will swim in her second final in the pool.

The Black Ferns now face China - who they already beat yesterday - in the quarterfinals at 7am. The other quarterfinals will see Great Britain against the US, France take on Canada and Australia will play Ireland.

Erika Fairweather takes to the pool again this morning after a strong showing in the 200m freestyle semifinal on day two.

Overnight, New Zealand's Erin Routliffe and Lulu Sun finally made it to the centre court of Roland Garros but lost to Itay's Sara Errani and Jasmine Paolini 6-2 6-3.

Meanwhile, New Zealand is still hunting for Olympic medals after Sam Gaze couldn't keep up with the pace in the men's short-course cross country mountain biking race.

The New Zealand equestrian eventing team finished outside medal contention, placing eighth overall as the competition concluded with show jumping at the Chateau de Versailles.
