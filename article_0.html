Police hold 'grave fears' for missing wife of man killed at Ellerslie home

Watch - Detective Inspector Scott Beard is due to hold a media briefing over the Ellerslie homicide investigation.

Watch - Detective Inspector Scott Beard is due to hold a media briefing over the Ellerslie homicide investigation.

The victim, 66-year-old Fuk-Fu Joseph Kwok, was named today.

Police were called to the man's house at Celtic Crescent on Monday evening after his body was found by a relative.

They have been seeking his 67-year-old wife Mei Han Chong.

Detective Inspector Beard, from Auckland City CIB, Beard said the inquiries to date also suggest that the wife may also be the victim of foul play.

They have grave concerns for her safety and welfare. Their son says it's totally out of character for her not to contact them, Beard says.

"We still need the public assistance in trying to locate where Mai is and where that blue Kia motor vehicle is."

Mei Han Chong was last seen at home on the weekend. She is of Asian descent, 5 foot tall (1.5 metres) and 48kg, and has grey hair and wears glasses, he said.
