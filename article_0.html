Watch: Christopher Luxon fronts media in Auckland

The Prime Minister is expected to speak o media after visiting a housing development.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon will be speaking to media after visiting a housing development in Auckland.

Topics likely to be on the agenda include the planned downsizing of the Dunedin Hospital upgrade and the subsequent backlash, and the push to get public service staff back in the office, announced at last week's post-Cabinet press conference.

Other potential topics include building reforms announced at the weekend, who benefits from the government's cuts to smoking reduction efforts and a major bank's approval of a capital gains tax.

Luxon is expected to speak from about 4pm.
