Live: Houses evacuated as fire crews battle three blazes in Canterbury

Sixteen fire trucks, tankers and support vehicles are at the scene along with five helicopters.

Flames from the fire could be seen on State Highway one. Photo: Tasha O Driscoll / Supplied

Firefighters are battling three grass fires near Burnham in Canterbury, closing a section of State Highway 1 as a result.

Fire and Emergency said calls came in about one big fire and two smaller ones, near the intersection on Selwyn Lake Rd with Telegraph Rd, at about 1.30pm on Thursday.

Sixteen fire trucks, tankers and support vehicles are at the scene along with five helicopters.

The public are asked to avoid the area and follow instructions of firefighters if they were already in the area.

NZTA said State Highway 1 was closed between Leeston Dunsandel Rd and Burnham Rd due to the fires.

It was likely SH1 would be "closed for some time", a Fire and Emergency spokesperson said.
