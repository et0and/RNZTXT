Super Rugby Pacific Live updates: Blues v Chiefs

Follow all the action from Eden Park, Auckland.

Naitoa Ah Kuoi of the Chiefs during a line out. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

It's a grand final rematch as the Chiefs head up State Highway One to return to Eden Park, where a very strong and confident Blues team awaits.

There's a stack of All Blacks across both teams, with Beauden Barrett and Damian McKenzie both wearing the 15 jersey for their first game of the season.

Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
