Man charged with murder after child's body found in vehicle

Police say the man arrived at the Manukau Police Station around 1am and the child's body was found soon after.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

A man has been charged with murder after the discovery of a child's body in a vehicle overnight.

Police said the man arrived at the Manukau Police Station about 1am on Monday and shortly afterwards the child's body was discovered.

The 37-year-old, who knew the child and its family, is due to appear in Manukau District Court on Monday, charged with murder.
