Watch live: Police reveal latest in hunt after fatal bus stabbing

People have been laying flowers as police search the Auckland suburb of Onehunga after the attack.

People have been laying flowers as police search the Auckland suburb of Onehunga after a deadly stabbing on a bus.

A woman was left in critical condition and taken to Auckland City Hospital on Wednesday afternoon, but police confirmed she had died a few hours later.

Police have been combing the suburb for the attacker, who they believe they have identified from footage.
