Live: Warriors v Bulldogs

Follow all the action from Accor Stadium, Sydney.

Te Maire Martin of the Warriors in possession. Photo: David Neilson / Photosport

The Warriors find themselves naming an unchanged line-up from last week's victory over the Broncos.

But after languishing near the bottom of the NRL for the better part of a decade, the Bulldogs have finally found a formula that gets them wins on the board. Can the Warriors prevail?
