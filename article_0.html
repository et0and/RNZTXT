Watch live: Luxon faces questions at weekly briefing

The Prime Minister is expected to face questions about the country's new tourism slogan, Brian Tamaki's latest protest and big resignations in the health sector.

Christopher Luxon is set to face questions about health officials, golden visas and the country's new tourism logo as he fronts after a meeting of the government's top ministers.

The prime minister will be hosting his regular post-Cabinet brief about 4pm on Monday. We'll be livestreaming the press conference.

He'll be joined by Conservation Minister Tama Potaka, who is expected to be making an announcement.

Topics of conversation are likely to include the actions of a group associated with Destiny Church who protested a children's event at the Te Atatū library on Saturday, the country's new tourism slogan, the new visas, senior officials quitting the public health service and more.
