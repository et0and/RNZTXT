Watch live: Luxon faces questions in Sydney

The Prime Minister is speaking to media as he lands for this third visit to Australia since taking office.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon is talking to media in Sydney, on the first stop on his third visit to Australia since taking office.

Security and defence will be front and centre when Luxon later meets counterpart Anthony Albanese, amid concerns over any potential AUKUS involvement.

He met NSW Premier Christopher Minns on Thursday, as well as with New Zealand company Beca.

Luxon and ministers Chris Bishop, Shane Jones and Simeon Brown will also be meeting with the chair and chief executive of Infrastructure New South Wales.

Luxon hopes New Zealand can learn from the state government's approach to funding and procurement for large scale infrastructure projects.

Christopher Luxon is due to speak about 2.30pm. You can watch via the stream at the top of this story.
