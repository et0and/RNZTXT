Live updates: Dylan Schmidt chases a medal in trampoline final

The Kiwi trampolinist qualified fourth for the medal round. Follow our live updates.

Dylan Schmidt during competition at the Bercy Arena in Paris. Photo: Iain McGregor / www.photosport.nz

Kiwi trampolinist Dylan Schmidt qualified fourth in the competition being held at the Bercy Arena in Paris.

Schmidt won the bronze medal at the Tokyo Olympics.

New Zealand athletes are back in action after a magic hour on Thursday saw our rowers bag three medals.

Sailors Isaac McHardie and Will McKenzie have claimed a silver medal in the men's 49er skiff, taking New Zealand's medal total to six.

Aotearoa's fastest-ever female 100m sprinter Zoe Hobbs made her Olympic Games debut at the heats on Friday night, coming second to advance to the next round.
