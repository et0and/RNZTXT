All Whites take the lead in FIFA World Cup Qualifier

The All Whites are heavy favourites against New Caledonia, but it's been a scrappy first half at Eden Park.

Chris Wood. Photo: Andrew Cornaga / www.photosport.nz

The All Whites stand on the verge of making only their third ever FIFA World Cup appearance, the only side left standing in their way is New Caledonia. Both sides had comfortable wins in their playoff games on Friday, but it all comes down to this: one match to book their place to the biggest stage of them all next year in the USA, Canada and Mexico. Kick-off is at 7pm NZT.
