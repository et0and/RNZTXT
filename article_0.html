Watch live: Abuse in State Care announcement

Erica Stanford is set to reveal the next steps of the government's reponse to the inquiry into historical abuse.

The minister in charge of the government's reponse to a major inquiry into historical abuse in state care is set to make an announcement.

Erica Stanford is set to speak from the Beehive at 12.30pm on Wednesday. The announcement will be streamed at the top of this page.

It comes after survivors of abuse in care were left feeling deflated two weeks on from the Crown's official apology.

The Royal Commission gave the government until late November to publish responses to each of the 138 recommendations in its report.

That deadline passed, though Stanford had long signalled she believed the four-month deadline was not enough time.
