Live: Can New Zealand achieve the world's biggest haka?

RNZ is at Eden Park where thousands of Kiwis will attempt to reclaim the world record for biggest haka from France.

Dame Hinewehi Mohi said ahead of the world record haka attempt that it “ really warms the heart” to understand the reality of kotahitanga (unity) and how Te Ao Māori can bring the nation together. Photo: RNZ / Layla Bailey-McDowell

Thousands on Sunday evening are expected to show up to Auckland's Eden Park in an attempt to reclaim the Guinness World Record for the largest haka.

The current official record is 4028, inexplicably held by France since 2014.

Gates opened for the event at 4.30pm, and will feature entertainment from Kiwi stars including Six60, Alien Weaponry, Che Fu, Rob Ruha and the Topp Twins.

The haka attempt itself is scheduled for about 8pm and RNZ will be featuring it live with footage from Whakaata Māori.
