Live: All Blacks v England

Follow all the action from Forsyth Barr Stadium, Dunedin as the new All Blacks era gets underway.

Mark Tele'a of New Zealand in the All Blacks' season debut in Dunedin. Photo: Andrew Cornaga / www.photosport.nz

We're finally here.

After all the drama surrounding Scott Robertson being appointed All Black coach and what the team has been through in that time, his first test side will run out on Forsyth Barr Stadium on Saturday night to face the music.

By that, of course, we mean a fired-up England side intent on creating a bit of history with a series win in New Zealand.

Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
