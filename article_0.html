Live updates: All Blacks v Springboks

The ABs are looking to bounce back from last week's defeat. Follow all the action from DHL Stadium in Cape Town.

Tyrel Lomax of the All Blacks. Photo: Halden Krog/ActionPress

The All Blacks are looking to bounce back from a narrow defeat last weekend at Ellis Park, and Scott Robertson has made a few notable changes to his side to try and do so.

Meanwhile, the Springboks are running out a changed-up team as well, so there's a lot of interest in this one. Kick-off is at 3am NZT.
