'Our heart breaks' - Police Minister, Gisborne mayor speak on party deaths

Police Minister Mark Mitchell and Gisborne Mayor Rehette Stoltz are speaking to media after a Saturday night birthday party turned deadly. Follow live coverage.

Police said a fight at a Gisborne party Saturday night that left two dead started after uninvited guests turned up.

Police Minister Mark Mitchell and Gisborne Mayor Rehette Stoltz are set to speak to media at 4pm.

One man has been arrested in relation to the brawl. The victims were stabbed, police said.
