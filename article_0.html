Watch: Christopher Luxon speaks at National Party conference

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon will be talking at the Palmerston North Function Centre on Sunday morning.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon will be talking at the Palmerston North Function Centre on Sunday morning.

Both National and the Greens will on Sunday lay out their respective visions for their parties, the country and the planet in several set-piece speeches.

Shortly after 10am, Luxon will address members at National's regional conference in Palmerston North.

At noon, the Greens' co-leaders will deliver scene-setting speeches, which they call their 'State of the Planet' addresses.

The events follow an address from Labour's leader Chris Hipkins on Saturday, where he laid out his ambitions for New Zealand, painting an image of a country with cheaper childcare and a bigger tax bill for mega-landlords.
