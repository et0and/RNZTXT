Live: Milton causes 'significant damage' as Florida waits to assess situation

The hurricane barrelled into the state overnight and as it moves off, it won't be until daylight that the extent of damage is clear. Video

Hurricane Milton has reached Florida's east coast after making landfall on the west this afternoon (NZ time).

Milton's eyewall has winds screaming at nearly 200km/h with higher gusts. However, it has been downgraded to a category 1 hurricane.

Meanwhile, another storm in the Atlantic, Leslie, has strengthened into a category 2 hurricane and may intensify into a major hurricane, the US National Hurricane Centre says.
