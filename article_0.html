Live updates: 'No stopping' fires, Kiwi in LA says

Tim O'Brien says the wildfire burning near his California home is the largest he's seen - as people he knows lose their homes.

Flames are seen on the hillsides above Hollywood Blvd. during the Sunset Fire on 8 January 2025 in Hollywood, California. Photo: AaronP/Bauer-Griffin / Getty Images

Crews battling multiple wildfires that raged across Los Angeles today were up against a near-perfect storm: intense wind, low humidity and, most troubling for residents, inadequate supplies of water to contain the blazes.

At least five people are dead and thousands have fled.

People living in the heart of historic Hollywood were ordered to evacuate Wednesday as a new fire erupted just a few hundred metres from Hollywood Boulevard.

"Immediate threat to life. This is a lawful order to LEAVE NOW. The area is lawfully closed to public access," the Los Angeles Fire Department posted alongside a map that included sections of the storied movie district.
