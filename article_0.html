Live: Luxon calls Wellington's councils 'pretty lame-o'

It comes after the region failed to put in a bid for an infrastructure deal. Audio

The Prime Minister says "it's pretty lame-o" Wellington councils didn't submit a regional funding deal to be considered by the government.

As part of its plan to boost infrastructure, the government plans to enter into deals with regions to co-ordinate investment.

A deadline was put in place to present a proposal to government by 21 February, but the region didn't put in submission.

"It's pretty lame-o, is my view of it," Luxon said.

"Here we have a government doing everything it can to make sure that we attract capital to this country, which has been a problem for a long period of time, and you can't even get Wellington councils to submit a regional or city deal."
