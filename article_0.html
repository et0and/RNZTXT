Live: Thousands flee deadly Los Angeles fires

Los Angeles is facing a perfect storm as multiple fires are raging across the city, with tens of thousands fleeing their homes. Follow our live updates.

An apartment building burns during the Eaton fire in the Altadena area of Los Angeles county, California on 8 January 2025. Photo: JOSH EDELSON / AFP

Crews battling multiple wildfires that raged across Los Angeles today were up against a near-perfect storm: intense wind, low humidity and, most troubling for residents, inadequate supplies of water to contain the blazes.

At least five people are dead and thousands have fled.

On Wednesday, at least three major blazes burned in LA County communities simultaneously, including a fire in the affluent Pacific Palisades neighbourhood, an area west of downtown LA dotted with multimillion-dollar celebrity homes built along steep canyons.
