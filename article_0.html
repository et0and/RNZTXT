Benbow sentenced to at least 17 years' jail for friend's murder

Christchurch man David Benbow was found guilty of murdering Michael McGrath in 2017.

Photo:

Christchurch man David Benbow has been sentenced to life in jail with no parole for 17 years for the murder of a childhood friend.

Michael McGrath was last seen at his Halswell home in May 2017, and he was due to visit Benbow the next day.

Benbow claimed his friend never turned up, but in October he was found guilty of murdering McGrath with a firearm.

McGrath's mother, Adrienne McGrath, said she could not believe her son would never come home again.

In her victim impact statement, she said still hoped her son would come knocking at her window.
