Four shot in 'neighbourhood dispute'

A man was killed and three wounded in Auckland's Grey Lynn, in what police say was a fight between two groups that started as broken windows and escalated.

A man was killed and three wounded in Auckland's Grey Lynn, in what police say was a fight between two groups that started as broken windows and escalated.

Emergency services were called to Tuarangi Road at 6.49pm on Thursday after reports of gunfire.

A man was found with critical injuries and despite first aid being administered, he died at the scene. Three others were also shot and injured. Armed officers have been guarding the scene on Friday.

At a press conference, police said the shooting had been part of an onoing 'neighbourhood dispute' between two groups that had begun with property damage - including broken windwows - and escalated.
