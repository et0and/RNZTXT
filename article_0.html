Watch live: Auckland Transport stripped of responsibilities

The government is introducing "significant reforms" for the city's transport policies.

The government is introducing "significant reforms" for Auckland's transport policies, Transport Minister Simeon Brown says.

Brown - also minister of Auckland and minister of local government - and Auckland Mayor Wayne Brown are revealing details of the reforms.

You can watch via the stream at the top of this story or follow with our blog below.

In October, the minister and the mayor agreed that the reins on Auckland Transport needed to be tightened. Work was planned on options to reform the organisation.
