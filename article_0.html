Warriors beaten by Sharks in their season opener

The Sharks have beaten the Warriors 16-12 at Go Media Mount Smart Stadium in Auckland. See how the match unfolded here.

Warriors halfback Shaun Johnson takes on Kiwis teammate and Cronulla Sharks forward Briton Nikora. Photo: Photosport

It's time for the Warriors to open their season, they welcome the Sharks to a sold out Go Media Mt Smart Stadium. It's the first time the park has been sold out for a Warriors opening game since the first one ever back in 1995. Here's a look at the teams:

Warriors: 1. Taine Tuaupiki 2. Dallin Watene-Zelezniak 3. Rocco Berry 4. Roger Tuivasa-Sheck 5. Marcelo Montoya 6. Luke Metcalf 7. Shaun Johnson 8. Addin Fonua-Blake 9. Wayde Egan 10. Mitchell Barnett 11. Jackson Ford 12. Kurt Capewell 13. Tohu Harris

Interchange: 14. Freddy Lussick 15. Tom Ale 16. Bunty Afoa 17. Dylan Walker
