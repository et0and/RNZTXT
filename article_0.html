Live: Parliament network breached by China-backed hackers

The confirmation from Judith Collins comes as the US and UK announce Beijing has also targeted their politicians.

GCSB minister Judith Collins. Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

New Zealand stands with the United Kingdom in its condemnation of People's Republic of China (PRC) state-backed malicious cyber activity.

The GCSB's National Cyber Security Centre (NCSC) completed a robust technical assessment following a compromise of the Parliamentary Counsel Office and the Parliamentary Service in 2021, and has attributed this activity to a PRC state-sponsored group known as APT40, GCSB Minister Judith Collins says.
