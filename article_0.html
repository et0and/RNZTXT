61-year-old charged with murder over woman's death at Lake Hāwea

Karen Gaylene White, 60, was found dead at her property nearly a year ago.

The house where Karen Gaylene White was found dead. Photo: Tess Brunton

A man has been charged with murdering a 60-year-old Invercargill woman found dead at Lake Hāwea last year.

Karen Gaylene White, 60, died at her Lake Hāwea property on 8 March 2024.

In November, eight months after her death, police launched a homicide investigation.

Otago Lakes Area Commander Inspector Paula Enoka said following a lengthy investigation, police had charged a 61-year-old man with murder.

He was due in the Queenstown District Court on Wednesday.

"Police continue to support the family and friends of Ms White, and our condolences go out to them," Enoka said.
