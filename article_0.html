Watch: Hipkins fronts media briefing

Labour leader Chris Hipkins has been visiting a homeless youth support organisation in Auckland, as well as the Auckland Central Budgeting service.

Labour leader Chris Hipkins has been visiting a homeless youth support organisation in Auckland, as well as the Auckland Central Budgeting service.

He heard from budget managers following the news that a third of budgeting services nationwide are losing funding.

He then travelled to KickBack Youth Homelessness on K Rd to meet with the community and learn about the work the organisation does.
