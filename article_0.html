Negotiators at 'active incident' at police station

Members of the public are being asked to stay away in the town of Murupara, where police have cordoned off a main street.

Photo: RNZ / Tom Taylor

A police negotiation team is at an an 'active incident' at the police station in Murupara in Whakatāne District.

Police said officers had cordoned off an area and were responding to the "active incident" on Wednesday morning.

They were speaking to one person at the scene.

Murupara Store manager Prabhjot Singh said there were a number of police cars on the street.

Pine Drive is closed between Oregon Drive and Main Road. Photo: Supplied / Google Maps

Pine Drive is closed between Oregon Drive and Main Road, and a number of staff are at the scene.

Members of the public were being asked to stay away.
