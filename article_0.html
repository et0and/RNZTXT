A-League live updates: Wellington Phoenix v Auckland FC

Follow all the action from the first ever NZ football derby at Sky Stadium, Wellington.

Auckland FC and Wellington Phoenix fans Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The most highly anticipated match of the new A-League season takes place in Wellington, where the Phoenix host their new NZ rival. Not only that, but Auckland FC come to town with two wins from two games, so the stage is set for the first of many memorable derbies.

Kick-off is at 5pm NZT.
