Two people killed in Auckland house fire

And a third has been seriously hurt in the blaze in the suburb of Hillpark.

The scene of the fatal house fire in Hillpark in Auckland. Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

Two people have died in a house fire in the Auckland suburb of Hillpark and a third has been seriously injured.

Fire and Emergency said it received reports of a well-involved house fire in Freshney Place shortly before 7am.

A spokesperson said five crews and one command unit were on the scene, and the fire was still burning.

Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

Police and St John are also attending.

Hato Hone St John said one patient was transported to Middlemore Hospital in a serious condition.

Three ambulances and a rapid response vehicle went to the scene.

Police have asked drivers to avoid the area.
