Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Crusaders v Moana Pasifika

Follow the action live from Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch.

Winger Macca Springer in action for the Crusaders. Photo: www.photosport.nz

The equation is simple, the outcome not so much for the Crusaders. They have to get a bonus point win against Moana, which is not a complete guarantee as both sides are coming off good wins last weekend.

They then face a nervous wait till tomorrow, because their fate will be decided in Lautoka in the Drua v Rebels game.
