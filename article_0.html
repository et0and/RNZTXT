NRL live updates: Warriors v Knights

Follow all the action from the must-win game at McDonald Jones Stadium in Newcastle.

Shaun Johnson and the Warriors take the field. Photo: NRL / Photosport

After the last few weeks, the Knights will know that they are in with a serious chance of beating the Warriors in Newcastle.

The game is shaping up to be a must-win, with both clubs looking to walk away with the two points this weekend to keep up with the rest of the pack, or risk getting left behind in the bottom eight.

Kick-off is at 4pm NZT.
