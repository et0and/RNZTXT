Live test rugby updates: All Blacks v Ireland

The All Blacks lead 9-6 at half-time and will start the second half one man down after Jordie Barrett yellow-carded.

New Zealand's Damian McKenzie with Jamison Gibson-Park of Ireland. Photo: ©INPHO/Dan Sheridan

This one sits right up there with the tests against the Springboks in terms of difficulty for Scott Robertson's All Blacks.

Win this and they prove a real point that things are on track, lose and the All Blacks' record this year gets into the status of most losses ever.
