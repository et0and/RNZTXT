Te Whatu Ora boss Margie Apa resigns

Newly minted Health Minister Simeon Brown has announced the Health New Zealand's chief's resignation.

Margie Apa Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Health New Zealand chief executive Margie Apa has resigned,

Dale Bramley will become acting chief while recruitment for Apa's replacement takes place.

Newly minted Health Minister Simeon Brown announced the resignation on Friday.
