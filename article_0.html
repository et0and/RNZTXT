Test rugby live updates: All Blacks dominate

Follow all the action from Nissan Stadium, Yokohama.

Mark Telea during the All Blacks v Japan rugby union test match. Photo: TSUTOMU KISHIMOTO /www.photosport.nz

The All Blacks open their end of year tour with a test against Eddie Jones' Japan in Yokohama, on what should be a beautiful day for running rugby.

Scott Robertson has rung the changes for this one, so all eyes will be on the players hoping to force their way into the top side for the next three tests.

Kick-off is at 6:50pm NZT.
