Live: Blues v Brumbies, Super Rugby Pacific round nine updates

Follow all the action from Eden Park in Auckland.

Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The Blues will take on the Brumbies in what can be described as the feature match of Super Rugby Pacific's ninth round.

The Brumbies are coming in on a good run of form and with enough firepower to cause the Blues some real headaches.

Blues coach Vern Cotter has named a strong side coming off their bye, but Ryan Lonergan and Noah Lolesio are the key men for the visitors. Out wide there should be some fireworks, with Corey Toole going up against Mark Tele'a in a match-up of the competition's two most exciting wingers.

Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
