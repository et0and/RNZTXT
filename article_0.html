Shots heard, Trump's face seen bloodied at rally

Video showed blood on Trump's ear and snipers on roof near the stage where Trump was standing.

Former US President Donald Trump arrives for his hush money trial at Manhattan criminal court on 26 April, 2024 in New York City. Photo: MICHAEL M SANTIAGO / AFP

Sounds of multiple shots were heard at Donald Trump's rally in Pennsylvania on Saturday, with the Republican presidential candidate raising a fist as he was escorted to a vehicle by the US Secret Service, video footage from event shows.

Video showed blood on his ear and snipers on roof near the stage where Trump was standing.
