Live: Nervous wait as first US polls close

The first results will start rolling in soon, after a day filled with hoax bomb threats, voting machine glitches, a forecast storm. Audio

Polls will begin closing in the US election in the next few hours.

It may be some time before the final result is known but initial results will start trickling in from midday.

Earlier there were queues, hoax bomb threats - believed to be from Russian sources - at polling stations, a ballot-scanning glitch in one county, and forecast storm for part of th country.

Opinion polls show the candidates running neck and neck in each of the seven states likely to determine the winner. In those key states polls close by 4pm; Pennsylvania (2pm), Wisconsin (3pm), Michigan (2pm), Georgia (1pm), North Carolina (1.30pm), Arizona (3pm), and Nevada (4pm).
