Ripiro Beach death: Man charged with murder

A 34-year-old man has been charged after Jasmaine Corin Reihana's body was discovered at a Northland beach.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Police have charged a man with murder after a woman's body was discovered in a burnt-out car.

Jasmaine Corin Reihana, 35, was found dead inside the car on 9 September on Ripiro Beach, Dargaville.

Police said they charged a 34-year-old man with murder on Friday.
