Train and car collide in Hamilton, two seriously injured

It is reported there have been deaths after a vehicle collided with a freight train at Peachgrove Road.

The scene near the crossing on Wednesday morning. Photo: RNZ / Libby Kirkby-McLeod

Two people have been seriously injured after a car collided with a freight train in Hamilton.

Police were called to the crash about 4.30am on Wednesday.

KiwiRail said a vehicle collided with one of its freight trains on Peachgrove Road.

It's been reported that there have been deaths.

The train was travelling from Mount Maunganui to Hamilton.

Two people have been taken to Waikato hospital in a serious condition, St John said.

The track is closed between Ruakura and Hamilton.

Earlier Peachgrove Road was closed and motorists were advised to take an alternative route.

The Serious Crash Unit has been notified.
