Power cuts across Northland and Far North

Power is out across almost the entire region after heavy rain.

Photo: Top Energy / Screenshot

Homes and businesses are without power across Northland and the Far North.

Top Energy's website is reporting 2839 reported outages from KaitƒÅia to Cape Reinga on Thursday.

Power and traffic lights are also out in Whangarei, where police have been sent to the city's main intersections to carry out traffic control.

All three of RNZ's transmission sites in Northland are running on back-up generators due to the power outages. The sites are Waipapakauri in the far north, Ohaeawai in the mid-north, and Otaika near Whangarei.

Meanwhile, parts of Northland have been hit by heavy rain overnight, with schools closed and flooding in outlying areas around Kaeo.

While the rain was easing in some places it was lingering in the eastern side of the Far North and surface flooding was expected, Northland Civil Defence said earlier.
