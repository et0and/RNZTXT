Live football: Auckland FC v Central Coast Mariners

Follow live updates as Auckland FC take on the Central Coast Mariners in round 10 of the A-League.

William Gillion of Auckland FC controls the ball during the round 10 A-League Men match between Central Coast Mariners and Auckland FC at Industree Group Stadium, on 28 December 2024, in Gosford, Australia. Photo: Scott Gardiner / Getty Images

Having crashed to the first defeat of their maiden A-League season, Auckland FC will look to come out firing when they take on the Central Coast Mariners in round 10.

AFC were humbled at home last week, suffering a shock 4-0 loss to Western United.

Coach Steve Corica accused his players of complacency, saying the whole team was "off".

Currently top of the table, just one point separates the Black Knights from Adelaide United. The mariners meanwhile sit in 10th, having won just two games this season.
