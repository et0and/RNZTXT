Former National minister Nikki Kaye dies at 44

A close friend of Kaye's has told RNZ the former MP has died after a long battle with cancer.

Nikki Kaye, National Party MP for Auckland Central. Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

Former National Party Minister Nikki Kaye has died at the age of 44.

A close friend of Kaye's, who did not want to be named, has told RNZ the former MP died after a long battle with cancer.

Her former colleague, Maggie Barry, also posted on Facebook a short time ago a picture of the pair with the caption "farewell my friend".
