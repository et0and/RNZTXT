Black Ferns score 158 against South Africa in T20 final

South Africa will require 159 runs to win the final being played in Dubai.

New Zealand's Amelia Kerr plays a shot during the ICC Women's T20 World Cup cricket final match between South Africa and New Zealand at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium. Photo: FADEL SENNA/AFP

South Africa will require 159 to win the Women's T20 World Cup final in Dubai, the highest total ever in a Women's T20 World Cup final.

Amelia Kerr top scored for New Zealand with 43 runs from 38 balls.
