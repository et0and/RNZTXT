Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Crusaders v Force

Follow all the action from Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch.

Mac Grealy of the Force and Sevu Reece of the Crusaders tussle for the ball during the Super Rugby Pacific - Crusaders v Force at Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch. Photo: John Davidson / Photosport

The Crusaders are hoping to continue their winning ways after a good performance last weekend against the Reds. They're not quite the finished project just yet though, and can't let this Force side get their tails up on what should be another entertaining edition of afternoon rugby in Christchurch. Kick-off is at 4:35pm NZT.
