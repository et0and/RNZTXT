A-League live updates: Auckland FC v Brisbane Roar

Follow all the action from Go Media Stadium in Auckland as the new football team hits the field.

Auckland FC Felipe Gallegos takes down Brisbane Roar Keegan Jelacic during the A-League football match between Auckland FC v Brisbane Roar. Photo: Brett Phibbs / www.photosport.nz

Auckland FC kick off their inaugural match in front of a big crowd at Go Media Stadium (Mt Smart) - can they live up to the hype against a Brisbane side that finished out of play-off contention last season?
