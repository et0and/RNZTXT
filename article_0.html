Trees down, power out after tornado wallops Mangawhai

A "widespread" power outage is affecting the area, while one local reports that multiple houses have lost their rooves.

Photo: Marika Khabazi / RNZ

Trees are down and there are widespread power outages after a tornado in Northland's Mangawhai.

Videos posted to social media showed several trees down across roads, with emergency services working to clear them.

Northpower's outage map showed three "widespread" outages covering Mangawhai, Mangawhai Heads and Langs Beach.

The power went out just before 3am Sunday.

"We have a widespread outage affecting all of our network due to a TORNADO damaging property and power lines in the wider Mangawhai Area," Northpower's website said.

"We are currently assessing the situation and will update the information when we can.

"Please treat all lines and equipment as LIVE at all times."

It is not yet known how extensive the damage is, but one local reported that there were multiple houses on one street that had lost their rooves.

Fire and Emergency NZ has been contacted for comment.
