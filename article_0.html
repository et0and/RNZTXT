Live updates: All Blacks v England

Follow all the action from Twickenham as the ABs are met by an English team out for revenge.

All Black Mark Tele'a evades Ellis Genge of England to score the opening try during All Blacks v England test. Photo: Joe Toth/ActionPress

The All Blacks head into the cauldron of Twickenham after a week of controversy and intrigue. They'll be met by 82,000 fans and a fired-up English team out for revenge after the All Blacks won two tightly contested tests back in July.

Kick-off is at 4:10am NZT.
