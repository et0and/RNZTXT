Person dies after jumping off a boat near Moutohora Island

Eforts to resuscitate him were unsuccessful.

Moutohora Island Photo: 123RF

One person has died off the coast of Moutohora Island in Whakatāne on Saturday night.

A man had been recovered from the water unresponsive after jumping off the side of a boat around 9pm.

CPR was commenced on Moutohora Island, however efforts to resuscitate him were unsuccessful.

Police Search and Rescue coordinated the response with Coastguard Whakatāne volunteers attending the scene and a helicopter transporting him to Tauranga Hospital.

The death is not being treated as suspicious and will be referred to the Coroner.
