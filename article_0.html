Watch live: Luxon in hot seat as school lunch woes deepen

The PM faces a grilling at Question Time in a day where he called Wellington's councils "pretty lame-o" and a school lunch provider went into liquidation.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon is set to face a grilling at Question Time in Parliament.

We'll be livestreaming the debate from the House from about 2pm on Tuesday.

Luxon earlier said it was "pretty lame-o" Wellington councils didn't submit a bid for a regional funding deal to be considered by the government.

Meanwhile, school lunch provider Libelle Group Auckland has gone into liquidation.
