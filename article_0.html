Live: Auckland FC v Wellington Phoenix

Follow all the derby day action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Wellington Phoenix's Scott Wootton challenges for the ball. Photo: Shane Wenzlick / www.photosport.nz

It's a massive day for football in Tamaki Makaurau, as Auckland FC host their first ever derby match against the Wellington Phoenix.

The two sides met last month in front of a big crowd at Sky Stadium, with Auckland taking the honours 2-0. Mt Smart is sold out, with over 26,000 expected in for this one, and with both sides at full strength, this should be a real Kiwi Clasico.

Kick-off is at 5pm NZT.
