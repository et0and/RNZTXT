Live: Kayakers chase more Olympic medals after multihull bronze

The women's and men's K4 crews are going back-to-back in their finals after sailors Micah Wilkinson and Erica Dawson took third in a late upset.

Dame Lisa Carrington, Alicia Hoskin, Olivia Brett and Tara Vaughan make up the women's K4 team. Photo: OLIVIER MORIN / AFP

Dame Lisa Carrington and her women's kayak fours crew are chasing more Olympic metal in their final at 11.40pm.

Carrington, Alicia Hoskin, Olivia Brett and Tara Vaughan flew through their heat and are the ones to beat in this final.

If they get a podium place, it will add to the bronze won by sailors Micah Wilkinson and Erica Dawson in the mixed multihull Nacra race in Marseille on Thursday night.

They were trailing the UK team on points, but gained a place after the British sailors were taken out of the race due to a false start, and came in for third.

The men's K4 team race in their final immediately after the women, at 11.50pm.

Meanwhile, speed climber Julian David has been eliminated in the men's speed climbing quarterfinal after taking on the world record-holder, Sam Watson of the US.

Lydia Ko is continuing her campaign at Le Golf National in the second round of the women's individual golf. Earlier, she moved up the leaderboard and tied for third after scoring another birdie.

And Maddi Wesche is also through to the Olympics shot put final after throwing 19.25m on her second attempt.
