Live: Heavy rain warnings remain following state of emergency declaration in Westland

A red heavy rain warning remains in Westland until 8am, but MetService says the worst is over with the region in the "easing phase".

Photo: Tess Brunton / RNZ

Westland, between Hokitika and Haast, is in a local state of emergency as heavy rain hit the region on Friday.

State Highway 6 from Ross to Makarora was closed as a result, with travellers asked to move to the closest township by 6pm.

Westland remains under a red heavy rain alert until 8am on Saturday.

The Grey District is under an orange heavy rain warning until 9am with peak rates of 25 to 30mm expected.
