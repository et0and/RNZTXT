Semi final live updates: Melbourne Victory lead 2-1 against Wellington Phoenix in extra time

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium in Wellington.

Bozhidar Kraev, Finn Surman, Scott Wootton and Alex Rufer of the block a free kick. Photo: Photosport / Scott Barbour

This is, unquestionably, the biggest game in the Phoenix's history, and with that should come a special atmosphere that the players will hopefully relish as much as the fans in attendance surely will. The stage is perfectly set. Big rivals as opponents, a stalemate first leg, a packed out stadium, an against-all-odds story. The final piece of the puzzle is a Phoenix victory. Kick-off is at 6.30pm NZT.
