Rugby Championship live updates: All Blacks v Pumas

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

All Black Damian McKenzie in action against Argentina in the Rugby Championship match in Mendoza. Photo: gaspafotos / PhotoSport

The All Blacks open their Rugby Championship with a test at a venue that hasn't been kind to them lately, having only won one of their last six tests at Sky Stadium.

Tonight should be a tough test against the Pumas of Argentina, who come in trying to replicate their feat two seasons ago when they beat the All Blacks in Christchurch.
