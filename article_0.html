Live updates: King 'so proud' of Kate as she reveals cancer diagnosis

King Charles III says he is "so proud" of Catherine for her courage as she reveals she is undergoing cancer treatment.

King Charles III says he is "so proud" of Catherine for her courage as she reveals she is undergoing cancer treatment, the BBC reports.

Messages from around the world are flooding in as the Princess of Wales put out a video statement, recorded by the BBC, saying she is in the early stages of treatment after cancer was found in tests.

The princess's statement explained that when she had abdominal surgery in January, it was not known that there was any cancer.
