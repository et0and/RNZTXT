Live Super Rugby Pacific: Blues v Crusaders

The Blues lead the defending champion Crusaders 9-6 at halftime. Follow all the action from Eden Park, Auckland.

Sevu Reece of the Crusaders during the round 5 Super Rugby Pacific rugby match between the Blues and the Crusaders at Eden Park. Photo: David Rowland/ Photosport

No wins after four games isn't something we'd ever think we'd say about the Crusaders, but here we are. The Blues will be licking their lips at the prospect of inflicting some revenge on their old enemy at home at Eden Park, in front of what should be a good crowd.

Can the defending champions rise to the occasion?

Kickoff is at 7:05pm NZT.
