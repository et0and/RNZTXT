Live weather: Rivers rise, some told to evacuate

Some Marlborough households are being asked to evacuate, as heavy rain and strong winds move north.

The Waiho River has breaches alert levels. Photo: RNZ/Nathan Mckinnon

Previous storms have weakened the Peninsula Road stop bank, and Marlborough Civil Defence is asking 70 households in Spring Creek to evacuate by 9am and stay away till 5pm.

As the weather moves up the country, there are severe thunderstorm watches for Northland, Auckland, Great Barrier Island, Coromandel Peninsula, Waikato and the Bay of Plenty.
