Live: Luxon faces questions at post-Cabinet conference

The prime minister speaks after Cabinet's meeting, as the government faces backlash over landlord tax changes and the removal of climate change from its transport policy.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon will speak to media shortly at his regular 4pm post-Cabinet press conference.

The coalition government has faced backlash after restoring the ability for landlords to claim tax deductions for interest on residential investment property from 1 April.

While the government and property investors have said this would ease pressure in the rental markets, opposition parties were sceptical the savings would be passed on to tenants.
