Ban on gene technology outside lab to end

Legislation overturning the near 30-year ban will go to the house this year, Science Minister Judith Collins says.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Science, Innovation and Technology Minister Judith Collins visit Plant and Food labs in Mt Albert on Tuesday 13 August 2024. Photo: RNZ / Melanie Earley

The government has confirmed plans to end to the country's near 30-year ban on gene technology outside the lab.

Science Minister Judith Collins announced legislation to end the ban and set up a dedicated regulator will be introduced this year.

She said it was a major milestone in modernising gene technology laws to enable us to improve health outcomes, adapt to climate change and deliver massive economic gains.
