Willis refuses to rule out further public sector cuts

Speaking ahead of the Budget this week, the Finance Minister said with the focus on the "front line", there could be "further changes to the back office".

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Finance Minister Nicola Willis. Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Finance Minister Nicola Willis are speaking about the upcoming Budget.

It follows Monday's Cabinet meeting.

The coalition government is just days away from revealing its first Budget to the country.

It sets out exactly how much money the government has, where it plans to find more, and where it plans to spend it.
