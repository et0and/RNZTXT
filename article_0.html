Live: Residents prepare to flee as out of control Canterbury blaze spreads

The state highway to Arthur's Pass has closed and school camps evacuated as crews battle a fire covering more than 140 hectares.

An out of control scrub fire burning at Bridge Hill in Canterbury has grown to 140 hectares.

Three school camps staying about a kilometre from the fireground have been evacuated as a precaution.

State Highway 73 is closed between Castle Hill and Arthur's Pass village.

Helicopters as ground crews have been fighting the fire in Bridge Hill, near Craigieburn, since early Thursday.

A fire service spokesperson said strong winds were hampering efforts.
