Live: Deluge heads south after dozens of Auckland homes flooded

Preparations being made for flooding lower in the North Island as heavy rain heads southwards.

Flooding in Māngere, south Auckland on 20 May 2024. Photo: Supplied

A severe thunderstorm that caused more than 70 floods in Auckland is heading south and east.

There is now a severe thunderstorm watch in place for Waikato, Bay of Plenty, Rotorua, Taupō and Gisborne until noon on Tuesday.

MetService said rainfall of this intensity could cause surface and/or flash flooding, especially in low-lying areas near streams, rivers or narrow valleys, and may also cause slips.

Hawke's Bay is forecast to receive up to 200mm of rain, mostly south of SH5..

Overnight torrential rain and thunderstorms hit Auckland and saw fire crews called to 71 flooded or weather-damaged homes.

Up to 100mm of rain fell in places between 9pm and 11pm on Monday night, MetService said.
