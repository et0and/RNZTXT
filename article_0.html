Three dead after crash near Aoraki Mount Cook

Three people have now died following a crash near Aoraki Mount Cook, police have confirmed.

Six people were reportedly injured in the crash near Lake Pukaki, which has closed State Highway 8 from the Aoraki turn off to Braemer Road since early this afternoon.

UPDATE 2:15PM SH8 is CLOSED east of the SH8/SH80 intersection near Hayman Rd/Lake Pukaki Viewpoint. Avoid the area. SH8 is highly likely to remain CLOSED for several hours, possibly well into this evening. An alt route via SH8, SH1, SH82, SH83 and back onto SH8 is available. https://t.co/SIf08nBnYq pic.twitter.com/fQVygOhV04
