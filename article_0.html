Live: MP Efeso Collins dies during charity run

The former Auckland mayoral candidate and father-of-two died after collapsing in central Auckland.

A group sings a waiata at the spot where MP Efeso Collins died. Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

Green MP Fa'anānā Efeso Collins has died after taking part in a charity event in central Auckland this morning.

The father-of-two and former Auckland mayoral contender was taking part in a charity run when he collapsed in central Auckland.
