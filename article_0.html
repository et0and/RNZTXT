Watch live: Govt wants to see better competition in banking sector

Christopher Luxon and Nicola Willis are speaking to media in the weekly post-Cabinet press conference.

The government has announced steps it will take to improve the competition in the banking sector during a post-Cabinet press conference.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon said cabinet had agreed to enable Kiwibank to access more capital so it can compete more vigorously.

It comes after the Commerce Commission's report into the banking sector found it was uncompetitive, and New Zealanders were not well-served by it.

Luxon and Nicola Willis are speaking to reporters at 4pm on Monday at the Beehive. We will be livestreaming it at the top of this page.

Meanwhile, a progress update shows more than half of the government's key targets are behind track or at risk of not being met.
