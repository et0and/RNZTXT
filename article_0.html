Live: Tom Mackintosh misses out on rowing medal

Emma Twigg has been pipped for the gold by the Netherlands in the event she won at Tokyo 2020, while the men's event is getting underway. Follow our live updates here.

Netherlands' gold medallist Karolien Florijn (R) hugs New Zealand's silver medallist Emma Twigg after the women's single sculls final rowing competition at Vaires-sur-Marne Nautical Centre in Vaires-sur-Marne during the Paris 2024 Olympic Games. Photo: BERTRAND GUAY / AFP

Emma Twigg has been pipped for the gold by the Netherlands in the event she won at Tokyo 2020.

Twigg was unable to defend her Olympic title, settling for silver as she was pipped in the women's single sculls final by Dutch rower Karolien Florijn.

Reigning single sculls champion Emma Twigg was defending the gold she won at Tokyo 2020 at 8.18pm, with Tom Mackintosh coming fifth in the men's single scull final at 9.30pm.

Earlier in the week, New Zealand rowers won three medals - a gold, silver and bronze - in quick succession in a magic hour on the water.

New Zealand shot putters and windsurfers, as well as swimmer Erika Fairweather, are also in with medal hopes overnight.
