Live: More than 5000 buildings razed in just one LA fire

At least six people are dead as seven fires cross the city cause more than $US50 billion of damage.

One of seven blazes raging in Los Angeles has alone destroyed more than 5000 buildings, while the death toll continues to rise.

The damaged "structures" include houses and businesses, but also smaller buildings like motorhomes and sheds, the BBC says.

More than 180,000 people have so far been ordered to evacuate.

Seven fires are now burning, with damage estimated at more than $US50 billion ($NZ89b) so far.

The official death toll rose to six on Friday (NZT) but is expected to keep growing.
