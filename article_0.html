Live: 'One man with a knife' - Five dead, suspect slain at Sydney shopping centre

'Hundreds of people were running for their lives' at a shopping centre in Sydney after a man with a knife reportedly attacked strangers. Follow RNZ's live updates. Video

Watch live: ABC coverage

Five people are dead after a knife attack by a man at Westfield Bondi Junction shopping centre in Sydney.

The alleged attacker was shot dead by police.

At least eight people are in hospital, including reportedly a baby.

Follow the latest with our live updates:

An eyewitnesses told the ABC shots were fired.

NSW Police said emergency services were called to Westfield Bondi Junction just before 4pm after reports of multiple people being stabbed.

"A critical incident has commenced following the shooting of a male at Bondi Junction," police said in a statement.

It said an operation was underway with shoppers being evacuated.
