Live: Crusaders v Blues

Follow all the action from Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch.

AJ Lam of the Blues is tackled by Johnny McNicholl of the Crusaders during the Super Rugby Pacific matc at the Apollo Projects Stadium on 25 May 2024. Photo: Photosport / John Davidson

Fresh off media controversy this week, Rob Penney's probably got the toughest task of his very tough season. The Blues are coming in so confident, they have rested Caleb Clarke and been upfront about getting Beauden Barrett back into the side, while the Crusaders are still missing Scott Barrett. Ethan Blackadder has been rushed back in at openside, which will be an interesting watch.
