Live: Schools, stores, homes without power, water across Northland

Nearly 100,000 homes and businesses have been affected by outages across nearly the entire region after a pylon fell near Kumeū.

Transpower says a pylon falling over is the cause of the outage. Photo: Supplied / Top Energy

Homes and businesses are without power across almost all of Northland and the Far North.

In a statement Transpower said Northland lost power after a tower fell near Kumeu about 11am on Thursday.

A spokesperson says most of its 65,000 customers are affected, but could not provide specific numbers.

Top Energy was also reporting outages from Kaitāia to Cape Reinga.
