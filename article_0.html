Live weather: Deluge heads north

Auckland is bracing for a downpour and heavy wind as rain that's lashed the South Island heads up the country.

The Hokitika River on Friday morning. Photo: RNZ / Niva Chittock

Auckland is bracing for a downpour and heavy wind as rain that's lashed the South Island heads up the country.

There are severe thunderstorm watches for Northland, Auckland, Great Barrier Island, Coromandel Peninsula, Waikato and the Bay of Plenty.

An evacuation order earlier in place in the Marlborough community of Spring Creek has been lifted.
