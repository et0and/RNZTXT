Live: TVNZ axes Fair Go and Sunday

The big-name programmes and the midday news look set to go off air as the broadcaster cuts jobs.

Photo: RNZ/Calvin Samuel

TVNZ is now announcing to staff which programmes are likely to be axed in a proposed restructuring.

Radio New Zealand has been told by several sources that Sunday programme, the long-running consumer affairs show Fair Go and Midday news are to end.

TVNZ on Friday have been learing which shows and teams would be affected after [broadcaster announced] 68 positions would go.
