Live: Desert Rd closed due to snow

While the SH1 link has closed, the weather has also caused flight cancellations and is keeping emergency services busy in Auckland and Waikato.

The NZ Transport Agency Waka Kotahi says State Highway 1 Desert is now closed Photo: Waka Kotahi NZTA

The Desert Road has been closed this evening due to a snowfall.

The NZ Transport Agency Waka Kotahi says State Highway 1 Desert is now closed and closure points are in place at the Rangipo and Waiouru gates. MetService issued a snowfall warning for the Desert Rd earlier in the day and said around 4pm that its cameras were picking up the start of the snow showers.

Three Air New Zealand regional aircraft were struck by lightning earlier today and are being reviewed by engineering teams to ensure they are safe to fly, the airline says. Twenty-four regional services were cancelled, and the customers are being put on the next available service to get them to their destinations.

Meanwhile, strong winds are keeping Emergency services busy in Auckland and northern Waikato this evening, with both regions facing power outages in some areas.
