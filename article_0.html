Live: Houses evacuated as fire crews battle blaze in Canterbury

Five helicopters, 22 trucks, tankers and support vehicles are working on the out of control blaze.

Flames from the fire could be seen on State Highway one. Photo: Tasha O Driscoll / Supplied

Three homes have been evacuated and a shed has been destroyed by a fire that has closed part of State Highway 1 near Burnham in Canterbury.

Fire and Emergency was initially called to multiple grass fires near the intersection with Telegraph Road at about 1.30pm on Thursday.

Photos on social media showed flames leaping into pine trees and huge plumes of smoke billowing into the air.

Five helicopters, 22 trucks, tankers and support vehicles were working on the blaze, which was not contained or controlled.

The highway was closed between Dunsandel and Burnham and people were urged to avoid the area and follow the instructions of firefighters.
