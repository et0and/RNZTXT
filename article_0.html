Super Rugby final live: Blues v Chiefs

The Blues are holding the line 27-3 against the Chiefs at halftime as the Super Rugby final is on. Follow all the action from a sold-out Eden Park in Auckland.

Wallace Sititi of the Chiefs during the grand final of the Super Rugby Pacific competition at Eden Park. Photo: Andrew Cornaga / Photosport

The scene is set for a memorable final at a sold-out Eden Park - will the Blues and Chiefs rise to the occasion?

That's the big question as the styles of both sides suggests a tough, tight encounter, with the keys to victory most likely dependent on who can win the breakdown battle. Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
