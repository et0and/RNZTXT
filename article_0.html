Live: Unpacking the new Stuff 6pm news

How will it fare? Join Mediawatch's Colin Peacock and Hayden Donnell as they unpack the new bulletin.

Laura Tupou hosts the first edition of Stuff's news programme. Photo: Screenshot

Branded as "a great marriage" of the Three brand and Stuff's reporting, veteran broadcaster Samantha Hayes takes on the lone task of presenting the new-look 6pm news bulletin on weekdays.

Questions have been swirling about how the production - which will run for one hour on weekdays and half-hour on weekends - will look, work and whether it will be an adequate replacement for Newshub's bulletin.

Former Newshub head of news Mark Jennings felt it might look inferior to traditional TV news broadcasts.

But, while announcing the joint production, Stuff chief executive Sinead Boucher had promised there would be proper investment and it wouldn't be "on the cheap" stuff produced on iPhones.

"We are producing something that will be both familiar and fresh, a great marriage of the Three brand and Stuff's live and lively news ethos," Stuff Digital managing director Nadia Tolich said.
