Live updates: Blues roll Drua 36-5

The Blues romped to a win in their Super Rugby Pacific quarter-final against the Fijian Drua at Auckland's Eden Park.

Blues Caleb Clarke scores a try. Photo: Photosport / Brett Phibbs

The Blues were odds-on favourites to roll the Drua in Auckland, mainly because they had not lost at Eden Park and the Fijians had not won away from home this season.

This was the Drua's first time ever playing at Eden Park, making it the most unlikely of settings for an upset.
