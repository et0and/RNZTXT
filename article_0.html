Watch: Christopher Luxon holds post-Cabinet press conference

The Prime Minister is speaking after his government announced it was bringing forward its rollout of a new structured maths curriculum.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and minister of education Erica Stanford are speaking to media at this week's post-cabinet briefing.

The National Party held a two-day conference over the weekend, during which they announced plans to bring forward a new maths curriculum.

Labour has raised concerns about the plans.
