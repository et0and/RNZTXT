Live Super Pacific Rugby: Chiefs vs Moana Pasifika

Follow all the action as the Chiefs battle Moana Pasifika from FMG Stadium Waikato, Hamilton.

Etene Nanai-Seturo of the Chiefs during the Super Rugby Pacific Round 2 match between Moana Pasifika and the Chiefs. Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

After a promising start to the year, Moana have conceded over 100 points in losses to the Brumbies and Blues, which they need to address promptly or risk this season falling off a cliff already.

The Chiefs are coming off a loss to the Crusaders and have brought Damian McKenzie back in at first five. He'll steer round an otherwise rotated backline.
