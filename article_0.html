NRL live updates: Warriors v Dolphins

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Adam Pompey of the Warriors makes a run during the NRL Round 27 match between the Redcliffe Dolphins and the New Zealand Warriors. Photo: Jono Searle / www.photosport.nz

The Warriors will head into this Sunday's match against The Dolphins at Mt Smart with a new-found confidence, after notching up such a big win last weekend. The Dolphins field a very similar-styled team to the Warriors, relying heavily on team work rather than individual brilliance. Kick-off is at 6:05pm NZT.
