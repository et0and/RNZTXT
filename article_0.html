Super Rugby Pacific live: Highlanders v Moana Pasifika

The Highlanders and Moana Pasifika open their seasons - keep up with RNZ's live updates.

Moana Pasifika midfield back Danny Toala in the Moana Pasiﬁka versus Highlanders Super Rugby Paciﬁc game at Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland on 31 March, 2023. Photo: Photosport / Brett Phibbs

The most intriguing game of the weekend? The Highlanders and Moana Pasifika open their seasons under the roof at Forsyth Barr Stadium in Dunedin, kicking off at 7:05pm.

Moana have a lot of new players and an entirely new staff, while the Highlanders are entering their post-Aaron Smith era. This one may well turn into a shootout as both sides will want to run the ball early and often.
