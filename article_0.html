Thousands protest over health system in Dunedin and Westport

An estimated 35,000 people are protesting against possible cuts to Dunedin Hospital, police say.

Thousands protest in Dunedin over changes to the hospital construction. Photo: RNZ / Tess Brunton

An estimated 35,000 people are protesting against possible cuts to Dunedin Hospital, police say.

Ministers announced on Thursday the planned redevelopment would either need to be reduced, or done in stages after budget blowouts.

Meanwhile, about 1000 people have taken to the streets of Westport in a silent protest over the state of the health system.
