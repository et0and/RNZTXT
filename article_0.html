Watch live: 'Inconsistencies' in details police getting about baby's death

Police say there are inconsistencies between statements they are getting and injuries the baby had.

Police say there are inconsistencies between statements they are getting and injuries a baby who died in Te Kūiti had.

The 10-month-old boy was brought unconscious to Te Kūiti Hospital on Saturday afternoon.

"Tragically, despite the best efforts of medical personnel, he was unable to be resuscitated," detective inspector Graham Pitkethley said. Police have launched a homicide inquiry.

Police at the house where the baby lived. Photo: RNZ/Natalie Akoorie

The boy's father has denied any wrongdoing.
