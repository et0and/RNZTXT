Live updates: Gallipoli Anzac Day Dawn Service

Live above: Coverage from the ABC

Anzac Day has been marked around New Zealand today, with the Dawn Service in Gallipoli this afternoon to feature Deputy Prime Minister Winston Peters.

The service at Gallipoli, Turkey is to begin at 2.30pm New Zealand time and Peters is among those set to speak.

Watch the ABC's live coverage above.

Here in New Zealand, the Ministry of Culture and Heritage pulled the plug on two services for Anzac Day in Wellington over safety concerns for veterans and visitors due to strong winds over safety concerns. Many other commemorations went off without issues.
