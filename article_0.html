Former All Black Norm Hewitt dies at the age of 55

Hewitt played nine tests between 1995 and 1998 and was a stalwart of Hawke's Bay, Hurricanes and Māori All Blacks rugby.

All Blacks hooker Norm Hewitt walks from the field after the All Blacks v England match on 6 December 1997. Photo: Photosport / Andrew Cornaga

Former All Blacks hooker Norm Hewitt has died at the age of 55, Hawke's Bay Rugby has confirmed.

Hewitt played nine tests between 1995 and 1998 and was a stalwart of Hawke's Bay, Hurricanes and Māori All Blacks rugby.
