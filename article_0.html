Live: Tyre blowout sent truck through barrier in triple fatal crash

Police have called some bystanders' filming of the aftermath "disgusting", and have explained how the truck collided with a van on the wrong side of SH1.

A truck blew a tyre, crashed through a barrier and hit a van carrying six people - along with two other cars - in collision that killed three people, police say.

The victims of the crash on State Highway 1 in Auckland include two people believed to be seasonal workers, while three more are in hospital, one with critical injuries.

Counties Manukau Road Policing Manager, Inspector Tony Wakelin on Tuesday explained how the crash occured on Monday, adding the victims were 45, 37 and 32-years-old.

Wakelin applauded members of the public who jumped in to help, but he was highly critical of people who had filmed the aftermath of the crash.

"I thought that was disgusting. It really was. I saw some of the footage. It was filmed before emergency services arrived," he said.

"I say to the people who were doing that: 'How would you feel if that was your family?"
