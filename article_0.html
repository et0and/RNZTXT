Live: Slip closes highway, warnings as 100km/h winds head north

Highways have been closed around the South Island as Aucklanders are warned to prepare for possible power cuts amid strong wind moving up the country.

Cold southerlies are sweeping up the country bringing snow warnings and road closures parts of the South Island.

MetService has heavy snow watches for Taihape, Fiordland, Southland, southern Otago and Stewart Island

Milford Road and Lindis Pass closed, and there are warnings for the other alpine passes and the Crown Range Road.
