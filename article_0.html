Live: All Blacks take on Fiji

The All Blacks are dominating Fiji 26-5 in a rare match in North America. Follow all the action from Snapdragon Stadium in San Diego with RNZ's liveblog.

Rieko Ioane scores a try. Photo: Andrew Cornaga / www.photosport.nz

The All Blacks have managed to get past a tough English side in their first two tests of the year, but now find themselves in San Diego for a match against a talented Fiji side.

Scott Robertson has rung the changes and has six debutants in his side, kick-off is at 2:30pm NZT.
