Live: Aratere refloat delayed, passengers disembark ferry

The ferry will not be refloated until later on Saturday but passengers and some crew are now on their way to Picton. Follow our live updates here.

The Aratere ran aground about 3km from Picton about 9.45pm on Friday. Photo: Samantha Gee

The Interislander ferry Aratere is grounded in a bay near Picton.

The ferry had been on a freight-only sailing when it suffered a steering failure and crashed about 9.45pm on Friday. No one was injured and there was no environmental damage, KiwiRail said.

The plan to refloat the vessel has been pushed out but authorities are helping crew and passengers off the ferry.
