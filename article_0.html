Watch live: Ministers reveal plans after call for banking overhaul

The Commerce Commission has called for Kiwibank and open banking to be boosted in a long-awaited report on the sector.

Government ministers are set to reveal how they'll respond to a major report calling for changes in the banking sector.

The Commerce Commission report on personal banking found there was little competition in the sector, with the dominant big four Australian-owned banks making high profits, while Reserve Bank's rules make it difficult for smaller banks and new entrants to challenge.

Kiwibank needed to be strengthened and open banking must be brought in to bring more competition to the bank sector, the report found.

Finance Minister Nicola Willis and Consumer Affairs Minister Andrew Bayly are set to speak from the beehive from about 11.30am.
