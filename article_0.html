Young person swept away in Fiordland river

Police, search teams and a helicopter has are mounting a rescue at the Hollyford River.

The Hollyford River in Fiordland. Photo: 123rf.com

Search and rescue are looking for a young person who has been swept down a river in Fiordland.

Emergency services were called at about 11.40am after it was alerted to an individual being swept down the Hollyford River, near Lower Hollyford Road.

A police spokesperson said police and Search and Rescue are in pursuit and that a helicopter has been deployed.
