Canada's PM Justin Trudeau to step down

The embattled prime minister will leave office once a new leader is chosen.

Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau. Photo: Bryan R. Smith / AFP

Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau said on Monday that he intended to step down but he would stay on in his post until the ruling Liberal Party has chosen a replacement for him.

Trudeau made his remarks in a live address a short time ago.

-Reuters
