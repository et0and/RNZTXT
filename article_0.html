Live: Ferry to be refloated after night spent grounded near Picton

Authorities will try to move the grounded Aratere after 9am. Follow our live updates here.

The Aratere ran aground about 3km from Picton late on Friday night. Photo: Supplied/Skyworks

The Interislander ferry Aratere is to be refloated after a night spent grounded in a bay near Picton.

The ferry had been on a freight-only sailing when it suffered a steering failure and crashed about 10pm on Friday. No one was injured and there was no environmental damage, KiwiRail said.

The plan is to refloat the vessel on the high tide after 9am on Saturday.
