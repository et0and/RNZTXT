Live: Warriors vs Rabbitohs in Sydney

Follow all the action as the Warriors attempt to make it three wins in a row at Allianz Stadium in Sydney starting at 5pm.

Shaun Johnson of the Warriors, Photo: JONO SEARLE

Following a gutsy win over the Newcastle Knights at home, the Warriors hold their heads high as they travel to Sydney for the first time this season.

Andrew Webster and his side are trying to make it three wins on the trot coming up against the South Sydney Rabbitohs, in what will be a real test of character to see if the Warriors can rediscover their away form from last season.
