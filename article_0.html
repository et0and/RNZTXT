Dunedin stabbing victim named as Enere McLaren-Taana

Friends and family are paying tribute to Enere McLaren-Taana, who died following a stabbing in the central city.

Photo: Stuff/Hamish McNeilly

Friends and family are paying tribute to Enere McLaren-Taana, who died following a stabbing in central Dunedin yesterday.

Emergency services were called to the Dunedin Bus Hub in Great King Street shortly after 3pm on Thursday.

The 16-year-old went to Trinity Catholic College and previously attended King's High School.

Both schools, as well as Otago Rugby League, have paid tribute to him.
