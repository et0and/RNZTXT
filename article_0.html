Former All Black Sid Going dies at age 80

Sid Going played 86 matches for the All Blacks over a decade-long span in the 1960s and '70s, including 29 tests.

Former All Black Sid Going. Photo: RNZ / Lois Williams

Former All Black Sid Going has died at the age of 80.

A statement from Northland Rugby confirmed the death of a player regarded as one of the country's great halfbacks.
