Watch live: PM Christopher Luxon gives post-Cabinet update

Watch RNZ's livestream of the press conference and follow our blog for all the latest updates.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon will speak shortly at his weekly post-Cabinet press conference.

The coalition government scrapped Labour's controversial Three Waters programme when it came into power and has now struck a deal with Auckland Council that will see water rates rise by 7.2 percent rather than the 25.6 percent forecast.

Watercare will be able to borrow more money to invest in infrastructure under the scheme with the cost of borrowing spread over a longer period.

Local mayors are now considering how a similar deal might work in smaller districts.
