ICC Champions Trophy final live updates: Black Caps v India

Follow all the action from Dubai International Stadium, where the Black Caps are one win away from ultimate glory in the ICC Champions Trophy.

Will Young plays a shot during the ICC Champions Trophy one-day international final cricket match between India and New Zealand at the Dubai International Stadium. Photo: RYAN LIM

The Black Caps are one win away from ultimate glory in the ICC Champions Trophy, which they last lifted back in 2000.

They have made it to the final despite a lot of travel back and forth between Pakistan and the UAE, while India's suspicious draw has seen them play all their games in Dubai.

Needless to say, the Black Caps will have a lot of support from the rest of the cricketing world in this one.

Play starts at 10pm NZT.
