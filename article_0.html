Live: 'You are the sucker,' Biden tells Trump as tempers flare in debate

The topics covered have included the economy, the Covid pandemic, abortion rights and immigration. Video

Joe Biden and Donald Trump have met in a tightly controlled television debate in their fight to be the next US president.

Neither acknowledged the other as they arrived on stage.

The debate has been without the interuptions and interjections of the last campaign, when ugly shouting matches broke out.

Rules are in place this time to prevent that, and both men know their microphones are off when it's not their turn to speak.

The topics covered have included the economy, the Covid pandemic, abortion rights and immigration.

The debate runs from 1pm to 2.30pm NZT on Friday.
