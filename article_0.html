Live: Auckland Harbour Bridge fully re-opens after hīkoi moves across it

The Transport Agency says all lanes in both directions have re-opened, and off-ramps and on-ramps that have been closed will reopen shortly.

All lanes on the Auckland Harbour Bridge have re-opened as the hīkoi mō te Tiriti has finished its cross.

The Transport Agency says all lanes in both directions have re-opened, and off-ramps and on-ramps that have been closed will reopen shortly.

The agency says motorists should allow extra time for travel as delays to traffic travelling north slowly ease.

Thousands of supporters of the hīkoi mō te Tiriti - a march traversing the length of Aotearoa in protest against the Treaty Principles Bill and government policies impacting Māori - have crossed the Auckland Harbour Bridge.

RNZ reporters with the march said it was swaying and rocking as the protesters descended on the Westhaven side of the bridge.
