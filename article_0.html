Super Rugby Pacific recap: Blues beat Fijian Drua after first-half blitz

The Blues opened their campaign at Semenoff Stadium in Whangarei at 4pm, welcoming the Fijian Drua. Relive the action with RNZ's live updates.

Sam Darry of the Blues scores a try as Simione Kuruvoli of Fijian Drua tackles. Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

The Blues opened their campaign at Semenoff Stadium in Whangarei on Saturday, beating the Fijian Drua.

The Blues named a powerful lineup that includes eight current or former All Blacks.
