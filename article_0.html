Ponsonby Road cordon after reports person killed

Armed police are guarding a section of the major road.

Cordons at a section of Ponsonby Road in central Auckland on 6 May, 2024, after an incident. Photo: RNZ / Maia Ingoe

Armed police have cordoned off a section of Auckland's Ponsonby Road amid reports a person has been killed.

An official police callouts database shows two 111 calls were received about 10.20pm on Sunday, one reporting firearms offending and the other reporting a homicide.

Police at the scene of a cordon after a reported death on Ponsonby Rd. Photo: Finn Blackwell

Police have declined to confirm the death.

The calls came from near the intersection of Douglas Street and Ponsonby Road, an area with shops and restaurants.

Police detectives on Ponsonby Road after reports of a death there. Photo: Finn Blackwell

Auckland Transport said a section of Ponsonby Road, between Richmond Road and Franklin Road, was closed due to an incident.

"Avoid this area or expect delays with diversions in place affecting all traffic, including bus services."

An RNZ reporter at the scene on Monday morning said two armed police could be seen inside the cordon.
