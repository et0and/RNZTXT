Super Rugby Pacific live: Blues race out to massive lead against Fijian Drua

The Blues open their campaign at Semenoff Stadium in Whangarei at 4pm, welcoming the Fijian Drua. Keep up with RNZ's live updates.

Sam Darry of the Blues scores a try as Simione Kuruvoli of Fijian Drua tackles. Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

The Blues open their campaign at Semenoff Stadium in Whangarei at 4.35pm, welcoming the Fijian Drua.

One thing is for sure about this game: there are going to be a lot of very tired bodies at the end of it, because the forecast is for just under 30C in the winterless north.

The Blues have named a powerful lineup that includes eight current or former All Blacks.
