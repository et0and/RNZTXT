Watch live: Longest nomination acceptance speech in presidential history

Donald Trump has broken his own record for the longest acceptance speech by a presidential nominee in history, reports say. Video

Former US president and 2024 Republican president hopeful Donald Trump has given his first speech since an assassination attempt last weekend.

He first reappeared, with a bandage on his ear, on Thursday when he was attending the Republican Convention with his family.

Trump is now officially the party's nominee for November's vote.

Melania Trump made a rare appearance to watch her husband's speech. Earlier, wrestling legend Hulk Hogan earlier ripped off his shirt and told Americans to embrace "Trumpamania".

While Republicans celebrate, US President Joe Biden is facing new questions over his candidacy for re-election, with top Democrats reportedly expressing concerns about his ability to win.
