Live: Black Caps secure historic test series win

NZ have claimed victory in Pune by 113 runs, clinching their first-ever test series win on Indian soil.

Glenn Phillips of New Zealand celebrates the wicket of Yashasvi Jaiswal of India. Photo: Arjun Singh / Sportzpics for BCCI

The Black Caps have claimed an historic series win in India.

They headed in with a lead of 301 on day three, and establishing a position to confidently take 10 Indian wickets was what the day was all about.

It was the first time New Zealand had won a test series on Indian soil.
