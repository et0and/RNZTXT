Watch: Police give media briefing about Ellerslie homicide investigation

Watch - Detective Inspector Scott Beard is due to give a media briefing about the homicide investigation into the death of Fuk-Fu Joseph Kwok and the disappearance of his wife.

Watch - Police are giving a further media briefing about the homicide investigation in Ellerslie after a man was found dead in his home and his wife is also a suspected victim of foul play.

The body of Fuk-Fu Joseph Kwok, 66, was found in his Celtic Crescent house on Monday, while police have been seeking his 67-year-old wife Mei Han Chong.

At a briefing on Thursday Detective Inspector Scott Beard, from Auckland CIB, told the media that police held grave fears for Chong and suspected that she was also a victim of foul play.

Chong who is described as 1.5m tall, 48kg with grey hair and glasses has been missing since her husband's death and the couple's son said it was totally out of character for her not to contact them.

On Friday morning police cancelled all their pre-booked media interviews on the Ellerslie case and subsequently scheduled a media briefing.
