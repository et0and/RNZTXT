Live: Wairoa urged to not panic buy despite state of emergency declaration

State of emergencies have been declared in the Heretaunga Ward in Hastings and Wairoa, due to the potential risk to lives and properties.

Wairoa's mayor says he spoke to the local supermarket operator and has been assured supplies will be able to get into the region.

A slip on State Highway 38 earlier closed the road but Craig Little says it has now reopened.

He says the owner of the local supermarket has told him he is already thinking about how to get stock in if the road does close.
