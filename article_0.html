Live: Tributes and criticism for outgoing Robertson

While Jacinda Ardern paid tribute to her "selfless" former colleague after an announcement he was leaving politics, Winston Peters' farewell came with heavy criticism.

Labour's Grant Robertson has announced his retirement from politics.

The former Deputy PM and Finance Minister was widely expected to retire this term after Labour's election loss, with speculation fuelled by his decision to opt out of the Wellington Central race last year.
