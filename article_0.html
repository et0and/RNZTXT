Watch live: Minister makes transport announcement in Auckland

Transport Minister Simeon Brown and acting Mayor Desley Simpson are speaking from Mount Eden Railway Station.

Transport Minister Simeon Brown and acting Auckland Mayor Desley Simpson are making an announcement at Mount Eden Railway Station.

They are expected to speak about 11am.

Public transport in Auckland was allocated more police last month after a fatal stabbing on a bus in Onehunga, and the minister said he would look into making attacks on public transport an aggravating factor at sentencing.

Works preparing for the City Rail Link - due to open in early 2026 - also disrupted train travel in Auckland over Labour weekend, with buses replacing all services.

The same was true in Wellington amid a major project aimed at providing faster and more frequent commuter train services into the city.
