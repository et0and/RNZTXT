Watch: Police confident remains are those of Yanfei Bao

A shallow grave was found on a farm today. Bao's family are "shocked and upset" by the discovery, police say.

Police have found remains in their search for missing Christchurch woman Yanfei Bao.

They were found in a shallow grave along a treeline on private farmland in the Greenpark area of Canterbury.
