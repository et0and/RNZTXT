Watch live: KiwiRail head fronts over next steps for ferry

KiwiRail's chief executive is giving an update on the stricken Aratere. Follow our live updates here.

KiwiRail's chief executive Peter Reidy is to front media over the next steps for the stricken Aratere.

The Interislander ferry ran aground just north of Picton while on a freight-only sailing on Friday night.

No-one was injured, but the ferry was not able to be refloated until nearly 24 hours later.

It would be anchored in Picton Harbour today and be placed under a Maritime NZ detention order, barring it from further movement, while the reason for the grounding is investigated.
