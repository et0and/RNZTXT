Live: 'Shocked and numb' - Hunt continues after deadly bus stabbing

Police have named the suspect in a fatal stabbing on a bus, as the city's mayor calls it a "senseless" attack.

Police have named the suspect in a fatal stabbing on an Auckland bus as Kael Leona.

Police say Leona is known to them, but hasn't been involved in violent crime before. The have described the bus attack as "random" and do not believe there was an altercation beforehand.

Leona's family is assisting police.
