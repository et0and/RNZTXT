Live updates: Man killed at Trump rally dived to protect family

Volunteer fire chief Corey Comperatore, 50, was killed at the Trump rally after he "dived on his family" to protect them, the governor says.

Republican presidential candidate former President Donald Trump is rushed offstage during a rally on July 13, 2024 in Butler, Pennsylvania. Photo: Anna Moneymaker/Getty Images/AFP

Pennsylvania Governor Josh Shapiro says he has been speaking to the families of two people who were shot and injured at a rally for former US president Donald Trump, and are still being treated.

He then went on to pay tribute to volunteer fire chief Corey Comperatore, 50, who he said was killed at the rally on Saturday (Sunday NZT time) after he "dived on his family" to protect them.

Shapiro said he was releasing the information after speaking to Comperatore's wife.
