Coromandel Peninsula highway closed after crash

A major road on the Coromandel Peninsula has been closed after a serious crash, and travellers are being told to take other routes.

The crash was between Te Puru and Valder Place, Waiomu, on the west side of the Coromandel Peninsula, police said. Photo: Supplied/ NZTA

A major road on the Coromandel Peninsula has been closed after a serious crash, and travellers are being told to take other routes.

Emergency services are responding to a crash on State Highway 25 between Thames and Coromandel town, reported just before 9am on Friday.

St John said it treated four patients with minor injuries at the scene.

"Motorists travelling between Thames and Coromandel are asked to delay travel or take an alternative route where possible, as the road is expected to be closed for some time," a police spokesperson said.

The road is heavily used during the peak summer holiday period.
