Live: TVNZ staff brace for bad news in announcement

Reports suggest staff cuts are expected at the state-owned broadcaster.

TVNZ staff have been told to watch their inboxes on Thursday for an impending announcement about its news operation.

The state-owned broadcaster's warning comes just one week after Newshub staff were informed of a proposal that would see its newsroom shut on 30 June.

TVNZ's interim financial results last week showed its total revenue has fallen 13.5 percent from last year to $155.9 million .
