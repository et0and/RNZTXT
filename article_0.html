Watch live: PM gives post-Cabinet update

The prime minister will provide updates and answer questions from the media in his weekly post-Cabinet conference.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon is providing a post-Cabinet update to media.

On Sunday, the government released further details on its plan to set up military-style academies and a new category, Young Serious Offender, which could land someone as young as 14 in one.
