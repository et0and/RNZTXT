Watch live: Nicola Willis talks tax at post-Cabinet briefing

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Finance Minister Nicola Willis are set to face questions.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Finance Minister Nicola Willis are set to face questions at the government's weekly briefing.

Willis is expected to talk about the government's tax cuts coming into effect this week at the post-Cabinet press conference at the Beehive on Monday.

The briefing is due to begin about 4pm and will be livestreamed at the top of this page.

The government's new boot camp pilot in Palmerston North also started on Monday.
