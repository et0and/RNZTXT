Live: 'His impact was just beginning' - MP Efeso Collins dies during charity run

Leaders across the political specturm are in shock as they remembered Collins as a passionate advocate, exceptional orator and devoted father.

Flowers laid at the scene of Collins' death. Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

Green MP Fa'anānā Efeso Collins has died after taking part in a charity event in central Auckland this morning.

The father-of-two and former Auckland mayoral contender was taking part in a charity run when he collapsed in central Auckland.
