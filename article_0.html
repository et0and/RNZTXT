Live: Stuff, Newshub call meetings amid reports of deal

Staff at both companies have have been called to meetings after talk of a third-party rescue for Newshub.

Photo: Getty Images / Phil Walter

Newshub staff have been called into a meeting on Tuesday for an "important business update".

Meanwhile, staff at media company Stuff have also been called to a meeting on Tuesday at the same time.

It comes after owner Warner Brothers Discovery confirmed Newhub's operations were to be shut down in July.

At the time Warner Brothers Discovery did not rule out the possibility of a deal with a third party.
