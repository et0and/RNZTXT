Olympics live: Dame Lisa Carrington claims gold in kayak showdown

The GOAT in the boat has taken her eighth Olympic gold, while Lydia Ko is now first outright on the last day of play. Follow RNZ's live updates.

Dame Lisa Carrington celebrates her gold medal on the winners' podium. Photo: AFP/BERTRAND GUAY

Dame Lisa Carrington has triumphed in the great Paris Olympics showdown on the water.

Dame Lisa won the K1 500m at the Tokyo Olympics, however, Aimee Fisher had beaten her at the distance twice this year, including at the last regatta before the Olympics, in Poland in late May.

But Fisher was not strong enough in the final, and Dame Lisa swept to victory to claim her eighth Olympic gold medal, with Fisher coming in fourth.
