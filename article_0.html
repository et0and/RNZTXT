Two bodies found at rural Auckland property

The deaths are being described as unexplained, with reports suggesting a rogue ram may have killed the pair.

Police cars at the property on Anzac Valley Rd in rural Waitākere. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Two people have been found dead at a rural property in Waitākere, west Auckland.

Police were called to a property on Anzac Valley Rd about 7.30am on Thursday.

Reports by NZ Herald and Stuff suggest police are probing whether the pair were killed by a rogue ram on the property.

Police say the circumstances of the deaths are unexplained, Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police said the circumstances of the sudden deaths were unexplained

Officers were talking to the families of the victims.

A scene examination is under way.
