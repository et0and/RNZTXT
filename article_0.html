Emergency services converge on Carterton

New World Carterton said the store was closed until further notice.

The Carterton New World. Photo: Google Maps / Screenshot

Police are releasing little information as emergency services converge on the Wairarapa town of Carterton.

One resident has told RNZ the main street is cordoned off while others have said ambulances and police are at the local New World.

In a Facebook post, New World Carterton said the store was closed until further notice.

"Our apologies for any inconvenience caused. We will keep you updated as to when we can reopen."

Police are expected to release a statement shortly as emergency services converge on the Wairarapa town of Carterton.

Local businesses have reported seeing police vehicles and ambulances in the area.

State Highway 2 is closed near Kaitoke Loop Road north of Upper Hutt due to what is described as a "police incident," but it is unclear if it is connected.
