Live: Women's kayak sprint duo breeze through to finals

New Zealand pair of Lisa Carrington and Alicia Hoskin have qualified for the final of the women's kayak sprint doubles. Follow RNZ's live updates.

Dame Lisa Carrington and Alicia Hoskin blitzed through their women's K2 heat earlier this week. Photo: OLIVIER MORIN / AFP

Fresh from claiming gold in the women's kayak fours on Thursday, Dame Lisa Carrington and Alicia Hoskin are back in the water on Friday night for the women's 500m kayak doubles.

New Zealand pair of Lisa Carrington and Alicia Hoskin have qualified for the final of the women's kayak sprint doubles at the Paris Olympics.

They won their semi-final, crossing the line ahead of Germany and the Netherlands and now will race in the final at 11.10pm for New Zealand's first chance of a medal on Friday night.

Meanwhile, Lydia Ko returns to Le Golf National for the third day of the women's individual golf tournament, after surging up the leaderboard and into medal contention on Thursday.

After 4am, cyclists Bryony Botha and Emily Shearman are back in action in the velodrome in the women's madison final.

They have already won silver in Paris for the women's team pursuit and will be looking to add to their haul.
