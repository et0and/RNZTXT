Live updates: Heavyweights Parker and Zhang face off

Follow all the action in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, as Joseph Parker takes on Zhilei Zhang.

Joseph Parker during the WBC International & WBO Intercontinental Heavyweight title fight against Deontay Wilder during the Day of Reckoning: Fight Night in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Photo: Getty Images / Richard Pelham

Joseph Parker is back in the ring, taking on Chinese heavyweight Zhilei Zhang for the interim WBO belt.

Parker is coming off arguably one of the best performances of his career when he beat American Deontay Wilder in a unanimous decision in December, which took his record to 34 wins and three losses.

Zhang comes in with a 26-1-1 record after defeating Joe Joyce twice last year.

The Knockout Chaos card is headlined by Anthony Joshua fighting Francis Ngannou.
