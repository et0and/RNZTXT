Live: 'He got it mixed up' - Luxon on minister's confusing prison announcement

The Corrections Minister admits he "muddied the water" after he and the Prime Minister repeatedly provided incorrect information about a $1.9 billion prison spend-up. Audio

Mark Mitchell. Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

In a confusing media conference at Parliament, Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Corrections Minister Mark Mitchell on Monday repeatedly provided incorrect information about their planned prison spend-up.

It raised more questions than it gave answers, - with Luxon and Mitchell at times contradicting one another.

Speaking to RNZ on Tuesday, Mitchell said "I muddied the waters a bit" while Luxon said Mitchell had 'got it mixed up in the moment' but had clarified immediately after. Luxon said Mitchell was doing a good job in his roles.
