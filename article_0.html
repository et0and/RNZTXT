Live: Bulk of properties should have power back tonight - Transpower

Seventy percent of properties that were plunged into darkness this morning should have power back on this evening, Transpower says.

There are widespread power outages in Northland and the Far North. Photo: Supplied / Top Energy

Seventy percent of properties that were plunged into darkness this morning should have power back on this evening, Transpower says.

Power has been out for nearly 100,000 properties across Northland after a transmission tower, that carries most of the region's power needs, fell shortly after eleven o'clock this morning.

Transpower's executive general manager grid delivery, Mark Ryall told Checkpoint its alternative supply is now up and running, and will soon power up properties across the region this evening.

But he warned people to conserve power to reduce the risk of overloading the network.

Ryall said he did not know why the tower fell over, but said it should not have done so in calm conditions.
