Two missing, four injured after quad bike crash

Two people are missing and four are injured after a quad bike crash in the Far North shortly after 5pm this evening.

Two people are missing after a quad bike crash in the Far North. (File image) Photo: 123rf

Two people are missing and four are injured after a quad bike crash in the Far North shortly after 5pm this evening.

The police say emergency services were called to Fern Flat Road in Peria after a quad bike rolled into a swimming hole on the swollen Waikainga Stream.

Four of the six on board were able to make it out of the water and they have been taken to hospital with minor to moderate injuries.

Two other people are still missing.

The police said the search for the missing people will continue into the evening.

The serious crash unit is investigating.

The incident comes just two days after a woman was killed in a quad bike incident in Marokopa.
