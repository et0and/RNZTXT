Live: Warriors vs Melbourne Storm, NRL round 2 updates

Catch all the action from tonight's round two clash at AAMI Park in Melbourne with RNZ's live blog.

Addin Fonua-Blake of the Warriors crosses the line to score a try during the NRL Round 8 match between the Melbourne Storm and the New Zealand Warriors. Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

Can the Warriors bounce back from a disappointing opening defeat to the Sharks?

It's certainly not an easy task tonight against the Storm, who last weekend knocked off last year's premiers the Panthers.

Things have been made more difficult with the news that hooker Wayde Egan is OUT, succumbing to an arm injury. He's replaced by Freddy Lussick in the starting team, with Jazz Tevaga coming onto the interchange bench.
