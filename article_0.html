Live: New downpour forecast as floods, landslips hit Wellington

Roads and garages have been flooded, workers have been urged to stay home, and a train hit rockfall amid downpours across the region.

A second downpour may be heading towards Wellington as roads and garages flood around the region.

MetService says there is a possibility of another heavy burst of rain between 3pm and 6pm on Monday.

It comes after reports of flooding across Hutt City, Featherston, Silverstream, Trentham, Porirua, Waiwhetu, Whitby, Stokes Valley and Naenae on Monday morning.

Earlier, a train travelling from Waikanae to Wellington collided rockfall, while SH58 was closed going west at Whitby .
