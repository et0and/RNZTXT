Three in hospital after multi-vehicle crash closes SH1

State Highway 1 has since been closed to southbound and northbound traffic, with diversions in place via State Highway 4 and 49.

Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

Multiple people have been seriously injured in a two-vehicle crash north of Mangaweka.

Police were alerted to the crash on State Highway 1 near the intersection with Ruahine Road around 12.42pm.

State Highway 1 has since been closed to southbound and northbound traffic, with diversions in place via State Highway 4 and 49.

Motorists are advised to expect delays.

Two people, one in a critical condition, and another in a serious condition were airlifted to Wellington Regional Hospital, and a third in a moderate condition was taken by road to Palmerston North hospital.
