Live: Debris covers Haumoana road after sea wall deteriorates in wild weather

State of emergencies have been declared in the Heretaunga Ward in Hastings and Wairoa, due to the potential risk to lives and properties.

Debris is covering the road in Haumoana after a sea wall near the village deteriorated.

A state of emergency has been declared and about 100 people have been evacuated from the Hawke's Bay town due to concerns about potential flooding.

RNZ reporter in Haumoana, Anusha Bradley, says everything is being done in preparation for high tide this morning, which may increase flooding risk.

At this time, officials say no homes have been flooded and no one is in danger.

Weather and swells are due to improve this afternoon.
