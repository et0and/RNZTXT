NZ Live: The Black Quartet

Aotearoa's premiere string ensemble performs a bewitching set of original compositions and ingenious covers. Video, Audio

The Black Quartet Photo: Supplied

Aotearoa's premiere string ensemble The Black Quartet delivers a cool, contemporary take on the classical sound.

Jessica Hindin, Mahuia Bridgman-Cooper, Joseph Harrop, and Rachel Wells perform original compositions and some ingenious covers.

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

The Black Quartet played:

'Deja': "'Deja' is the name of a lovely young woman I met on a park bench at 1am in Byron Bay and had a couple of hours of cool conversation with... I never spoke to her again but she seemed to be able to solve all the world's problems or my problems... It was just a lovely meaningful moment and this was the tune" - Jessica Hindin.

'Purple Haze' (a Prince cover)

'Exhale' (original)

'Welcome Home' (a Dave Dobbyn cover)
