'Fresh ideas, younger muscles' - taking on the family farm

28-year-old Sarah Maxwe is an AI technician and stock manager in the Manawatū. Audio

Sarah manages the stock on the family farm near Bunnythorpe Photo: RNZ/Sally Round

Sarah Maxwell switches from inseminating cows in the morning to shifting cows and sheep in the afternoon, working at the local sale yards, and then in the evening sometimes connecting with her young farming mates as part of Young Farmers.

The 28-year-old is busy as an AI technician, sheep and beef stock manager on the family farm near Bunnythorpe and Young Farmers rep.
