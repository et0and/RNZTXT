Golden Globes 2025: All the best looks from the red carpet

Gold, silver, sparkles and glitz were big trends at this year's Golden Globes, with the stars turning up the elegance to the max.

All the best looks, from the Golden Globes red carpet, 2025. Photo: AFP, Getty

The 82nd Golden Globes are in full swing in Beverly Hills, with all eyes on the fashion as well as the prizes. We have collected some of the brightest and best looks, and the biggest splashes.

The Golden Globes are the first big awards outing for the year for Hollywood, and are known for casting a wide spotlight, with extra variety and room for fun.

Gold, silver, sparkles and glitz were big trends at this outing, with the stars turning up the elegance to the max.
