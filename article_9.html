Oscars 2025: See the full list of winners

The 97th annual Academy Awards is here - check out who took the big awards.

Anora claimed five 2025 Oscars. Photo: RNZ Graphic/Samuel Robinson

Anora sweeps up at the Oscars, bagging five awards including best picture.

The Brutalist won three awards - Best Cinematography, Best Score, and Best Actor (Adrien Brody).

Wicked won two awards in tech categories - Best Production Design and Best Costume Design.

Dune: Part Two won two awards - Best Sound and Best Visual Effects.

Emilia Perez also won two awards - Best Original Song and Best Supporting Actress (Zoe Saldana).

Conclave, The Substance, I'm Still Here, and A Real Pain won one award each - the latter for Best Supporting Actor (Kieran Culkin).

All celebrities walked the red carpet in style at the 97th annual Academy Awards, hosted this year by Conan O'Brien.
