NZ Live: Wurld Series

Internationally acclaimed indie rock band Wurld Series perform live in our Christchurch studio. Video, Audio

Photo: supplied / Wurld Series

"Thoughtful and witty" is how Pitchfork described What's Growing - the 2021 album by Christchurch indie rock band Wurld Series.

Their latest album The Giant's Lawn reached number 6 on Rolling Stone's Top 25 New Zealand albums of 2023.
