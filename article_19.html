Serebii live in the RNZ studio

Meet Tāmaki Makaurau-based composer, multi-instrumentalist and rising talent Serebii, who was live in the RNZ studio with his band to play songs from his new album Dime. Audio

Tāmaki Makaurau-based composer, multi-instrumentalist and rising talent Serebii has just released a new album Dime.

Serebii, who has previously focused on production - often in collaboration with Arjuna Oakes - has taken the brave step of putting his own voice centre stage on this album. 

Callum Mower, who goes by the artist name Serebii, was live in the RNZ studio with his band Rachel Clark (Elisa) on vocals and Michael Howell playing guitar. 

SEREBII album release tour: 

04 April – Meow, Wellington 05 April – 18a, Nelson 10 April – Double Whammy, Auckland
