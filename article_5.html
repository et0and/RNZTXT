Live: Christopher Luxon, Nicola Willis on changes for banking sector

The government is promising a swathe of changes to bolster KiwiBank, and bring about more competition in the banking sector.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Finance Minister Nicola Willis speaking about the OCR on Wednesday 27 November, 2024. Photo: RNZ / Reece Baker

The government is promising to inject more capital into Kiwibank to increase competition in the sector.

It has agreed to adopt all 14 recommendations from the Commerce Commission's banking sector market study.

It's also considering letting Kiwisaver funds and other major investors invest in the New Zealand-owned bank, with a view to raising an additional $500 million dollars.

Finance Minister Nicola Willis and Prime Minister Christopher Luxon will speak to Morning Report today.
