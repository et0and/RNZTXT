Live: Luxon defends 'outsourcing' of surgeries to private hospitals

Hospitals say the government is hiding waiting queues, but the Prime Minister says what matters is patient outcomes.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon says using the private healthcare system to help ease public waitlists helps to improve outcomes.

Hospital specialists have accused the government of farming out operations to private hospitals to give "false" impression that it's improving wait times.

Luxon says whether someone gets a surgery done through the public or private system, what's important is patient outcomes.
