Politics live: Christopher Luxon says still room for tourism growth

The Prime Minister says there is room for growth in tourism because the sector is not operating at full capacity.

Follow all the latest politics news with RNZ's live blog:

The Prime Minister says there is room for growth in tourism because the sector is not operating at full capacity.

The government launched a campaign over the weekend to bring in more tourists, and is announcing funding for upgrades to popular visitor sites.

Christopher Luxon said the sector was operating at 88 percent of what it was six years ago and tourism leaders were united in wanting to get the numbers back to 100-percent.

Meanwhile, the Labour Party has accused the government of a cover-up for refusing to release details of the reoffending rate for the teenagers in its boot camp pilot scheme.
