Live: Pledge to fund new cancer drugs in question

"When we've got the funding to be able to start that initiative, we will," the Health Minister said when asked about this week's Budget.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

National's pledge to fund 13 cancer treatments from this year is looking increasingly shaky - with numerous ministers saying they cannot do everything in one Budget.

During the campaign, National pledged to give Pharmac an extra 70 million dollars a year over four years in ring-fenced funding to target 13 specific cancers.
