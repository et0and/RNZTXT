Live: Hīkoi heads to Rotorua as Treaty Principles Bill gets first reading

The hīkoi will leave from Huntly this morning and hold a gathering in Hamilton. Audio

Photo: Lucy Xia

Thousands of people will continue their North Island hīkoi as the legislation they are protesting gets its first reading in Parliament.

The hīkoi enters day four Thursday and will head off from Huntly, destined for Rotorua today, after it advanced through Auckland on Wednesday.
