Live: Would fewer public servants working from home make a difference?

Nicola Willis says some public sector staff are 'taking advantage', Labour says civil servants are being demonised, and a recruitment expert says 'people don't want to do it'.

Public Service Nicola Willis has told government departments to tighten up working from home arrangements. Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Public Service Minister Nicola Willis has directed department bosses to tighten up on working-from-home arrangements.

A union says the move is a distraction and it's the government's public sector job cuts that have caused woe for Wellington's retail and hospitality sectors.

However the Wellington Chamber of Commerce wants the city council to follow the government's lead and encourage more people to stop working from home.
