New Zealand music legend Willie Hona dead

His daughter, Natalie Hona, said he died on Sunday night surrounded by his whānau in Paraparaumu.

Photo: Supplied / Givealittle

New Zealand music legend Willie Hona has died.

Hona was a member of the iconic reggae band Herbs.

His daughter Natalie Hona said he died on Sunday night surrounded by his whānau in Paraparaumu.

The death comes after a battle with cancer.
