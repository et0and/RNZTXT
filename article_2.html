Livestream: Watch the protest at Parliament

Thousands have marched through the streets of a capital and to the Beehive.

We are livestreaming the hīkoi at Parliament at the top of this page. You can follow our liveblog here.

Thousands have marched through the streets of a capital and to the Beehive, to protest the ACT Party's Treaty Principles Bill.

A hīkoi against the controversial legislation reached Wellington on Tuesday.

Green Mp Tamatha Paul said those gathering were united in their determination: "The energy, the power, it's palpable here on the ground."

Police said there have been no significant issues on Friday morning.
