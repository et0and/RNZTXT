A-League live updates: Newcastle Jets v Auckland FC

Follow all the action from McDonald Jones Stadium, Newcastle.

Auckland FC's Hiroki Sakai scores and celebrates his goal. A-League Men, Round 21, Auckland FC v Adelaide United, Go Media Stadium. Photo: Shane Wenzlick / www.photosport.nz

Auckland FC still hold an eight-point lead at the top of the A-League ladder, despite their thrilling draw last weekend against Adelaide United. They face a Jets side hungry to climb up into the top eight, who are also coming in having won their last three games in a row. Kick-off is at 7pm NZT.
