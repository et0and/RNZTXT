A-League live updates: Brisbane Roar v Auckland FC

Follow all the action from Suncorp Stadium, Brisbane.

Auckland FC Dan Hall and Brisbane Roar Thomas Waddingham battle it out during the A-League football match between Auckland FC v Brisbane Roar held at Go Media Stadium. Photo: Brett Phibbs / www.photosport.nz

Three draws in their last three matches means Auckland's lead on the A-League table has been cut to four points, with Western United hot on their tail. They will be expecting the full three points out of this one though, with Brisbane struggling back in 12th place with only two wins all season. Kick-off is at 7pm NZT.
