T20 Cricket live updates: Black Caps v Afghanistan

New Zealand are opening their T20 World Cup campaign today against Afghanistan - follow all the action on RNZ's liveblog.

Kane Williamson of the Black Caps Photo: PHOTOSPORT

New Zealand open their T20 World Cup campaign today as they face Afghanistan.

The Black Caps are one of the last teams to get started and after more than a week preparing in Guyana, captain Kane Williamson said they were keen to get going.

Three matches have been played so far at the Providence Stadium in Guyana, with Afghanistan managing the highest score at the ground of 183/5 in a big win over Uganda.
