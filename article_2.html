Live: 500 homes lose power amid downpours, risk of tornadoes around country

Warnings are getting more severe as much of the country is either already being hit or about to face heavy rain. Audio

File pic Photo: RNZ / MARIKA KHABAZI

MetService is warning there could be small tornadoes in coastal areas on Wednesday, as heavy downpours and thunderstorms are being forecast for parts of the country.

Heavy rain watches are in place for Northland (until 9am), Coromandel (until 2pm) and Bay of Plenty (until 10pm).

A watch is also in place for the Tasman, west of Motueka through until about 5pm. It says there will be periods of heavy downpours and the possibility of thunderstorms.
