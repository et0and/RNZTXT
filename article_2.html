Live: All Whites v Tunisia heads to penalty shootout

Follow the action live from Cairo International Stadium.

Dane Ingham of New Zealand in action against Egypt, Cairo, 2024. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The All Whites take on Tunisia for the first ever time in an international fixture on Wednesday morning, one which will determine the third and fourth-place finishers at the ACUD International Cup in Egypt.

The All Whites lost their semi-final match to the hosts 1-0 while Tunisia lost to Croatia on penalties after heroics from Kockasti goalkeeper Ivica Ivusic. For All Whites coach Darren Bazeley, it is a chance to have one final look at his players ahead of a first competition fixture in two years.
