Live: Black Caps v West Indies at T20 World Cup

The Kiwis have been left chasing 150 as the West Indies staged a late rally.

Devon Conway. Photo: Ramon Espinosa/AP

It's do or die time for the Black Caps in the ICC Men's T20 World Cup - they take on the Windies in a game they must win to keep their tournament hopes alive.

They face a tough task against a home team that's won both of their games so far in the tournament, while the Black Caps suffered a defeat to Afghanistan in their opening match.
