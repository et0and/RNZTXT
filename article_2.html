Live boxing updates: Kiwi Joseph Parker set to square off against Deontay Wilder

Parker is due in the ring to face the American shortly as part of a stacked card in Saudi Arabia.

Joseph Parker reacts during a weigh-in, the day before the bout in Riyadh. Photo: AFP

Kiwi Joseph Parker will soon step into the ring to face the American Deontay Wilder as part of a stacked card in Saudi Arabia.

Parker's bout was due to start around 11am but some bouts have been over quickly so it may start earlier.

Earlier today Kiwi Junior Fa was beaten in a technical knowckout by Cuban Frank Sanchez as part of a stacked card in the Day of Reckoning event in Saudi Arabia.

Meanwhile, in another main event Anthony Joshua will face Otto Wallin.
