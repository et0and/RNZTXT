Live Super Rugby Pacific: Crusaders v Reds

Follow all the action from Apollo Projects Stadium in Christchurch.

Cullen Grace of the Crusaders is denied a try during the Super Rugby Pacific match, Crusaders Vs Reds in Christchurch. Photo: John Davidson / Photosport

If the Reds can put on the sort of attacking display they did last weekend, this one should be a cracker against the resurgent Crusaders.

Les Kiss's side welcomes back Tate McDermott and Fraser McReight. They have all the capability and just need consistency, while the defending champs are keeping everyone guessing after their horrific start to the year.

Kick-off is at 4:35pm NZT.
