Motorcyclist critical after crash, busy Auckland road blocked

Motorists are asked to avoid using Pakuranga Road if possible.

Motorists are asked to avoid using Pakuranga Road if possible. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A crash in the Auckland suburb of Pakuranga has blocked a main road.

Emergency services were called to the intersection of Pakuranga Road and Stanniland Street shortly after 8:50am on Saturday.

The crash was between a car and motorcycle, with the motorcyclist said to have critical injuries.

The Serious Crash Unit is attending, and motorists are asked to avoid using Pakuranga Road if possible.

There are diversions in place.
