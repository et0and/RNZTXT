Olympics live: Black Ferns to face US in semi after blitzing China

The Kiwis ran in nine tries in their lopsided quarterfinal against China at a hot and steamy Stade de France.

Black Fern Jazmin Felix-Hotham celebrates after the match. Photo: Iain McGregor / www.photosport.nz

The Kiwis ran in nine tries in their lopsided quarterfinal against China at a hot and steamy Stade de France and will now tackle the USA in the semifinal at 1.30am on Wednesday.

The Americans ended the hopes of Great Britain with a 17-7 win.

In the second semi Australia will take on Canada, who silenced the home crowd by beating France.

Meanwhile, Erika Fairweather finished seventh in 200m freestyle semifinal where the Australians Mollie O'Callaghan won gold with Ariarne Titmus taking the silver.
