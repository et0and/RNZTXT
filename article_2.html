Large structure fire breaks out at Papakura scrap metal yard

People are being asked to avoid the area as 15 firetrucks battle the blaze.

A large fire has broken out at a scrap metal yard in South Auckland.

Fire and Emergency says 15 trucks are attending the fire on Hunua Road, Papakura, which was reported about 4.30pm.

Smoke seen coming from an industrial property in Papakura. Photo: Supplied / Anthony Picknell

"...We are asking people to avoid the area so we can do our work safely," a spokesperson says.

Photos and videos on social media show huge clouds of black smoke billowing from an industrial area.

No one is believed to be in danger and the cause of the fire is currently unclear.
