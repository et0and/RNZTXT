Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Highlanders v Reds

Follow all the action from Forsyth Barr Stadium, Dunedin.

Tom Lynagh of the Reds charges forward during the round six Super Rugby Pacific match between Highlanders and Queensland Reds at Forsyth Barr Stadium, on 22 March 2025, in Dunedin. Photo: Joe Allison / Getty Images

Two teams with a reputation for running the ball have the perfect environment to do just that under the roof in Dunedin. The Highlanders had a narrow loss to the Hurricanes, while the Reds scored a decisive win over arch-rivals the Waratahs last weekend, however the student section at Forsyth Barr will be packed again for this one. Kick-off is at 4:35pm NZT.
