Woman dies east of Auckland in water-related event

A woman has died in a water-related event east of Auckland, on Labour Day.

(File photo) Photo:

A woman has died in a water-related event east of Auckland, on Labour Day.

Police said the woman was in her 40s.

"Emergency services were called to the scene around 3:30pm," they said in a statement.

Attempts were made to resuscitate the woman, but she died at the scene, they said.
