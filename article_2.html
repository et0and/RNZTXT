Boy, 6, reported missing in south Auckland

Anton was last seen at his Randwick Park home earlier today, police say.

Anton, age 6 went missing in Randwick Park. Photo: Supplied/ Counties Manukau Police

A 6-year-old boy is missing from his south Auckland home, police say.

Counties Manukau Police said in an announcement about 6.30pm on Sunday that Anton, 6, was missing from his Randwick Park home.

He was wearing a white T-shirt and black pants when last seen earlier today.
