In pictures: Adrenaline high on sixth day of Olympics

Rowing dominated the competition in Paris, but there were plenty of other highlights too. Here are some of the best photos.

Thomas Saunders competes in race one of the men's ILCA 7 single-handed dinghy event in Marseille. Photo: AFP/CHRISTOPHE SIMON

Rowing dominated the competition on the sixth day of the Paris Olympics, but there were plenty of other highlights too.

Here are some of the best photos.

Ollie Maclean, Logan Ullrich, Tom Murray and Matt Macdonald take a bite of their silver medals after coming second in the men's fours. Photo: AFP/BERTRAND GUAY
