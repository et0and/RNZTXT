Olympics live: Gold rush for Kiwis on Day 15

Hamish Kerr's high jump win caps off a remarkable day in our sporting history, in which New Zealand collected three gold medals on day 15 of the Paris Games. Follow RNZ's live updates.

Dame Lisa Carrington celebrates her eighth gold medal after the K1 final - her third of the Paris Games. Photo: AFP/BERTRAND GUAY

Hamish Kerr's win in the dramatic high jump final caps off a remarkable day in New Zealand sporting history, in which New Zealand collected three gold medals on day 15 of the Games.

Kerr's victory followed Lydia Ko's two-shot win in the golf, and Dame Lisa Carrington's stunning win in the K1 500m final to collect her third gold of these Games.

New Zealand has now won nine medals of the sparkliest colour in Paris - the highest gold medal tally ever.
