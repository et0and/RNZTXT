Watch live: Rocket Lab's latest launch

Watch live: Rocket Lab is aiming to launch a small Earth-imaging satellite for a Japanese company from Māhia this evening. Video

Photo: Supplied / Rocket Lab

Watch live: New Zealand company Rocket Lab is aiming to launch a small Earth-imaging satellite for a Japanese company from Māhia this evening.

The take-off is due to happen some time around 5pm.

Watch it live here:

It held a dress rehearsal for the launch last week - the company's first mission since a rocket failed in September shortly after take-off.

The mission will be Rocket Lab's 10th launch of the year, beating the previous annual launch record of nine.

The mission's name 'The Moon God Awakens' is in honour of a Japanese god.

The satellite will join another one already in orbit. Both belong to Japanese firm iQPS which is aiming to have a 36-satellite constellation capable of monitoring Earth at specific fixed points every 10 minutes.
