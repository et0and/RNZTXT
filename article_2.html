Live: Golden Globes kick off as stars arrive on red carpet

The annual ceremony celebrates the best shows and films to hit big and small screens over the past 12 months.

US actress Abby Elliott arrives for the 82nd annual Golden Globe Awards at the Beverly Hilton hotel in Beverly Hills, California, on January 5, 2025. Photo: Etienne Laurent / AFP

The 82nd annual Golden Globes Awards has kicked off with Hollywood stars arriving on red carpet in Beverly Hills, Los Angeles.

Spanish-language musical thriller Emilia Perez and The Brutalist, a three-and-a-half-hour post-World War II drama, lead the movies vying for Globe trophies.

Others include box office smash Wicked, adapted from the Broadway play about the witches in The Wizard of Oz, and papal selection story Conclave.

Restaurant tale The Bear and mystery comedy Only Murders in the Building will compete for TV honours.

Stand-up comedian Nikki Glaser will host the event, making history as the first woman to host the show solo. Dwayne Johnson, Elton John, Andrew Garfield and Awkwafina will be among the award presenters.

Globe honourees are chosen by 334 entertainment journalists from 85 countries.
