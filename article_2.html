Live: Drivers warned to stay off roads as 700mm of rain heads to West Coast

Civil defence, council staff and other agencies are bracing for an incoming deluge.

Civil Defence and locals are preparing for a deluge set to hit the West Coast.

A heavy rain red warning is in place for the Westland District, until Saturday morning, with 500 to 700 mm of rain expected to accumulate about the ranges, and 100 to 200 mm about the coast.

An orange rain warning is in place for the Grey District and headwaters of Canterbury lakes and rivers from Arthur's Pass southwards until Saturday morning and Fiordland until Friday night.
