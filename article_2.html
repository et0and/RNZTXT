Liam Lawson to partner Max Verstappen in 2025

After months of speculation, the NZ driver has been named among Red Bull's Formula 1 team.

Red Bull driver Liam Lawson. Photo: LIAM K. SWIGGS / PHOTOSPORT NZ

New Zealand Formula One driver Liam Lawson will be a permanent driver for the Red Bull team in 2025.

On Thursday, Red Bull announced Lawson had been promoted from its second team and would replace Mexican Sergio Perez for next year's full 2025 calendar of races.

Lawson, 22, will be the team-mate of Dutch multiple world champion Max Verstappen.
