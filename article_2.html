Live updates: Wallabies v Springboks

Follow all the action from Optus Stadium, Perth.

Jesse Kriel (left) of the Springboks and Tom Wright (right) of the Wallabies contest for the ball. Photo: Darren England/Photosport

The world champion Springboks are looking to build on their dominant 33-7 win last weekend in Brisbane, while the Wallabies are just looking for answers. This will be an interesting one as the All Blacks prepare to play both these sides in the coming weeks. Kick-off is at 9:45pm NZT.
