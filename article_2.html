Severe thunderstorms forecast to hit Auckland to Waikato today are now expected to start from late morning.

MetService has issued a severe thunderstorm watch for Auckland, Great Barrier Island, Coromandel Peninsula and the Waikato, from 10am on Wednesday until evening.

Parts of the North Island are also bracing for severe winds, with an orange warning in place for Auckland from 3pm.

Severe wind watches are in place from Wednesday morning for parts of the north and south.

Gusts are expected to reach 120km/h in some parts, made worse with thunderstorms.
