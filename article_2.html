Watch Mata: Nanaia Mahuta gives first interview since election

Outgoing Foreign Affairs Minister Nanaia Mahuta on the latest in Gaza, while former Labour MP for Northcote Shanan Halbert and outgoing Māori Affairs Minister Tau Henare discuss coalition talks.

In her first interview since the election, outgoing Minister for Foreign Affairs and Trade Nanaia Mahuta speaks about the latest in Gaza. Then former Labour MP for Northcote Shanan Halbert and outgoing Minister of Māori Affairs Tau Henare discuss coalition talks and the latest here at home.
