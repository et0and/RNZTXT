Tiwai Point aluminium smelter to stay open until 2044

The package includes a long-term fixed price contract for wholesale electricity price.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

The Tiwai Point aluminium smelter is set to stay open until 2044 after it reached a new power supply deal with suppliers Meridian and Contact.

The package includes a long-term fixed price contract for wholesale electricity price cover and a significant demand response agreement.

Meridian Energy Chief Executive Neal Barclay says the agreement is an excellent result after many years of hard work.
