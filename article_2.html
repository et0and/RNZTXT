Olympics live: Fiji Sevens team aims for gold

Fiji faces France in the gold medal match of the men's Sevens. Follow along with RNZ Pacific's liveblog.

Fiji team huddle before the game against Uruguay on day one of the Paris 2024 Olympic Games at Stade de France on 24 July 2024 in Paris. Photo: World Rugby / Mike Lee - KLC

The Fiji men's sevens team is aiming to defend its Olympic Games gold medal this morning.

Kickoff is 5:45am, Fiji time.

It has not all come easy, despite an unbeaten run through the group stage.

On Friday morning they beat Ireland 19-15 in the quarterfinal, but Ireland led a lot of the match and pushed for a winning try after time had run out.

But the Rio 2016 and Tokyo 2020 gold medal winners held on to win.

In the semifinal against Australia earlier this morning, it was 7 - 7 at halftime before Fij pulled away in the seond half to win 31 - 7.
