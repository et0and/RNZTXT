Search resumes for man who fell into Manawatū River

Police said the man was walking his dog at Ahimate Reserve when he fell into the water on Saturday afternoon.

Ahimate Reserve by the Manawatū River. Photo: Google Maps

The search resumed Sunday morning for a man who fell into the Manawatū River in Palmerston North on Saturday afternoon.

Police said the man was walking his dog at Ahimate Reserve when he fell into the water about 1.30pm.

His companion raised the alarm when he did not reappear.

The search was called off Saturday night when it got dark, but resumed at 8am Sunday.

Police said the missing man's friends and family remained in the area overnight.
