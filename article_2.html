Major delays on Auckland's Southern Motorway

There are major delays for traffic in both directions on Auckland's Southern Motorway after a crash between Bombay and Ramarama.

A crash causing traffic to back up along Auckland's Southern Motorway on Sunday. Photo: Supplied/ Waka Kotahi

There are major delays for traffic in both directions on Auckland's Southern Motorway after a crash between Bombay and Ramarama.

A crash there earlier had snarled the traffic, and the back-ups continued as vehicle recovery was being carried out along that section of State Highway 1, Waka Kotahi said.

Northbound and southbound lane closures were in place as crews worked at the scene.

Motorists were being warned about delays and major congestion between Bombay and Drury, and were asked to merge carefully.
