Panthers make it four Premierships in a row with win over Storm

The Penrith Panthers have taken the trophy in what was tipped as one of the biggest NRL showdowns of all time.

Jarome Luai of the Panthers runs with the ball. Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

In what was tipped to be one of the biggest NRL showdowns of all time, Dally M medal winner Jahrome Hughes and the Melbourne Storm took on Nathan Cleary and the Penrith Panthers in the NRL grand final.
