Firefighters working to contain fire at West Auckland beach

The fire is at Anawhata Beach, near Piha.

Smoke could be seen from Piha. Photo: supplied

A fire has broken out at one of Auckland's West Coast beaches.

Fire and Emergency confirmed they were notified of the blaze on Anawhata Beach just before 5.30pm on Wednesday.

Eight firetrucks, three helicopters, and multiple support vehicles were at the scene, a spokesperson confirmed.

Crews were still working to contain the fire which was approximately 200 by 200 metres in size.

A Piha resident said she could see smoke from the Anawhata fire from her home.

"I saw a helicopter with a bucket fly over and local [fire] crews are there now."
