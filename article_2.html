Football live: All Whites v Samoa

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium in Auckland.

New Zealand captain Chris Wood. Photo: Trevor Ruszkowski / www.photosport.nz

As expected, New Zealand are through to the next stage of FIFA World Cup 2026 qualifiers, and almost certainly as group winners. However, they still have one game left - against Samoa for the first time since 1987. Kick-off is at 7:30pm.
