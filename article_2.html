Cricket live updates: Black Caps v Pakistan

Follow all the action from Hagley Oval, Christchurch.

Zac Foulkes of the Black Caps during the first T20 cricket match New Zealand Black Caps Vs Pakistan, at Hagley Oval in Christchurch. Photo: John Davidson / Photosport

The Black Caps are chasing 92 runs to beat Pakistan in the opening T20 international at Hagley Oval in Christchruch.

Pace bowlers Kyle Jamieson and Jacob Duffy took seven wickets between them as Pakistan were dismissed in the 19th over for 91.

Pakistan lost their first three wickets for just one run.
