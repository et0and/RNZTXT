Coromandel's SH25a to reopen in time for Christmas

The crucial Coromandel link will reopen three months ahead of schedule.

Photo: Waka Kotahi

The crucial Coromandel link State Highway 25a will reopen on 20 December - three months ahead of schedule.

The road, a key link to towns like Whangamatā and Hāhei, was damaged earlier this year when its bridge was washed out in storms.

Since then a 124-metre bridge has been built which spans the abyss in the severed highway.

SH25a was washed away in the severe weather. Photo: Tom Taylor / RNZ

In October, Thames-Coromandel Mayor Len Salt said the local economy had lost $30m since its closure.
