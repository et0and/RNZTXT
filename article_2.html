World record haka attempt: In pictures

From pēpi to kaumātua, people travelled from far and wide to bring the kaupapa home. Here are some of the best photos.

Unique and Nopera were among the thousands who were there to "enjoy and support the kaupapa". Photo: RNZ / Layla Bailey-McDowell

Thousands of people have packed Tāmaki Makaurau's Eden Park in an attempt to reclaim the Guinness World Record for the largest haka.

The current official world record for number of people doing a haka at once is 4028 and has been held by France since 2014.

Everyone from pēpi to kaumātua was there to show their support, many with tino rangatiratanga flags on display.
