Pilot rescued after light plane crash off Tasman Coast

The pilot of a light plane that crashed in the Tasman Bay on Tuesday evening has been rescued, and flown to Nelson Hospital.

The plane crashed off the Tasman Coast around Ruby Bay. File picture. Photo: CC 4.0 BY-SA / Andr√© Brett

The pilot of a light plane that crashed in the Tasman Bay on Tuesday evening has been rescued, and flown to Nelson Hospital.

Maritime New Zealand said the plane crashed into the sea, 42km north of Nelson.

Its rescue coordination centre was notified about 7.30pm.

The pilot, who was the only person on-board, was rescued by the GCH Nelson Rescue Helicopter.
