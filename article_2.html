Weather: Soggy start to Christmas holidays as rain, thunderstorms forecast

There's a brief lift in the wet weather before Christmas but showers and rain are forecast for much of the country for the next few days.

Photo: MetService

There's a brief lift in the wet weather before Christmas but showers and rain are forecast for much of the country for the next few days.

Showers continued through the night in central parts of North Island but are due to ease with the risk of severe thunderstorms over for most.

But in the South Island, parts of Marlborough, Canterbury and Otago could get severe thunderstorms on Monday afternoon along with downpours of up to 40mm an hour.

The best of the weather in the lead-up to Christmas Day looks to be on Tuesday.

MetService is forecasting dry weather for most of the country, though Fiordland gets rain in the evening.
