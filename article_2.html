Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Crusaders v Moana Pasifika

Follow all the action from Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch.

Noah Hotham of the Crusaders grabs the ball from William Havili of Moana Pasifika to score. Photo: Photosport
