Live: Shaun Johnson's final game for the Warriors

It's the end of the road for the Warriors' troubled NRL season, as departing Shaun Johnson plays his last match.

Shaun Johnson celebrates fate scoring. Photo: Photosport

It's the end of the road for the Warriors' troubled NRL season, as departing Shaun Johnson plays his last match.

Johnson runs out for one last time to take on his former club the Cronulla Sharks in Sydney.

The Warriors will be playing for nothing but pride as their finals hopes were dashed weeks ago.

Kickoff is at 9.35pm.
