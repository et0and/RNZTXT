Person trapped after tree falls on caravan

Firefighters are using a digger to try to free the person.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

A person is trapped after a tree fell on a caravan in Canterbury on Sunday night.

Firefighters are using a digger to try to free the person.

Fire and Emergency NZ said it was not clear if the incident, in Balmoral, was caused by the weather.

St John Ambulance has been approached for comment.
