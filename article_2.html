Second serious crash closes road in the Bay of Plenty

There has been another serious crash in the Bay of Plenty in 24 hours, after the first crash left one dead.

File photo. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

There has been another serious crash in the Bay of Plenty in 24 hours, after the first crash left one dead.

Emergency services were currently at the scene of a two-vehicle collision on SH29 in Lower Kaimai, after police were notified about the incident just after midday.

The road was likely to be closed for sometime and motorists were asked to expect delays, said police.

Meanwhile, a person has died after a serious crash in Mount Maunganui, Bay of Plenty on Wednesday night.

Police said emergency services were notified of the crash at 10.17pm at the intersection of Aerodrome Road and Hewletts Road, Mount Maunganui.

A second person was taken to hospital with moderate injuries.

Police said inquiries were ongoing to determine the cause of the crash.

The Christmas-New Year holiday road toll now stands at eight.
