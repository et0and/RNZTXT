Person injured in assault at Wellington Girls' College

Police say emergency services were called to Wellington Girls' College just after 10am.

Police said emergency services were called to Wellington Girls' College just after 10am. Photo: RNZ/Nick James

A person has been injured in an assault at Wellington Girls' College.

Police said emergency services were called to the school on Pipitea Street in Thorndon just after 10am on Friday.

One person had moderate injuries and was being treated by ambulance staff, they said.

Police said they were speaking to a second person and said the two people involved were known to each other.

Wellington Girls' College declined to comment.
