Police chase across Auckland ends with car on fire, arrests

One of the cars was caught in police spikes but continued to be driven through the city and onto the Southern Motorway.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Two people have been arrested after a police chase across Auckland involving two stolen cars - one of which caught fire.

Police were called about a car being stolen in the North Shore suburb of Hauraki about 5.20am on Monday.

They said the police helicopter found the car, but it drove away from officers who signalled it to stop.

Even after being caught in police spikes, it continued to drive through the city and onto the Southern Motorway.

Inspector Rod Carpinter said the car was dumped in Botany, and the pair inside stole another one.
