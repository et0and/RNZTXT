A-League live updates: Auckland FC v Melbourne City

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Auckland FC's Louis Verstraete and Melbourne Victory's Kasey Bos. Photo: Shane Wenzlick / www.photosport.nz

With only one win in their last five games, Auckland FC have slipped to fourth on the A-League ladder.

Captain Hiroki Sakai is out for today's match against new league leaders Melbourne City, kick-off is at 5pm NZT.
