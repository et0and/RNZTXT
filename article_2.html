Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Hurricanes v Fijian Drua

Follow all the action from McLean Park, Napier.

Cameron Roigard during the Hurricanes v Fiji Drua at Sky Stadium in Wellington. Photo: Marty Melville/Photosport

The Hurricanes are looking to bounce back after a first up loss to the Crusaders last weekend, and will meet a Drua side that has traditionally struggled away from home. The game has been taken to Hawke's Bay, where a big crowd is expected.

Kick-off is at 4:35pm NZT.
