Live: Power cuts as fast-moving storm sweeps over Auckland

A rapidly moving storm has cut power to homes before passing, while heavy wind still looms.

Severe thunderstorms and strong winds set to hit Auckland to Waikato are arriving earlier than forecast.

The Metservice has issued a red thunderstorm warning across Auckland, Thames Coromandel, Kaipara, Auckland and Waikato.

Gusts are also expected to reach 120km/h in some parts.
