Gunshots reported in central Wellington, glass windows in shops shattered

Workers arrived to find broken glass on the ground and what looked like bullet holes in the windows.

Shattered windows at Wholly Bagel on The Terrace, Wellington, on 2 September 2024 after gunshots were reported overnight. Photo: RNZ / Pretoria Gordon

Two shops on The Terrace in Wellington have had glass windows shattered after gunshots were reported early on Monday morning.

Workers at Wholly Bagel and TJ Superette arrived to find broken glass on the ground and what look like bullet holes in the windows

TJ Terrace Superette owner Rekha Patel said she came in early and saw a lot of glass everywhere.

Rekha Patel said it didn't look like anything had been taken but but she was really scared.

"I looked at the glass outside and I thought it's something maybe someone broke (a) bottle or something."

She said two customers

came in and helped her get in and check the shop.

The bagel shop manager had arrived after a call from the owner but said she wasn't yet sure what had happened.

Police said they received a report of wilful damage about 3am on Monday from a passerby who noted a smashed window, and would be following up with the shops.
