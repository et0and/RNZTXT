South Korea president impeached over declaration of martial law

President Yoon Suk Yeol has been removed from his post after 204 lawmakers voted to oust him.

A television screen shows South Korea's President Yoon Suk Yeol announcing the lifting of martial law, in Seoul, South Korea on December 4, 2024, hours after Yoon imposed it. Photo: YONHAP / AFP

South Korea's parliament has voted to impeach President Yoon Suk Yeol over his declaration of martial law on 3 December.

Reuters reported that 204 lawmakers voted for the impeachment.

Yoon appointed Prime Minister Han Duck-Soo to become South Korea's acting president after the impeachment.
