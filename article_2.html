33-year-old charged with murder of Dunedin man

Gurjit Singh, 27, was found dead a week ago, as a result of multiple stab wounds inflicted with a sharp object.

Police cordoned off Hillary Street in the Dunedin suburb of Liberton after the sudden death on 29 January, 2024. Photo: RNZ / Tess Brunton

A 33-year-old has been charged with the murder of Dunedin man Gurjit Singh.

Singh, 27, was found dead in Liberton a week ago as a result of multiple stab wounds inflicted by a sharp object.

Detective Senior Sergeant Kallum Croudis said a large team of investigators have worked extensively on the case.

He said several vehicles, another residential property and workplaces were still part of the ongoing investigation.

The murder-accused will appear in Dunedin District Court on Monday.

Mr Singh's father is in Dunedin today and is being supported by the Punjabi community and police.
