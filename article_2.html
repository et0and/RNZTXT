Auckland High Court evacuated after bomb threat

The High Court in Auckland has been evacuated following a bomb threat.

The High Court in Auckland. Photo: RNZ / Simon Rogers

The High Court in Auckland has been evacuated following a bomb threat this morning.

The evacuation comes amid an admission ceremony for new lawyers, with dozens of family and friends in attendance for the day.

All court procedures have been cancelled for the day.

A police spokesperson says the source of the threat is the same as those sent to other organisations including schools and hospitals yesterday and other organisations today.

Police have said they do not believe there is an actual threat to those receiving the emails.
