Live Super Rugby Pacific: Chiefs, Blues battle it out

Follow all the action from FMG Stadium Waikato, Hamilton.

Xavi Taele of the Blues at the Super Rugby Pacific at FMG Stadium Waikato, Hamilton. Photo: Jeremy Ward / Photosport

The Chiefs are looking to make it two from two against their northern rivals, after dismantling the Blues in the opening game of the season. They welcome back Damian McKenzie at first five, while the Blues have made a number of changes in a bid to get their season back on track. Kick-off is at 7:05pm.
