Email threat: Nelson airport evacuated

Nelson Airport has been evacuated this morning, in the latest email threat to target New Zealand organisations and institutions.

Photo: Supplied

Nelson Airport has been evacuated this morning, in the latest email threat to target New Zealand organisations and institutions.

Police said the airport was evacuated as a precaution after an email "of a concerning nature" was received.

"We are working to establish the origin of the email and have yet to establish any actual threat."

Members of the publics have been advised to avoid the airport until the incident is resolved, and travellers have been urged to follow instructions from their airline.

Several schools and courts were evacuated in recent weeks after a flurry of threatening emails were send to the organisations.

"Police acknowledge how unsettling these emails are for all concerned. We'd like to thank all involved for their cooperation as we investigate these emails."
