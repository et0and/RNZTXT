Tsunami warning for California after strong quake off coast

Warnigs for parts the US state have been isssued after the shallow 6.6 quake.

Photo: USGS/Supplied

A strong and shallow earthquake has struck California, sparking a tsunami warning.

The warning is for parts of the California coast.

The magnitude of the earthquake that struck Ferndale, California has been revised to 7 from 6.6, the United States Geological Survey (USGS) said.

The quake was at a depth of 10 kilometers, USGS said.

The US National Tsunami Center also issued a tsunami warning.
