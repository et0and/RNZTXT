Live: PM defends new public sector targets amid job cuts

"I want the public service focused," Christopher Luxon says.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

The government is expected to announce the Ministry of Education will be required to publish weekly school attendance statistics as part of its crackdown on truancy.

RNZ understands the announcement will be made by Associate Education Minister David Seymour and Prime Minister Christopher Luxon in Wellington later on Tuesday morning.

It comes after Luxon announced nine targets for the next six years, including health, crime and education goals.
