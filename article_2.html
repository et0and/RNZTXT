Live: 60,000 properties in Northland get power back

Northpower says 65,000 of the properties it supplies were affected, and it's now back on for all but 5000.

Power outages across the north as at 6.30pm on Thursday. Photo: Supplied / Top Energy

Northland lines company Northpower says 60,000 properties now have power back on after a region-wide outage today - but there is no hot water.

Northpower says 65,000 of the properties it supplies were affected, and it is now back on for all but 5000, which are businesses and industrial customers that do not need power right now.
