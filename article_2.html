Live weather: Wairoa resident anxious about state of home after flood

A Wairoa resident says she and her partner would have kayaked out of their property if emergency services had not been able to pick them up. Follow all the latest developments with RNZ's live blog.

Flooding on Wairoa's Black Street on Wednesday. Photo: RNZ / Calvin Samuel

A Wairoa resident at an evacuation centre says she and her partner would have kayaked out of their property if emergency services had not been able to pick them up.

Marcella Reedy and her partner evacuated in knee-deep water after not at first realising people were evacuating.

"On Wednesday morning I heard knocks at my door and didn't think anything of it but I never got an emergency text, phone must have been playing up, and then next minute well, looked out the window and then saw that the flood was coming."

Reedy said they were now waiting for the all-clear to return home and were very anxious about what state they would find their house in.
