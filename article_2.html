Rugby Championship live updates: Wallabies v Springboks

Follow all the action from Suncorp Stadium, Brisbane.

Frans Malherbe of South Africa (right) is tackled by Rory Arnold of Australia. Photo: DAN HIMBRECHTS/Photosport

The Rugby Championship opens in Brisbane with the Wallabies hosting the world champion Springboks in a rare afternoon match.

Suncorp Stadium has sold out for the game, which is kicking off at 4:30pm NZT.
