Multiple firefighters believed injured in wall collapse at Hawke's Bay station

RNZ understand multiple people have been injured.

Photo: Google Maps

Multiple firefighters have been injured in a possible wall collapse next to a Hawke's Bay fire station, RNZ understands,

Several fire crews were at the scene on Thursday.

Five people have been hurt, two seriously, with one being flown to Hawke's Bay Hospital.

Police are assisting Fire and Emergency at a campground near the Te Pohue Fire Station after they were notified shortly after 9am Thursday.

A local told RNZ they heard sirens and had seen a portable home being delivered to the Te Pohue firestation on Wednesday.
