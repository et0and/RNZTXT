PM Christopher Luxon makes joint call with allies for a ceasefire in Gaza

New Zealand is one of three countries that is calling for urgent international efforts for a "sustainable ceasefire" in Gaza.

Christopher Luxon Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

New Zealand is one of three countries that is calling for urgent international efforts for a "sustainable ceasefire" in Gaza.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon has joined his counterparts in Australia and Canada in saying: "The price of defeating Hamas cannot be the continuous suffering of all Palestinian civilians."

They say they are alarmed at the "diminishing safe space" for civilians in Gaza.

"Australia, Canada, and New Zealand mourn every Israeli and Palestinian innocent life which has been lost in this conflict and express our condolences to all families and communities affected by the violence."
