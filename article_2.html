Jetski found in search for man trying to cross Cook Strait

Police say the man left the Tasman District, heading for Porirua, and never arrived.

The person failed to arrive at their destination on Plimmerton Beach. Photo: RNZ/Reece Baker

A jetski believed to belong to a missing man has been found in the Cook Strait.

The man vanished after setting out to cross the Cook Strait from the Tasman District.

Police say the person launched on Monday morning but never arrived at their destination at Plimmerton Beach in Porirua.

His jetski had been found at Cook Rock, off the top off the South Island.

Police and SAR teams had been conducting a water search with the assistance of Coastguard and RCCNZ.

The search and rescue operation began on Monday with police boat Lady Elizabeth IV, Marlborough Search and Rescue and coastguard vessels currently in the Cook Strait, north of the Queen Charlotte Sound.
