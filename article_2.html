Two seriously injured after shooting in Auckland - report

Two people are believed to be in hospital with serious injuries after a firearms incident in Hobsonville on Sunday night.

Photo: 123RF

Two people are in hospital with serious injuries after a firearms incident in Auckland's Hobsonville on Sunday night, https://www.stuff.co.nz/nz-news/360532502/two-people-injured-after-shooting-auckland-suburb-police-cordon-street Stuff is reporting].

A police spokesperson confirmed an investigation was underway after two people "presented at a hospital with gunshot wounds".

A nearby resident told Stuff he heard what sounded like an "explosion" around 11pm.

Officers could be seen cordoning off Memorial Lane Drive and inspecting a vehicle, according ot the report.
