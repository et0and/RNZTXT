Live: Phoenix v Wanderers

Follow live updates as the Wellington Phoenix take on Western Sydney Wanderers in round nine of the A-League.

Phoenix forward Kosta Barbarouses celebrates a goal against Western United. Photo: photosport

The Wellington Phoenix will be looking to get a win on the board after back-to-back losses in recent weeks.

They face the Western Sydney Wanderers at Commbank Stadium at 7pm NZT on Sunday, who come off the back of a win against Sydney FC.

The 'Nix are currently sixth on the A-League ladder, with a record of three wins, three losses and a draw.

Follow along with live updates on RNZ's blog.
