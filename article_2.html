Watch live: Crowds gather to farewell Efeso Collins at funeral

Large numbers of mourners are expected in South Auckland to farewell Green Party MP Fa'anānā Efeso Collins. Video

Mourners are gathering in South Auckland to farewell the late Green Party MP Fa'anānā Efeso Collins.

The 49-year-old husband and father-of-two died on 21 February after [https://www.rnz.co.nz/news/national/509744/updates-mp-efeso-collins-dies-during-charity-run collapsing during a charity even in Auckland's central city.

Fa'anānā's unexpected death came as a shock to many, with his aiga - including wife Fia and daughters Kaperiela and Asalemo - saying he was "the anchor of our tight-knit family".

Fa'anānā's funeral was being held at Due Drop Event Centre in Manukau from 1pm on Thursday.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

Tipene Funerals said: "With large numbers anticipated please arrive early to secure parking and be seated by 12.30pm for a 1pm start.

"Be advised, traffic management will be in place."
