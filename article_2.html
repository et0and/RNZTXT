Person seriously injured at Spring Hill Corrections Facility

The details of what happened are not yet clear.

Photo: Corrections / Chai Patel

A person is in a serious condition after an incident at Spring Hill Corrections Facility in the Waikato.

St John confirmed it responded to the area on Friday afternoon and took a person with serious injuries to Waikato Hospital.

Any further details needed to be directed to Corrections NZ, a St John spokesperson said.

Police said they were were notified of a possible fire at 5.10pm but at this stage have been advised police are not required to attend.

Fire and Emergency northern shift manager Hayley Prince said they sent three fire trucks to the facility at 5:12pm.

Corrections have been contacted by RNZ for comment.
