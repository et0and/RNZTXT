Netball live updates: Silver Ferns v Australia

Follow all the Constellation Cup action from Auckland's Spark Arena.

Silver Ferns Grace Nweke during the Constellation Cup 2024. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The Silver Ferns head into the second test against Australia full of confidence after a stunning 14-goal win in Wellington on Sunday.

A win tonight will hand the Ferns the Constellation Cup for only the third time, play starts at 7.30pm.
