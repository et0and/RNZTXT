Six people hospitalised after Whangamatā house fire

The house fire was on Tairua Road, that road is blocked as is part of SH25.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Six people are in hospital and roads are closed after a house fire in Whangamatā.

Twelve Fire and Emergency crews were called to the Tairua Road address around 4.45am on Wednesday.

A St John spokesperson said one ambulance was sent and six people were taken to hospital with minor injuries.

State Highway 25 was closed after the fire on Tairua Road.

Tairua Road has been blocked between Brenton Place and the Smartway sewage ponds near Gregory Lane.

The highway was closed between Port Road and Harry Watt Drive.

A detour was in place.
