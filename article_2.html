Public warned to stay away from cordon at Auckland shopping area - police

Police responding to an incident at Auckland's Pukekohe shopping centre are warning people to stay away from the area.

(File photo) Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

Police responding to an incident at Auckland's Pukekohe shopping centre are warning people to stay away from the area.

Officers were sent to The Zone shopping centre on Manukau Road on Sunday morning, after a suspicious item was discovered, police said in a statement.

"Police are asking members of the public to keep their distance," they said.

"Anyone in the area will be directed away by staff at the scene until we can establish the item is safe."

Cordons were in place in the area, and enquiries were underway.
