Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Highlanders v Hurricanes

Follow all the action from Forsyth Barr Stadium, Dunedin.

Brayden Iose scores for the Hurricanes against the Highlanders. Photo: Michael Thomas/Photosport

The Highlanders have had a week off and will be keen to pick up where they left off after winning two games in a row. They face an out of sorts Hurricanes side that's only won one game so far this season, but welcome back a couple of big name All Blacks for this clash under the roof at Forsyth Barr Stadium.

Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
