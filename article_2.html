One dead after motorcycle crash in Waikato

The crash was reported to emergency services around 5.35am.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

A person has died after a motorcycle crash in Waikato on Sunday morning.

The crash on Morrison Road in Pukekawa was reported to emergency services around 5.35am.

There do not appear to have been any other vehicles involved in the crash.

Enquiries into the circumstances of the crash are underway.
