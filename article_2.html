Scrub fire threatens homes in Taupō

Fire and Emergency crews were alerted to the blaze on the southern shores of the lake shortly before midday.

Fire and Emergency crews were alerted to the blaze on the southern shores of the lake shortly before midday (file image). Photo: 123rf

Several homes are under threat from a scrub fire burning in the Taupō suburb of Wharewaka.

Fire and Emergency crews were alerted to the blaze on the southern shores of the lake shortly before midday.

An inital Fire and Emergency report indicated a building was on fire.

Two crews are at the scene working to extinguish the fire.

Police say they were also in attendance, but only to keep the peace between people in the vicinity.

Initial indications suggested the fire was accidental, police said.
