Crews respond to dramatic fire over Coromandel beach

FENZ are responding to a scrub fire sending smoke over sunny beaches in the Coromandel.

Crews are responding to a scrub fire in Kūaotunu. Photo: Supplied

FENZ are responding to a scrub fire sending smoke over sunny beaches in the Coromandel.

The agency said it received multiple calls to a scrub fire in Kūaotunu just before 4pm.

Five fire trucks are in attendance, one helicopter is at the scene and another is on the way.

The blaze is 300 meters by 600 meters in size and is burning through pine slash and regrowth.
