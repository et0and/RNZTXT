NRL live updates: Warriors v Sea Eagles

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Mitchell Barnett in possession. Photo: David Neilson

It's a tough test tonight at home for the Warriors, who are coming off a heavy loss in their opening match only to face the in-from Manly-Warringah Sea Eagles. It should be an interesting experience for former Warrior Jazz Tevaga as he makes his first return to his old club, while interchange forward Tof Sipley is another who starred for the Warriors juniors, before heading across the ditch. Kick-off: 8pm NZT.
