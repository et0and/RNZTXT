NRL preseason live updates: Warriors v Sharks

Follow all the action from Shark Park, Sydney.

Photo: Photosport / Mark Evans

The Warriors are back in action in a trial match against the Sharks, with more than a few interesting position choices for coach Andrew Webster to think over. Top of mind will be the halves in the post-Shaun Johnson era, as well as the best make up of a typically physical forward pack.

Kick-off is at 9:30pm NZT.
