Easter weekend traffic: Auckland motorway blocked

Aucklanders heading south are likely to face heavy traffic on the Southern Motorway from Manukau to Bombay.

File image. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Aucklanders heading south out of the city may face heavy traffic on the southbound lanes of the Southern Motorway from Manukau to Bombay.

Traffic was also heavy around Wellsford, north of Auckland.

Meanwhile, traffic is backed up on the southbound lanes of the Waikato Expressway at Taupiri, just north of Hamilton.
