Freight train hits person at Invercargill level crossing

A freight train has hit a person at Elles Road level crossing in Invercargill.

File picture. Photo: 123rf.com

A freight train has hit a person at Elles Road level crossing in Invercargill.

Police said they were called to the scene near Turnbull Thomson Park at 5.20pm.

St John said they sent one ambulance, an operations manager and a rapid response unit to the scene.

Police have not confirmed whether the person has died.

Elles Road is closed between Tweed and Forth Street while emergency services attend.

KiwiRail said the crossing has flashing lights, warning bells and barrier arms.

They also confirmed the train was only carrying coal at the time and the train driver is being offered support.
