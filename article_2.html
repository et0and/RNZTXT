Live updates: NZ Breakers v Tasmania JackJumpers

The Breakers head into another big match up, this time against the second-placed Tasmania JackJumpers at Spark Arena in Auckland. Catch all the action with RNZ's live blog.

Zylan Cheatham of the Breakers. Photo: Alan Lee / www.photosport.nz

The Breakers head into another big match up, this time against the second-placed Tasmania JackJumpers at Spark Arena in Auckland.

The home side are eighth on the ladder and need a win to stay in finals contention. Coach Mody Maor is promising an improvement after the Breakers' last outing ended in a disappointing 90-79 loss to South East Melbourne.

One positive: the Breakers played one of their best games of the season the last time these sides met, a 97-92 win in Launceston.
