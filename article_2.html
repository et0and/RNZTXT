The must-see looks from the VMAs red carpet

Stars dazzled on the red carpet as they arrived for the MTV Video Music Awards, with Taylor Swift and Post Malone nabbing the most nominations.

Taylor Swift arrives to attend the MTV Video Music Awards at UBS Arena in Elmont, New York, on September 11, 2024. Photo: AFP / Angela Weiss

Stars dazzled on the red carpet in New York as they arrived for the MTV Video Music Awards, with Taylor Swift and Post Malone nabbing the most nominations. Both artists are nominated in nine separate categories.

Among those getting multiple nominations are Eminem, SZA, Chappell Roan, Sabrina Carpenter, and Megan Thee Stallion.
