Watch live: Luxon faces questions as he visits South Island

The prime minister is visiting Ashburton and Timaru and is due to speak to media.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon who is visiting southern areas today is due to hold a briefing in Ashburton.

Luxon will address media after going on a walkabout to chat to residents in the town.

The briefing is due to start at 12.30pm and willl be livestreamed at the top of this page.

The Mid-Canterbury town is hoping for a new bridge and when Transport Minister Simeon Brown visited in April he was repeatedly asked when it might be built.
