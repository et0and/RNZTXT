Live: Auckland FC v Western United

Follow live updates as the Auckland FC take on Western United FC in round nine of the A-League.

Fans at Go Media Stadium, Auckland. Photo: Shane Wenzlick / www.photosport.nz

Auckland FC will be looking to get back to their winning ways when they take on Western United at Go Media Stadium in Auckland tonight.

Their winning run came to an end with a 2-2 draw with Melbourne City last weekend - the first time they had not tasted victory since the A-League season started.

The match starts at 5pm.
