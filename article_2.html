Man dies after being swept down river

The man was pulled ashore but could not be revived, police say.

West Coast police are responding to a report of a person being swept down a river near the town of Rapahoe. Photo: Google Maps

A man is dead after being swept down a river near Greymouth.

Police said they were notified at 10am on Wednesday that a person had been swept away from Davy Creek and towards the ocean, near the town of Rapahoe.

"The man was recovered from the water and brought to shore, where CPR was performed," police said.

A rescue helicopter was deployed.
