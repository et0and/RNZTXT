Weather live: North Island braces for severe winds, snowfall warnings in the south

Gusts are expected to reach 120km/h in some parts of Auckland, made worse with thunderstorms.

Parts of the North Island are bracing for severe winds Wednesday afternoon, with an orange warning in place for Auckland from 3pm.

Gusts are expected to reach 120km/h in some parts, made worse with thunderstorms.

The wind could bring down powerlines, damage trees and unsecured structures, MetService says.

There is an orange snowfall warning in place for the Crown Range Road in the lower South Island.
