Live: 'Extreme fog' halts flights, delays ferries in Auckland

Flights and ferries have been disrupted as heavy fog blankets Auckland, with drivers warned to take care.

Fog is causing flight disruption in Auckland (file photo). Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

Flight have been cancelled and ferries delayed as fog a blanket of fog hangs over Auckland.

Dozens of domestic flights have been cancelled and delayed, while drivers around the city are being warned to take care.

Auckland Transport said delays to all ferry services "due to extreme fog in the harbour" should be expected.
