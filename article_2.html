Live: 22,000 Coromandel homes lose power, lanes closed on Harbour Bridge

Snow closed highways around the South Island as Aucklanders are warned to prepare for possible power cuts amid strong wind moving up the country.

About 22,000 properties across the Coromandel Peninsula were without power for nearly an hour as heavy wind batters the North Island.

Meanwhile, the Auckland Harbour Bridge has had lanes reduced as a result of severe wind gusts.

Earlier, highways across the South Island were closed by heavy snow.
