Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Blues v Brumbies

Follow all the action from Eden Park, Auckland.

AJ Lam makes a break for the Blues in their Super Rugby Pacific semi-final against the Brumbies in Auckland. Photo: photosport

The Blues had a win last weekend over the Hurricanes that should feel like a real relief, as the defending champs now head home to take on the Brumbies. The Australians haven't quite got off to a great start in 2025 with two losses, but have scored plenty of points along the way. Kick off is at 7:35pm NZT.
