Serious injuries after two-vehicle crash in Southland

Emergency services are responding to the crash near Longridge this afternoon.

A sign saying 'road closed'. Photo: Pretoria Gordon / RNZ

Emergency services are responding to a serious two-vehicle crash in Southland this afternoon.

Police were called to the crash near the intersection of Mandeville-Kingston Crossing Road and Dunn and Cody Road at Longridge shortly after 2pm.

Police said: "It appears two people are in a serious and critical condition. The Serious Crash Unit has been advised. The road is closed, and diversions are being put in place."

Fire and Emergency were also at the scene.
