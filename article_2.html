Unexplained death at Auckland home

Police say two people are helping with the investigation following the overnight death.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police are describing the death of a person on Auckland's North Shore overnight as "unexplained".

A police spokesperson says they are investigating the death, which happened in Waiake at about midnight.

They say two people at the Sharon Road address are assisting with the investigation.

A greater police presence is expected in the area with a scene examination on Tuesday.
