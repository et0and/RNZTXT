Sydney shopping centre evacuated after stabbing, shots reports

A shopping centre in Sydney's east has been evacuated after reports of a stabbing inside.

Shots have reportedly been fired in a New South Wales shopping centre. (File photo) Photo: 123RF

A shopping centre in Sydney's east has been evacuated after reports of a stabbing inside.

The ABC has been told by eyewitnesses that shots were fired.

NSW Police says an operation is underway at Westfield Shopping Centre in Bondi Junction, with shoppers being evacuated.
