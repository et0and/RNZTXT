Air NZ flight forced to turn back over engine problem

The airline says flight NZ5166 landed safely after shutting down an engine.

Photo: FlightRadar24

An Air New Zealand flight from Gisborne to Auckland has returned to Gisborne Airport after an engine problem.

Air New Zealand says flight NZ5166 landed safely after shutting down an engine shortly after departure.

Fire and Emergency say two fire crews and two water tankers - sent to the airport in support of airport emergency staff - were stood down just before midday.

An Air New Zealand spokesperson says the matter is being investigated.

The event came after an Air NZ flight from Sydney to New Zealand had to turn back, also over an engine issue.
