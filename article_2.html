Cricket live updates: Black Caps v Pakistan

Follow all the action from Hagley Oval, Christchurch.

Daryl Mitchell of New Zealand plays a shot. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The Black Caps are back in action after an ICC Champions Trophy campaign that saw them make it all the way to the final. They now face a tough Pakistan outfit in the first of five T20s. Play starts at 2:15pm NZT.
