The books and films in the new curriculum

A draft of the new Year 7-13 English curriculum reveals the poetry, novels, short stories, film and plays students will be learning.

Spirited Away is one of the films Year 7 and 8 students may watch. Photo: [STUDIO GHIBLI Spirited Away]

The draft Year 7-13 English curriculum has been released along with a list of suggested texts covering poetry, novels, short stories, film and plays.

How many of the films have you seen or stories have you read?
