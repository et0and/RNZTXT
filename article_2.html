Dixon goes to top of Indycar championship with victory in Detroit

Scott Dixon has won the latest Indycar race in Detroit to go to the top of the championship.

Scott Dixon (#9 Chip Ganassi Racing) Photo: PHOTOSPORT

Scott Dixon has won the latest Indycar race in Detroit to go to the top of the championship.

Fellow New Zealander Marcus Armstrong was third, his first top three finish of his Indycar career.

Dixon a six time Indycar championship winner again put on a master class of tactical driving and saving fuel to win a caution-strewn street race.

"At about the lap 50 I said I think we can make it on no more stops if we do one now.. we didn't go that risky but the team called it perfectly and we were on the right strategy."

It's Dixon's 58th career victory, putting him within nine wins of AJ Foyt's record of 67 career wins.
