Police respond to brawl at Auckland's Newmarket Station

One shopkeeper said she saw someone taken away on a stretcher, but it is not yet clear whether anyone has been seriously injured.

A bench has been taped off at Newmarket Train Station. Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

An area of Auckland's Newmarket Station has been cordoned off by police as emergency services respond to what witnesses said was a fight between a group of young males.

One shopkeeper said she saw someone taken away on a stretcher, but it is not yet clear whether anyone has been seriously injured.

Police gather at Newmarket Station, Auckland on 17 January 2025. Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

An RNZ reporter at the scene said there appeared to be blood on the ground near a bench.
