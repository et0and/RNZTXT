Weather: Blanket of fog delays flights at Auckland Airport

Meanwhile, heavy rain warnings remain in place for Coromandel and Bay of Plenty.

Fog is causing flight disruption in Auckland (file photo). Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

There are flight delays and cancellations at Auckland Airport as fog blankets the area.

Auckland Airport's website shows one flight from Napier have been cancelled and several flights were delayed.

The airport said it was experiencing fog and passengers should check with their airline for the latest flight information.

Meanwhile, orange heavy rain warnings remain in place on Tuesday for Coromandel Peninsula and Bay of Plenty, west of Kawerau.

A yellow heavy rain watch was in force for Bay of Plenty about and east of Kawerau and Gisborne north of Ruatoria, and Tasman District about and west of Motueka.
