Live: Luxon 'open' to sending NZ peacekeeping troops to Ukraine

And the Prime Minister says he wants to lift defence spending to close to 2% of GDP. Audio

The government would be open to sending soldiers as peacekeepers in Ukraine if a ceasefire was reached.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon told Morning Report New Zealand stood by Ukraine.

He said the war there was one of several shifts from rules to power in international relations.
