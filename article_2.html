Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Blues v Crusaders

Follow all the action from Eden Park, Auckland.

Mark Tele'a is tackled by Sevu Reece during the Super Rugby Pacific match, Crusaders v Blues, Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch. Photo: Joseph Johnson/ActionPress
