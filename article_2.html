Formula 1 live updates: Australian Grand Prix

Follow all the action from the Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit as NZ's Liam Lawson is in his first race with the top Red Bull team.

Liam Lawson, centre, for Red Bull Racing at the 2025 Formula 1 Louis Vuitton Australian Grand Prix in Melbourne. Photo: Clay Cross / Photosport

New Zealand's Liam Lawson will be in the hot seat for his first race with the top Red Bull team. This is undoubtedly the biggest moment of the 23-year-old's career so far, however it will be interesting to see just how things pan out in his role behind team mate and four-time world champion Max Verstappen. Racing is scheduled to start at around 5pm NZT.
