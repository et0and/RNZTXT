Live NRL: Warriors take on the Roosters

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Warriors player Wayde Egan during the Warriors v Roosters. Photo: Andrew Cornaga / www.photosport.nz

Are we on a vintage Warriors rollercoaster season again? One heavy loss followed by one impressive win is how it stands so far for Andrew Webster's side. They face a Roosters side that's had a similar start to 2025, scoring a big upset win over the Panthers last weekend. Kick-off is at 8pm NZT.
