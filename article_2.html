New Zealand woman drowns in Cook Islands

The 49-year-old New Zealand woman was on holiday.

File image. Photo: RNZ Pacific/ Lydia Lewis

A 49-year-old New Zealand woman has died in a water based incident in Cook Islands while on holiday.

A Cook Islands police spokesperson says they received an emergency call shortly after 3pm local time (Wednesday).

The woman was responsive when they arrived at Avaavaroa.

The spokesperson told RNZ Pacific the woman was pronounced dead at the hospital.

Investigations into whether the woman was part of a turtle tour when the incident took place were still underway.
