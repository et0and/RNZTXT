Homicide investigation launched after death at Whangārei property

Police arrested a 53-year-old at the address, who was known to the victim.

Emergency services were called to a property on Whangārei's Norfolk Street after man was injured on Saturday night. Photo: Supplied/ Google Maps

A homicide investigation has begun after a man died at a property in Whangārei.

Emergency services responded to a report of a man injured at a Norfolk Street address, just before 5pm Saturday, Detective Senior Sergeant Michelle Harris said.

But the man was unresponsive, and he died at the scene.

Harris said early Sunday morning that no charges had been laid, however officers had arrested a 53-year-old man at the address, who was known to the victim.
