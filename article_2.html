Live football: Auckland FC take on Macarthur

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Ivan Vujica of Macarthur and Logan Rogerson of Auckland FC challenge for the ball during the fifth round of the A-League in Sydney. Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

Auckland FC are back on top of the A-League table and can stay there with a win today, but it won't be easy against fourth placed Macarthur.

The last time they met was a narrow 1-0 win for Auckland, but they're back at home in front of their big supporter base at Mt Smart. Kick-off is at 5pm NZT.
