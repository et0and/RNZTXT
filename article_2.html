Test rugby live updates: Black Ferns v Canada

Follow all the action from Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch.

Photo: PHOTOSPORT

In one of the most compelling games of the Pacific Four Series, the world champion Black Ferns host Canada.

A win for the visitors in the second test match in Christchurch would move them above New Zealand in the rankings, while the Black Ferns could close the gap with number one-ranked England with a victory.
