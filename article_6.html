All Blacks World Cup loss becomes personal for golfer Ryan Fox

The fallout from the All Black world cup loss to the Springboks has become very personal for New Zealand golfer Ryan Fox.

Ryan Fox in Springbok colours. Photo: DP World Tour

The fallout from the All Black world cup loss to the Springboks has become very personal for New Zealand golfer Ryan Fox.

Fox, the son of former All Blacks Grant Fox, has had to wear a Springbok jersey during a golf tournament after losing a bet with his South African caddie.

"This feels like blasphemy to me, to be honest," said Fox who's ranked number 27 in the world.

"We had a little bet on the final, no money at all, but purely pride. If the Springboks won I had to wear this for three holes in the (Sun City) pro-am.

"We all know the result."
