A-League live updates: Auckland FC v Adelaide United

Follow all the action from Go Media Stadium, Auckland.

Auckland FC players celebrate a goal against the Wellington Phoenix. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

It's a top of the table clash in front of another big crowd at Go Media Stadium. Auckland FC hold a commanding eight point lead at the top of the A-League ladder and just destryoed the Wellington Phoenix in their last game, while Adelaide come in having dropped their last two games. Kick-off is at 5pm NZT.
