NRL recap: Sharks pummel Panthers 26-6

The Penrith Panthers are now sitting just one win away from making rugby league history after taking down the Sharks.

Paul Alamoti of the Panthers scores a try. Photo: Getty Images / Mark Metcalfe

Defending champs Penrith Panthers are now sitting just one win away from making rugby league history - winning four consecutive premierships, a feat that has never been achieved during the NRL era.

Standing in their way was an extremely resilient Cronulla Sharks side who have been on a rollercoaster ride all season long - but Penrith took them down 26-6 at Accor Stadium in Sydney.
