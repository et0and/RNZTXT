Warriors name side for NRL opener in Las Vegas

The round one clash against the Raiders, the Warriors first visit to Las Vegas, kicks off at 1pm on Sunday.

Roger Tuivasa-Sheck of the Warriors claims the ball against the Canberra Raiders. Photo: photosport

Warriors vs Raiders

1pm Sunday March 2

Allegiant Stadium, Las Vegas

Live Blog Updates on RNZ

The Warriors have named their side of the round one opener in Las Vegas against the Raiders.

Charnze Nicoll-Klokstad has maintained his hold on the number one jersey, with Roger Tuivasa-Sheck shifting from the centres to the wing.

Chanel Harris-Tavita and Luke Metcalf will combine for the first halves pairing of the season, with Te Maire Martin named on an extended bench.

Taine Tuaupiki has been named on the wing in the wake of Dallin Watene-Zelezniak's wrist injury.

Up front, co-skipper Mitch Barnett and James Fisher-Harris will prop the scrum, while Wayde Eagan takes the hooker role.

Erin Clark has been named to make his Warriors debut return at lock.

Coach Andrew Webster said his side has done well not to be distracted by the glitz of Las Vegas.

"We want to complete, if you do that you go a long way to winning the game. People tell me Vegas is a bit of a finals atmosphere, so I expect it to be really physical."
