Recap: Dragons trounce Warriors 30-12

A late try and goal proved too little, too late for the Warriors in Wollongong.

Addin Fonua-Blake of the Warriors goes in for a try. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

The Warriors travelled across the ditch to Wollongong this weekend, taking on the resurgent St George-Illawarra Dragons outfit with both sides being in a similar position on the table.

They were looking to back up last Saturday's epic performance, which resulted in a 22-all draw against the Sea Eagles - but were thoroughly burnt.
