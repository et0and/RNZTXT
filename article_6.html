Live: Rain delays White Ferns v Australia opener

The T20 world champions are back on home soil to play Australia in three one-dayers. Follow all the action here

White Ferns celebrate their T20 World Cup victory at Dubai. Photo: AFP / GIUSEPPE CACACE

The first Rose Bowl one day cricket international between the White Ferns and Australia has not started on time in Wellington.

Rain has kept the covers on the Basin Reserve wicket.

New Zealand last held the Rose Bowl trophy in 1999.

Games two and three will also be played in Wellington on Saturday and Monday.
