England defeat Silver Ferns in second test

The Silver Ferns have now lost their last three tests against England.

Silver Ferns Grace Nweke with England Francesca Williams during game 2 of the Taini Jamison Trophy Photo: Marty Melville

The equation for the Silver Ferns tonight is a straight forward one: Must win.

Dame Noeline Taurua said her players are not shying away from the challenge of the do or die situation, after dropping the first test 59-58 on Sunday night.
