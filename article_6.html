In photos: Sold-out crowd for Phoenix semi-final

The stands were a sea of yellow as fans cheered on their team - and shared in their loss. Here are some of the best pictures from the night.

Phoenix fans show their excitement ahead of the teams taking the field. Photo: Photosport / Marty Melville

A sold-out crowd packed Wellington's Sky Stadium to watch the Phoenix chase their dream of a Football A-League grand final.

It was the first time in the club's 17-year history that they faced the prospect of getting into the final - but it was not to be, with the game ending in a 2-1 loss to Melbourne Victory.

The stands were a sea of yellow as fans cheered on the team and shared in their loss.
