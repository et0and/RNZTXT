Recap: White Ferns v Australia one-dayer, third match

Australia have won the third and final one day cricket international against New Zealand by 75 runs.

Australia have won the third and final one day cricket international against New Zealand by 75 runs.

Chasing 291 for victory, the White Ferns were all out for 215 in the 44th over at the Basin Reserve.

Suzie Bates top scored for New Zealand with 53 runs and Alana King was the best of the Australian bowlers with three wickets for 34 runs.
