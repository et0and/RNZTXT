Cricket: White Ferns level T20 series against Sri Lanka

New Zealand won the toss, before chasing down Sri Lanka's target with nine balls remaining.

Georgia Plimmer drives during the White Ferns' match against Sri Lanka at the Women's T20 World Cup in Sharjah, October 2024. Photo: Photosport

The White Ferns have won the second match in their T20 series against Sri Lanka in the international double-header at Hagley Park in Christchurch on Sunday.

The Sri Lankans won the opening match on Friday night and the series will now be decided by the third match in Dunedin on Tuesday.

The White Ferns won by seven wickets today, after winning the toss and restricting Sri Lanka to 113 for seven.
