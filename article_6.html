Cricket: England cruises to big victory

England beat New Zealand by 323 runs to secure an unassailable 2-0 lead in the three-match series.

Devon Conway heads back to the dressing room on the third day of the second cricket test between New Zealand and England at the Basin Reserve, Wellington. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

England beat New Zealand by 323 runs after tea on the third day of the second test in Wellington on Sunday to secure an unassailable 2-0 lead in the three-match series.

Captain Ben Stokes took 3-5 for England, while Tom Blundell was New Zealand's highest scorer with 115.
