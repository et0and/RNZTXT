Test cricket live updates: Black Caps v England day 2

Follow all the action from Hagley Oval, Christchurch.

Glenn Phillips batting on the first day of the first cricket test between New Zealand and England at Hagley Oval in Christchurch. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

The Black Caps start day two of the first test 319/8, due to an Engllish rally late yesterday that saw three wickets fall in the last session. Glenn Phillips and Tim Southee are coming out to resume the innings at 11am NZT.
