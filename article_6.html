UFC 304: Belal Muhammad snatches welterweight title

Muhammad beat Leon Edwards by unanimous decision in their main event bout in Manchester, England.

Belal Muhammad is announced the new welterweight champion at UFC 304 in Manchester, England. Photo: Screenshot / X

Belal Muhammad has stolen the welterweight title from Leon Edwards, winning by unanimous decision in their main event bout at UFC 304 in Manchester, England.

Muhammad (23-3-0) and Edwards (22-4-0) had met back in 2021, but that fight ended in a no contest because Edwards accidentally poked Muhammad in the eye.

But on Saturday (UK time), the two went the distance, with Muhammad taking the cards 48-47, 48-47, 49-46 to snag the welterweight belt.

Tom Aspinall (15-3-0) and Curtis Blaydes (18-5-0) were also in a championship fight, and Aspinall was able to defend his heavyweight title by knocking out Blaydes in the first round.

Other winners Saturday included lightweight Paddy Pimblett (22-3-0), middleweight Gregory Rodrigues (16-5-0) and featherweight Arnold Allen (20-3-0).
