Recap: Blues Women win Super Rugby Aupiki final

The Blues Women went from 18-5 down to storm the gates and beat Chiefs Manawa at Eden Park.

Katelyn Vahaakolo of the Blues during the Super Rugby Aupiki Final rugby match between the Blues and the Chiefs Manawa at Eden Park. Photo: David Rowland / Photosport

The Blues Women went from 18-5 down to storm the gates and beat Chiefs Manawa, snatching the Super Rugby Aupiki title 24-18 in wet and slippery conditions in the final at Eden Park.
