Super Rugby Aupiki live updates: Hurricanes Poua v Blues

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

Blues wing Katelyn Vaha'akolo on the attack. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

Super Rugby Aupiki opens its weekend with a clash between the defending champion Blues and new look Hurricanes Poua sides. There's more than just domestic glory on the line as players from both teams will be gunning for spots in the Black Ferns World Cup plans later in the year, Kick-off is at 4:35pm.
