Olympics 2024: Oly Whites open Olympic campaign with win

The Oly Whites have beaten Guinea 2-1 in their opening group game at the Olympics.

The Oly Whites have beaten Guinea 2-1 in their opening group game at the Olympics.

Captain Matt Garbett missed a penalty early in the game but responded with a goal after 25 minutes.

Guinea scored the equaliser after 72 minutes, before Ben Waine grabbed the winner two minutes later.

All Whites captain Matthew Garbett celebrates his goal. Photo: Raghavan Venugopal

New Zealand's next group game is against the United States on Monday morning.

After the match, Garbett told Sky Sport he was happy with the team's efforts.

"I thought the first half was excellent. We had a few chances and could have gone two or three nil up. I missed a penalty unfortunately but straight after luckily we got that first goal.

The second half I think we showed that New Zealand character to dig deep and get stuck in and fight it out to the last minute. I'm very proud of to lead out the boys for the first time and get the win."
