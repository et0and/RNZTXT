Auckland to no longer host SailGP

SailGP has made the decision to not race in Auckland next year.

The SailGP F50 catamaran fleet in action on Lyttelton Harbour 2023. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The unavailability of a spectator facility has seen Auckland withdraw from hosting SailGP in March 2024.

Wynyard Point land as a spectator facility was part of SailGP's original vision for the sailing competition.

However, its unavailability proved to be a barrier to holding the event, leaving SailGP needing a new venue.

"SailGP continues to work with its partners, including relevant local and central government agencies, to progress planning for its season four New Zealand event 23-24 March 2024," they said in a statement.

The SailGP event is the second in a four-year deal to host alternative regattas Lyttelton and Auckland.

Thousands of spectators turned out earlier this year to watch the racing at Christchurch's Lyttelton Harbour.
