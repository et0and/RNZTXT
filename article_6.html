As it happened: Brumbies take down Crusaders, 31-24

Follow all the action from GIO Stadium, Canberra.

Fergus Burke of the Crusaders during the Super Rugby Pacific Rugby match, Crusaders Vs Brumbies, at Orangetheory Stadium, Christchurch, New Zealand, 24th March 2023. Photo: John Davidson / Photosport

Can the Crusaders salvage something out of their miserable 2024 season? The one ray of hope is their very impressive record in Canberra, having not lost there in 20 years, but clearly history hasn't counted for much for the Crusaders lately. Kick-off is at 4.35pm NZT.
