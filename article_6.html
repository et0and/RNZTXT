Melbourne Victory to head to A-League grand final

Phoenix failed to keep Victory from scoring again after Oskar Zawada managed an equaliser in the final moments.

Bozhidar Kraev, Finn Surman, Scott Wootton and Alex Rufer of the block a free kick. Photo: Photosport / Scott Barbour

The Wellington Phoenix's A-League season has come to an end with a extra time 2-1 loss to Melbourne Victory in their semi-final second leg game in Wellington.

The Victory went ahead against the run of play in the 61st minute, and were set for the win until Phoenix substitute Oskar Zawada grabbed the equaliser with the last kick of regulation time.

The visitors then went ahead again in the first-half of extra time, and while the Phoenix continued to press and have shots on goal, they couldn't find a second goal.
