USA beats Pakistan at T20 Cricket World Cup in shock win

In a shock win, the United States have beaten Pakistan at the T20 Cricket World Cup being held in the US and West Indies.

USA's Harmeet Singh during the ICC men's Twenty20 World Cup 2024 group A cricket match between the USA and Pakistan at the Grand Prairie Cricket Stadium in Grand Prairie, Texas, on 6 June, 2024. Photo: AFP

In a shock win, the United States have beaten Pakistan at the T20 Cricket World Cup being held in the US and West Indies.

The hosts won the match in a super over, after the scores were tied after the regulation 20 overs.
