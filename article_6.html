Warriors claim victory in Shaun Johnson's final game

In a close-run contest, the Warriors pipped the Sharks 30-28 in Sydney.

Shaun Johnson goes up against the Cronulla Sharks in Sydney. Photo: Photosport / David Neilson

It was the end of the road for the Warriors' troubled NRL season, as departing Shaun Johnson played his last match.

Johnson ran out for one last time to take on his former club the Cronulla Sharks in Sydney.

The Warriors were playing for nothing but pride as their finals hopes were dashed weeks ago.

After trailing the Australian side going into halftime, the Warriors fought back to win the game 30-28.
