Bledisloe Cup: All Blacks beat Wallabies 33-13

The All Black have beaten their Wellington curse, defeating the Wallabies 33-13.

Sevu Reece of New Zealand evades a tackle from Len Ikitau of Australia. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

The All Black have beaten their Wellington curse, defeating the Wallabies 33-13.

They had not won in the capital since 2018 and face a Wallabies team that pushed them all the way until the 80th minute last weekend.

Scott Robertson has made a few changes to his side that won 31-28 in Sydney, with this test being seen as a pretty important warm-up for the end of year tour next month.
