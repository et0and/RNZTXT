Women's Super Smash final live updates: Wellington v Otago

Follow all the action from the Basin Reserve, Wellington.

Blaze captain Melie Kerr (L) with her sister Jess Kerr. Photo: Marty Melville/Photosport

Wellington and Otago do battle today to find the women's domestic T20 champion for the summer, with the Blaze coming through a tight elimination semi yesterday against Northern Districts. The Sparks have qualified top, but Wellington have home advantage thanks to the entire finals weekend beuing held at the Basin. Play starts at 12:40pm.
