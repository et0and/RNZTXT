Pulse defeat Steel to win Robyn Broughton Legacy Trophy

Follow the latest ANZ Premiership action from Invercargill.

Georgia and Kate Heffernan are in action for the Steel tonight. Photo: Alan Lee / www.photosport.nz

The Central Pulse have beaten the Southern Steel in their ANZ Premiership clash in Invercargill tonight.

The teams were playing for the Robyn Broughton Legacy Trophy for the first time, honouring a woman who coached both teams during a decorated domestic career.
