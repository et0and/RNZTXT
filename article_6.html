Springboks smash Wallabies 30-12

The Springboks have built on their dominant 33-7 win last weekend in Brisbane with another triumph.

Jesse Kriel (left) of the Springboks and Tom Wright (right) of the Wallabies contest for the ball. Photo: Darren England/Photosport

The world champion Springboks have built on their dominant 33-7 win last weekend in Brisbane, triumphing over the Wallabies 30-12 in Perth.

The All Blacks will face both sides in the coming weeks.
