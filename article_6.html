Super Rugby live updates: Moana Pasifika v Force

Follow all the action from HBF Park, in Perth.

Julian Savea of Moana Pasifika. Photo: Bianca De Marchi / www.photosport.nz

Ardie Savea debuts as captain of Moana Pasifika tonight in Perth, leading a new look side in what's shaping up to be a defining season for Super Rugby Pacific's ambitious expansion team.

The Force have made a few off season moves of their own, including Wallaby inside back duo Nic White and Ben Donaldson, so Savea's first outing should be a tough one.

Kick-off is at 9:35pm Saturday, NZT.
