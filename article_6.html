Live updates: NZ Breakers v Adelaide 36ers

Join us for all the NBL action as the Breakers try and get their season back on track.

NZ Breakers Parker Jackson-Cartwright Photo: Brett Phibbs/ Photosport

The NZ Breakers are in a 3-7 hole as we near the halfway point of the 2023-24 NBL season and head into round 9 hosting the 4-7 Adelaide 36ers at Wolfbrook Arena in Christchurch.

Coach Mody Maor has enjoyed a 10 day break following the disappointing 69-65 home defeat to the Illawarra Hawks, while the 36ers are coming off a 96-93 win over South East Melbourne Phoenix.
