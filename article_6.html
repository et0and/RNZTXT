Cricket recap: Black Caps overcome Pakistan in 10 overs

Relive all the action of the Black Caps victory from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

Black Caps celebrate a wicket Photo: Photosport

The Black Caps have ended the five match T20 series against Pakistan on a high, having already sealed it with a win on Sunday night.

After New Zealand won the toss and chose to bowl, Pakistan could only muster 128 for 9 in the first innings, with James Neesham taking 5 for 22 from his four overs.

The Black Caps took just 10 overs to reach that target, with the loss of only two wickets.

Tim Seifert top scored for NZ, with 97 runs off 38 balls, finishing the match with three successive sixes.
