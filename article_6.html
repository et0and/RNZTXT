Recap: Black Caps best Sri Lanka by eight runs in first T20 match

The NZ men fought back against the touring Lions in cricket's shortest format at Tauranga.

One-day captain Mitch Santner in action for the Black Caps. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The Black Caps have bested Sri Lanka by eight runs in the first T20 in Mount Maunganui.

Sri Lanka elected to bowl when they won the toss.

The Black Caps put forward more of a full-strength team for this domestic showpiece, starting Saturday at Mt Maunganui's Bay Oval.

The second encounter is scheduled for the same venue on Monday.
