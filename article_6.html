NRL live updates: Warriors v Raiders

Follow all the action from Allegiant Stadium, Las Vegas.

Luke Metcalf dives in to score. Photo: David Neilson

Is it their year? We'll have a good idea after the opening game of the Warriors season, which comes to us from the magnificent Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas. They face a hungry Raiders side that's keen to get their season off to the best possible start, in front of thousands of Kiwi and Aussie fans who've made the trip over. Kick-off is at 1pm NZT.
