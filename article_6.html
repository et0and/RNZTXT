Hurricanes defeat Rebels in Super Rugby Pacific quarter-final

Hurricanes have secured their spot in the semi-finals, beating out the Rebels 47-20 in Wellington.

The Hurricanes celebrate a try. Photo: Photosport / Elias Rodriguez

The Hurricanes have secured their spot in the semi-finals, beating out the Rebels in Wellington.

At full-time, the score stood at 47-20.

Hurricanes captain Jordie Barrett said the Rebels have always been a joy to play against.

"We wish them all the best in whatever direction they go.

"We had a great week, everyone is contributing at the moment which is pleasing but it's not going to get any easier."
