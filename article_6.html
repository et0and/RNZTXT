The best photos from Day 10 of the Games

There was drama and upsets aplenty on the final day of the artistic gymnastics competition.

Brazil's Rebeca Andrade celebrates after winning gold in the women's floor event. Photo: GABRIEL BOUYS/ AFP

There was drama and upsets aplenty on the final day of the artistic gymnastics event.

Brazil's Rebeca Andrade won the women's floor event, with hot favourite Simone Biles taking the silver.

Earlier in the beam, Biles fell off and was out of contention - leaving room for Italy's Alice D'amato to take gold.

Elsewhere, Poland women gave Spiderman a run for his money in the speed climbing competition, and three Kiwis are through to the final of the women's pole vault.
