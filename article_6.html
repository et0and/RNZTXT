A record tenth wicket partnership has helped Australia post 383 in their first innnings of the first cricket test against New Zealand in Wellington.

Starting day two at 279-9, Cameron Green and Josh Hazelwood put on 116 for the last wicket - a record for Australia against New Zealand.

Josh Hazelwood was the last batter out making 22 with Cameron Green finishing unbeaten on 174.

Matt Henry was the most successful of the New Zealand bowlers with 5 for 70.

Matt Henry Photo: Kerry Marshall / www.photosport.nz

Will O'Rourke and Scott Kuggeleijn took 2-87 and 2-70 respectively while Rachin Ravindra took 1-24.

There were 41 extras in Australia's total which will be of concern to the Black Caps with 20 of these being wides.
