Super Rugby Pacific live: Moana Pasifika v Highlanders

Follow all the action from North Harbour Stadium, Albany.

Moana Pasifika mid fielder Danny Toala tackles Highlanders centre Fetuli Paea. Photo: Brett Phibbs / PhotoSport

Moana Pasifika have been great at scoring tries but not so great at defending them, meanwhile the Highlanders were just great all round last weekend in their upset win over the Blues. This one promises to be a high-scoring encounter as Moana take the field in their new home, kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
