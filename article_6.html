16 All Blacks return for final round of NPC

A large contingent of All Blacks are set to get some game time in NPC rugby this weekend

A large contingent of All Blacks are set to get some game time in NPC rugby this weekend

Sixteen members of the All Blacks squad will represent their provincial unions in the final round-robin stage of the competition.

Halfback Cam Roigard is also set to return to rugby after rupturing the patella tendon in his knee during the Super Rugby competition in March.

He is down to play for Counties Manukau along with Dalton Papali'i when they take on Manawatū on Saturday.

Tasman will be boosted by the return of Ethan Blackadder, Noah Hoffman and David Havili for their Ranfurly Shield defence against Taranaki in Nelson on Sunday.

Stephen Perofeta is eyeing a return from his calf injury to assist Taranaki.

Five teams are still battling for the last two spots in the NPC quarter-finals.

The All Blacks squad for the end of year tour will be named on Monday.
