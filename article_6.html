As it happened: Shock win for Crusaders over Blues

The Crusaders' win over old rivals the Blues in Christchurch has kept their slim hopes of a play-off spot a reality.

Noah Hotham celebrates his try with Codie Taylor, Sevu Reece and Dallas McLeod of the Crusaders during the Super Rugby Pacific match, Crusaders Vs Blues, at the Apollo Projects Stadium in Christchurch. Photo: Photosport

The Crusaders' tight win over old rivals the Blues in Christchurch has kept their slim hopes of a play-off spot a reality.

The hosts managed to pull through 29-27 on Saturday night at Apollo Projects Stadium.
