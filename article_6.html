Super Rugby Aupiki live updates: Hurricanes Poua v Chiefs Manawa

Follow all the action from the NZCIS Ground in Upper Hutt.

The Hurricanes Poua are officially out of the running for the Aupiki finals, but the Manawa desperately need a bonus point win and then a result next weekend if they want to challenge the Blues in the final. Kick-off is at 4:35pm NZT.
