Cricket recap: Stunning Phillips catch gives Black Caps much needed breakthrough

Nathan Smith has picked up two wickets in the final over before lunch as England chase 348.

Nathan Smith celebrates the wicket of Jacob Bethell on the second day of the first cricket test between New Zealand and England at Hagley Oval in Christchurch. Photo: Chris Symes / www.photosport.nz

The Black Caps start day two of the first test 319/8, due to an Engllish rally late yesterday that saw three wickets fall in the last session. Glenn Phillips and Tim Southee are coming out to resume the innings at 11am NZT.
