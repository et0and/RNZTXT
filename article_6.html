Recap: All Blacks scrape out win over England

The All Blacks have made a winning start to new coach Scott Robertson's tenure - but only just.

Tupou Vaa'i is tackled by England's Sam Underhill at Forsyth Barr Stadium in Dunedin on 6 July, 2024. Photo: AFP/SANKA VIDANAGAMA

The All Blacks have made a winning start to new coach Scott Robertson's tenure but only just, beating England 16-15 in front of a sold out crowd in Dunedin.

The hosts were far from flawless but the one-point victory means they retain the Hillary Shield with a test to spare.
