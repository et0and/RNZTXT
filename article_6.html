Six Nations live updates: England v France

Follow all the action from Twickenham, London.

Ben Earl of England hands off Leo Barre of France. Photo: ©INPHO/Dave Winter

It's round two of the Six Nations, with England in desperate times already after going down to Ireland last weekend. A loss today would be their eighth in a row against top ranked opposition, and it certainly won't be easy against a red-hot French side that demolished Wales. Kick-off is at 5:45am NZT.
