Super Rugby Aupiki live updates: Chiefs Manawa v Matatū

Follow all the action from FMG Stadium Waikato, Hamilton.

Luka Connor of Chiefs Manawa in action during the 2023 Super Rugby Aupiki Final against Matatu at FMG Stadium, Hamilton. Photo: Jeremy Ward / PhotoSport

Super Rugby Aupiki kicks off in Hamilton with a couple of new look Manawa and Matatū sides. There's more than just domestic glory on the line as players from both teams will be gunning for spots in the Black Ferns World Cup plans later in the year, Kick-off is at 2:05pm.
