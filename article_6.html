A-League live updates: Phoenix v Melbourne City

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

Isaac Hughes, second from right, celebrates after scoring a goal for the Wellington Phoenix against Brisbane Roar during the A-League men's match at Sky Stadium, Wellington, 6 Feb, 2025. Photo: Photosport

Can the Phoenix salvage something from their disappointing season and make a charge towards the playoffs? They're currently sitting 11th and need to get the full three points against sixth-placed Melbourne City. Kick-off is at 7:30pm NZT.
