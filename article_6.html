Super Rugby Aupiki live updates: Chiefs Manawa vs Blues Women

Follow all the action from FMG Stadium Waikato, Hamilton.

Katelyn Vahaakolo of the Blues during the Super Rugby Aupiki Final rugby match between the Blues and the Chiefs Manawa at Eden Park. Photo: David Rowland / Photosport

The two favoured teams to make the Aupiki final meet again, this time in Chiefs country. The Blues won the last match between the two with a last minute try a fortnight ago, will we see another thrilling encounter? Kick-off is at 4:35pm NZT.
