Live: Rain delay as White Ferns take on Sri Lanka in decider

The New Zealand side look to take the 3-match series as they bat first in Dunedin.

Rain has delayed the start of the T20 decider between the White Ferns and Sri Lanka after the visitors chose to field.

The White Ferns levelled the three-match series with a comfortable seven-wicket win at Hagley Oval in Christchurch on Sunday.

Tuesday's game, in Dunedin, is the opening match in the double-header at the ground, with the Black Caps playing Pakistan in the third of a five T20 match.
