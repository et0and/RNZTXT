A-League football live updates: Wellington Phoenix v Melbourne Victory

Follow all the action from AAMI Park, Melbourne.

Kosta Barbarouses of the Phoenix, up against Melbourne Victory's Roderick Miranda in 2024, during the A-League men's semi final 1, at Sky Stadium. Photo: Photosport

The Phoenix are almost at the playing-for-pride stage of the season, currently sitting 10th and with a tough battle against the sixth-placed Victory.

A win will only move them up one place on the table but it's a big one for the home side, who could climb as high as third.

Kick-off is at 9:35pm Friday, NZT.
