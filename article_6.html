Cricket live updates: Black Caps v England, test 3 day 3

The covers are still on the Seddon Park as New Zealand wait to resume their second innings.

New Zealand's Glenn Phillips, Will Young, Tom Latham and Daryl Mitchell celebrate the wicket of England's Joe Root on the second day of the third cricket test at Seddon Park in Hamilton, 15 December, 2024. Photo: Photosport

Play hasn't started on time on day three of the third cricket Test between New Zealand and England in Hamilton.

The covers are still on the Seddon Park wicket because of rain.

When play does start New Zealand will resume their second innings on 136 for three, a lead of 340 runs.
