Wellington through to Women's Super Smash final

The Wellington Blaze have made the Super Smash women's cricket final.

Carol Agafili of the Northern Brave. Photo: John Davidson / www.photosport.nz

The Wellington Blaze have made the Super Smash women's cricket final after beating the Northern Brave by 23 runs in Wellington today.

The Blaze will play the Otago Sparks in tomorrow's final at the Basin Reserve.
