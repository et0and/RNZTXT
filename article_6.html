Recap: Wellington Phoenix vs Central Coast Mariners FC

The Central Coast Mariners have beaten the Wellington Phoenix 2:1 in a hard fought game in New South Wales. See how it all went down on RNZ's play-by-play blog.

Angel Torres of the Mariners is tackled by Matt Sheridan of The Phoenix during the A-League Men Round 23 match between the Central Coast Mariners and Wellington Phoenix at Industree Group Stadium in Gosford Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

The Central Coast Mariners have beaten the Wellington Phoenix 2:1 in a hard fought game in New South Wales.

Going into Saturday's match the Phoenix held a three-point lead at the top of the A-League ladder, with four rounds remaining, pitted against their challengers for the Premiers Plate.

The two sides last met on Waitangi Day at Sky Stadium, with the match finishing in a scoreless stalemate. Three of the last four matches between the two sides have finished in draws.
