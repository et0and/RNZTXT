Super Rugby Aupiki live updates: Chiefs Manawa v Hurricanes Poua

Follow all the action from FMG Stadium Waikato, Hamilton.

Renee Holmes in action during the round one Super Rugby Aupiki match between Chiefs Manawa and Hurricanes Poua. Photo: Michael Bradley

Both sides are looking to bounce back after disappointing losses last weekend, the Manawa getting pipped by the Blues and the Poua on the wrong end of a big scoreline in Christchurch. Kick-off is at 4:35pm NZT.
