Live updates: Warriors v Bulldogs

The Bulldogs are edging in front heading towards the final whistle. Follow RNZ's live updates.

The game is the last for retiring Shaun Johnson. Photo: Photosport

The Warriors ran out in front of their home crowd for the last time in 2024 to face the Bulldogs at Mt Smart Stadium at 8pm on Friday.

The game is the last at The Fortress for retiring Shaun Johnson, along with Addin Fonua-Blake and Jazz Tevaga who both depart the club at the end of the season.

With their own season all but over, the Warriors are looking to upset the Bulldogs, who currently sit fifth on the NRL ladder and are charging toward the finals.
