Defending champ Coco Gauff advances to final four

The first two semi finalists have been decided at the ASB Tennis Classic in Auckland.

Coco Gauff of the USA in her match against Varvara Gracheva from France. Photo: Brett Phibbs/www.photosport.nz

The first two semi finalists have been decided at the ASB Tennis Classic in Auckland.

The defending champion and top seed Coco Gauff is through to the final four after a straight sets win over France's Vavara Gracheva.

Gauff was untroubled in advancing 6-1, 6-1 and is still yet to drop a set at the tournament.

She will play fourth seed and compatriot Emma Navaro for a place in the final.

Navaro also won her quarter final in straight sets, beating Croatia's Petra Martic 6-4, 6-3.
