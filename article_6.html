Cricket recap: Black Caps set England massive total in third test

England need a total of 658 for victory in the third cricket Test in Hamilton.

New Zealand's Glenn Phillips, Will Young, Tom Latham and Daryl Mitchell celebrate the wicket of England's Joe Root on the second day of the third cricket test at Seddon Park in Hamilton, 15 December, 2024. Photo: Photosport

The Black Caps have set England a total of 658 for victory in the third cricket Test in Hamilton.

New Zealand were dismissed for 453 in their second innings late on day three.

Kane Williamson top scored for New Zealand with 156. England was 18 for two at stumps at 7.30pm.
