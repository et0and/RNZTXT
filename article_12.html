It's back! Sign up for our free daily newsletter

Engaging and unmissable stories, handpicked by our editors and delivered to your inbox!

Photo: RNZ

Today we are relaunching our daily news email, Ngā Pitopito Korero.

After an extended vacation, our curated newsletter is returning, to bring you a roundup of the best of RNZ's content every weekday.

In Ngā Pitopito Korero, you'll get engaging and unmissable stories from New Zealand and around the world, handpicked by our editors and delivered to your inbox (for free!) at 4.30pm. This new send time will ensure we're delivering you the most up-to-date information, with plenty of time to enjoy some of our special features in the evening.

You'll also get recommendations on what to watch, eat, wear and listen from our Life and Style team. Plus, every now and then, you'll get extra content from our shows like Music101, 30 with Guyon Espiner and our award-winning Podcast team.
