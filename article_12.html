Winners of the Welly Hospo Awards named

The winners of a Wellington awards night set up days after the Felix Wellington Hospitality Awards were postponed have been released.

Graze won the outstanding restaurant category, while Dee's Place took the bar category and Parrotdog Bar was the best outstanding regional venue. File picture. Photo: 123rf

The winners of a Wellington awards night set up days after the Felix Wellington Hospitality Awards were postponed have been released.

Felix organisers said after a challenging year in hospo, it made the sad and difficult decision to postpone the awards night until 2025.

After the announcement a small group of Wellington hospitality owners got together to organise The Welly Hospo Awards which saw them raise over $10,000 via a Pledge Me campaign in under 24 hours to hold the event.

It was estimated that between suppliers, makers, producers, brewers, distributors, restaurants, and the physical award makers the event received $60,000 in support.

The awards were held this evening at Foxglove and the winners were voted on by the industry.

A legacy award was given to Scott Kennedy and Malcolm Vaughan, better known as Scotty and Mal of S & M.

They have been in Wellington's hospitality scene for over 25 years and have owned multiple hospitality venues that provide community for Wellington's queer scene.
