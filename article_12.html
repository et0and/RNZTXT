Police appeal for information about missing Chinese resident

Yamei Chen, 78, was last seen at her home in the Auckland neighbourhood of Clendon on Sunday.

Yamei Chen Photo: Supplied

Police are seeking help from the public in finding Chinese resident Yamei Chen, who has been reported missing.

The 78-year-old was last seen at her home on Moncrieff Ave in the South Auckland neighbourhood of Clendon at around 10am on Sunday.

Chen does not speak English, and her family and police have expressed concern about her safety.

Anyone who has information about her whereabouts is asked to contact police on 105, quoting file number 240819/4552.
