Gold for New Zealand in the women's rugby sevens

Paris Essentials - Stacey Waaka and Kendra Cocksedge talk to RNZ in wake of New Zealand's first gold medals.

Photo: CARL DE SOUZA/AFP

Follow and listen to Paris Essentials every day during the 2024 Olympic Games on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, iHeart, YouTube Music or whereever you get your podcasts.

The Black Ferns Sevens team has won gold at the Paris Olympics.

They beat Canada 19-12 in a tense, tight final to claim back to back sevens titles at the Olympic Games.

It was New Zealand's first medal at the Paris Olympics.

Stacey Waaka and Kendra Cocksedge both talked to RNZ in the wake of the win.

We also hear about the water quality of the River Seine ahead of a possible triathlon today, and from the nana of kayaker Finn Butcher.
