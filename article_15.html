Country Life: Fleecing the flock all in the name of charity

Dozens of shearers slipped on their moccasins recently to sheer hundreds of sheep to raise funds for charity.

Dozens of shearers slipped on their moccasins recently for the Shear For Life charity shearing event at Hinds last weekend.

The community event raised $150,000 for the Ashburton Cancer Society and Hospice Mid Canterbury.

Country Life went along to watch the action.
