New Zealand's most golden Olympic Games come to a close

Paris Essentials: Ben Strang looks back on our best-ever effort at the Olympics.in RNZ's final Games podcast

Photo: AFP/SEBASTIEN BOZON

Follow and listen to Paris Essentials every day during the 2024 Olympic Games on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, iHeart, YouTube Music or wherever you get your podcasts.

Ellesse Andrews and Ally Wollaston secured gold and bronze on the last day of the Paris Olympics to sew up New Zealand's best ever effort at the Olympics.

New Zealand ended with 10 gold medals, a new record surpassing the 1984 Los Angeles Olympics.

And their 20 total medals was equal with the Tokyo Olympics in 2021, but with more gold in the mix.
