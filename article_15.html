The best photos from the ninth day of the Paris Games

Tennis greats Novak Djokovic and Carlos Alcaraz clash and Ukraine wins its first gold. Here's all the action.

Lea Meyer from Germany in the women's 3000m steeplechase. Photo: AFP/MICHAEL KAPPELER

Tennis greats Novak Djokovic and Carlos Alcaraz clashed and Ukraine won its first gold medal of the Paris Olympics in an action-packed ninth day of the Games.

Here are some of the best pictures.

New Zealander Lauren Bruce during the hammer throw qualifiers. Photo: AFP/ANDREJ ISAKOVIC

Ukraine's Olga Kharlan, right, goes up against South Korea's Jeon Eun-hye in the women's sabre team gold medal bout. Photo: AFP/FRANCK FIFE

Olga Kharlan dances with the Ukrainian flag after her team won the gold. Photo: AFP/FABRICE COFFRINI

New Zealander Ryan Fox chips a shot off the edge of the water on day four of the men's golf competition. Photo: AFP/EMMANUEL DUNAND

Denmark's Viktor Axelsen plays a shot to India's Lakshya Sen in their men's singles badminton semi-final match. Photo: AFP/ANTONIN THUILLIER

Germany's Isabell Werth, riding Wendy, in the individual dressage final. Photo: DPA/ROLF VENNENBERND

The sailing gets underway in race five of the mixed double-handed dinghy competition in Marseille. Photo: AFP/NICOLAS TUCAT

Cyclists ride in a peloton during the women's road race through the streets of Paris. Photo: AFP/LI YIBO

Serbia's Novak Djokovic serves to Spain's Carlos Alcaraz during their men's singles final tennis match at Roland Garros. Photo: AFP/PATRICIA DE MELO MOREIRA

Belgium's Alexander Hendrickx during the hockey quarterfinal between Belgium's national team the Red Lions and Spain. Photo: AFP/DIRK WAEM
