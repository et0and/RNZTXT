Live: Looting as ring of fire tightens around Los Angeles

California continues to battle several wildfires that have left at least five people dead and more than 180,000 evacuated.

A beach house is engulfed in flames as the Palisades Fire burns along Pacific Coast Highway in Malibu, California, on 8 January 2025. Photo: AGUSTIN PAULLIER / AFP

The Los Angeles wildfires have now spread to Hollywood as 180,000 people have so far been ordered to evacuate.

Six fires are now burning, after the blaze began in the affluent Pacific Palisades area Wednesday.

The death toll stands at five. Hundreds of homes have been destroyed, but strong winds are hindering firefighting efforts, as water supplies run low.
