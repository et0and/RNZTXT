Dozens of people evacuated over suspected chemical leak in central Wellington

About 50 people in central Wellington have been evacuated after reports of a chemical leak in the area.

Generic fire truck. Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

About 50 people in central Wellington have been evacuated after reports of a chemical leak in the area.

Fire and Emergency said it was responding to reports of the leak around the intersection of Salamanca Rd and Kelburn Parade.

They are asking people to avoid the area.

About 50 people from a 200m perimeter around the area have been evacuated by police since the first reports were received shortly before 9pm.

The substance has not yet been identified and is reported to be odourless.

Fire and Emergency said anyone who is in the area and is feeling unwell should call 111 without delay.
