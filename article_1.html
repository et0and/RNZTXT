Watch live: Transport Minister makes pre-Budget announcement

Simeon Brown and Matt Doocey are set to reveal another bit of next week's budget.

Transport Minister Simeon Brown is due to make a pre-Budget transport announcement. The event will be livestreamed at the top of this page from about 9am.

Earlier this week Prime Minister Christopher Luxon said he did not expect any curveballs in the Budget, nor any bells-and-whistles, but reaffirmed a commitment to tax relief.
