ICC Champions Trophy live updates: Black Caps v South Africa

Follow all the action from Gadaffi Stadium, Lahore.

Devon Conway (L) and Kane Williamson. Photo: FAROOQ NAEEM

The Black Caps are tantilisingly close to a spot in the ICC Champions Trophy, where India awaits. They take on South Africa in tonight's semi-final, who haven't played for almost a fortnight after their last game was washed out.
