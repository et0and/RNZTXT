Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Hurricanes v Moana Pasifika

Clark Laidlaw has rotated his team but will be wary of Moana. Follow RNZ's blog for the latest updates.

Hurricanes Harry Godfrey during the Super Rugby Pacific - Hurricanes v Moana Pasifika at Sky Stadium in Wellington on 13 May 2023. Photo: Photosport

Get in, get the job done, get out. That's the assignment for a Canes team that is fully capable of getting a bonus point win here and staying in touch with the Blues at the top of the table. Clark Laidlaw has rotated his team but will be wary of the trouble that Moana caused the Chiefs in the first half of their game last weekend. Kick-off is at 7.05pm NZT.
