A-League live updates: Wellington Phoenix v Sydney FC

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

Paulo Retre and Wellington Phoenix team-mates celebrate his goal during their A-League match in Wellington, Dec 28, 2024. Photo: Photosport

It is desperate times for the Wellington Phoenix, who sit 10th on the A-League ladder. They take on the sixth-placed Sydney FC who are unbeaten in their last five games, so the Phoenix will be doing it the hard way if they can start a resurgence tonight. Kick-off it at 7pm.
