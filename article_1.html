Three injured, one seriously, in Christchurch apartment blaze

Fire and Emergency said the building was well ablaze when crews arrived.

Generic image. Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

One person has been taken to hospital in a serious condition and two others injured after a fire in a Christchurch apartment.

Emergency services received reports of an apartment on fire in Shirley just before 10pm on Tuesday.

Fire and Emergency said the building was well ablaze when crews arrived.

Four fire trucks attended the scene, a spokesperson said.

Hato Hone St John said three people were injured, with one transferred to hospital in serious condition.
