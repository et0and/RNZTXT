The first official day of the 2024 Olympic Games has begun in Paris, following a colourful opening ceremony on the banks of the River Seine.

The schedule for the first day is jam-packed, with New Zealand athletes competing in sports as varied as equestrian dressage, canoe slalom, surfing, rugby sevens and freestyle swimming.
