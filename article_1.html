Two people reported missing from West Auckland, Hamilton

Police are looking for two people who both went missing on Friday.

Missing people Phoenix (left) and Raumiria. Photo: Police / supplied

Police are looking for two people who both went missing on Friday.

Phoenix was last seen at his Ranui, Auckland home about 3.30pm. He was wearing a black T-shirt with moths on front and back, dark trackpants and black workboots, police said.

"Police and Phoenix's family have concerns for his welfare and want to see him return home," police said in a statement on Saturday morning.

Phoenix. Photo: Police / supplied

Anyone with information should call 111 and quote reference number 250328/2132.

Raumiria was last seen on Friday wearing a dark grey Star Wars T-shirt with "Mandalorian" written on it, light grey trackpants, and white New Balance sneakers.

She was reported missing from her home in Grandview Heights, Hamilton.

"Police and her family are concerned for her welfare and would like to find her as soon as possible."

Raumiria. Photo: Police / supplied

Call 111 and quote reference number 250328/2012 if you have information.
