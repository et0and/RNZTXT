Live: Hurricanes v Highlanders

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

The Hurricanes are looking to go clear at the top of the Super Rugby Pacific leaderboard with a bonus-point win today. Photo: www.photosport.nz

A bonus-point win and a wait are what the Canes want - five points will put them outright first and they'll stay there if the Blues can't get that return against the Chiefs. The Highlanders are coming right at the right end of the season, though, so the first part isn't a given.

Kick-off is at 4.35pm (NZT).
