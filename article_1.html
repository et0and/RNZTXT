Interislander ferry Kaiarahi being towed back to Wellington

The Wellington to Picton ferry had a technical issue and was being towed back to port.

The Interislander ferry Kaiarahi. Photo: Interislander

The Interislander ferry Kaiarahi is being towed back to Wellington due to a technical issue.

A passenger on the ferry said the 1pm sailing from Wellington to Picton was still in the harbour, about halfway between the port and Matu Somes Island, when a call came over the loudspeaker that there was a technical issue.

The announcement said it would not be a quick fix, and the ferry would have to return to Wellington.
