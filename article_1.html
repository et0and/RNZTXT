Person found critically injured in Naenae, Lower Hutt

Police have cordoned off a street in the Lower Hutt suburb of Naenae after finding a person critically injured.

File picture Photo: RNZ / Patrice Allen

Police have cordoned off a street in the Lower Hutt suburb of Naenae after finding a person critically injured.

Emergency services were called to Seddon Street at about 3.20pm and said they were still trying to establish what happened.

They are asking Seddon Street residents to follow the directions of officers at the cordons.

A resident says there are armed police, paramedics, fire trucks and ambulances at the scene.

They say a sheet has been lain over something that is in the middle of the road.
