Live: Auckland FC v Newcastle Jets

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Logan Rogerson during the round six A-League Men match between Auckland FC and Newcastle Jets at Go Media Stadium. Photo: Michael Bradley/Getty Images

The Auckland FC Black Knights are flying, currently top of the A-League and defying even the most optimistic predictions for their debut season so far.

Today they face the struggling Newcastle Jets, sitting down in 11th, so yet another win will be on the minds of the Auckland fans streaming into Mt Smart Stadium.
