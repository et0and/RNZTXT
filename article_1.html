Firefighters battle large blaze in Auckland's Onehunga

About 50 firefighters are tackling the fire at a commercial building.

File photo. Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

About 50 firefighters are tackling a large blaze at a commercial building in Auckland's Onehunga this morning.

Fire and Emergency says it was called to the incident on Neilson Street just before 6am.

It says the building on fire has two storeys and is around 50 metres by 100 metres.

Eight fire trucks and three ladder trucks are on the scene.

Meanwhile, firefighters have now extinguished a house fire in Avondale which broke out about 4.30am this morning.

About 20 firefighters were fighting the blaze at the property on Blockhouse Bay Road.
