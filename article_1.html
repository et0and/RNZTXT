Emmys 2024 live: Kiwi-born Anna Sawai wins for role in Shōgun

"I was crying before my name was announced, I am a mess today," Anna Sawai says, after winning for her lead role in historical drama Shōgun.

The who's who of television have arrived on the red carpet at the The 76th Primetime Emmy Awards. Kiwi contenders include Taika Waititi, Anna Sawai and the series What We Do in the Shadows.
