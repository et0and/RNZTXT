Person critically injured after assault on Auckland bus

Parts of two main roads in Onehunga are closed following the incident and motorists are asked to avoid the area.

File photo. Photo: 123rf.com

A person has been injured after an assault on a bus in Auckland's Onehunga.

A police spokesperson said the incident happened at 2.30pm on Wednesday.

The spokesperson said the person was being taken to hospital and police were working to figure out the circumstances surrounding the assault.

Other media are reporting the person is critically injured.

In a statement, police said parts of Captain Springs Road and Church Road were closed while emergency services responded.

"Motorists are asked to avoid the area and expect delays while the incident is resolved."
