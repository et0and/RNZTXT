Oscars live: Oppenheimer wins Best Actor, Director

Oppenheimer can't be stopped, nabbing Best Actor and Director as Best Picture nears.

Irish actor Cillian Murphy accepts the award for Best Actor in a Leading Role for "Oppenheimer" onstage during the 96th Annual Academy Awards at the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood, California Photo: PATRICK T. FALLON / AFP

It's Hollywood's biggest day, as the 96th Annual Academy Awards are underway, with the biopic of the atomic bomb creator winning the most awards so far.

Cillian Murphy won Best Actor, Christopher Nolan Best Director and Robert Downey Jr won Best Supporting Actor Oscar all for Oppenheimer, while Da'Vine Joy Randolph won Best Supporting Actress for The Holdovers.
