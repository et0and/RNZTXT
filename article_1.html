13 injured as car and van crash in Taranaki

Helicopters are flying people to hospital with at least two seriously hurt.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Thirteen people are thought to have been injured in a crash between a car and a van in Taranaki.

Police said the crash happened at Hastings Road in Matapu, South Taranaki, about 9.55am on Wednesday.

Most of the injuries were believed to range from minor to moderate, but two people are believed to be in a serious condition, police said.

Four helicopters were airlifting people to hospital.

Hastings Road, between Skeet and Eltham roads, was likely to remain closed for some time while the serious crash unit investigated, police said.
