Watch: Auckland's Gulf Harbour Country Club building on fire

Locals say a building at the golf course and country club was burning from at least 3am.

A building at Auckland's Gulf Harbour Country Club was on fire.

It had been burning since at least 3am Saturday, according to locals. They said it was now out.

The club had been closed for several months awaiting redevelopment.
