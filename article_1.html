Multiple people with critical injuries after Auckland crash

All lanes on SH1 between Bombay and Ramarama are closed due to a significant crash.

A crash involving multiple cars and a truck has closed SH1. Photo: SUPPLIED

Multiple people have critical injuries after a serious crash on State Highway One in Ramarama, Auckland.

NZTA says all lanes on SH1 between Bombay and Ramarama are closed due to a significant crash.

A crash involving multiple cars and a truck has closed SH1. Photo: SUPPLIED

Those travelling south will be diverted at Ararimu Road, Ramarama and northbound motorists will be diverted at Mill Road, Bombay.

Were you a witness? Email RNZ at iwitness@rnz.co.nz.

People should expect delays.

Photo: Supplied / Google Maps

Police say the crash was reported about 1.47pm.

The Serious Crash Unit has been advised.
