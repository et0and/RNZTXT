Auckland FC beat Phoenix 2-1

Auckland held their unbeaten record defeating the Phoenix before a crowd of thousands.

Auckland FC captain Horoki Sakai celebrates after scoring the first goal during the A-League football match between Auckland FC v Wellington Phoenix at Go Media Stadium - Auckland. Photo: Brett Phibbs / www.photosport.nz

Auckland FC have beaten Wellington Phoenix 2-1 in a frenetic clash in the all-New Zealand A-League derby at Mount Smart Stadium.

Auckland led 1-0 at halftime after a Phoenix own goal, but had to withstand a late Phoenix rally to seal the win.

Auckland are now unbeaten after six matches.

Mt Smart saw a crowd of more 26,000.
