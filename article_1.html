Live: Snow begins falling on Desert Road

Snow on the Desert Road is making conditions hazardous for motorists while a host of warnings and watches remain in place for the upper North Island.

Photo: Waka Kotahi

Snow has begun falling on the Desert Road (State Highway 1) making conditions hazardous for motorists.

MetService says its webcam monitoring the top of the Desert Road is now showing "some wintry weather".

The weather agency issued a road snowfall warning for the summit this afternoon, saying show showers were expected through until 9pm.

It's among a host of warnings and watches that remain in place for the upper North Island which has been experiencing strong winds, thunderstorms, hail, and lightning in the last few hours.
