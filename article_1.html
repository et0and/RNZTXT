13-year-old charged with murder after Dunedin bus station stabbing

A 16-year-old Trinity Catholic College student died from his injuries after the attack.

Photo: Stuff/Hamish McNeilly

A 13-year-old is due to appear in court over the fatal stabbing of a Dunedin high schooler.

The teenager, who cannot be identified due to their age, has been charged with murder.

Emergency services were called to the Dunedin Bus Hub, on Great King Street, just after 3pm Thursday as students were heading home from school.

A 16-year-old Trinity Catholic College student was rushed to hospital but later died from his injuries.

Detective Senior Sergeant Kallum Croudis said police want to hear from anyone who has photos or video footage of what happened.
