Live updates: Wellington Phoenix vs Melbourne Victory

Follow all the action from this A-League round 21 match at Sky Stadium in Wellington.

Youstin Salas of the Phoenix during the A-League - Wellington Phoenix FC v Melbourne Victory FC at Sky Stadium, Wellington. Photo: Masanori Udagawa / Photosport

After giving up up first spot on the A-League ladder to Central Coast last weekend, the Phoenix have to keep the pressure on with a win here tonight against the third-placed Melbourne Victory.

The 'Nix welcome back skipper Alex Rufer and Tim Payne has been cleared to play after picking up a hamstring injury in the loss to the Mariners.
