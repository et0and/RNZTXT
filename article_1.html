Watch live: Aotearoa Music Awards - all the music, glam and winners

Follow all the glitz and the glamour as some of New Zealand's best artists are celebrated at the Aotearoa Music Awards.

The Aotearoa Music Awards take place tonight, where New Zealand's biggest names in the music industry will gather to celebrate their success.

The awards, organised by Recorded Music NZ, have been presented annually since 1965.

They recognise the biggest musical successes of the year, promote emerging artists and highlight the mahi of those who work behind the scenes.

Some of the biggest names in New Zealand music will attend.

Artists Stan Walker, Aaradhna, Marlon Williams, Home Brew, Unknown Mortal Orchestra, L.A.B, and The Beths are some of the finalists who will be there.

Broadcasters Kara Rickard and Jesse Mulligan will be hosting the awards ceremony.

Many sports stars, politicians and media personalities have been known to attend the awards too. Past AMA attendees include the likes of MMA fighter Israel Adesanya and former New Zealand prime minister Jacinda Ardern.
