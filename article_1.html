Live: We went to a Trump rally as shock poll threatens upset

Along with familiar talking points on immigration and the economy, Trump criticised Democrats for their handling of Harris' ascension to party nominee.

The rally on Monday (NZT) Photo: AFP / Ryan Collerd

There are just two days of campaigning left before polls close in a historically close United States presidential election.

RNZ has been at a Donald Trump rally in Lititz, Pennsylvania, as he focuses on the critical state.

Democrat Kamala Harris campaigns in Michigan on Monday (NZ time) while Trump will go on to hit two more eastern battleground states North Carolina and Georgia.

A poll has shown Harris leading in Iowa, a state that Trump won easily in both 2016 and 2020, raising the possibility of an upset there.
