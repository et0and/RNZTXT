Suspect in killing of US health CEO arrested and named

26-year-old Luigi Nicholas Mangione was arrested after a McDonald's employee recognised him.

CCTV footage of the shootage. Photo: NYPD via CNN

New York City's Police Commissioner has announced the prime suspect in the shooting of a health insurance chief executive has been arrested.

Commissioner Jessica Tisch said the man is 26-year-old Luigi Nicholas Mangione, who was born and raised in Maryland.

He was arrested after a McDonald's employee recognised him in Pennsylvania and called local police, Tisch said.

Mangione was found acting suspiciously, had firearms and fraudulent items - believed to be fake IDs - on his person.

Mangione has been arrested on gun charges, but Tisch did not elaborated further on the charges.
