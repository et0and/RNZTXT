Government designates political wing of Hamas a terrorist entity

The 7 October attack "reinforces we can no longer distinguish between the military and political wings of Hamas", the Foreign Minister says.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

The coalition government has designated the political wing of Hamas as a terrorist entity.

New Zealand designated the military wing of Hamas as a terrorist entity in 2010.

"What happened on 7 October reinforces we can no longer distinguish between the military and political wings of Hamas," Foreign Minister Winston Peters said.

"The organisation as a whole bears responsibility for these horrific terrorist attacks."

The designation means any assets of the terrorist entity in New Zealand are frozen.

It will become a criminal offence to participate in or support Hamas' activities.
