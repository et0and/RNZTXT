Severe thunderstorms, heavy rain on the cards for Auckland, Northland and Tai Rāwhiti

MetService warns rainfall could reach warning criteria especially north of Gisborne City.

Photo: Unsplash / Erik Witsoe

A severe thunderstorm watch is in place for Auckland and Northland with a heavy rain watch for Tai Rāwhiti this afternoon.

Slow moving thunderstorms may produce localised downpours from midday through to 7pm for Auckland and Northland with a possibility of 35 to 40mm per hour.

This intensity of rain can cause surface flooding and flash flooding.

A heavy rain watch is in place for Tai Rāwhiti from 9pm through to 9am Wednesday.

Heavy rain may approach warning criteria, especially north of Gisborne City, MetService said.
