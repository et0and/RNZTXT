Police searching hospitals and schools across the country after threats made

Police are searching hospitals and schools across the country after threats were made against a number of organisations in New Zealand.

They are searching Wellington and Bowen Hospital in the Capital and Burwood Hospital in Christchurch.

RNZ understands police have also been at Auckland City Hospital and Saint Kentigern College says on its website that it was evacuating.

Saint Kentigern College said on its website that it was evacuating. Photo: SCREENGRAB

Police have confirmed they are responding to emails of a concerning nature directed at organisations, including schools and hospitals.

They are working to establish the origin of the emails and are yet to establish any actual threat.

Staff are working with those impacted and will respond based on individual circumstances.

Police acknowledge how unsettling these emails are for all concerned.
