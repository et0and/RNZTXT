Super Rugby Pacific recap: Blues v Brumbies

The Blues are into the final after a comfortable win over the Brumbies in Auckland.

Caleb Clarke of the Blues dives down to score a try during the Super Rugby Pacific match against the Brumbies at Eden Park, 2024. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The Blues have scored a comfortable win over the Brumbies in the Super Rugby Pacific semi-final.

They were favourites with good reason, as they finished the regular season in second spot and had not lost at home in 2024.
