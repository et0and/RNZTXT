Six Nations rugby live updates: France v Wales

Follow all the action from Stade de France, Paris.

France’s Antoine Dupont. Photo: photosport

The Six Nations kicks off in Paris this morning with a test between France and Wales. The All Blacks will have a keen eye on this French side, who won the last test between the two and are touring New Zealand in July. Meanwhile, Warren Gatland's Wales are serious underdogs, but will be playing for British & Irish Lions squad places for later in the year.
