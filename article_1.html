Green Party MP Efeso Collins collapses during charity run

The Green Party confirmed the incident but are still working to find out how he is doing.

Green Party MP Efeso Collins. Photo: Supplied / Efeso Collins

Green Party MP Efeso Collins has collapsed while taking part in a charity run in central Auckland.

The party confirmed the incident had happened but were still working to find out how he was doing.

The Childfun Water Run took was taking place in Te Komititanga Square, outside Commercial Bay at 8am on Wednesday.

The charity event was to raise awareness and funds to bring safe, clean drinking water to children in the Pacific.

As well as Collins, presenter Hilary Barry, community leader Dave Letele, radio host Mariner Fagaiava, actor Shavaugn Ruakere and ChildFund chief executive Josie Pagani took part in the run.
