Live: 'For 47 years we've been battling': Fair Go team on plan to end show

"We are not ready for that to end," staff say, as the iconic show - along with Sunday - goes on the chopping block.

Photo: Supplied

TVNZ is announcing to staff which programmes are likely to be axed in a proposed restructuring.

The broadcaster has confirmed it has proposed cancelling current affairs show Sunday, after earlier meeting with staff.

Radio New Zealand has been told by several sources say current affairs show Fair Go, the midday news and 1News Tonight may all be closed under the proposal.
