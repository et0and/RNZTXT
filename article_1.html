Live updates: Highlanders v Crusaders - who will take the win?

The Highlanders will be out to break their duck against the Crusaders at home - a fixture they haven't won since 2018.

Sam Gilbert of the Highlanders (left) tackles Sevu Reece of the Crusaders. Photo: James Ross / www.photosport.nz

The Highlanders will be out to break their duck against the Crusaders at home - a fixture they haven't won since 2018.
