White Ferns on top against South Africa in T20 final

With just two overs to go South Africa face a very difficult task to overcome the Black Ferns' total of 158.

New Zealand's Amelia Kerr plays a shot during the ICC Women's T20 World Cup cricket final match between South Africa and New Zealand at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium. Photo: FADEL SENNA/AFP

South Africa still need another 42 runs from the final two overs, chasing New Zealand's 158-5 in the first innings.

The South Africans are also eight wickets down.

The White Ferns have never won a World Cup.
