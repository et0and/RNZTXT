Live: Highlanders v Chiefs, Super Rugby Pacific

Follow all the action from FMG Stadium Waikato in Hamilton as the Highlanders face a big challenge from the Chiefs.

Shaun Stevenson of the Chiefs during the Super Rugby Pacific round 5 match between the Chiefs and Highlanders at FMG Stadium in Hamilton. Photo: Aaron Gillions / www.photosport.nz

The Highlanders face a big challenge at home under the roof with the powerful Chiefs are in town. These two sides have a history of putting on absolute barn-burners at this venue, so the odds of an excellent game are high.

Kickoff is at 4:35pm NZT.
