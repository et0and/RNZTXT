Warriors captain retiring with immediate effect

Tohu Harris has retired with immediate effect, according to a report out of Australia.

Warriors captain Tohu Harris. Photo: Bruce Lim /www.photosport.nz

In a major blow for the Warriors ahead of the NRL season starting, captain Tohu Harris is retiring with immediate effect.

Harris told teammates and the club he will not play again after a wrist injury that ended his 2024 season.

The 32-year-old played 14 matches last season before having surgery on his wrist, however, he did not resume playing.

Harris has played 234 NRL games in his career, exaclty half of them for the Warriors over a seven season stretch.

The other half of the games he played for the Melbourne Storm between 2013-2018.
