Live: Grammys 2024 - All the glam, winners and who was led away in handcuffs?

Follow all the updates as the best in the music business are gathering in Los Angeles.

The best in the music business are gathering in Los Angeles for the 66th annual Grammy Awards.

The ceremony kicked off at 2pm New Zealand time.

SZA is the most nominated artist this year with nine nods.

Taylor Swift is nominated for Album of the Year for the fourth time. If she wins, she will be the only artist to win that award four times.

Early prizes at the mid-afternoon "premiere ceremony" have gone to Billie Eilish, Kylie Minogue and The Beatles.
