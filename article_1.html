Watch live: Luxon and Niue Premier speak

PM Christopher Luxon is answering questions on his first Pacific trip alongside Niue's Dalton Tagelagi.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Niue's Premier Dalton Tagelagi are speaking to media.

It is Luxon's his first trip to the Pacific since becoming prime minister.

He has been in Niue since yesterday and will travel next to Fiji.

Luxon is expected to speak to media shortly after 9.30am.
