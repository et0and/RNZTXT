Golf: Lydia Ko wins 23rd LPGA title in Singapore

New Zealand golfer Lydia Ko has won the LPGA Women's World Championship in Singapore this evening.

Lydia Ko with the trophy after winning the HSBC Women's Championship golf tournament at Sentosa Golf Club in Singapore. Photo: AFP / Roslan Rahman

New Zealand golfer Lydia Ko has won the LPGA Women's World Championship in Singapore this evening.

Ko carded a final round 69 to win the tournament at the Sentosa Golf Club by four shots.

It was Ko's 23rd LPGA title.
