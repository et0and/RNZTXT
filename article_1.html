Live: Heavy rain, snow across central North and South Island

MetService is warning of heavy rain, gales - and heavy snow - across a number of central and southern parts of the country on Saturday.

Snow at Castle Hill Village. Photo: Supplied / Paula Warman

MetService is warning of heavy rain, gales - and even heavy snow - across a number of central and southern parts of the country on Saturday.

Heavy rain warnings are in place for the Nelson-Tasman region and the Buller, Grey and Westland Districts, as well as the Canterbury High Country.

Snow is forecast to fall above 600 metres from Canterbury southwards, with heavy snow warnings for the Canterbury High Country, Central Otago and the Queenstown Lakes District.

Strong wind watches are also in place for Taihape, Taranaki, Westland and Fiordland.
