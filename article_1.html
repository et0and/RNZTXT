Warning as fire burns through 10 hectares in Porirua

Two helicopters are helping firefighters battle a vegetation fire in Titahi Bay.

Fire and Emergency responded to the fire just after 11pm on Thursday. Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

Two helicopters are helping firefighters battle a vegetation fire in Titahi Bay, Porirua.

Fire and Emergency received a call about the fire at about 11pm on Thursday and the first crews were onsite 10 minutes later.

By 5.20am on Friday the fire had burnt through 10 hectares of vegetation, Fire and Emergency said in a statement.

Residents have reported a strong smell of smoke across the area, with firefighters urging people who suffer from respiratory conditions to keep their doors and windows closed.

A spokesperson said no homes have been told to evacuate but the public should stay away from the area.
