Live football: Auckland FC v Melbourne Victory

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Auckland FC players celebrate victory over Wellington Phoenix with their fans. Photo: Photosport

Auckland FC are back on home turf - and will be looking to make up for the last time they played at Mt Smart, where they suffered a 4-0 loss to Western United.

Still, it's their only loss of a remarkable debut season and they're sitting top of the A-League ladder.

Meanwhile, Melbourne Victory are right behind them in third, so this should be a really tight match.
