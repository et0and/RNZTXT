US President Joe Biden tests positive for Covid-19

He had been scheduled to speak in Las Vegas.

Photo: Kevin Dietsch / Getty Images / AFP

US President Joe Biden has tested positive for Covid-19.

He was scheduled to speak in Las Vegas but could not attend.

Biden and former president Donald Trump are in the midst of campaigning ahead of the US election on 5 November.

Biden's doctor said he had a runny nose, cough and general mailaise. He had received his first dose of paxlovid.

The White House said Biden was vaccinated and boosted, and his symptoms were "mild".

He was returning to Delaware to isolate but would continue his duties.

-Reuters
