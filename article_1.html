Serious crash on SH5, Rotorua

The crash involved two cars, police said, and it was too early to confirm any injuries.

Photo: 123RF

Emergency services are at the scene of a serious crash on State Highway 5, Whakarewarewa, Rotorua.

The crash, involving two cars, was reported to police shortly before 8pm, a police spokesperson said.

It was not yet clear how many people had been injured.

Motorists have been advised to take alternative routes where possible.
