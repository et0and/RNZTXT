Live: Day three and the hīkoi is due to reach Auckland

Two lanes on the Harbour Bridge will be closed as are some on and off ramps on the motorway network.

The hīkoi which began in Northland on Monday is making its way to Parliament, it will reach Auckland Wednesday. Photo: Layla Bailey-McDowell / RNZ

Auckland commuters are advised to plan ahead this morning as the hīkoi to protest against the Treaty Principles Bill makes its way over the Harbour Bridge.

Waka Kotahi and Police say the two outer northbound lanes will be closed from early Wednesday until it was safe to open. Some other on- and off-ramps will also be closed until further notice.

The hīkoi which began in Northland on Monday is making its way to Parliament.
