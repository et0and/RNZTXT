University of Auckland says armed offender on city campus

Police say they are are currently responding to a report of a firearm being sighted at the campus.

The University of Auckland. File photo Photo: RNZ / Dan Cook

The University of Auckland says an armed offender is on its city campus.

Police say they are are currently responding to a report of a firearm being sighted at the campus.

"A male Offender armed with a long weapon of some description was last located in Sector 100," the university's campus security posted on Facebook.

"Police are in attendance and are moving through the sector to locate the offender.

"The last known location was on the Barrack lawn behind the 104 Old Choral Hall construction site."

Police say information recently provided to them suggests it relates to a planned bird cull in the area.

The university says police have cordoned off the area.
