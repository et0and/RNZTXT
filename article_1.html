Watch live: Aotearoa Music Awards - all the music, glam and winners

Follow all the glitz and the glamour as some of New Zealand's best artists are celebrated at the Aotearoa Music Awards.

For the first time in two years, the New Zealand music industry's finest have stepped out for the Aotearoa Music Awards at Auckland's Viaduct Events Centre on Thursday night.

The start of the ceremony has seen a slew of awards presented, including Best Pop Artist to Georgia Lines, Best Soul/R&B to Aaradhna and Best Rock Artist to Racing.

Tawaz was named Best Māori Artist, while Six60 scooped the Highest Selling Artist of the year but was not there to receive it.

Hori Shaw won the People's Choice Award, while Corrella won Breakthrough Single of the Year for 'Blue Eyed Māori'.

Dame Hinewehi Mohi received a standing ovation when she was inducted into the New Zealand Music Hall of Fame.

Broadcasters Kara Rickard and Jesse Mulligan are hosting the awards ceremony.
