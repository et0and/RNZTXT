Live: Black Caps close in on historic test series win

Follow all the action from Maharashtra Cricket Association Stadium, Pune.

Glenn Phillips of New Zealand celebrates the wicket of Yashasvi Jaiswal of India. Photo: Arjun Singh / Sportzpics for BCCI

The Black Caps are on the verge of an historic series win in India, with victory reliant on a good first session in Pune. They head in with a lead of 301 into day three, so establishing a position to confidently take 10 Indian wickets is what today is all about.
