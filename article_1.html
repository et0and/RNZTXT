Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Highlanders v Blues

Follow all the action from Forsyth Barr Stadium, Dunedin.

Hoskins Sotutu of the Blues in action during the round two Super Rugby Pacific match between Highlanders and Blues at AAMI Park. Photo: Getty Images

It's O-Week in Dunedin but the Blues haven't come to party, at least not till after they've made a statement in their match against the Highlanders tonight. Both sides are coming off first up losses and will be looking to give the ball a bit of air under the roof at Forsyth Barr. Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
