White Ferns score 158 against South Africa in T20 final

South Africa will require 95 runs to win the final being played in Dubai.

New Zealand's Amelia Kerr plays a shot during the ICC Women's T20 World Cup cricket final match between South Africa and New Zealand at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium. Photo: FADEL SENNA/AFP

South Africa still need another 95 runs from the final 10 overs, chasing New Zealand's 158-5 in the first innings.

Amelia Kerr took two wickets in the 10th over to leave South Africa at 64-3 after 10 overs.
