Two dead, three hurt in multi-car crash on SH1

Two people have died and three are injured after a collision between two vehicles north of Christchurch.

File photo. Photo: WESTPAC RESCUE HELICOPTERS

Two people have died and three are injured after a collision between at least two vehicles north of Christchurch this morning.

Police have described it as a multi-vehicle crash, which occurred shortly after 10am on Monday on State Highway One, just north of Waikuku.

Two people in one vehicle died at the scene, while a third person was moderately hurt. Two people in the other vehicle were also injured.

Three ambulances, two helicopters, a rapid response vehicle attended the crash.

UPDATE 11:05AMThe road is now CLOSED between Wyllies Rd and Broad Rd, due to a serious crash. Please follow directions by emergency services on-site. ^AP https://t.co/L1BcuGugCg

"Motorists are asked to avoid the area," police said.

"Diversions are in place at Wyllies Road and Amesbury Road."
