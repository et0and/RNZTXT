A-League live updates: Auckland FC v Western Sydney Wanderers

Follow all the action from CommBank Stadium, Sydney.

Logan Rogerson. Photo: Photosport

After a thrilling top of the table draw against Adelaide FC midweek, the Black Knights have traveled to Sydney to face the eighth placed Wanderers. A win today could put them back as outright A-League leaders, kick-off is at 7pm NZT.
