All Blacks lose to France after close match

Follow all the action from Stade de France, Paris.

All Black Peter Lakai scores a try. Photo: Bob Martin/ActionPress

A massive test for the All Blacks this morning in Paris, the scene of their heartbreaking World Cup final loss last year.

Scott Robertson has made a number of changes to his side that beat Ireland last weekend, notably Beauden Barrett and Codie Taylor coming back into the starting side, and Ardie Savea moving to openside.
