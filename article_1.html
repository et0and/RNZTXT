Fire breaks out in Wellington council-run flats

Residents of the eight storey building in the Pukehinau flats are lining the streets after the blaze broke out on the third floor.

A fire has broken out in a block of council-run apartments in Wellington. Photo: Mary Argue

A fire has broken out in a block of council-run apartments in Wellington.

Residents of the eight storey building in the Pukehinau flats are lining the streets after the blaze broke out on the third floor.

Fire at 320 Willis Street. Photo: Tessa Guest

Six fire trucks, a cherry picker and numerous firefighters are at the scene on the corner of Brooklyn Road and Willis Street.

Surrounding roads are cordoned off.

It is not yet known whether any one has been hurt.
