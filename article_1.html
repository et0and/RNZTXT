Oscars live: Da'Vine Joy Randolph wins Best Supporting Actress

And Sir Peter Jackson is thanked from the stage by a winner.

Da'Vine Joy Randolph accepts the award for Best Actress in a Supporting Role for 'The Holdovers'. Photo: PATRICK T. FALLON / AFP

It's Hollywood's biggest day, as the 96th Annual Academy Awards kick off.

Christopher Nolan's epic three-hour biography of the mastermind of the atomic bomb, Oppenheimer, leads the nominations and has been picked to take home the Best Picture honour, but there's always a few surprises on Oscar night as well.

The ceremony airs on subscription streamer Disney+ in New Zealand this year, but RNZ is here to bring you blow by blow and award by award the highlights.

Follow along with RNZ's live blog for all the red carpet hot looks, the awards ceremony and more!
