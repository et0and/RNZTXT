Watch live: Police announce latest after woman found dead in Gulf Harbour

Follow as police speak to the media after launching a homicide probe in Auckland.

Police are speaking to the media after a woman's body was pulled from Auckland's Gulf Harbour.

A fisherman found the body wrapped in plastic bags on 12 March by the Gulf Harbour ferry terminal.

On Thursday, Acting Detective Inspector Tim Williams revealed the body was "a female of Asian-decent, possibly Chinese, and small in stature".

Police asked anyone who may have information on a woman matching the description who they have concerns for to get in touch with them.
