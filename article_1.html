Olympics Live: NZ aiming to qualify in discus, pole vault

Follow all the live updates from the athletics, canoe slalom and Simone Biles in the individual gymnastics event.

Discus thrower Connor Bell - who broke the New Zealand record twice last year - is also in qualifying rounds tonight. Photo: ALISHA LOVRICH

New Zealand pole vault star Eliza McCartney is in action in the qualifying round at the Stade de France.

She is joined by fellow Kiwis Olivia McTaggart and Imogen Ayris, as they all hope to advance to the next stage of the competition.

Earlier, the mixed triathlon team - Dylan McCullough, Nicole van der Kaay, Ainsley Thorpe and Hayden Wilde - were out of contention from the start, when Hayden Wilde collided with a French athlete in the opening cycle leg.

Discus thrower Connor Bell - who broke the New Zealand record twice last year - is also in qualifying rounds tonight. And speed climber Sarah Tetzlaff will race up the wall.

In the early hours of Tuesday, Luuka Jones and Finn Butcher are in the kayak cross quarter-finals.

Meanwhile, Simone Biles could join an elite club of greatest female Olympians as she goes for titles eight and nine in the balance beam and floor exercise finals on the last day of artistic gymnastics competition at the Paris Games. The beam finals are at 10.36pm Monday NZT, with the floor exercise finals at 12.20am Tuesday NZT.
