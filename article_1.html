Prisoner on the run after escaping from van in Taupō

Police are hunting a prisoner who has escaped from a transport van in Taupō, and are warning people not to approach him.

People are being warned not to approach Samir Derek Belhajjam, but to call 111 if they see him. Photo: NZ POLICE

Police are hunting a prisoner who has escaped from a transport van in Taupō, and are warning people not to approach him.

Samir Derek Belhajjam, 30, escaped custody in Taupō on Monday about 6pm, a police spokesperson said.

"Anyone who sees him or has information on his whereabouts is asked not to approach him, but instead to call 111 immediately."

Belhajjam had been in a police transport van that was at a court building on Story Place when he escaped, the spokesperson said.

He was wearing green prison-issue shorts and T-shirt and an orange hi-vis vest at the time.

There would be an increased police presence in the area, as officers hunt for Blhajjam, they said.
