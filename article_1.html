Formula 1 live updates: Chinese Grand Prix

Oscar Piastri has won the Chinese Grand Prix for McLaren, with NZer Liam Lawson finishing 16th.

Red Bull Racing driver Liam Lawson in action during the Formula One Chinese Grand Prix. Photo: AFP

Oscar Piastri has won the Chinese Grand Prix for McLaren, with NZer Liam Lawson finishing 16th.

Norris came second for McLaren, with Mercedes driver George Russell coming third.
