Have you seen Steven Rolls?

The Waikato man failed to arrive in Huntly as expected, after leaving his Ōtorohanga home about noon on Christmas Day.

Missing Waikato man Steven Rolls. Photo: Supplied / New Zealand Police

Waikato police are appealing for any sightings of a man who went missing on Christmas Day while driving from Ōtorohanga to Huntly, a journey of just over an hour.

Steven Rolls' family reported him missing when he failed to arrive in Huntly as expected, after leaving his Ōtorohanga home about 12pm on Wednesday.

The police said they and Rolls' family had serious concerns for his welfare.

They asked anyone who may have seen Steven or his car - a grey Kia Rio, registration FRG486 - to contact them and quote the file number 241225/1782.
