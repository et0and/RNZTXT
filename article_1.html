One arrested after fire breaks out at backpackers' lodge

A fire at the City Garden Lodge in Parnell, Auckland left smoke rising across the city, as police say one person was arrested following a disorder incident.

Emergency crews in Auckland are battling a blaze at City Garden Lodge in the central suburb of Parnell.

Images showed flames leaping high from the second floor through the roof, but an RNZ reporter at the scene said by 6:15pm the fire appeared more controlled.

Fire and Emergency NZ said people had been evacuated from the building, but could not say how many.

No injuries have yet been reported.

Police say one person was arrested after they were called to a "disorder" at City Garden Lodge, in St George's Bay Road, just after 4.30pm.

There are ten trucks working to bring the structure fire under control.

FENZ is asking the public to avoid the area.
