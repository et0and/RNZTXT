Air NZ Wellington to Sydney flight diverted to Auckland

An Air New Zealand flight from Wellington to Sydney has been diverted to Auckland.

Photo: Supplied / Screenshot

An Air New Zealand flight from Wellington to Sydney has been diverted to Auckland.

It is unclear if the flight is having engine trouble or other difficulty.

Fiight ANZ249 departed Wellington shortly before 3pm.

The website Flight Radar shows the flight sharply diverting away to Auckland shortly after leaving Wellington. It has now landed in Auckland.

Air New Zealand has been contacted for comment.
