Armed police in Auckland suburb after two men found injured in vehicle

Armed police are patrolling a street in the Auckland suburb of Manurewa after two men were found injured in a vehicle.

Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

Armed police are patrolling a street in the Auckland suburb of Manurewa after two men were found injured in a vehicle.

Part of Addington Avenue in Manurewa was cordoned off after the men were discovered with serious injuries. The pair have been taken to hospital.

Police were asking people to avoid the area.

There was no immediate risk to public safety, they said.
