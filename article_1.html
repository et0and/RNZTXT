Unexplained death of woman in Whangārei investigated

A police spokesperson said emergency services were called to the Moody Avenue address shortly before 5pm on Saturday after the woman was found unresponsive.

The woman was found unresponsive at a property on Moody Ave. Photo: Google Maps

A police investigation is underway after a woman was found dead at a Whau Valley, Whangārei home on Saturday.

Emergency services were called to the Moody Avenue address shortly before 5pm after the woman was found unresponsive, a police spokesperson said.

The circumstances surrounding her death were unexplained, and a post-mortem examination would be carried out on Sunday.

The hosue would undergo a scene examination on Sunday, police said.

Police are seeking reports from anyone who may have information about any incidents that might relate to the death, or those involved.

Any information can be reported using the police 105 service with the reference number 250118/376 or anonymously through Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
