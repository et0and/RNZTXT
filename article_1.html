Live: Trump set to announce pick for vice-president

Just hours before the major election annoucenment, the Republican presidential candidate also had one of this criminal cases dismissed by a judge.

Photo: AFP/JOE RAEDLE

US Senator Marco Rubio of Florida has been told he will not be Republican presidential candidate Donald Trump's running mate, sources told Reuters on Monday.

Advisers for Trump, who is expected to formally announce his vice presidential pick later on Monday (Tuesday NZT), had viewed Rubio's residency in Florida as a drawback.

The US Constitution bans the electors who formally select the president and vice president from voting for both from their home state - and both Trump and Rubio call Florida home.
