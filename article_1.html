Witness describes man pulling gun in central Auckland

Armed police have entered a central Auckland apartment building with nearby roads cordoned off.

Police on Hobson Street in Auckland. Photo: RNZ/Lucy Xia

Officers have closed off sections of Hobson and Union streets and have since gone into the Goodview Apartment Hotel.

Police were called to the scene about 8am on Monday.

A witness told RNZ he saw two people arguing on the street. Soon after one of the men "got up, put his hand in his pocket, pulled out a gun and loaded it and pointed it at him and said he's going to shoot him".

Photo: RNZ / Felix Walton

No injuries have been reported.

The public is being asked to avoid the area.
