Live: Auckland FC v Adelaide United

Follow live as Auckland FC look to break Adelaide United's three-game win streak and regain top spot on the A-League ladder.

Isaias of Adelaide United is tackled by Felipe Gallegos of Auckland FC during the A-League Men Round 14 match between Adelaide United and Auckland FC at Coopers Stadium in Adelaide. Photo: PhotoSport / Matt Turner

With just one point separating them at the top of the ladder, Auckland FC will be looking to end Adelaide United's three-game win steak when they face off at Coopers Stadium.

Auckland FC come off the back of an impressive win, where they hammered home three goals in a blistering 15 minutes of football, knocking over then-table-toppers Melbourne City.

The match starts at 9:30pm.
