Wastewater overflowing onto central Wellington street

People are warned to avoid Featherston Street.

Wastewater overflow on Featherston Street, Pipitea in central Wellington. Photo: Supplied / Wellington Water

Wastewater is running onto a central Wellington street.

Wellington Water was on Thursday warning people to avoid Featherston Street, Pipitea until the overflow problem has been resolved.

Photo: Supplied / Wellington Water

"We're investigating and will follow normal procedure regarding signs and warnings once we have further information.

"We'll provide further updates as this work progresses."

The leak has created a small pool on the corner of Whitmore and Featherston Streets.

It is contained to the side of the road and cars are not being affected.

The wastewater overflow in Wellington central is being investigated. Photo: RNZ / Krystal Gibbens

Steph Lusted Jewellery owner, Steph Lust, has been watching the leak from her window across the road.

She says she saw it increased in size over an hour.

There is no noticeable smell other than a light sewage perfume right by the water.

The leak is forming a small pool on the corner of Whitmore and Featherston Streets. Photo: RNZ / Krystal Gibbens

Water is flowing down the street into the storm drain.
