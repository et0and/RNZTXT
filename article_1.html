Coromandel highway closed after car crashes into water

State Highway 25 in the Coromandel is closed for several hours after a car hit a power pole and crashed into the water early this morning.

State Highway 25 in the Coromandel is closed for several hours after a car hit a power pole and crashed into the water early this morning.

File photo Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

Police said the highway will closed south of Kereta for several hours and there are no detours.

Anyone needing to travel to or from Coromandel will need to go through Whitianga.

The car reportedly hit a power pole and crashed into the water near Russek Road at about 3.25am.

The Serious Crash Unit is investigating.
