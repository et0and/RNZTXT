Mayor, councillors call for Wellington Water board chairperson to resign

On Tuesday Nick Leggett said he was "taking soundings" on whether he should resign.

Wellington Water board chairperson Nick Leggett. Photo: RNZ / Reece Baker

Wellington mayor Tory Whanau and a group of councillors are calling for the resignation of Wellington Water's board chairperson and another board member.

On Tuesday Nick Leggett said he was "taking soundings" on whether he should resign after a scathing report revealed possible theft and problems at his organisation.

"I could be the sacrificial lamb," he told Morning Report on Tuesday.

He could also help with the fix, he told the programme.
