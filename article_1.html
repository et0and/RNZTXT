In pictures: Celine Dion, Lady Gaga and the spectacular, strange Olympic opening ceremony

Watch: From divas to Moulin Rouge can-can dancers, boats, an aircraft flyover - This year's high-energy, rain-soaked opening had it all.

Celine Dion, Lady Gaga, Moulin Rouge can-can dancers, an aircraft flyover, a heavy metal band, and athletes on boats parading up the river have delighted crowds.

You can also read our full coverage of the extravaganza as it happened here.
