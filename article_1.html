Live: Stuff to take over Three's 6pm news bulletin

The show will not be called Newshub and will run for an hour on weekdays, while Stuff will also take over Newshub's website.

Photo: Getty Images / Phil Walter

Media company Stuff will take over and run a 6pm bulletin for TV station Three from the 6th July, staff have been told.

The bulletin will not be called Newshub, and will run a one-hour bulletin from Monday to Friday, and a 30 minute bulletin on weekends/

Stuff's Sinead Boucher has told staff the company will "definitely be bringing some Newshub staff" to produce the 6pm bulletins.

Both companies held meetings with staff on Tuesday after Newshub owner Warner Brothers Discovery confirmed Newhub's operations were to be shut down.
