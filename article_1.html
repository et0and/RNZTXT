Listen live: Wellington choir Nota Bene

Wellington chamber choir Nota Bene is bringing Christmas cheer to Nine to Noon's final show of the year. Listen live

Nota Bene. Photo: RNZ

Wellington chamber choir Nota Bene is bringing Christmas songs to Nine to Noon's final show of the year.

Nine to Noon regulars, comedians Te Radar and Irene Pink, outdoors man and adventurer Kennedy Warne, sports specialist Sam Ackerman and music commentator Kirsten Zemke also join Kathryn Ryan to bid farewell to 2024.
