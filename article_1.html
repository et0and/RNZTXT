Lightning strike causes power outage in Hawke's Bay

A lightning strike has caused a large power outage in Hawke's Bay, Transpower NZ reports.

Power is out in much of Hawke's Bay and Napier. Photo: 123RF

A lightning strike has caused a large power outage in Hawke's Bay, Transpower NZ reports.

The strike hit their equipment and caused a fault, the company said on its Facebook page.

"We have teams now looking into how power can be restored," the company said.

"We'll keep you all updated when we know more."

RNZ listeners have been texting in about the power outage.

"What's happening with the power? Out in Clive, Napier ... perhaps even further," one said.

"No power as far north as Gisborne," another person reported.

MetService has warned of thunderstorms in much of the North Island today.
