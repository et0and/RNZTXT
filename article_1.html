Severe thunderstorm warning for parts of Canterbury

MetService has issued a severe thunderstorm warning for Hurunui and Waimakariri in Canterbury.

Photo: Public Domain via Wikimedia Commons

The Metservice has issued a new severe thunderstorm warning for parts of Canterbury.

Residents of the Hurunui and Waimakariri districts can expect the storms to bring very heavy rain and hail.

The storms are expected to hit Amberly, Rangiora, Ashley Forest, Sefton and Hanmer Springs around 5.20pm.

National Emergency Management Agency advice is to take shelter, preferably indoors but away from windows, secure loose objects, and move cars away from trees.

Meanwhile, a severe thunderstorm watch remains in place for Christchurch, the Canterbury Plains and Canterbury High Country until 7pm.

MetService said heavy showers and thunderstorms could develop, along with a strong southerly change.
