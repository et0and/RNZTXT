Live: Stronger winds yet to come as storm sweeps over Auckland

The city has seen downed trees, power cuts, hail and rapidly plummeting temperatures.

Winds have reached 120km/h in parts of Auckland, but there could be worse to come.

MetService says winds are expected to peak on Wednesday afternoon and evening, with gusts much higher and more frequent than the morning.

It comes after a line of severe thunderstorms rapdily passed over Auckland, bringing hail, power cuts and plunging temperatures.
