Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Crusaders v Rebels

Follow all the action from Apollo Projects Stadium in Christchurch.

Sevu Reece Photo: AAP

Lose this one and the Crusaders are in the unbelievable position of having to potentially win out to make the play-offs.

Given their current form that is unlikely, so all of a sudden, a win over the Rebels is a big deal. But it is certainly no given - while the Rebels have had a favourable draw so far, they aren't in fourth place by accident. Kick-off is at 7.05pm.
