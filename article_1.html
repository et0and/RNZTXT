Armed police in Auckland suburb after two men found injured in vehicle

Forensic specialists are examining a green sedan car in South Auckland after two men were found injured inside.

Photo: RNZ / Finn Blackwell

Forensic specialists are examining a green sedan car in South Auckland after two men were found injured inside.

The vehicle on Addington Avenue in Manurewa, appeared to have crashed into a van.

Part of residential street was cordoned off and armed police were patrolling the road.

The two men who were found with serious injures have been taken to hospital.

Police were asking people to avoid the area but said there was no immediate risk to public safety.
