Pair arrested as police investigate Auckland abduction

Police say the victim, who was abducted in Beach Haven yesterday evening, is recovering from her injuries in hospital.

Police at the scene of a reported kidnapping on Auckland's North Shore, 3 April, 2024. Photo: Jordan Dunn / RNZ

Two people have been arrested following a kidnapping in Auckland's North Shore last night.

Police say the pair were found in central Auckland this afternoon.

A 32-year-old man is facing multiple charges including kidnapping.

A 29-year-old woman was also arrested.

Police say the victim, who was abducted in Beach Haven yesterday evening, is recovering from her injuries in hospital.
