Sam Cane announces retirement from international rugby

He's decided to focus on spending more time with family - and sign a long-term contract with Suntory Goliath in Tokyo.

Sam Cane Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

All Black captain Sam Cane has announced his retirement from international rugby nearly 12 years after making his debut.

The 32-year-old has decided to focus on spending more time with family and sign a long-term contract with Suntory Goliath in Tokyo.
