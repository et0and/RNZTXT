Dozens of people evacuated over suspected chemical leak in central Wellington

About 50 people who were evacuated from their homes in central Wellington after a suspected chemical leak have now been allowed to return home.

Emergency crews at the scene of a suspected chemical leak in central Wellington. Photo: RNZ / Jemima Huston

About 50 people have been evacuated from their homes in central Wellington after a chemical leak.

Emergency services were alerted to the problem around the intersection of Salamanca Road and Kelburn Parade shortly before 9pm.

There were reports of a strong chemical smell and a misty cloud were seen earlier at a property on the road, and that property was evacuated because the occupants had trouble breathing and started coughing.

Everyone within a roughly 200 metre perimeter were forced to leave their properties, but the cordon opened at about 10.45pm.

The substance has not been identified, although the police had described it as a potential gas leak.

People are being asked to avoid the area and anyone who feels unwell should phone 111 immediately.
