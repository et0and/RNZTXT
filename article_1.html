Queues build at Auckland International Airport as network outage hits

The outage is affecting international flight check-ins.

Photo: LDR / Stuff / Stephen Forbes

A network outage is causing queues and backlog at Auckland International Airport.

The airport said an external network outage was creating delays.

Domestic travel was not affected.

However, there was congestion in the international check-in area and processing was slower, the airport said.

Travellers were advised to get in touch with their airlines.

Last month a security breach at the airport caused disruption for travellers as all passengers had to be rescreened.
