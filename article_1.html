Recounts confirm Helen White, Takutai Tarsh Kemp as electorate MPs

The final recount results from the election have come through, confirming the seat results for Tāmaki Makaurau and Mt Albert.

Photo: RNZ / Kymberlee Fernandes

The final recount results from the election have come through, confirming the seat results for Tāmaki Makaurau and Mt Albert.

Neither electorate recount resulted in a change in MP.

Helen White retained the Mt Albert seat over National's Melissa Lee, the margin decreasing by two votes to 18, compared to the Electoral Commission's final result published on 3 November.

Takutai Tarsh Kemp retained the Tāmaki Makaurau seat, increasing from a four-seat margin in the final results to a 42-vote margin.
