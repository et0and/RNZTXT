State of Origin game III live updates: Queensland v New South Wales

Follow all the action from Suncorp Stadium, Brisbane.

Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

The series is tied at one game apiece as the old enemies head to the cauldron-like atmosphere of Suncorp Stadium in Brisbane, where 50,000 Queenslanders will be doing their best to put off a fired up NSW side. History is on the home team's side, NSW have only won a decider at Suncorp twice in Origin's 44 year history.

Kick-off is a 10pm NZT.
