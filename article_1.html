Swathe of Auckland without water after main burst

Suburbs affected include Henderson, Henderson Valley, Glen Eden, Oratia, Kauriland and McLaren Park.

Suburbs affected include Henderson, Henderson Valley, Glen Eden, Oratia, Kauriland and McLaren Park. Photo: Watercare

Several suburbs in Auckland are without water after a water main burst on Thursday night.

The suburbs include Henderson, Henderson Valley, Glen Eden, Oratia, Kauriland and McLaren Park.

Watercare said homes may experience low water pressure or no water at all until the fault is repaired.

Mountain Road in Henderson Valley. Photo: Watercare

Water tankers are available on several streets.

Watercare said it does not yet know when supplies will be restored.
