Live: Warriors v Dragons

The Warriors are looking to back up last Saturday's epic performance as they take on the resurgent St George-Illawarra Dragons.

Addin Fonua-Blake of the Warriors goes in for a try. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

The Warriors travel across the ditch to Wollongong this weekend, taking on the resurgent St George-Illawarra Dragons outfit with both sides being in a similar position on the table.

They're looking to back up last Saturday's epic performance, which resulted in a 22-all draw against the Sea Eagles.

Kick-off is at 8pm NZT.
