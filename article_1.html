Live: Day three and the hīkoi begins crossing Auckland Harbour Bridge

As thousands march two lanes on the bridge are closed as well as some on and off ramps.

Auckland commuters are advised to plan ahead this morning as the hīkoi to protest against the Treaty Principles Bill makes its way over the Harbour Bridge.

Waka Kotahi and police say the two outer northbound lanes closed from 8.30am on Wednesday and would not re-open until around 11am. Some other on- and off-ramps will also be closed until further notice.

The hīkoi which began in Northland on Monday is making its way to Parliament.
