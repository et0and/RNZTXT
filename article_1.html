Fire engulfs industrial building in South Auckland

Police are advising nearby residents to keep their doors and windows closed because of smoke.

(file image). Photo: 123RF

A fire has broken out at an industrial building in Ōtāhuhu.

Emergency services were called to Titi Street just after 10:30am.

A number of surrounding roads were closed, and diversions were in place, police said.

They were advising nearby residents keep their doors and windows closed due to the large volume of smoke in the area.
