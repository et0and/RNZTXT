Search for person who leapt from Wellington Harbour crane

The vintage crane on a boat is permanently moored at the city's waterfront.

The Hikitia floating crane in Wellington. Photo: RNZ / Michael Cropp

A person believed to have jumped from a crane in Wellington Harbour has failed to surface, police say.

The vintage crane is on a boat which is permanently moored at the waterfront.

Police dive squad have been deployed to begin a search.

The incident was reported to police at 12.45pm.

In 2015, the death of a man who jumped off the crane prompted its owners, Maritime Heritage Trust, to look at ways to prevent copycat incidents.
