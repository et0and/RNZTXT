Third charge laid over shoplifting investigation 'subject to public interest'

The charge relates to alleged offending at a Wellington store in October.

Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

A woman faces a third charge as part of a shoplifting investigation "subject to public interest", police say.

The alleged offending took place at a Wellington store on 22 October, 2023.

The woman is due to appear on all three charges in Auckland District Court on 1 February.

Earlier this month, former Green MP Golriz Ghahraman was accused of three counts of shoplifting, prompting her resignation from Parliament.
