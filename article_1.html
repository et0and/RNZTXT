Live: Black Caps v Sri Lanka T20, second match

Follow all the action in the second T20 cricket match between NZ and Sri Lanka here.

New Zealand's Michael Bracewell hits a boundary during his innings in the first T20 international against Sri Lank at Mt Maunganui, 28 December 2024. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

After an unlikely win in the opener, the Black Caps are out to clinch the series with a game to spare at Mt Maunganui's Bay Oval.

The new-look New Zealand team beat Sri Lanka by eight runs on Saturday, after it had looked as if the visitors were cruising in their chase.

Sri Lanka were 121 for no loss in their pursuit of 173 runs, but a Jacob Duffy triple-strike turned the tide of the contest.

The second game begins at 7.15pm today, with the final game at Saxton Oval in Nelson scheduled for 1.15pm on Thursday.
