Man accused of murdering police officer pleads not guilty

But the 32-year-old, accused of killing officer Lyn Fleming, did plead guilty to two driving charges.

The man at a previous appearance. Photo: MARTIN DE RUYTER / Stuff

The man accused of killing Nelson police officer Lyn Fleming has pleaded not guilty to her murder.

The 32-year-old appeared in the High Court at Blenheim on Friday morning.

He pleaded not guilty to murder, attempted murder, causing grievous bodily harm with reckless disregard for safety and three charges of dangerous driving.

He pleaded guilty to two charges - driving while disqualified and driving with a blood alcohol level of more than 80 milligrams per 100 millilitres of blood.

Justice Dale La Hood granted his name suppression to continue.
