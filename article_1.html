Watch live: PM, Health Minister speak to media

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Health Minister Shane Reti are answering questions from reporters ahead of getting their annual flu vaccinations. Follow live.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Health Minister Shane Reti are answering questions from reporters ahead of getting their annual flu vaccinations.

With the pair in Auckland, the usual Cabinet meeting for the week was held online this morning.

Luxon is expected to speak from about 3pm.

It follows the release of the coalition's 36-point action plan for its second quarter, due for completion by 30 June, which includes a mix of concrete tasks and more ambiguous easily achieved objectives.

While 100-day plans have been utilised by numerous former governments, the notion of an ongoing public quarterly programme is much less common but in keeping with Luxon's self-professed CEO-style.
