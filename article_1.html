Live: Communities isolated, some roads still closed and water supply limited

The Transport Agency still has numerous road warnings around Otago and Southland. Follow RNZ's blog for the latest weather updates.

Otago's state highway network is beginning to reopen with the worst of the wild weather now passed.

Light drizzle is continuing to linger but roads and streets are beginning to drain after the deluge which brought Dunedin's wettest day in a century.

There are still a number of road and highway closures.

The Transport Agency has numerous road warnings around Otago and Southland.
