Live: Hīkoi mō te Tiriti heads towards Auckland

Emotions are running high as the hīkoi has been welcomed to Laurie Hill Park in Whangārei by mana whenua.

Te Hīkoi mō te Tiriti in Whangārei. Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

Emotions are running high as the hīkoi has been welcomed to Laurie Hill Park in Whangārei by mana whenua.

Thousands have arrived to support the kaupapa - young and old, tangata whenua and tangata tiriti, all to make a stand for the rights of Māori.

The crowd have joined in waiata beforebeing addressed by rangatira.

An RNZ reporter at the scene says amongst the crowd, emotions are high and tears can be seen in some in people's eyes.
