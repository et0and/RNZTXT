Te Whatu Ora pays back staff $246m of owed $2b

Te Whatu Ora has paid out almost a quarter of a billion dollars owed to staff for breaches of the Holidays Act.

File photo. Photo: 123RF

Te Whatu Ora has paid out almost a quarter of a billion dollars owed to staff for breaches of the Holidays Act.

It is tied up in a many-years-long project to pay back an estimated $2 billion, as well as updating two dozen outdated hospital payroll systems.

It has so far paid $246.5 million to clear 13 years of holiday pay mistakes for 34,000 people.

The 17 districts outside Auckland will not get payouts until next year and are mostly running behind.

Former staff also will not see any payouts until next year at the earliest.
