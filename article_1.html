Reports of armed police at Waitakere Hospital

Flight-tracking sites also showed the presence of a police helicopter circling the region.

Waitakere Hospital. Photo: RNZ / Dan Cook

There are reports of armed police at Waitakere Hospital in West Auckland.

An eyewitness told RNZ they saw at least three officers, of which two appeared to be armed, running toward the staff carpark of the hospital.

Flight-tracking sites also showed the presence of a police helicopter circling the region.
