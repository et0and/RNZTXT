Live: Peters accused Hipkins of a 'bare-faced lie' over mega ferries

The NZ First leader is rejecting claims he was responsible for what Labour says 'wasn't the wisest decision'. Audio

Finance Minister Nicola Willis is insisting the overall cost of replacing the Cook Strait ferries will be cheaper than the previous government's plan.

Minister of Rail Winston Peters outlined the plan on Monday but gave no information on the cost of the new proposal.

The government will buy two ships with tracks for roll-on, roll-off rail freight, to replace its current Interislander fleet in 2029. The coalition had scrapped Labour's IReX mega-ferry project which was projected to cost $4 billion.
