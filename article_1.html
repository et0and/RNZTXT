Major delays as crash causes chaos on Auckland's Southern Motorway

One person has been critically injured and three others seriously injured in the overnight crash.

A screenshot showing where the crash occurred earlier today. Photo: X / NZ Transport Agency

There are major delays northbound on the Southern Motorway this morning after a crash near Otara.

The Great South Rd/ SH20 Link Northbound is closed, along with the Te Irirangi Road and Redoubt Road on ramps northbound.

People are being warned to expect multiple delays.

SH1 SOUTHERN MWY - 6:00AMDue to an earlier serious crash #SH1 northbound lanes are CLOSED at Manukau (from SH20 Link West, including Redoubt Rd & Te Irirangi Dr on-ramps) until East Tamaki Rd on-ramp. Delay your journey or expect delays/diversions. Southbound lanes are open ^TP pic.twitter.com/PNQKUDi4p0

The single vehicle crash happened on the road ahead of Te Irirangi Drive just after 2am on Friday.

One person was taken to hospital in a critical condition and three others are in a serious condition.

NZ Transport Agency says the southbound lanes are open.
