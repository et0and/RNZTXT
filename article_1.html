Christmas weekend traffic: Two dead in accidents

Two people have now died on the roads today, as travellers jam highways in the run-up to Christmas Day.

Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

Two people have now died on the roads today, as travellers jam highways in the run-up to Christmas Day.

There have been three major crashes in the past few hours.

One person was killed in an accident around 1.30pm on State Highway 32, on Whakamaru Road in the Bay of Plenty.

A second person is now confirmed to have died after a crash between a car and motorbike about 12.45pm on State Highway 25 at the 25A turnoff on the Coromandel Peninsula between Hikuai and Whangamata.

That road is likely to be closed another hour, police said just before 4pm.

There were also injuries reported following a crash on State Highway 1, the Waikato Expressway this morning about 10.30am.

Updates on any road closures are available on the NZTA/Waka Kotahi Journey Planner website.
