Live updates: NRL Warriors v Roosters

Follow all the action from Allianz Stadium, Sydney.

Marcelo Montoya of the Warriors Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The Warriors have traditionally struggled in the modern era against powerhouse club Sydney Roosters, who are known for its ability to grind out tight contests.

They welcome back the much-needed experience and no-nonsense attitude of veteran middle Jazz Tevaga, but the massive news is that Roger Tuivasa-Sheck is out for the next four weeks, replaced by Adam Pompey at centre.

Ed Kosi comes in for Marcelo Montoya on the wing. Kick-off is at 4pm.
