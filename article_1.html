People reportedly trapped after crash closes SH2

RNZ understands there are people are trapped at the scene, two people are in a critical condition and another two have suffered serious injuries.

Photo: ELLIE FRANCO

Emergency services are responding to a two-vehicle crash on State Highway 2 (SH2) near Tauranga Road, Waihi, Hauraki District.

Police said emergency services received reports of the crash about 5.40pm.

Initial indications suggest there are serious injuries.

RNZ understands there are people are trapped at the scene, two people are in a critical condition and another two have suffered serious injuries.

SH2 WAIHI - ROAD CLOSED - 6:10PMDue to a serious crash south of Waihi, #SH2 is closed between Waihi Beach Rd & Trig Rd. This section of SH2 is likely to remain closed for some time today. Delay your journey or allow extra time for a detour: https://t.co/R2VLWovsLT. ^ND pic.twitter.com/jO3SxAjjsX

Posts on social media said that three helicopters were reportedly on the way.

SH2 is blocked and diversions are in place at the intersections of SH2, Trig Road South and Waihi Beach Road.

Motorists are advised to take the alternative route.
