Watch live: Luxon in hot seat as Question Time kicks off

The PM is fielding questions in Parliament on a day where a case of mistaken identity has put the heat on police and Health NZ.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon has described the case of an 11-year-old being mistaken as a 20-year-old and put in a mental health unit as "extremely distressing".

"As a parent, you identify with what is a horrific set of circumstances... it's just incredibly distressing and concerning," Luxon told reporters at Parliament.

"Massive amounts of empathy and concern for her and her family."
