Live weather updates: Wairoa damage 'catastrophic', Mark Mitchell says

Six-metre swells aggravated heavy rainfall in the Hawke's Bay and East Coast regions, the Emergency Management Minister says.

Minister for Emergency Management Mark Mitchell is speaking to media from Haumoana Fire Station about the significant weather event effecting the East Coast. Watch live above from about 2pm or follow updates via our blog below.

Mitchell says damage from flooding in Wairoa is "pretty catastrophic".

He intends to try to get to the township and assess the situation first hand. He says the government wants to do everything it can to get support to people on the ground.

He has released $300,000 - with Wairoa, Hastings, and Gisborne, getting a third each - from a mayoral fund.
