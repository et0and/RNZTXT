Three men charged over death of a New Zealand woman in California

Newport Beach police have named the woman as Patricia McKay.

Newport Beach police say three men were charged with homicide, robbery with a firearm and criminal conspiracy. Photo: KCAL News / CBS / Screenshot

Three men have been charged with homicide after the death of a New Zealand woman outside a shopping centre in California yesterday.

The woman was hit by a car and killed during a robbery in Newport Beach on Tuesday (local time).

Newport Beach police have named the woman as Patricia McKay, 68.

In a statement, the police department said two people, one armed with a gun, attempted to rob McKay and another person near Santa Rosa and Newport Centre drives.

The NZ woman was killed outside a shopping centre in Newport Beach. Photo: Supplied / Google Maps

"During the robbery, one suspect fired a handgun three times, though no one was struck by gunfire."

McKay was hit by a car being driven by a third suspect.

"The victim tragically died at the scene," police said.

The three men left in the white sedan and a police chase on the freeway ensued.

The three, all Californians, eventually fled on foot and were arrested.

Leroyernest McCrary, 26, Jaden Cunningham, 18, and Malachi Darnell, 18, were charged with homicide, robbery with a firearm and criminal conspiracy.

Newport Beach police said they were continuing to investigate.
