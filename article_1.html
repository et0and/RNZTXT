Further 'concerning' emails sent to organisations - police

Police say they are confident the emails are coming from the same source and do not believe there is any actual threat.

The email being sent to parents of Ōrewa College students. Photo: RNZ

Another round of the same threatening emails received by hospitals and a school on Thursday has been sent to more organisations this morning, police say.

RNZ understands four hospitals and one school, Auckland's Saint Kentigern College, were searched by police or evacuated as a precaution yesterday.

A police spokesperson has confirmed the same email has been sent to another round of organisations today.

This morning, Auckland's Ōrewa College sent a message to parents saying it was closing immediately due to health and safety.

RNZ understands it may be a target one of the emails.

Police said they are confident the emails are being sent from the same source, and do not believe there is any actual threat to the places receiving them.

Any organisations that receives an email are asked to report it to police to help with the ongoing investigation to find its origin.
