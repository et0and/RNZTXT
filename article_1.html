Olympics live: Dame Lisa v Aimee Fisher in kayak showdown

Will the seven-time gold medallist or the World Cup champion triumph on the water? Follow RNZ's live updates.

New Zealand canoe sprint paddlers Aimee Fisher and Dame Lisa Carrington. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The great Paris Olympics showdown - between seven-time gold medallist Dame Lisa Carrington and World Cup champion Aimee Fisher - is here.

Dame Lisa won the K1 500m at the Tokyo Olympics, however, Fisher has beaten her at the distance twice this year, including at the last regatta before the Olympics, in Poland in late May.

Dame Lisa was the fastest qualifier for the K1 500 semi-finals with a time of 1:48.51 in her heat, while Fisher qualified second-fastest in a time of 1:49.16.
