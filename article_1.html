Live SailGP updates: Drama on water as Denmark tip almost 90 degrees

New Zealand have narrowly missed a place in the final of the SailGP regatta on Auckland Harbour.

Photo: Photosport/Felix Diemer for SailGP

New Zealand have narrowly missed a place in the final of the SailGP regatta on Auckland Harbour.

The Black Foils finished fifth overall after seven fleet races but only the top three boats advanced to this afternoon's decider.

In today's three races, New Zealand placed fourth, sixth and second but their overall score was headed off by Australia, Great Britain and Spain.

Meanwhile, Denmark won their second straight after their close shave in the warm-ups.
