Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Crusaders v Hurricanes

Follow all the action from Apollo Projects Stadium in Christchurch.

Brett Cameron of the Hurricanes takes high ball during Super Rugby Pacific - Hurricanes v Crusaders match. Photo: Elias Rodriguez

It's crunch time for the Crusaders, who have opened their season with three losses in a row for the first time since 1996. Meanwhile, the Hurricanes are flying with three wins in a row, so this game shapes up as a big one for both sides early in this Super Rugby Pacific season.
