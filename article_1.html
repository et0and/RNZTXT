Watch live: Luxon on where 500 new police will be assigned

The Prime Minister is speaking from police HQ after the announcement of where newly recruited officers will be going.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon is set to explain where an additional 500 officers the government has promised to recruit will be assigned.

In a statement, Police Commissioner Andrew Coster said 160 would be put into community beat teams, and another 77 would be added to the National Gang Unit and frontline gang disruption units.

The remaining 263 roles will be in public safety teams, youth policing, and filling vacancies in districts.

Luxon is speaking from the police national HQ in Wellington from about 2.30pm.
