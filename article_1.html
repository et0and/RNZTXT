Incident unfolding in Auckland's Manukau Harbour

Helicopters have been circling over the harbour and the Coastguard is in attendance.

One of the helicopters involved in a search over Manukau Harbour in Auckland. Photo: Screenshot / adsbexchange.com

An emergency servcecs incident is unfolding in Auckland's Manukau Harbour.

A RNZ employee said they could see two helicopters circling the area shortly after 7.30pm on Tuesday.
