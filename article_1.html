Six Nations rugby live updates: Ireland v England

Follow all the action from the Aviva Stadium, Dublin.

Ireland’s James Lowe scores against England. Photo: ©INPHO/Andrew Fosker

The biggest game of the opening weekend of the Six Nations sees England travel to Dublin to take on Ireland in a true heavyweight clash. Both sides will need a win to keep pace with the red-hot France, who demolished Wales yesterday. Kick-off is at 5:45am NZT.
