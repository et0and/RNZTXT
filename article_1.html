NRL live updates: Warriors v Tigers

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Tigers captain Apisai Koroisau and Warriors interchange Jazz Tevaga exchange words. Photo: Bruce Lim /www.photosport.nz

Things were starting to feel very serious around Mt Smart this week at the press conference with coach Andrew Webster and the team all very aware of the challenge that lies ahead.

As it stands, nothing short of winning every game for the rest of the regular season will guarantee the Warriors finals football.
