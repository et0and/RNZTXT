Dozens of fire fighters battle blaze at Hamilton business

There are 12 fire trucks and 35 firefighters trying to extinguish the fire.

A commercial building is on fire in the Hamilton suburb of Frankton Photo: Supplied

Dozens of fire fighters are battling a fire at a large commercial building in Hamilton.

Fire and Emergency (FENZ) has sent 12 fire trucks and 35 firefighters to extinguish the blaze on Ellis Street in the suburb of Frankton.

Photo: Supplied

A FENZ spokesperson said it was too early to determine the cause of the fire.

FENZ said the street is closed and people in neighbouring areas had been evacuated.
