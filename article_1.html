Heavy rain in Hawke's Bay causes flooding, road closures

Hawke's Bay Civil Defence says trees have come down and flood debris is causing hazards on the roads.

Part of State Highway 2 and SH38 were closed after heavy rain in Hawke's Bay caused flooding. Photo: Waka Kotahi

Multiple state highways in the eastern North Island are closed after heavy rain soaked the region overnight.

State Highway 2 is closed between Waikaremoana and Gisborne because of flooding and State Highway 38 is closed from Wairoa to Waikaremoana.

Hawke's Bay Civil Defence said trees had come down and flood debris was causing hazards on the roads. It advised drivers to delay travel if possible.

Nuhaka School in Wairoa is closed for the day because of flooding and road closures.

Principal Raelene McFarlane said it had been a rough night, and her thoughts were with people in Nuhaka and Wairoa who had been affected by the flooding.
