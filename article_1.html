Police car crashes into Crown limo carrying PM

An investigation has been launched after the incident.

Christopher Luxon and Nicola Willis were on their way to Wellington Airport when a police car crashed into the back of their limo. File photo Photo: Marika Khabazi / RNZ

Police say one of its vehicles crashed into a Crown limousine carrying both the Prime Minister and the Finance Minister.

Christopher Luxon and Nicola Willis were on their way to Wellington Airport at about 3.30pm on Wednesday, when a police car crashed into the back of them on Cobham Drive.

A police spokesperson says it was a minor nose-to-tail collision, with no injuries reported, and no members of the public were involved.

An investigation has been launched.

A spokesperson for the Department of Internal Affairs confirmed the crash and said in a statement that the Crown car "sustained damage to the rear of the vehicle".
