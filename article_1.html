Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Chiefs v Crusaders

Follow all the action from FMG Stadium Waikato, Hamilton.

Chay Fihaki of the Crusaders scores a try in the tackle of Xavier Roe of the Chiefs during the Super Rugby Pacific match, Crusaders Vs Chiefs, at the Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch, Photo: John Davidson / www.photosport.nz

Two teams that made a hot start to the season are on course to collide in Hamilton, with the Crusaders desperate to make up for last season's disaster and the Chiefs keen to go one further and win the whole competition. Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
