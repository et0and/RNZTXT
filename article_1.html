US election live: 'It's a cult': Kiwis voting in US describe 'nightmare' election

We catch with New Zealanders in Arizona, as polls show Harris and Trump neck-and-neck in two key states. Video, Audio

The latest polls have vice president Kamala Harris and former president Donald Trump even in the critical southwest states of Arizona and Nevada.

On Wednesday, Harris will give her final speech on the Ellipse - the patch of park where Trump rallied his supporters on 6 January 2021, within view of the White House.

Trump is still working to distance himself from the controversial Madison Square Gardens rally insisting he is "the opposite of a Nazi".
