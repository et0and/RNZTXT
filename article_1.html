Support for Te Pāti Māori has surged in the latest One News Verian poll.

The latest poll has shown Te Pāti Māori is up 3 points while the Green party is down 2 points.

Te Pati Māori co-leaders Rawiri Waititi and Debbie Ngawera-Packer Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

The latest One Newa Verian poll results have been released and show a surge for Te Pāti Māori.

National is stead on 37 per cent, Labour steady on 29 per cent, and ACT steady on 8 per cent.

The Green Party is down 2 points to 10 per cent (its worst result this year) while Te Pāti Māori is up 3 points to 7 per cent.

New Zealand First is up 1 point to 6 per cent.
