Watch live: Marama Davidson speaks at Waitangi on return to politics

It comes two days after the Green Party co-leader announced her return to politics following a breast cancer diagnosis.

Green Party co-leader Marama Davidson is speaking to media at Waitangi.

You can watch Davidson speak from about 4pm via the livestream at the top of this story.

It comes just two days after Davidson announced her return to politics after more than six months following a breast cancer diagnosis.

Waitangi Day will be her first public engagement since returning.

Davidson was diagnosed with breast cancer following a routine mammogram last year, she had a part mastectomy followed by cancer treatment.

In an interview with RNZ's Saturday Morning programme, she said the past few months had been both "challenging and humbling".
