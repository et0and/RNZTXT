Emergency services responding to incident at Auckland Hospital

Fire trucks have converged outside the hospital.

Fire trucks outside Auckland Hospital on 27 December, 2023. Photo: Supplied

Emergency services are responding to an incident at Auckland Hospital.

An eyewitness, who asked not to be named, told RNZ 10 fire trucks were outside the hospital in Grafton on Wednesday.

The witness believes they are attending to a gas leak.

Fire and Emergency's (FENZ) online incident report said eight different brigades were called to Grafton at 10.20am

A spokesperson for FENZ has not been able to confirm details.

Auckland Hospital has not responded to questions about the incident.
