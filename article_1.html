Five injured after buses collide in Auckland

Five people have been injured in a crash between two buses in central Auckland.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Five people have been injured in a crash between two buses in central Auckland.

Police said they were called to the corner of Queen Street and Wellesley Street at 6.30pm.

A spokesperson said two buses were involved in a collision.

Fire and Emergency rescue teams and St John ambulance are also at the scene.

Two people were taken to Auckland Hospital and three others were treated at the scene.
