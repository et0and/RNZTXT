Watch live as Luxon faces questions in Auckland

The Prime Minister and Education Minister have been visiting a school in Browns Bay.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Education Minister Erica Stanford have been visiting Browns Bay School in Auckland's North Shore.

Luxon is due to speak about midday.

It comes after Parliament resumed in Wellington for the New Year this week.
