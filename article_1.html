Live updates: Wellington Phoenix vs Macarthur FC

The Phoenix lead 2-0 but the Premiers Plate won't be decided tonight thanks to Mariners scoring over the Jets - Follow all the action at Sky Stadium in Wellington. Audio

Tim Payne of the Phoenix celebrates after scoring a goal during the A-League Men - Wellington Phoenix v Macarthur FC at Sky Stadium, Wellington. Photo: Masanori Udagawa / Photosport

The A-League has flown the men's minor premiership trophy from Australia to Wellington, and it will be presented to the Phoenix if they beat the Bulls and the Central Coast Mariners lose their match to the Newcastle Jets.

The Mariners and Jets are playing at the same time - we will be providing scoring updates from that game too. Kick-off for both is at 7:15pm NZT.
