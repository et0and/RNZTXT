Watch live: Police announce details about woman found dead on beach

Police are expected to share new information about the mysterious death of Joanna Mai Sione-Lauaki.

Police are set to share new information about the mysterious death of a Dargaville woman.

Joanna Mai Sione-Lauaki, 38, was reported missing on the morning of 2 August after she failed to return home the night before.

She was discovered later that day by a member of the public on Ripiro Beach, on the west coast north of Dargaville.

Police had not given any updates since saying they were treating the death as unexplained.
