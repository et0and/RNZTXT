Bus catches fire on Wellington motorway

Drivers are being warned to slow down on State Highway 1 as they pass the scene.

Photo: Supplied / Waka Kotahi

Fire and Emergency have responded to a Metlink bus fire on the urban motorway north of Wellington.

The crash is blocking two northbound right lanes of the road just before the State Highway 1 and State Highway 2 split.

A FENZ spokesperson said crews assisted ambulance with the bus that was on fire.

They were unable to say how many people were on the bus.

Motorists are advised to reduce speed and pass with care.
