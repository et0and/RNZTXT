FIFA World Cup 2026 qualifier live updates: All Whites v Fiji

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

All Whites celebrate a goal Photo: Shane Wenzlick / www.photosport.nz

The All Whites are just two wins away from booking their ticket to next year's FIFA World Cup in North America. First they need to get past Fiji and another Oceania qualifier on Monday night, needless to say they're going in as heavy favourites. Kick-off is at 7pm NZT.
