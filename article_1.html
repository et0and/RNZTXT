Live: Ice warnings for South Island highways, thick fog blankets Wellington

Visibility is poor in the capital amid fog while drivers in the South Island are being warned of ice on several major highways.

Fog is affecting Wellington this morning (file image). Photo: Twitter / @WgtnCC

Wellington motorists are being warned by The New Zealand Transport Agency to take extra care this morning, as low fog blankets the region's state highways.

Visibility is poor from the central city to Lower Hutt along State Highway 2 and at the Ngauranga Gorge Flyover on State Highway 1.

So far, no warnings have been issued in Auckland - which has been blanketed by fog in recent days.
