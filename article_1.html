Live: SailGP Auckland, day 2 - all the action from the harbour

The Black Foils will want to carry on good form in what is looking like blustering conditions today.

The Black Foils helmed by Peter Burling cross the finish line during Race 4 on Race Day 1 of The Rolex SailGP 2025 Championship. Photo: Iain McGregor for SailGP

After a rough start yesterday, the Black Foils rallied to win the fourth and final race of SailGP Auckland. They'll want to carry on that good form in what is looking like blustering conditions today, racing is scheduled to start at 4pm.
