Museum cordoned off after mysterious package found

Police have put cordons in place around a museum in Napier while they try to find the owner of a package that has been left behind.

The MTG Museum in Hawke's Bay. Photo: Supplied / Napier City Council

Police have put cordons in place around a museum in Napier while they try to find the owner of a package that has been left behind.

The police said they were alerted to the package at MTG Hawke's Bay on the corner of Marine Parade and Tennyson Street about 9.30am.

The museum has closed as a precaution and cordons have been set up around the building.

Police said they are still trying to identify the person who left the package behind.

They are asking people to avoid the area.
