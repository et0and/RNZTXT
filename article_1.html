Man hanging rainbow flag outside Destiny Church reportedly assaulted - police

Auckland police are investigating claims a man was assaulted outside the Destiny Church in Wiri.

(File photo) Photo: 123rf.com

Auckland police are investigating claims a man was assaulted outside the Destiny Church in the Auckland suburb of Wiri.

Police say they responded to a report a fight had broken out between a man and two occupants of a property on Druces Road.

It happened after the man was seen attaching a rainbow flag to the top of a nearby Norfolk pine tree.

The police say they are making enquiries to find out what exactly took place.

They say no injuries were reported and an ambulance was not needed.
