Israel resumes combat against Hamas in Gaza - military

Israel's military says it has resumed combat against Hamas in the Gaza Strip, and accused the Palestinian group of violating the terms of the truce and firing toward Israeli territory.

Smoke billows during the Israeli military bombardment of the northern Gaza Strip on 14 November (file image). Photo: JACK GUEZ / AFP

Israel's military said on Friday that it has resumed combat against Hamas in the Gaza Strip, and accused the Palestinian group of violating the terms of the truce and firing toward Israeli territory.
