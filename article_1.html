Teen dies after New Year's Day shooting

The 19-year-old died after being taken to a hospital in Auckland, while another is still critically hurt.

The teenager who was shot while in a vehicle in Manurewa on 1 January has died in hospital. Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

Police have launched a homicide investigation after the death of a man who was shot in South Auckland in the early hours on New Year's Day.

Officers were called after a 19-year-old was shot while in a vehicle in Addington Avenue, Manurewa at 3.20am.

He was taken to hospital but has since died as a result of his injury.

A 17-year-old male, who also suffered a gunshot wound, remains in hospital in a critical condition.

In a statement, Detective Inspector Karen Bright of Counties Manukau CIB said a scene examination was completed on Tuesday.

"Our focus is now on establishing the circumstances surrounding the incident and supporting both victim's families at this difficult time.

"We know this will have been a shock to the community, however we would like to reassure them this appears to be an isolated incident."

Police are appealing for information.

They want to hear from anyone who was in the Addington Avenue area on the evening of 31 December through to around 3.30am on 1 January, including those with dashcam footage.
