Armed robbers hit North Shore restaurant

The duo stole a number of items, fleeing the scene in a vehicle before police arrived.

Investigators at the scene of a robbery at Tokyo Bay restaurant, North Shore, Auckland. Photo: Finn Blackwell / RNZ

Police are searching for those responsible for an armed robbery at an Auckland on Sunday night.

Police were called just after 9.30pm to Tokyo Bay on The Strand in Takapuna, North Shore after two people entered, armed with weapons and threatened staff.

No injuries were reported.

Police said the weapons were not guns.

The duo stole a number of items, fleeing the scene in a vehicle before police arrived.

A broken door at the scene of a robbery at Tokyo Bay restaurant, North Shore, Auckland. Photo: Finn Blackwell / RNZ

Photos showed glass on the front door of the restaurant smashed in.

They urged members of the public with information to come forward - contact police online or via the 105 phone service and quote job number P061336625.

Information could also be provided anonymously via Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
