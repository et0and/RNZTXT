Watch: Seymour holds stand up in Auckland

Acting Prime Minister David Seymour will answer questions from media.

Acting Prime Minister David Seymour is speaking to media in Auckland on Wednesday afternoon.

Seymour is the acting Prime Minister this week, with Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Winston Peters both overseas.

Luxon is in Vietnam to further trading relationships and grow economic opportunities. He returns on Saturday.

Peters is in the Middle East and North Asia this week and will visit with the Chinese Foreign Minister in Beijing.

Seymour is expected to talk more about charter schools during his media stand-up.

Together the coalition government has set aside $153 million for 15 new charter schools and the conversion of 35 state schools for 2025 and 2026.

Ealier this month, it was reported some state schools could convert to charter school status by the end of this year.
