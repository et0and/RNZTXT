Live: Thousands lose power as Cyclone Alfred nears

The category two system is may to cross the coast on Friday night as residents brace.

Tropical Cyclone Alfred's centre is expected to hit Australia on Friday night or Saturday morning, with impacts already being felt across south-east Queensland and northern NSW.

The category two system was expected to remain of similar intensity until the centre started to interact with the coast and islands.

Energex, the electricity supplier for Queensland, was reporting widespread disruption to its power network on the Gold Coast.
