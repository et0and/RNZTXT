Football: Auckland FC and Adelaide United draw 2-2

Auckland FC have drawn 2-2 with Adelaide United in their clash to climb to the top of the A-League ladder.

Isaias of Adelaide United is tackled by Felipe Gallegos of Auckland FC during the A-League Men Round 14 match between Adelaide United and Auckland FC at Coopers Stadium in Adelaide. Photo: PhotoSport / Matt Turner

Auckland FC have drawn 2-2 with Adelaide United in their clash to climb to the top of the A-League ladder.

The NZ team struck first in their game at Coopers Stadium in Adelaide, with a goal to Neyder Moreno in the 66th minute.

Adelaide then hit back with two goals to Archie Goodwin, before Logan Rogerson scored the equaliser in the dying seconds of the game.
