Fire crews battling blaze in Waitākere Ranges

Three helicopters are responding, and ground crews are unable to access the blaze due to the gradient of the terrain.

A section of Bethells Road (file image). Photo: RNZ / Jean Bell

Fire crews are responding to a bush fire near Te Aute Ridge Road and Bethells road in Waitākere this evening.

Crews were called about 6pm.

Three helicopters are responding, and ground crews are unable to access the blaze due to the gradient of the terrain.
