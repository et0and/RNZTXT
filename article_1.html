Blaze at abandoned building in Wellington

A fire investigator will attend the aftermath of a central Wellington overnight blaze.

FENZ said the fire was extinguished by 2am and the structure was still standing. Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

A fire investigator will attend the aftermath of a central Wellington overnight blaze.

Fire and Emergency New Zealand (FENZ) said it responded to reports of a fire in an abandoned three-storey building on Tasman Street in the suburb of Mount Cook just before midnight on Saturday night.

Nine vehicles responded, including five fire appliances, two ladder trucks and a breathing apparatus unit to put out the blaze.

FENZ said the fire was extinguished by 2am and the structure was still standing.

The fire investigator will be on the scene Sunday morning to determine the cause.
