Test cricket live updates: Black Caps v India day 2

Follow all the action from Maharashtra Cricket Association Stadium, Pune.

Mitchell Santner of New Zealand appeals. Photo: Vipin Pawar / Sportzpics for BCCI

The Black Caps have posted 259 in their first innings and now face the daunting task of getting through the world class Indian top order.

All eyes will be on spinners Ajaz Patel, Mitchell Santner and Glenn Phillips as they will be getting through plenty of overs.

Play starts at 5pm.
