Police negotiators at scene of 'active incident'

Members of the public are being asked to stay away in the town of Murupara.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

A police negotiation team is at the scene in the small town of Murupara in Whakatāne District on Wednesday morning.

Police said officers are responding to an "active incident."

Members of the public are being asked to stay away.
