Taupō school in lockdown amid reports of person with gun

Police have put a cordon up in the suburb of Nukuhau.

Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Police are responding to a report of a person with a gun in the suburb of Nukuhau in Taupō.

The incident is unfolding on Acacia Bay Road.

A nearby school has been locked down as a precaution, police said in a statement.

Cordons are in place blocking the road at Acacia Bay Road and Norman Smith Street.

The public are being asked to avoid the area while police work to resolve the situation safely.

It is the second incident affecting a school this week.

On Tuesday a school in Addington in Christchurch was put into lockdown as police sought a man who was believed to be armed.
