NPC final live updates: Wellington v Bay of Plenty

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

Julian Savea of Wellington dives over to score a try during the NPC Final - Wellington v Bay of Plenty at Sky Stadium, Wellington. Photo: Masanori Udagawa / Photosport

Wellington host this year's National Provincial Championship final against an in-form Bay of Plenty, with the Bay looking for their first NPC title since 1976. Kick-off is at 3:05pm NZT.
