Central Auckland roads cordoned off after gun sighting

Cordons are in place in central Auckland after a gun sighting.

Police on Hobson Street in Auckland. Photo: RNZ/Lucy Xia

Armed police have closed off sections of Hobson and Union streets after an incident at about 8am.

No injuries have been reported.

Photo: RNZ/Marika Khabazi

The public is being asked to avoid the area.
