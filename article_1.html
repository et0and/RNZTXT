Two dead after Christchurch crash

Two people have died following an overnight crash involving two cars and a motorbike in Christchurch.

Two people were killed and three injured after a crash in Christchurch just before midnight on Saturday. Photo: 123rf.com

Two people have died following an overnight crash involving two cars and a motorbike in Christchurch.

Police said they received a report about the crash on Tunnel Road just before midnight on Saturday.

Two other people were critically injured while a third sustained serious injuries, police said.

Tunnel Road remains closed in both directions, from the intersection with Ferry Road to Bridle Path Road and motorists are asked to follow the diversions that are in place.

Police said the circumstances of the crash are being investigated.
