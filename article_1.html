Live: Greens front on Darleen Tana investigation findings

The Green Party leadership is fronting a media conference on the findings of the Darleen Tana investigation. Watch live.

Watch the media conference live at the top of the page and follow along with RNZ's blog below

The Green Party is responding to the findings of an independent investigation into Green MP Darleen Tana.

Tana was suspended by her party leadership on 14 March following accusations about her husband's business.

Independent investigator and barrister Rachel Burt has been carrying out the inquiry. By May, the probe had cost $43,000, from the the Green Party leaders' office budget.

Separately, the Electoral Commission has referred Tana and a publisher to police over an alleged failure to include a promoter statement in an election ad.
