Crash causes taxi to flip on roof in Wellington

Police said the crash happened on Friday afternoon on Wellington Rd and left one car on its roof.

A crash in Wellington involving multiple vehicles blocked a main road on State Highway One this afternoon.

Police said the crash happened before 2.30pm on Friday on Wellington Rd, and left one car on its roof.

The crash closed a lane on State Highway 1, however, the lane has since been reopened.

Motorists were advised earlier on Friday to take diversions on Crawford Rd, Rongotai Rd and Kilbirnie Crescent.

NZTA said drivers should expect delays travelling between downtown Wellington and the eastern suburbs.

Police were yet to determine if anyone was injured.

The crash on Wellington Rd in Wellington left one car on its roof. Photo: NZTA / SUPPLIED

The car which flipped was a Wellington Combined Taxi.

Wellington Combined Taxis contracts manager Harpreet Kohli told RNZ he was made aware of the crash at 3pm.

Kohli said when his staff arrived at the scene ambulance workers and police were attending to their driver.

He said to his knowledge he was not injured badly besides some concussion.

He was taken to hospital for some x-rays, Kohli said.

The company would speak to the driver about the potential cause of the crash when they were able to, he said.
