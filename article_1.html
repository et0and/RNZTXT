Man found dead in Christchurch suburb

Police have launched a homicide probe after finding a 38-year-old's body on a street.

Emergency services were called about 2.30am on Thursday. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Police have launched a homicide probe after finding a 38-year-old man dead in the Christchurch suburb of Aranui.

Emergency services were called about 2.30am on Thursday, after a body was found on Carisbrooke Street near Portchester Street, Detective Inspector Nicola Reeves said.

"Local residents can expect to see a large police presence in the area as a scene examination and area enquiries will commence this [Thursday] morning."

Cordons are expected to be up in the area for some time and Carisbrooke Street is blocked.

"Our focus is now on establishing the circumstances surrounding the incident and notifying and supporting the victim's family at this difficult time," Reeves said.

Police said they wanted to hear from anyone who heard or saw anything unusual in the area about 2am or from anyone who may have CCTV footage.
