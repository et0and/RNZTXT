Fire breaks out in multiple houses in Māngere

A Fire and Emergency spokesperson said they were alerted to the blaze just before 3am.

Generic fire truck. Photo: RNZ / Rebekah Parsons-King

Firefighters are trying to contain a blaze in multiple houses in South Auckland.

A Fire and Emergency spokesperson said they were alerted to the fire just before 3am.

No one is believed to be injured.

At the peak, 14 fire trucks were at the scene in Māngere Bridge.

The spokesperson said they were still working to contain the fire at 6am but was more under control than a few hours earlier.
