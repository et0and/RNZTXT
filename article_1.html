Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Hurricanes v Waratahs

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

Cameron Roigard of the Hurricanes is tackled by Charlie Gamble of the Waratahs Photo: Masanori Udagawa/Photosport
