Live updates: Black Caps v Sri Lanka

Can the Kiwi team defeat the visitors again? All the first ODI action from Wellington's Basin Reserve.

Black Cap Mark Chapman during the T20 series in Mount Maunganui. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The Black Caps are facing off against Sri Lanka again, this time in their first ODI at Wellington's Basin Reserve.

It comes after the Kiwi team trounced the visitors in the T20 series, claiming the win with a game to spare in Mount Maunganui in December.

Play starts at 11am Sunday and runs until 6pm.
