Live: Resident plans to move away after repeated flooding

An Auckland resident who lives on a road that has been repeatedly flooded - including again last night - says it would be risky to spend another winter there.

Flooding in Māngere, south Auckland, after heavy rain on 20 May 2024. Photo: Supplied

A Māngere, Auckland, resident, who lives on a road that has been repeatedly flooded, says she's planning to move away from the area.

Over 160 homes in Māngere were marked partially or fully unliveable after the Auckland Anniversary floods, and the area has again been flooded last night.

Avana Taufitu, who saw her street inundated last night, says it would be risky to spend another winter there.
