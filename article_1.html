Live: Black Caps hunt for victory in Hamilton

New Zealand are resuming hunt for a win in the third test against England, with tourists needing mammoth 658 for victory

Matt Henry takes a catch off his own bowling to dismiss Zak Crawley on the second day of the third cricket test between New Zealand and England at Seddon Park in Hamilton, 15 December. Photo: Photosport

New Zealand have resumed the hunt for a win in the third test against England, with tourists needing mammoth 658 for victory.

Play on day four opened with England on 18/2 with Jacob Bethell on nine and Joe Root yet to score.

Bowler Tim Southee is playing his 107th and last test for New Zealand.
