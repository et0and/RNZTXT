Live: Black Ferns v Australia

Follow all the action from North Harbour Stadium in Auckland.

Katelyn Vahaakolo scored two tries when the Black Ferns played the Wallaroos in September 2023. Photo: DJ Mills / www.photosport.nz

The Black Ferns take on Australia in their final Pacific Four test and opening O'Reilly Cup match - and they've made a raft of changes to their matchday 23.

Canada claimed their first win over the Black Ferns last weekend in Christchurch, taking the Pacific Four Series title off the defending champions and leap-frogging New Zealand to second in the world rankings behind England.
