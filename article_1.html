Police searching for person in Kaikohe after man injured

A man has been injured at a house in Kaikohe in the Far North, prompting a search.

Photo: RNZ

A man has been injured at a house in Kaikohe in the Far North.

Police were called to Tawa Street around 12.20pm on Sunday.

They were unable to say how the man was injured, but are searching for a person.

Police said there was no danger to the public.
