13 people injured, some seriously, in Taupō crash

Two vehicles crashed on State Highway 5 near Rangitaiki School Road, Taupō District.

(File photo) Photo: RNZ / Tom Kitchin

Emergency services are responding to a crash in which multiple people have been seriously injured.

Two vehicles crashed on State Highway 5 near Rangitaiki School Road, Taupō District, about 10.30am.

"Early reports indicate our crews have assessed and treated thirteen patients, with their conditions ranging from moderate to serious," Hato Hone St John says.

Four ambulances, one rapid response unit, and four helicopters were sent to the scene.

"At this time, two patients in serious condition have been airlifted to Waikato hospital, and one patient in a serious condition has been airlifted to Hawke's Bay hospital."

The road is blocked and motorists are advised to expect delays.
