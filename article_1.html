'Supicious' fires at four Masterton churches

"Damage to the churches ranged from moderate to significant," the fire service said.

Anglican Church Of The Epiphany in Masterton. Photo: Google Maps

Four churches were on fire in the Masterton area overnight.

Fire and Emergency New Zealand said fires were started between 4.25am and 4.35am at Anglican Church Of The Epiphany, St Patrick's Catholic Church Masterton, Masterton Baptist Church and Equippers Church Masterton.

"Damage to the churches ranged from moderate to significant," the fire service said.

"The fires are being treated as suspicious and have been referred to police," it said.

FENZ said the fires had crews responding from across the Wairarapa region and from as far away as Avalon Station in Lower Hutt.
