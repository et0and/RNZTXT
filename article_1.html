Hunt on after man pulls gun on police officer

The offender also aimed a gun at a member of the public earlier in the day, police say.

Photo: Google Maps

Police are on the hunt for a man who aimed a firearm at a police officer before fleeing the scene in Highbury in Palmerston North.

At around 8:30pm on Tuesday night police approached a man and established there was a warrant for his arrest. They attempted to take him into custody.

The man then became agitated and presented a firearm to an officer.

Cordons were put up around Brentwood Avenue but officers could not locate him.

Those cordons have since come down.

Earlier that day the same man had also aimed a gun at a member of the public who was walking on Featherston Street.

Police put cordons in place around the area, and armed officers responded, however, he was not located.
