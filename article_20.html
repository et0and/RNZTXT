Dancers to join bassist at Wellington Jazz Festival

Nick Tipping talks with bassist and singer esperanza spalding, who's making her Aotearoa debut at this year's Wellington Jazz Festival. In a Festival first, she'll be joined by two dancers from the New York City-based AntonioBrownDance company. Audio

esperanza spalding Photo: Holly Andres

Bassist, singer and composer esperanza spalding is coming to Aotearoa later this month to perform in the Wellington Jazz Festival. 

She'll be playing with percussionist Eric Doob with Matt Stevens on guitar and, in a Festival first, they'll be joined by two dancers from AntonioBrownDance.

RNZ Concert’s jazz expert (and fellow bass player) Nick Tipping caught up with the five-time GRAMMY-award winning musician recently, and he began by asking what it was about the double bass that attracted her to it in the first place.

esperanza spalding plays at Wellington Opera House on Sunday 20 October, 7pm.
