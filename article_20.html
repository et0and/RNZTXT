LIVE: Lexus Song Quest Final 2024

Watch the five finalists sing for the biggest prize in opera!

The 2024 Lexus Song Quest in association with the Kiri Te Kanawa Foundation is LIVE from 7:30pm Saturday 3 August.

2024 Lexus Song Quest in association with the Kiri Te Kanawa Foundation, runner-up Manase Latu, winner Katie Trigg, third place Austin Haynes Photo: Hagen Hopkins

International Head Judge, Sumi Jo announced the prize winners in the 2024 Lexus Song Quest at the Michael Fowler Centre on Saturday night.

Winner: Katie Trigg

Runner-up: Manase Latu

Third place: Austin Haynes

See the five young Finalists in action as they sing it out for the coveted prize and a share of $92,000.

Each finalist sings one song with piano accompaniment and two arias accompanied by the New Zealand Symphony Orchestra.

The finalists and judge of the 2024 Lexus Song Quest. (L-R) Austin Haynes, Manase Latu, Sumi Jo (head judge), Katie Trigg, Morgan-Andrew King, Tayla Alexander. Photo: Hagen Hopkins / Lexus Song Quest

Finalists: Austin Haynes (counter-tenor), Tayla Alexander (soprano), Morgan-Andrew King (bass), Katie Trigg (mezzo), Manase Latu (tenor)
