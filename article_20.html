Indie pop band Marlin’s Dreaming is live in the RNZ studio to celebrate the release of their fourth studio album HIRL.  

Formed by longtime friends in Dunedin, the new album touches on feelings of both disconnection and inspiration after a move to Tāmaki Makaurau.

Marlin’s Dreaming have toured with Parquet Courts, The Chills and Dope Lemon and have played sold out shows in New Zealand and Australia..

Marlin’s Dreaming upcoming tour dates: 

12/09 - Totara St. , Mount Maunganui 13/09 - Theatre Royal, New Plymouth 14/09 - Meow Nui, Wellington 19/09 - Hollywood Avondale, Auckland 20/09 - Loons, Lyttelton 21/09 - Errick’s, Dunedin
