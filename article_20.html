New Horizons: A Tribute to William Dart

Tim Dodd presents a special edition of New Horizons to pay tribute to the man who has hosted the programme for 45 years, William Dart. We hear from artists, producers and fellow critics who speak about William's unique contribution to music and radio in Aotearoa. Audio

We hear from several who speak about William's unique contribution to music and radio in Aotearoa: artists Charlotte Yates, Dudley Benson, Jan Hellriegel, Plan 9, Bryce Galloway, Matthew Bannister, Graeme Downes & Shayne Carter, fellow music commentators Nick Bollinger & Chris Bourke, and RNZ production staff who helped put the programme together over the years, Barry Hartley and David McCaw.

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.
