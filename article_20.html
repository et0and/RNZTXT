Delaney Davidson speaks honestly about new album

Singer/songwriter and visual artist Delany Davidson gives Charlotte Ryan an intimate introduction to his 10th studio album Out of My Head and explains why it could be his last. 

The highly anticipated album has guest appearances from Marlon Williams (Drive Me Wild) and Reb Fountain (Heaven Is Falling) and co-writes with Phoenix Foundation’s Sam Scott, Hayley Westenra and country singer Miranda Eastern.

Delaney Davidson has been described as an artist that “just appeared one day fully formed”, selling out venues and winning awards such as Arts Laureate 2015, the Aotearoa Music Awards Country Album of the Year - twice - and is four times winner of the NZ Country Music Song of the Year Award. 

Delaney will tour his album ‘Out of My Head’ with five special shows with Chamber Music New Zealand: April 4  Nelson Theatre Royal April 5  Hawkes Bay  Toitoi, Hawkes Bay Arts and Events Centre April 19 Wellington Public Trust Hall April 20 Auckland Rangitira, Q Theatre April 27 Christchurch Great Hall
