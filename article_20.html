LIVE VIDEO: NZCT Chamber Music Contest 2024

Thousands of Kiwi secondary school musicians enjoy the thrill of making music in the largest chamber music event of its kind. Watch the live video stream of the Chamber Music Contest Final here from 6:30pm.

Participants in the NZCT Chamber Music Contest 2023. Photo: Jeffery Wen

Thousands of Kiwi secondary school musicians and composers perform together and enjoy the thrill of making music each year in the largest and longest running chamber music event of its kind.

Watch the live video stream of the NZCT Chamber Music Contest Final here, direct from Wellington’s Michael Fowler Centre starting at 6:30pm on Friday 2 August 2024.

The Chamber Music Contest is always an exciting event in Aotearoa's annual music calendar. It is the largest and longest running youth chamber music competition in Aotearoa and many of its past participants have gone on to successful musical careers both here and overseas. Each year it provides the opportunity for thousands of secondary school students to perform together and enjoy the thrill of making music.

The adjudicators for the 2024 National Final are Bridget Douglas, Monique Lapins and Liam Wooding.

RNZ Concert’s Music Alive host Clarissa Dunn is the MC for the Contest Final beginning 6:30pm on Friday 2 August at Wellington's Michael Fowler Centre.
