LIVE: 2024 Lexus Song Quest - Semi-Final 1

Finding the voices of tomorrow. Welcome to the RNZ Concert live broadcast of the first semi-final of the Lexus Song Quest starting at 6:30pm. Five top young New Zealand opera singers compete for a place in the grand final.

The five finalists in the first semi-final of the Lexus Song Quest 2024. Top: Bass Morgan-Andrew King, tenor Manase Latu, baritone Alfred Fonoti-Fuimoano. Bottom: mezzo Katie Trigg, soprano Madison Horman. Photo: courtesy Lexus Song Quest

Finding the voices of tomorrow - welcome to the RNZ Concert live-broadcast of the first semi-final of the Lexus Song Quest starting at 6:30pm on Saturday 27 July 2024.

RNZ Concert presenter Clarissa Dunn is your host for this event on stage at St Andrew’s on The Terrace, Wellington.

Hear five of the ten semi-finalists: soprano Madison Horman, mezzo Katie Trigg, tenor Manase Latu, baritone Alfred Fonoti-Fuimoano, and bass Morgan-Andrew King compete for a place in the Grand Final, accompanied by pianist Terence Dennis.

Each singer performs two songs and after the interval their chosen aria.

Dame Kiri Te Kanawa, Dame Malvina Major, Jonathan Lemalu, Sol3 Mio’s Amitai Pati and Filipe Manu, are all previous Lexus Song Quest winners who now grace the opera stages of the world.
