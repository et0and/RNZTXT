Lawrence Arabia on performing Sgt. Pepper’s Lonely Hearts Club Band

James Milne (Lawrence Arabia) joins Charlotte Ryan for a deep dive of the album that confirmed the Beatles as the finest craftsmen of their generation. Audio

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

The Come Together series creates Kiwi supergroups to perform the world’s biggest rock albums.   

James Milne (Lawrence Arabia) will join Dianne Swann (When the Cats Away) and The Phoenix Foundation legends Sam Scott and Luke Buda to recreate The Beatles Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band. James joins Charlotte Ryan for a deep dive of the album that confirmed the Beatles as the finest craftsmen of their generation.

Come Together 'Sgt Pepper’s Lonely Heart Club Band' show dates:

Christchurch - Issac Theatre Royal - Aug 30 Wellington - Opera House - Aug 31 Auckland - Kiri Te Kanawa Theatre - Sep 7
