New Horizons: The Power of the Heart

William Dart features an enticing new tribute album reminding us of the magic and the loss of Lou Reed. The Power and the Heart has a stellar line-up of contributors: Keith Richards, Rufus Wainwright, Rosanne Cash, Lucinda Williams, Rickie Lee Jones and others. Audio
