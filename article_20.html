Reb Fountain on the creation of 'How Love Bends'

Reb Fountain gives a personal interview to Nick Bollinger on some of the trials and tribulations behind her new album How Love Bends. Audio

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

American-born, Auckland-based singer-songwriter Reb Fountain has a new album just out, How Love Bends. On the eve of its release, she spoke to Nick Bollinger for Music 101 about some of the trials and tribulations that contributed to its creation.
