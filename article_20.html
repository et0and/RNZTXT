New Horizons: Kiwi Surprise and Joy

In his fourth programme for New Zealand Music Month, William Dart takes a trip around some music from our land that will hopefully spark joy – tracks by Phil Dadson, Billy Farnell, the G&T Trio, Linn Lorkin, Chris Knox, Greg Malcolm and Jenny Ward, Nola McCrorie, and that marvellous Maori show band, The Quin Tikis.
