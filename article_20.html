Celebrating Ireland on the harp

Award-winning Irish harp player Úna ní Fhlannagáin performs live in the RNZ Concert studio ahead of St Patrick's Day. Audio

This audio is not downloadable due to copyright restrictions.

Irish harp player Úna ní Fhlannagáin Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

St Patrick's Day is coming up on 17 March, but we're celebrating it a little early.

Award-winning Irish Harp player Úna ní Fhlannagáin performs live in the RNZ Concert studio, and chats with RNZ Concert's David Morriss.

She's on tour in New Zealand for the next few weeks, with concerts in both the North and South Islands - from Invercargill to Whangateau. Details at una.ie
