New Horizons - Songs and Symphoniques: The Music of Moondog

For many, Moondog was an obscure outsider musician, working on the streets of New York. However a new album of his "songs and symphoniques" might change your mind, especially with Kronos Quartet, the Ghost Train Orchestra, and Rufus Wainwright taking his case. William Dart is enthralled. Audio
