Inside Out: Māreikura Part 1

Some of New Zealand's top jazz women select the female jazz musician who most inspires them. Audio

Chelsea Prastiti and band perform 'Olympic Girls' by Hollie Fullbrook's (Tiny Ruins) Silver Scrolls 2019 Photo: RNZ / Screen grab

 

On this week's two-part show, fifteen of NZ's top women in jazz each select their own female jazz hero. On this hour - Lana Law chooses Ella Fitzgerald, Louisa Williamson's choice is Terri Lyne Carrington, Caitlin Smith goes for Chelsea Prastiti and Chelsea Prastiti nominates Naamah Cheiban.
