Coalition to press ahead with law to make tech giants pay for news

The legislation was introduced by the former Labour government.

Photo: RNZ / Samuel Rillstone

The coalition will progress the Fair Digital News Bargaining Bill, the Media and Communications Minister has confirmed.

The legislation, which will force tech giants to pay for the news content they use, was introduced by the former Labour government.

The National-led government had been considering the bill and on Tuesday morning confirmed it will press ahead with the law, but with amendments.
