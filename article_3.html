A-League live updates: Auckland FC v Western United

Follow all the action from Ironbark Fields, Melbourne.

Guillermo May of Auckland FC. Photo: Mark Evans / www.photosport.nz

Auckland FC are wanting to maintain their table-topping status today in Melbourne, where they face fourth placed Western United.

The last time the sides met, Western United handed the Black Knights their first ever loss, so Steve Corica's men will certainly have that in the back of their minds.

Kick-off is at 8pm NZT.
