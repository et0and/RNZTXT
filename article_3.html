UFC 312 live: Kiwi fighter knocked out in 35 seconds

Follow all the action from the Qudos Bank Arena in Sydney.

Tallison Texeira knocked out NZ/Australian UFC fighter Justin Tafa in just 35 seconds. Photo: Screenshot / X

A middleweight championship rematch between current champion Dricus du Plessis and former champion Sean Strickland headlines UFC 312 in Sydney.

The fighters last met at UFC 297, where du Plessis captured the title by split decision.

Zhang Weili and Tatiana Suarez face off for the Women's Strawweight title, while big men Justin Tafa and Tallison Texeira went at it in a heavyweight fight, but Tafa was knocked down within 35 seconds. The main card starts at 4pm NZT.
