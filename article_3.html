'We are sorting it': Official messages Fred Again about Kiwi DJ's passport application

The message was inappropriate, says Internal Affairs Minister Brooke van Velden.

UK DJ Fred again.. (Fred Gibson) performs at Waitangi Park in Wellington, on Friday 22 March, 2024. Photo: Supplied

A screenshot of a message between an Internal Affairs official and DJ Fred Again. Photo: Screenshot / Instagram
