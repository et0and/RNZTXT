Live: Homes lose power, risk of tornadoes as thunderstorm moves

Downpours and lightning are crossing the North Island.

A MetService screenshot of rain sitting over the North Island and the top of the south at 11.35am today. Photo: MetService screenshot

Thunderstorms have been lashing the Bay of Plenty and Coromandel regions .

MetService says the thunderstorms are moving towards the southeast and will bring downpours and possible tornadoes on on Wednesday. Hundreds of homes earlier lost power in Thames.

MetService says there have been more than 800 lightning strikes over the North Island in the past two hours as thunderstorms move through the Bay of Plenty.
