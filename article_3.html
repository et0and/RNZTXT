Amy Maynard the winner at NZ Gold Guitar Awards

The Hamilton singer has won a fully produced single and video clip and $3k alongside the coveted trophy.

Amy Maynard is the overall winner of the 2024 Gold Guitar Awards in Gore. Photo: RNZ/Samuel Robinson

Amy Maynard from Hamilton has been named the overall winner of the NZ Gold Guitar Awards on Sunday night.

The awards are part of the 10-day Tussock Country festival and see some of our best performers flock to Aotearoa's country music capital, Gore.

Gore's Brendon Fairbairn was named the runner-up.

There were also prizes for best male and female solo, gospel, traditional, new country, country rock, duet, vocal group, New Zealand song and instrumental.
