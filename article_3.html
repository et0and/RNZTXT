Live: Chiefs vs Drua, Super Rugby Pacific

The Chiefs are back home in Hamilton after suffering a shock defeat to the Reds last weekend. Follow along with our live blog.

The Chiefs are back home in Hamilton after suffering a shock defeat to the Reds last weekend.

Coach Clayton McMillan has named a strong team, with Josh Lord set to make his first appearance of the season. Meanwhile, the Drua pulled off their second consecutive home win over the Crusaders in Lautoka, which has kickstarted their campaign after two losses on the road.
