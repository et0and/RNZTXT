Live: Chiefs lead Drua in Fiji

Follow all the action from Churchill Park, Lautoka.

Spectators in the crowd shade themselves with umbrellas from the sun during the round four Super Rugby Pacific match between Fijian Drua and the Chiefs at Churchill Park on March 08, 2025, in Lautoka, Fiji. Photo: Pita Simpson / Getty Images

The unbeaten Chiefs face a tough test as they head to perhaps the most daunting away venue in the competition: Churchill Park, AKA The Graveyard of the Pacific. The Drua and the local weather have forged a formidable reputation at the venue, which should be brimming with local fans. Kick-off is at 4:35pm NZT.
