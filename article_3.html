Car crashes into building causing fire and evacuations

Fire and Emergency says the car caught fire after the crash in Auckland, before the blaze spread to the building.

A car has crashed into a building in Mt Eden and then caught fire in the early hours of Tuesday morning. Photo: NZ Herald/Jason Oxenham

An Auckland building has been evacuated after a car crashed into it, causing a fire.

Emergency services were called to a building on Esplanade Road in Mount Eden just before 5.30am.

Fire and Emergency said a car had crashed into the building and caught fire, which spread to the structure.

Police said a number of people at the property had been evacuated.

The blaze has since been extinguished and a fire investigator is on scene.
