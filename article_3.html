Rockfall closes only route between Otago and West Coast

Travellers face an almost 11-hour detour after rockfall closed part of SH6.

Rockfall near Orman Falls has closed State Highway 6 between Haast and Makarora. Photo: NZTA / Waka Kotahi

Drivers between the West Coast and Otago are being told to postpone their journeys after a massive rockfall closed State Highway 6.

NZ Transport Agency Waka Kotahi said on Tuesday the major route between Haast and Makarora was closed after the rockfall near Ormand Falls.

UPDATE 11:30AMSH6 remains CLOSED between Haast to Makarora due to rockfalls. Please continue to avoid the area and delay your travel along this route. The next update will be at 4pm. ^JP https://t.co/hYUdFX06Xq pic.twitter.com/jdp8zdfzrP

"Drivers are advised to delay or postpone their journeys today - there is no alternative direct route connecting the West Coast and Otago."

Google Maps was showing the trip between Haast and Wānaka would currently take 10 hours 48 minutes, via Arthur's Pass, Geraldine and Twizel.

Travellers between Haast and Wānaka face an almost 11-hour detour after rockfall closed State Highway 6. Photo: Google Maps

NZTA said it would be providing an update at 4pm.
