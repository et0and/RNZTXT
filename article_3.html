MFAT looking into reports of Kiwi killed in Gaza

The foreign affairs ministry says it is aware of reports that a New Zealand-Israeli citizen has died in Gaza.

File photo. A picture taken from southern Israel near the border with the Gaza Strip on 8 December 2023. Photo: AFP / Jack Guez

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade says it is aware of reports that a New Zealand-Israeli citizen has died in Gaza.

A spokesperson said the government agency was currently seeking further details.

The NZ Herald reported that posts about the death had appeared online, with one image showing a wreath dedicated to the mother of a deceased person in Gaza who is identified to be a New Zealand citizen.

"The New Zealand Jewish community stands together with you in this difficult time ... on the falling of your son," a tribute beside the wreath says.
