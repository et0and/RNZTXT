Body found in Hastings believed to be missing man

A body has been found on the shore of Ocean Beach in Hastings.

(file image) Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

A body has been found on the shore of Ocean Beach in Hastings on Sunday morning.

Police said the person was yet to be formally identified but they believed it was the man who had been missing since Friday.

A search had launched on Friday after the man got into trouble in the water about 3pm.

Formal identification and a post-mortem will be carried out on Monday.
