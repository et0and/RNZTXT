Music titan Quincy Jones dies at 91.

Legendary American music producer and composer Quincy Jones has died at the age of 91. Audio

Quincy Jones in 2019. Photo: AFP / Arnold Turner

Legendary American music producer and composer Quincy Jones has died at the age of 91.

Jones was a major influence on American music, with artists ranging from Count Basie to Frank Sinatra.

He then reshaped pop music by collaborating with Michael Jackson.

He died on Sunday, his publicist said.
