Live: Black Caps v Sri Lanka ODI - game three at Eden Park

New Zealand are chasing a series whitewash against the tourists.

New Zealand's Matt Henry fielding during the third ODI cricket match between New Zealand and Sri Lanka at Eden Park in Auckland on 11 January. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

With the one-day series already wrapped up, the Black Caps are chasing a whitewash over tourists Sri Lanka in the final match at Eden Park today.

New Zealand won the second ODI at Seddon Park by 113 runs, to go along with their nine-wicket victory in the series opener in Wellington.

The Black Caps will be keen to carry over their dominance, as players fight for Champions Trophy selection, which starts in February.

For the visitors, they are chasing just their second win on tour against New Zealand, having also lost the T20 series.
