All Whites v Tahiti: Live updates

Follow all the OFC Nations Cup semi-final action from VFF Freshwater Stadium in Port Vila.

New Zealand's Cam Howieson. Photo: Shane Wenzlick / Photosport

The All Whites take on Tahiti in the OFC Nations Cup semi-final action from VFF Freshwater Stadium in Port Vila, Vanuatu. They finished top of Group A with a 3-0 win over Solomon Islands and a 4-0 victory over hosts Vantuatu, with the final set for Sunday afternoon.

The other semi-final sees Fiji take on Vanuatu later today.
