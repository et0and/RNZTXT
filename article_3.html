Live: Severe thunderstorm and strong wind warnings for the north

Power cuts are possible across the upper North Island as severe gales are expected to hit this afternoon.

A strong wind watch for the upper North Island has been upgraded to an orange strong wind warning.

Severe gusts of up to 120km/h are possible until 9pm this evening, MetService said.

A severe thunderstorm watch has also been issued for Northland, Auckland, Great Barrier Island and Coromandel Peninsula until 9pm.

Meanwhile, the Auckland Harbour Bridge has had lanes reduced as a result of the severe wind gusts.

Earlier, highways across the South Island were closed by heavy snow.
