NRL live updates: Warriors v Eels

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Jake Tago of the Parramatta Eels, Dallin Watene-Zelezniak of the Warriors and Charnze Nicoll-Klokstad of the Warriors. Photo: Andrew Cornaga / www.photosport.nz

With tough games against the Dolphins, Manly, Bulldogs and the Sharks on the horizon, the Warriors will need to use tonight's game to get their attack clicking against a lesser ranked opponent.

In saying that, the Eels will be playing with nothing to lose so the Warriors shouldn't take them lightly. Kick-off is at 8pm NZT.
