Live: Peters' Gaza comments long overdue - Labour

The Foreign Minister has accused the UN Security Council of failing in its responsibilities.

The government has announced the Ministry of Education will be required to publish weekly school attendance statistics as part of its crackdown on truancy.

The policy also includes a proposal for a "traffic light" system for student attendance.

Meanwhile, Prime Minister Christopher Luxon is defending the announcement of new public sector targets for health, crime and education at the same time job cuts are being announced in the civil service.

Earlier, Foreign Minister Winston Peters heavily criticised the UN Security Council over the situation in Gaza.
