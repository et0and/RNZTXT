Wellington road closed due following serious crash

One person is seriously injured following a two-vehicle crash in Wellington.

File photo. Photo: RNZ / Tim Brown

One person is seriously injured following a two-vehicle crash in Wellington.

The crash, reported at 12.36pm on Sunday, has closed Paekakariki Hill Road.

Police said the serious crash unit has been informed.

Motorists are being advised to avoid the area.
