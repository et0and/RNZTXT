Moana have taken this game to the Blues' home in the hope of attracting a decent crowd, but also to try and replicate their effort at Eden Park last year.

They were leading by six as time expired, but lost two men to the bin and conceded a penalty try off the last play of the game.

Moana are a better side than that one this time around, but it's unlikely the Blues will be taking them lightly. Kickoff is at 4:35pm.
