Photographers' perspective: Shooting at Trump rally

Amid a sea of red caps, the crowd dropped as shots rang out while Trump spoke. Here's what photographers captured of the events.

Chaos unfolded at the election rally, where people in the crowd can be seen scrambling for cover and searching for the source of the bangs. Photo: Rebecca DROKE / AFP

Amid a sea of red caps, the crowd dropped as a wave as shots rang out and Secret Service agents swarmed over former US President Donald Trump to protect him.

The scenes, at a Republican election campaign rally in Butler, Pennsylvania, have shocked the world.
