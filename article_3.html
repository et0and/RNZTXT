Residents evacuated after fire in Auckland apartment building

Smoke can bee seen at the top of the building on Cook and Nelson streets.

Fire and Emergency were at an Auckland apartment building that had smoke coming out of it. Photo: Lucy Xia/RNZ

At least 100 residents have been evacuated from a central Auckland apartment block after a fire Thursday morning.

Smoke could be seen coming out of the top of the building, and from a vent on the roadside.

Five fire trucks were at the scene at the corner of Cook and Nelson streets.

More than 100 residents were evacuated. Photo: Lucy Xia/RNZ
