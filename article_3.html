Freight train hits person at Invercargill pedestrian crossing

A freight train has hit a person at a pedestrian crossing in Invercargill.

File picture. Photo: 123rf.com

A freight train has hit a person at a pedestrian crossing in Invercargill near Elles Rd.

Police said they were called to the scene near Turnbull Thomson Park at 5.20pm.

St John said they sent one ambulance, an operations manager and a rapid response unit to the scene.

Police have not confirmed whether the person has died.

Elles Rd is closed between Tweed and Forth St while emergency services attend.

KiwiRail said the person was hit on the Ness St crossing which had a pedestrian maze and signage.

They also confirmed the train was only carrying coal at the time and the train driver is being offered support.
