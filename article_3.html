Follow all the action from FMG Stadium Waikato, in Hamilton.

As it stands, the result of this match may be the deciding factor in who finishes above who, and therefore who may well host a semi-final rematch.

The Canes did a number on the Chiefs in Wellington the last time they met, in what was arguably their best performance of the season. However, the Chiefs are banking on a big crowd and a very strong looking line-up.

Kick-off is at 7.05pm NZT.
