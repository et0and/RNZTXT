Heavy winds force cruise ship to turn around from Wellington

The Ovation of the Seas cruise ship has turned around from Wellington because of the wind.

The cruise ship 'Ovation of the Seas' in Bremerhaven, Germany in 2016. Photo: AFP / dpa Picture-Alliance / DPA

The Ovation of the Seas cruise ship has turned around from Wellington this morning because of the wind.

Wellington's Harbourmaster said the ship's captain likely thought it would to be too windy for the massive ship to remain in port all day.
