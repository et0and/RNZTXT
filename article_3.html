England lose two wickets, surpass NZ total

Century-maker Harry Brook has been dismissed this morning in the first test at Hagley Oval, in Christchurch.

Glenn Phillips on the second day of the first cricket test between New Zealand and England at Hagley Oval in Christchurch. Photo: Chris Symes / www.photosport.nz

Century-maker Harry Brook has been dismissed for 171 this morning in the first test between New Zealand and England at Hagley Oval, in Christchurch.

Matt Henry took the wicket.

At the start of play on day three, England pair Brook and Ben Stokes returned to the crease at 319/5 as their side closed in on the Black Caps' first innings total of 348.

Wickets are desperately needed, otherwise it may well be an uphill battle at New Zealand's next turn at bat.
