One person injured following Hamilton bus crash

Emergency services are responding to a bus crash near Hamilton.

File photo. Photo: RNZ / Robin Martin

Emergency services are responding to a bus crash near Hamilton, that has left at least one person injured.

The bus came off the road at Whatawhata, at the intersection of SH23 and Te Pahu Road, at about 11.40am this morning.

One person has sustained minor injuries.

Police said the road was slightly blocked and motorists are being warned to expect delays.
