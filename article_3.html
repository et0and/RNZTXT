Crash snarls Auckland Harbour Bridge traffic

A person is in a critical condition after a crash involving a truck and a motorbike on Auckland's Harbour Bridge.

Photo: Supplied / Waka Kotahi

A person is in a critical condition after a crash involving a truck and a motorbike on Auckland's Harbour Bridge this afternoon.

Emergency services were called to the bridge just after 2pm on Friday, and one person was taken to hospital.

Two southbound lanes had to be closed and motorists have been advised to avoid the bridge - or expect delays.

All lanes have since reopened.

FINAL UPDATE - 3:05PMAll lanes are now OPEN on the Harbour Bridge. Allow extra time for delays in the area to ease. ^HJ https://t.co/NzsR61m34W

Traffic was heavy on and around the bridge, Google Maps showed. Traffic was also moving slowly on State Highway 1 on the North Shore.
