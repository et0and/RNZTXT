Smoke from Parnell fire seen throughout central Auckland

Fire and Emergency New Zealand crews are responding to a residential structure fire in Parnell, Auckland.

Emergency crews in Auckland are battling a blaze at a residential building in the central suburb of Parnell.

Fire and Emergency staff were called to the scene just after 4.30pm Sunday.

There are ten trucks working to bring the structure fire under control.

A FENZ spokesperson said the fire is well-involved, and Saint John services said no injuries have been reported.

FENZ is asking the public to avoid the area.
