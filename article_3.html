Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Blues v Highlanders

Follow all the action from Eden Park, Auckland,

Sam Gilbert of the Highlanders. Photo: James Ross / www.photosport.nz

The Blues have seen fit to rotate their side a bit but they will need to still be able to hit the high gears if the Highlanders can play the way they did last weekend. It's probably a bit early to anoint Cam Millar as the answer to their problems, but he certainly controlled the Highlanders backline well in their memorable win over the Crusaders. Kick-off is at 7.05pm NZT.
