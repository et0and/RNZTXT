Cricket live updates: Black Caps v Pakistan

Follow all the action of the first ODI from McLean Park, Napier.

Daryl Mitchell during the Black Caps v Pakistan 4th T20I at Bay Oval, Tauranga. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

Fresh off a big 4-1 series win in the T20 series, the Black Caps are looking to heap more misery on Pakistan in a three-match one day international series as well. The action starts at McLean Park in Napier at 11am NZT.
