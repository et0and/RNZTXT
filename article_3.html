SH6 on West Coast closed after collision involving a bus

At least one person has been seriously injured after a crash between a bus and a vehicle this evening.

Photo: Waka Kotahi / NZ Transport Agency

At least one person has been seriously injured after a crash between a bus and a vehicle on the West Coast of the South Island on Friday evening.

Emergency services were called to the crash on State Highway 6, near the Kakapotahi River, south of Hokitika, about 6.30pm.

Fire and Emergency says a person was trapped inside a vehicle, but has now been freed.

It says it understands there were multiple people injured.

Police say one person has been seriously injured.

NZTA says the crash has blocked the road north of Waitaha Road.

It is warning motorists of delays and appealing to them to follow the directions of emergency services who are at the site.
