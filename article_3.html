Further 'concerning' emails sent to organisations, schools in Auckland

Murray's Bay Primary School on Auckland's North Shore has been evacuated after receiving a threatening email.

Photo: Screenshot / Google Maps

Murray's Bay Primary School on Auckland's North Shore has been evacuated after receiving a threatening email.

Staff and students have been taken to the gymnasium at Murray's Bay Intermediate School and families are asked to pick their children up from the entrance on Sunrise Ave.

The school will be closed for the rest of the day.

Police have told the school it will take some time to deal with the incident.

Browns Bay School has also closed "due to the threat".

People were asked to stay away from school grounds, a statement on the school's website said.

Sherwood School has closed and said this was due to a "bomb threat we received via email".
