Police asking for help to find missing Christchurch boy

Darcy was last seen near the Washington Way skate park on Sunday.

Police say this is the most up-to-date photo available of Darcy, who has been reported missing from his residence in the Christchurch suburb of St Albans. Photo: Supplied / New Zealand Police

Police are asking for help in finding Darcy, who has been reported missing from his home in the Christchurch suburb of St Albans.

He was last seen near the Washington Way skate park in Sydenham on Sunday afternoon.

Police would not reveal his age, and said the photo was the most up-to-date available.

A number of enquiries have been carried out since Darcy was reported missing, but he has not yet been located, a spokesperson said.

Anyone who has seen Darcy or has any information about where he could be is asked to call 111 immediately, quoting reference number 241013/1760.
