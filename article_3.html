Dunedin school in lockdown after email threat

Bayfield High School was placed into lockdown as a precaution.

Armed police outside Bayfield High School, Dunedin. Photo: RNZ / Tim Brown

Armed police are at a cordon outside a Dunedin high school which has been into lockdown after receiving an emailed threat.

Police said they were advised of the threat to Bayfield High School at 8.45am Tuesday.

The school was placed in lockdown as a precaution.

A social media post from the school said all students were safe.

It asked parents and caregivers to not come to the school grounds and said an update would provided as soon as possible.

Some senior students were scheduled to sit exams on Tuesday.
