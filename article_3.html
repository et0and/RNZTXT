Cricket: Woeful India dismissed for 46 by New Zealand

India were bowled out for 46 as NZ's fast bowlers ran riot in overcast conditions on the second day of their rain-hit series opener on Thursday.

New Zealand players celebrates the wicket Sarfaraz Khan of India during day 2 of the 1st Test Match between India and New Zealand held at the M.Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru, India on the 17th October 2024 Photo by Vipin Pawar/ Sportzpics for BCCI Photo: Photosport

India were bowled out for 46 as New Zealand's fast bowlers ran riot in overcast conditions on the second day of their rain-hit series opener on Thursday.

It was India's third-lowest innings tally in the longest format and their lowest at home since making 75 against the West Indies in New Delhi in 1987.

Matt Henry took 5-15 while William O'Rourke claimed 4-22.
