Armed police cordon off street in seaside community

Armed police have cordoned off a street in the isolated seaside community of Mākara.

The Police Armed Offenders Squad and armed police have responded to a report of a person acting threateningly with a gun in a small Wellington community. (File photo) Photo: RNZ / Angus Dreaver

Armed police have cordoned off a street in the isolated seaside community of Mākara.

Wellington police were called to a house there just before 2pm on Waitangi Day, following reports a person was threatened with a firearm.

Mākara Road was closed, with one cordon near Mākara Model School and a second at the intersection with Takarau Gorge Road.

There were no reports of injuries, police said, but the armed offenders squad had been deployed as precaution.
