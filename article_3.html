Body found in Christchurch's Avon River

Emergency services were called to an area near Riccarton Avenue on Friday morning.

Police have brought stretchers, gumboots and waders into the cordoned off area of Hagley Park in Christchurch. Photo: RNZ / Tess Brunton

A body has been found in a river in central Christchurch.

Emergency services were called to the scene after 10.30am on Friday.

The police were called to an area of the Avon River near Riccarton Ave.

Officers have brought stretchers, gumboots and waders into the cordoned off area.

Police confirmed to Stuff that a body had been found in the river near the botanic gardens in North Hagley Park.

An area of park next to Riccarton Ave has been cordoned off and multiple police cars and officers are at the scene. Photo: RNZ / Tess Brunton
