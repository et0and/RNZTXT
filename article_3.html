What movies got plane travellers buzzing this year?

Air New Zealand's tally of their most popular in-flight viewing for the year shows their passengers want their ride comfortable, but their viewing full of thrills and spills.

Jason Statham's beekeeper-turned-vigilante topped the charts for Air NZ this year. Photo: MIRAMAX / AFP

Air New Zealand's tally of their most popular in-flight viewing for the year shows their passengers want their ride comfortable, but their viewing full of thrills and spills.

Jason Statham's action thriller The Beekeeper was the most chosen viewing for 2024, while other top picks included Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga, Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes and stunt man thriller The Fall Guy.

For those not picking up on the thrills and spills vibe, humour was the next most popular choice to tide them through until they touched down, with romantic comedy Anyone But You, starring Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell, stealing second place. While comedy sitcoms The Big Bang Theory and Friends were also popular.

Littlies on board were loyal to Aussie cartoon Bluey, which had topped their watch list last year, but Peppa Pig was also well watched.

The Beekeeper - 128,607 views

Dune: Part Two - 102,663 views

Napoleon - 95,909 views

Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga - 95,318 views

Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes - 90,840 views

Mean Girls - 89,608 views

The Equalizer 3 - 84,893 views

The Fall Guy - 79,437 views

One Life - 78,343 views
