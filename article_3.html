Recap: Fiji falls short as France claim Sevens gold

France claimed their first Olympic Games sevens rugby gold medal with a 28-7 win over Fiji at the Stade de France this morning.

Fijiâ€™s Terio Veilawa launches an attack against the France defense on day two of the Paris 2024 Olympic Games at Stade de France on 25 July 2024 in Paris. Photo: World Rugby / Mike Lee - KLC

The Fiji men's sevens team's reign as Olympic champions is over - beaten 28-7 by France in the gold medal match at Stade de France this morning.

Fiji scored first and early, but that was the last time they crossed the tryline.

The French scored four tries, including a double to Antoine Dupont.

In Fiji's semifinal against Australia earlier this morning, it was 7 - 7 at halftime before Fij pulled away in the seond half to win 31 - 7.

South Africa earlier claimed the bronze medal over Australia.
