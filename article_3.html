Man dies after being shot in Clendon Park, homicide investigation launched

No arrests have been made at this stage.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

A person has died after being shot in Clendon Park in South Auckland on Thursday afternoon.

Police have confirmed the man entered Clendon Park Medical Centre with injuries, but sustained the gunshot wound at another location.
