Police respond to assault in Ngāruawāhia

Police say a person has been critically injured in an assault in the Waikato town of Ngāruawāhia this afternoon.

Photo: RNZ / Nate McKinnon

Police say a person has been critically injured in an assault in the Waikato town of Ngāruawāhia this afternoon.

Emergency services were called to a fight involving several people on Waipa Esplanade near the intersection with Newton Street about 1.30pm.

Police said minutes later a critically injured person was taken to an ambulance station on nearby Jordan Street.

St John said it was called to the assault, and Fire and Emergency says it was also called to assist ambulance staff.

They said there is a large police presence in the area and cordons are in place at both scenes.

Members of the public are asked to avoid the area, police said.
