Live: Chiefs v Eagles in Superbowl LIX

Follow all the action from Caesar's Superdome in New Orleans.

The Lombardi Trophy next to Kansas City Chiefs and Philadelphia Eagles helmets. Photo: Michael Owens/Getty Images

We're here: the biggest day in American sport sees the defending champion Kansas City Chiefs face the Phidelphia Eagles in the Superbowl. This year's game is down in The Big Easy, New Orleans, but the challenge will be anything but for these two highly talented sides.

There will also be a multitide of celebrities attending, including US President Donald Trump and the world's most famous football fan, Taylor Swift. Kendrick Lamar will be performing at halftime. Kick-off is at 12:30pm NZT.
