Live: Black Caps claim key scalp just before tea break

The New Zealanders have elected to bowl in the second test against England at the Basin Reserve.

Harry Brook bats on the first day of the second cricket test between New Zealand and England at the Basin Reserve, Wellington. Photo: Kerry Marshall/www.photosport.nz

England head into the second test holding a 1-0 series lead after a very comfortable victory at Hagley Oval. Despite the Basin pitch having a bit of turn, the Black Caps have resisted the urge to change their lineup, so the pressure is on the bowling unit to come up with some spells of dominance - and everyone to hold onto some catches. Play starts at 11am.
