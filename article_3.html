Wanted woman missing with her three young children

Police say 36-year-old Emma Ferguson is somewhere in the North Island.

Emma Ferguson Photo: NZ POLICE

A woman wanted by police is missing from Auckland with her three young children.

Police have said there is a warrant to arrest 36-year-old Emma Ferguson from Mount Roskill.

It's believed she is somewhere in the upper North Island with her children who are aged one, two and five.

"Police have concerns for her children's safety," the spokesperson said.

Anyone who has information on Ferguson's whereabouts is asked to contact police on 111 and quote file number 240403/6972.

Information can also be provided anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
