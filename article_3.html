Dunedin high school in lockdown

Families were asked to keep students at home if they had not yet reached school.

Bayfield High School Photo: Google Maps

Bayfield High School in Dunedin is in lockdown.

A message on the school's website said says all students on site would be held safely.

Families were asked to keep students at home if they had not yet reached school.

Stuff has reported there was a large police presence in the area.
