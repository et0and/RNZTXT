A-League live updates: Auckland FC v Macarthur

Follow all the Unite Round action from Allianz Stadium, Sydney.

Auckland FC's Nando Pijnaker celebrates scoring the winning goal in their A-League match against Sydney FC. Photo: photosport

Auckland FC are back in action after a long break thanks to a bye and international breaks. They face Macarthur in one of the final matches of this weekend's Unite Round, played entirely at Allianz Stadium in Sydney. Kick-off is at 6pm NZT.
