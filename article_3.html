Live: TVNZ youth platform Re: News faces cuts

It's not just TV current affairs facing cuts at TVNZ - follow the latest developments here.

TVNZ has confirmed to staff which programmes are likely to be axed in a proposed restructuring.

The broadcaster is proposing to cancel current affairs show Sunday, after earlier meeting with staff, as well as Fair Go, the midday news and 1News Tonight.

RNZ understands youth platform Re: News is also being trimmed back significantly.
