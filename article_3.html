Live: Biden to address U.S. after Trump's election victory

It is the first time the sitting president has spoken after projections showed Donald Trump would retake the White House.

President Joe Biden at a pre-election campaign rally for Kamala Harris and running mate Tim Walz. Photo: AFP / Ting Shen

United States President Joe Biden is due to address the nation after Republican Donald Trump stormed to victory in the presidential elections.

Projections have Trump returning to the White House after clinching a number of battleground states in the country's east.

Biden will speak about 5am Friday NZT.

On Thursday, Democratic nominee Kamala Harris called Trump to concede the race.
