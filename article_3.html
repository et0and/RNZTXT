UFC 312 live updates: Du Plessis v Strickland 2

Follow all the action from the Qudos Bank Arena in Sydney.

Sean Strickland of the United States fights against Dricus Du Plessis of South Africa in a middleweight title bout during UFC 297. Photo: Vaughn Ridley/Getty Images

A middleweight championship rematch between current champion Dricus du Plessis and former champion Sean Strickland headlines UFC 312 in Sydney.

The fighters last met at UFC 297, where du Plessis captured the title by split decision.

Zhang Weili and Tatiana Suarez face off for the Women's Strawweight title, while big men Justin Tafa and Tallison Texeira will go at it in a heavyweight fight. The main card starts at 4pm NZT.
