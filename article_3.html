Person seriously injured in Auckland crash, part of SH16 closed

Part of a main road linking Auckland to Helensville has been closed after a "serious" crash.

The intersection of State Highway 16 and Wharepapa Road. Photo: Google Maps

Part of a main road linking Auckland to Helensville has been closed after a "serious" two-vehicle crash.

It happened at the intersection of State Highway 16 and Wharepapa Road on Monday just before 7.30am.

Police said one person was seriously injured, and another moderately.

A section of SH16 is closed.

"Diversions are in place and motorists are asked to follow signage in the area," police said.

Fire and Emergency NZ attended the scene but said no one was trapped.
