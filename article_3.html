Hamilton schools in lockdown as police respond to reported gun sighting

Schools are locked down as a precaution.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

Two schools in Hamilton are in lockdown as police respond to a reported firearms sighting on Silverdale Road.

Police said some staff are armed as a precaution and a cordon is in place.

Members of the public are advised to avoid the area.

Hillcrest High School went into Lockdown at 3:13pm, just minutes before the end of the school day.

Parents and caregivers are being asked to not go to the school.

Buses will return to school when the lockdown ends to take bus students home, the school said.

Silverdale Road, Hamilton. Photo: Google Maps

RNZ understands Silverdale Normal has also been affected.

People are reporting roads around the schools are being blocked by police.

The schools are in lockdown as a precaution, police said.
