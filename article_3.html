In photos: All the red carpet looks from the Aotearoa Music Awards

The who's who of NZ's music industry are out in force for the Aotearoa Music Awards. Here are all the red carpet looks.

MC Tali at the Aotearoa Music Awards. Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

The who's who of New Zealand's music industry were out in force for the Aotearoa Music Awards on Thursday night.

The awards, which have been presented annually since 1965, recognise the biggest musical successes of the year, promote emerging artists and highlight the mahi of those who work behind the scenes.

Many sports stars, politicians and media personalities have been known to attend the awards too. Past AMA attendees include the likes of MMA fighter Israel Adesanya and former New Zealand prime minister Jacinda Ardern.

Here are the red carpet looks from the awards.
