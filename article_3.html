Delays expected after multi-vehicle crash on Auckland's Southern Motorway

The crash happened on SH1 between Papakura and Drury near the Beach Road interchange.

Photo: RNZ / Tom Kitchin

Police are responding to reports of a multi-vehicle crash on the Southern Motorway in Auckland.

The crash happened on State Highway 1 between Papakura and Drury near the Beach Road interchange which is now closed.

Police say two people have been seriously injured.

Motorists are asked to avoid the area and detours are in place.

The transport agency is advising motorists to expect delays.
