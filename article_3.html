Two hospitalised after quad bike accident in Waimārama

Two people are in hospital in a serious condition after a quad bike crashed in Hawke's Bay this afternoon.

Emergency services were called to the scene of a quad bike crash in Waimārama just before 2pm. File photo. Photo: 123rf.com

Two people are in hospital in a serious condition after a quad bike crashed in Hawke's Bay this afternoon.

Emergency services were called to the scene in Waimārama, just outside Havelock North, just before 2pm.

Hato Hone St John took two patients in a serious condition to Hawke's Bay Hospital.

One patient in a moderate condition was treated at the scene.
