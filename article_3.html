Metal Yoda sculpture stolen from backyard

Police are asking for the public's help to find the sculpture stolen from a Wellington property.

The stolen sculpture of Yoda. Photo: Facebook/Police

A 1.2 metre tall metal sculpture of Yoda has been stolen from a Madras Street, Khandallah property on Tuesday night.

The sculpture, made from steel, took the creator hundreds of hours to craft.

Police said the sculpture would not have a high scrap metal value, and the theft has left the owner understandably upset.

If you have any information that may be useful to police, contact them online or call 105.

Please use the reference number 250220/2990.
