Firefighters injured in wall collapse near Hawke's Bay station

Five people have been injured.

Photo: Google Maps

Two firefighters and three locals have been injured after a wall collapse next to a Hawke's Bay fire station, RNZ understands,

Several fire crews were at the scene on Ohurakura Road in Te Pohue onThursday.

Two people have serious injuries and three with moderate injuries. One of the injured has been flown to Hawke's Bay Hospital in Hastings.

Fire and Emergency said two of the five are local volunteer firefighters and they are supporting them and their families.

They said the group had been assisting to erect a structure on private land.

A local told RNZ they heard sirens and had seen a portable home being delivered to the Te Pohue firestation on Wednesday.
