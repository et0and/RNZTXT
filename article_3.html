Police hunt man with 'evil' tattooed on his face

Police want the public's help to find Regan McKinley, who is wanted for breaching his court release conditions.

Police said Regan McKinley was considered dangerous. Photo: NZ POLICE

A manhunt is under way in West Auckland for a man with the word 'evil' tattooed across his face.

Police wanted help from the public to find Regan McKinley, 34, who they said was considered dangerous.

McKinley was wanted for breaching his court release conditions.

Police said McKinley shouldn't be approached and anyone who had seen him or had information on his whereabouts should contact them on 111 or anonymously through Crime Stoppers on 0800 555 111.
