Cricket live updates: Black Caps v Pakistan

Follow all the action from the fourth T20 match at Bay Oval, Mount Maunganui.

Black Caps celebrate a wicket Photo: Photosport

Pakistan struck back at Eden Park to keep the five-match T20 international series alive, but the Black Caps can seal it with a with tonight at the Mount.

The visitors batters were in ominous form at Eden Park in their nine-wicket win, so the Black Caps will need to tighten up their bowling on what should be another run-heavy pitch.

Play starts at 7:15pm NZT.
