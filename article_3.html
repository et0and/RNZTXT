Elon Musk makes 'odd-looking' salute at inauguration

At the end of his speech, Elon Musk held his hand to his chest and raised his arm straight, outwards towards the sky.

Photo: AFP/ANGELA WEISS

Elon Musk has raised eyebrows after making an 'odd-looking' gesture at Capitol One Arena during US President Donald Trump's inauguration celebration.

At the end of his speech, Musk held his hand to his chest and then raised his arm outwards towards the sky, before repeating the act.

um pic.twitter.com/ISwJiM4EsH

The gesture is causing a stir on social media platforms, with some commenters saying it resembled a Nazi salute.

CNN's Erin Burnett described it as an 'odd-looking salute' after recapping the billionaire's speech, which she said had the biggest cheer of the event.
