Live: Super Rugby Aupiki - Matatū v Blues Women

Live: We're underway in Super Rugby Aupiki, and this afternoon's match is at Rugby Park in Invercargill where defending champs Matatū host the Blues Women.

We're underway in Super Rugby Aupiki, and this afternoon's match is at Rugby Park in Invercargill where defending champs Matatū host the Blues Women.

Matatu come in having lost a couple of key players but also having welcomed back Liv McGoverne, while the Blues have a solid look to their team.
