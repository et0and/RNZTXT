Fire breaks out on Auckland's Māngere Mountain

Large flames and billowing smoke can be seen in the area.

A fire has broken out on Māngere Mountain in Auckland on Saturday night.

A number of users on social media have posted images of the blaze, which has sent smoke billowing into the sky.

Fire and Emergency and police have been approached for comment.

A fire engine could be seen up the maunga by 11pm.

Fire and Emergency had closed the road up the maunga, and firefighters at the scene said grass was on fire.
