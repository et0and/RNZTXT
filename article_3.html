Prisoner escapes from custody at Waikato Hospital

Police are hunting for a prisoner who escaped from Corrections staff at Waikato Hospital in Hamilton this afternoon.

The prisoner was at Waikato Hospital when they escaped custody this afternoon. Photo: Supplied/ Waikato Hospital

Police are hunting for a prisoner who escaped from Corrections staff at Waikato Hospital in Hamilton this afternoon.

Police were notified about 1.30pm that a prisoner had escaped from Corrections custody at the hospital on Pembroke Street.

The prisoner is on remand at Spring Hill Corrections Facility.

Spring Hill general manager Scott Walker said the prisoner was handcuffed and wearing a GPS monitor when he escaped from staff.

Walker said police were informed immediately and that public safety was the prison's top priority.

A police spokesperson said officers were actively searching for the escapee.

Police have asked anyone in the immediate surrounding areas, who has seen anything suspicious, to please call police immediately on 111.

Walker said any escape was unacceptable and an immediate review into how this incident occurred will be carried out.
