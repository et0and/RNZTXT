Lulu Sun's Wimbledon match against Emma Raducanu goes to third set

Kiwi tennis player Lulu Sun and British star Emma Raducanu have gone to a third set in their fourth round match at Wimbledon. Audio

New Zealand's Lulu Sun during her fourth round match against Britain's Emma Raducanu at the 2024 Wimbledon Championships. Photo: AFP

Kiwi tennis player Lulu Sun and British star Emma Raducanu have gone to a third set in their fourth round match at Wimbledon.

Sun won the first set 6-2, and Raducanu came back to take the second 7-5.

Early in the third set Raducanu slipped on the grass and had a brief time out.

Sun is the first New Zealand woman to make the fourth round since Dame Ruia Morrison in 1959.

Sun had announced her arrival at the UK's Grand Slam tournament by ousting world number eight Qinwen Zheng in the first round before beating fellow qualifier Yuliia Starodubtseva of Ukraine.

She has caused a major surprise with her progress at Wimbledon, beating China's Zhu Lin early on Saturday in their third round match.
