Police asking for public help to find missing man Geoffrey Kelly

Kelly was last seen on Thursday night, wearing grey knee-length shorts, jandals, a tan sweatshirt and glasses.

Geoffrey Kelly. Photo: Supplied/Police

Police are asking for the public's help in the search for missing Hikumutu man Geoffrey Kelly.

Police said Kelly was last seen on Thursday night, wearing grey knee-length shorts, jandals, a tan sweatshirt and glasses.

Kelly's car was found by police abandoned in a ditch on Makokomiko Road at 10:30am on Friday.

Police said they believed he walked to get help, became disoriented and took shelter or got a ride with someone passing by.

If you have any information that might help police find Kelly call 105 and quote reference number P061689135.
