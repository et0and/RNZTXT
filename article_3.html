ANZ Premiership Grand Final live updates: Pulse v Mystics

Follow RNZ's live blog for all the action from the TSB Arena in Wellington.

Carys Stythe and Amelia Walmsley, Mystics v Pulse. Photo: Andrew Cornaga/www.photosport.nz

Back-to-back titles are on the mind of the Mystics as they head into a clash with the table-topping Pulse in the ANZ Premiership Grand Final this afternoon.

All eyes are on the match-up between Mystics shooter Grace Nweke and Pulse captain Kelly Jackson, the key could be in the clash between Mystics playmaker Peta Toeava and Pulse midcourter Fa'amu Ioane - although we can expect fireworks across the court in this highly anticipated final, which begins at 4:05pm.
