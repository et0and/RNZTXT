Cricket live updates: Black Caps take lead

It is day two of the third and final test between the Black Caps and England in Hamilton. Follow live updates.

The Black Caps rush to congratulate after he dismissed England batter Jacob Jacob Bethell in third test at Seddon Park, Hamilton, 15 Dec 2024. Photo: Photosport

The Black Caps have demolished England's first innings, with Mitchell Santner's arrival at the bowling crease paying great dividends.

New Zealand have a lead of 204 after England were all out for 143.

Santner has taken three wickets for just seven runs, including that of England skipper Ben Stokes, who failed to get an LBW decision overturned on review.
