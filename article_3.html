Severe thunderstorm watch for most of North Island

Severe thunderstorms have been detected in Northland.

Photo: Supplied / MetService

Severe thunderstorms have been detected in Northland.

Metservice says its weather radar has picked up the bad weather near Hikurangi, Whakapara, Puhipuhi, Whananaki and Matapouri.

The storms are expected to strike at around 5:30 this evening and are likely to bring very heavy rain and lightning, which could cause surface and flash flooding.

MetService has issued a severe thunderstorm watch for large parts of the North Island today.

Thunderstorms could also develop in Auckland, Coromandel, Waikato, Bay of Plenty and Hawkes Bay, with localised downpours this afternoon and early evening.

The watch extends until 9pm Saturday.

Rotorua, Taupo, Gisborne and Taihape are also set to be hit by rain.
