Watch: Govt announces details for changing Sentencing Act

The sentencing reforms are a culmination of everything the government is doing on justice and crime, Prime Minister Christopher Luxon says.

Prime Minister Christopher Luxon and Justice Minister Paul Goldsmith have spoken to reporters at Parliament after the weekly Cabinet meeting.

They have announced changes to the Sentencing Act.

They include capping the sentence discounts that judges can apply at 40 percent and introducing a sliding scale for early guilty pleas with a maximum sentence discount of 25 percent, reducing to a maximum of 5 percent for a guilty plea entered during trial.
