Actor Val Kilmer dies aged 65

The actor was known for playing Batman in 1995's Batman Forever,

Val Kilmer in Deja Vu (2006). Photo: AFP

Hollywood star Val Kilmer has died at the age of 65, his daughter has confirmed.

Kilmer's daughter Mercedes said he passed away on 1 April and the cause of death was pneumonia, the New York Times has reported.

Kilmer was diagnosed with throat cancer in 2014 and later recovered, she said.

The actor played Batman in 1995's Batman Forever, and also had memoriable roles in Top Gun, Willow, Tombstone, Top Secret, The Doors, Heat, Kiss Kiss Bang Bang and many other films.
