Rugby league live updates: Kiwi Ferns v Papua New Guinea

Follow all the action as the Kiwi Ferns take on the PNG Orchids in Port Moresby.

Shanice Parker of New Zealand, on the run during the 2023 Pacific Championships. Photo: SCOTT RADFORD-CHISHOLM

The Kiwi Ferns take on the PNG Orchids in a match that will decide who advances to the Women's Pacific Championship final, on next weekend.

Follow all the action from Santos International Stadium, in Port Moresby.
