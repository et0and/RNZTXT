Live T20 cricket: Sri Lanka score more than 200 runs

Follow all the T20 action at Saxton Oval, Nelson.

New Zealand's Mark Chapman attacks during the second T20 cricket international against Sri Lanka at Mt Maunganui, 30 December, 2024. Photo: PHOTOSPORT

The Black Caps will need to chase down an imposing 218 runs posted by Sri Lanka in their innings in their third T20 match.

NZ are looking to make it three from three and a winning start to the New Year against Sri Lanka, as they come off a commanding couple of wins at Bay Oval, but will have to overcome Sri Lanka's 218/5.

A moment of silence was held with players and police officers before the match following the death of long-serving Nelson police officer Senior Sergeant Lyn Fleming.
