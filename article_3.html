Sports building evacuated after threatening message

New Zealand Campus of Innovation and Sport in Trentham said it was working with police on site.

Photo: RNZ / Cole Eastham-Farrelly

A sports institute in Upper Hutt has been evacuated after it received a threatening message.

The New Zealand Campus of Innovation and Sport in Trentham, which has training facilities and accommodation for high performance athletes, said it was working with police on site.

"In the interest of the safety of all on campus, while the risk is low - everyone onsite has been evacuated and we expect an all clear for business as usual early this afternoon," it said.

Police said they did not believe there was any immediate safety risk, but it " these sorts of threats are taken seriously".

"Enquiries remain ongoing into the source of the message," police said.
