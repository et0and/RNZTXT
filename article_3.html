Live: NZ Gold Guitar Awards in Gore

Some of NZ's best performers are congregating for a star-studded celebration of all things country music.

Jon Collins has travelled from Melbourne to perform at the Gold Guitar Awards. Photo: RNZ/Samuel Robinson

The NZ Gold Guitar Awards are being held on Sunday night - a star-studded celebration of all things country music.

Part of the 10-day Tussock Country festival, the awards see some of our best performers flock to Aotearoa's country music capital, Gore.

There are awards for best male and female solo, gospel, traditional, new country, country rock, duet, vocal group, New Zealand song and instrumental.
