Police respond to report of gunshots, road delays

Police north of Auckland said there were no indications of anything amiss, but motorists may experience delays.

Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A police incident is unfolding in Auckland on State Highway One, north of Warkworth township, New Zealand Transport Agency says.

The incident is on SH1 north of the SH1/Twin Coast Highway roundabout (Pukerito Roundabout).

However, police said that while they responded to reports of gunshots in the area, "there is nothing to immediately suggest that was the case".

The incident appears to be wrapping up with no further issues, police said.

Road users should expect delays or avoid the area.
