How to cast your vote for the 2024 Aotearoa Music Awards

Next week, judges will decide who will win the 2024 Aotearoa Music Awards - and you can be one of them.

Photo: Liam Swiggs / RNZ

Next week, judges will decide who will win the 2024 Aotearoa Music Awards - and you can be one of them.

Voting for the Aotearoa Music Awards JD Sports Tā Te Iwi | People's Choice Award, which is decided entirely by public vote, is now open.

Voting is open to fans from around the world and closes at midnight on 28 May (NZT).

To cast your vote, visit the voting portal.

The winner of the 2024 JD Sports Tā Te Iwi | People's Choice Award will be announced at the Aotearoa Music Awards on 30 May.

The awards recognise and celebrate the biggest musical successes of the year, while also providing a platform to promote emerging artists.

This year's awards will be held at Auckland's Viaduct Events Centre.

RNZ is the official media partner for the awards and will be streaming live video of the ceremony across multiple platforms on the night.
