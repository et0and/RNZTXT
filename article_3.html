New World supermarket security guard stabbed in Auckland

The incident happened after the security guard tried to stop a suspected shoplifter from leaving the New World store in Mount Roskill.

File photo. Photo: RNZ / Marika Khabazi

A security guard has been stabbed after stopping a suspected shoplifter in Auckland this afternoon.

The police were called to a New World supermarket on May Road in Mount Roskill at 3:40pm, after the guard was attacked.

The incident happened after the guard tried to stop a suspected shoplifter leaving the store

The offender ran away, heading north on May Road, said police.

The security guard was treated at the scene.
