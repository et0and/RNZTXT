Rainbow community stand in solidarity against Destiny Church

Members and allies of the LGBTQ+ community have gathered in Auckland's Albert Park to protest Brian Tamaki's demonstrations which targeted pride events last week.

Demonstration against Brian Tamaki and the Destiny Church. Photo: Finn Blackwell

Members of the rainbow community and supporters have gathered in Auckland's Albert Park this afternoon to stand in solidarity against Destiny Church demonstrations targeting pride events last week.

Members of Brian Tamaki's Destiny Church "Man Up" group disrupted pride events in Auckland last Saturday, which prompted a number of calls to have its charitable status revoked.

Protesters attending a rally against Brian Tamaki and the Destiny Church on 23/02/2025. Photo: Finn Blackwell

One demonstrator at Albert Park today told RNZ they personally knew some of those confronted by Destiny Church members last weekend.

No counter protesters are present at the rally.

There were a number of security staff present.
