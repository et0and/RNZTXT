One dead after incident in Mount Wellington, Auckland

One person is dead following an incident in Auckland's Mt Wellington on Saturday afternoon.

Emergency services respond to an incident on Tomuri Place in Mount Wellington. Photo: Stuff / Ricky Wilson

One person is dead following an incident in Auckland's Mt Wellington on Saturday afternoon.

Emergency services are at the scene on Tomuri Place - including police and two ambulances.

Police said a male was pronounced dead at the scene.

Emergency services respond to an incident on Tomuri Place in Mount Wellington. Photo: Stuff / Ricky Wilson

A "person of interest" was being spoken to by police, a police spokesperson confirmed and cordons were in place while the investigation began.

A spokesperson for St John said paramedics were called to the scene about 1.30pm and sent two ambulances, a rapid response vehicle and one manager.

Two people also reportedly had moderate injuries.
