Follow all the action from Newark, New Jersey.

Islam Makhachev faces off with Dustin Poirier during the UFC 302 Ceremonial Weigh-in. Photo: Luke Hales/AFP

The pound-for-pound best fighter on the planet returns to the octagon this weekend to put his lightweight title on the line, while two fan favourites will go to war at middleweight in the co-main event.

UFC 302 is headlined by reigning lightweight king Islam Makhachev against perennial contender Dustin "the Diamond" Poirier. The co-main event takes place at middleweight with the controversial Sean Strickland taking on Brazilian powerhouse Paulo Costa.
