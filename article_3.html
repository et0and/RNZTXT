Live: Strong winds cause damage to plane, flight delayed

A Jetstar plane has been damaged in Auckland as severe gales hit the upper North Island, with gusts of up to 120km/h possible.

Photo: Supplied / MetService

A Jetstar plane parked at Auckland Airport was damaged after strong winds blew a set of boarding stairs into the left wing.

A spokesperson said it caused minor damage to the aircraft, requiring repair.

Severe gusts of up to 120km/h are possible in the upper North Island until 9pm this evening, MetService said.

A severe thunderstorm watch has also been issued for Northland, Auckland, Great Barrier Island and Coromandel Peninsula until 9pm.

Earlier, highways across the South Island were closed by heavy snow.
