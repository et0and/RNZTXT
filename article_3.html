Live updates: Māori All Blacks v Japan XV

Follow all the action from Chichibunomiya Rugby Stadium, Tokyo.

Māori All Blacks perform the haka Photo: Photosport

Coach Ross Filipo has named a strong side for the first game against Japan XV, with Crusaders first five Rivez Reihana set to start on debut.

Chiefs vice-captain Rameka Poihipi will also represent the Māori All Blacks for the first time, while in the second row Laghlan McWhannell earns his first cap too.

Coles Forbes also pulls on the jersey for the first time at fullback, after an impressive season for the Blues.
