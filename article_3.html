Live updates: Black Caps v Sri Lanka

The Blackcaps are on track for a big win in the first of the three ODI match series against Sri Lanka, being played at the Basin Reserve in Wellington

New Zealand's Mitchell Santner during the first ODI international cricket match between New Zealand and Sri Lanka at the Basin Reserve, Wellington. Photo: Kerry Marshall/www.photosport.nz

The Blackcaps are on track for a big win in the first of the three ODI match series against Sri Lanka, being played at the Basin Reserve in Wellington.

It comes after the Kiwi team trounced the visitors in the T20 series, claiming the win with a game to spare in Mount Maunganui in December.

Play starts at 11am Sunday and runs until 6pm.
