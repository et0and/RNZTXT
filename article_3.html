Live: Black Caps chase series win in Sri Lanka ODI

Fresh from their resounding win at Wellington, the NZ men hope to clinch their three-match series at Hamilton.

Black Caps captain Mitch Santner bowls against Sri Lanka. Photo: Kerry Marshall/www.photosport.nz

After their Twenty20 series victory over Sri Lanka, the Black Caps now have a 1-0 lead over the tourists in the one-day format and can clinch that series in Hamilton on Wednesday.

On Sunday, opener Will Young narrowly missed a century with the bat, while Matt Henry led the team with the ball, capturing 4/19, as New Zealand dismissed their opponents cheaply at Wellington's Basin Reserve.

The weather forecast in 'The Tron' is cloudy with a slight chance of rain, so fingers crossed we get a full day's play.
