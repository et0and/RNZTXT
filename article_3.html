Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

The A-League high-fliers can already start looking towards the play-offs, they currently have a six point lead at the top of the ladder and want to stay there to secure a first round bye.

Central Coast are the defending A-League champs but have suffered a real fall from grace, currently sitting in 10th. Kick-off is at 5pm NZT.
