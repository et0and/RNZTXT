Live netball updates: Silver Ferns claim Constellation Cup

The Silver Ferns have won the Constellation Cup for just the third time with a 61-43 victory over Australia.

Kimiora Poi of New Zealand receives the ball during the Constellation Cup match on 27 October in Perth. Photo: AAP / www.photosport.nz

The Silver Ferns have won the Constellation Cup, defeating the Diamonds 3-nil.

The New Zealand side won 61-43 in Perth to hoist the cup for just the third time since 2010.

It is the first time since 2006 that the Silver Ferns have beaten the Diamonds three games in a row.
