NRL live updates: Warriors v Storm

Follow all the action from Go Media Mt Smart Stadium, Auckland.

Marcelo Montoya of the Warriors scores a try during the Melbourne Storm v New Zealand Warriors match during the Telstra NRL Premiership at AAMI Park, Melbourne Photo: Photosport / Scott Barbour

The Warriors come into this game on an absolute high after stringing three wins in a row over some quality opponents, whilst the Storm are under strength missing multiple key players. On top of that, the Warriors are welcoming back a rested and recovered Shaun Johnson, but they'll do well to remember the three things that are guaranteed: Death, taxes and a late Storm comeback. Kick-off is at 7.30pm NZT.
