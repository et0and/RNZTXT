Pro-Palestine protester climbs onto council roof

Police and firefighters were called to the scene.

Photo: RNZ / Nathan McKinnon

A pro-Palestine protester has climbed onto the roof of the Christchurch City Council building, prompting an emergency response.

Police and firefighters are at the scene as about 20 protesters gather near the Christchurch Art Gallery.

Police were called to the scene near Worcester Boulevard about 11.20am on Monday.

A section of the road had been closed off to traffic, trams and pedestrians while emergency services talk with with the person on the building.
