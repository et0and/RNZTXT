Recap: Crusaders take down Chiefs 37-26 in comeback

The winless Crusaders staged a comeback tonight, leading the Chiefs 37-26 in Christchurch.

Crusaders celebrates Chay Fihaki's try during the Super Rugby Pacific match, Crusaders Vs Chiefs, at the Apollo Projects Stadium, Christchurch. Photo: John Davidson / www.photosport.nz

The Super Rugby Pacific try-fest was the Crusaders' first win of the season.

With eight points separating the teams on the scoreboard in the tense final 10 minutes, the Crusaders converted a late penalty to rediscover their winning form.

Veteran Johnny McNicholl scored two of their five tries.

Heading into the clash in Christchurch the defending champions had lost five straight games.
