Boxing live updates: Mea Motu v Ellie Scotney

Follow all the action from Motorpoint Arena, Nottingham.

Mea Motu makes her way ringside. Photo: Mark Evans/Photosport

New Zealand's undefeated Mea Motu faces the biggest test of her career this morning in the UK, taking on current WBO, IBF, and Ring Magazine Super Bantamweight champion Ellie Scotney. The fighters are expected to make their entrance at around 9am NZT.
