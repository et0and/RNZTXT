Fire in hills of Whitby, Porirua

Nine fire crews are battling the blaze.

A vegetation fire in Spinnaker Reserve in Whitby. Photo: RNZ / Krystal Gibbens

Fire and Emergency is responding to a vegetation fire in the Spinnaker Reserve in the Porirua suburb of Whitby.

The fire was reported at around 1.40pm, and was around 480sqm in size at 2.30pm.

There are nine crews on site, with more trucks responding.

People are asked to stay away from the area, so trucks can get through.

The entrance to Mercury Way is blocked by police.

It is very windy.

There is some smoke in the area, so those nearby should keep their windows closed.

Police at the scene of the fire have told RNZ residents are not being asked to evacuate at this stage.
