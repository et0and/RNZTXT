Live: Wellington Phoenix v Adelaide United

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium, Wellington.

Hideki Ishige of the Phoenix and Jay Barnett of Adelaide compete for the ball during the A-League Men - Wellington Phoenix v Adelaide United at Sky Stadium, Wellington, New Zealand on 11 January 2025. Photo: Masanori Udagawa / Photosport
