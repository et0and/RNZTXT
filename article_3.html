Evacuations as large fire spreads near Lake Pukaki

Evacuations are underway in Canterbury after a large fire broke out near Lake Pukaki.

The fire has burned through about 50 hectares, and people have been evacuated from nearby areas that could be in danger. Photo: Supplied/ Scott Aronsen

Evacuations are underway in Canterbury after a large fire broke out near Lake Pukaki.

Fire and Emergency says they were called to the scene after a farmer's burn-off got out of control shortly before 3pm on Monday.

The blaze has been burning in the area where State Highway 80 and State Highway 8 intersect.

The flames spread through about 50 hectares of grass and scrub during the afternoon, and FENZ said the fire was not contained, but was not spreading to the north.

Photo: Supplied/ Scott Aronsen

Ten fire trucks were at the scene, with a team of about 40 firefighters tackling the blaze.

RNZ was unable to confirm the number of homes that had been evacuated.
