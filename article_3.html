Watch live: Luxon speaks from Samoa as Commonwealth leaders meet

The prime minister is joining other heads of government in Apia and will speak to reporters shortly.

Christopher Luxon has joined other heads of government at the Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting in Apia.

Luxon is expected to speak some time after 2.45pm on Friday.

Earlier, Luxon had a private one-on-one meeting with British Prime Minister Sir Keir Starmer at the Taumeasina resort. Free trade between the two countries was expected to feature in their discussion, as well as support for Ukraine and conflict in the Middle East.
