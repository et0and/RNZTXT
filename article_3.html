Live: 'Absolutely bucketing': Homes, businesses lose power as winds batter north

Properties from Northland to Whangamatā have been losing power as a storm makes its way down the country.

A Northland Regional Council webcam image shows a flooded Kāeo River just centimetres from State Highway 10 at 8am on Tuesday. Photo: Supplied / NRC

A new power cut has hit almost 1000 homes and businesses in the tourist hotspot of Russell as high winds and heavy rain batter the north of the country.

The Upper North Island is expected to be in for a drenching on Tuesday, with a subtropical low sweeping down from Northland. A heavy rain warning is in place for Northland, Coromandel Peninsula and from 4pm in Tai Rāwhiti.
