Watch: Peters outlines NZ's foreign policy shift

Foreign Affairs Minister Winston Peters is speaking at Pacific Islands Leaders Meeting Video

Foreign Affairs Minister Winston Peters is speaking at Pacific Islands Leaders Meeting, where he is representing New Zealand.

The 10th meeting (PALM10) is hosted by Prime Minister Fumio Kishida in Tokyo, Japan.

The speech was an opportunity to outline New Zealand's foreign policy shift.
