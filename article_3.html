Live: Porirua had 'one in 20 year' amount of rainfall

Downpours have left parts of the lower North Island inundated with water, with "significant flooding" across roads.

Porirua has had a "one in 20 year" amount of rainfall on Monday, with other areas across the Wellington Region facing a "one in five year" event, the Wellington Region Emergency Management Office says.

Its regional manager Jeremy Holmes told Checkpoint rain started to recede this afternoon, but there was still more rain expected for the Tararua Ranges overnight.
