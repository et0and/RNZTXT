Super Rugby Pacific live updates: Hurricanes v Waratahs

Follow all the action from Sky Stadium in Wellington with RNZ's live blog.

Billy Proctor of the Hurricanes scores try during Super Rugby Pacific match - Hurricanes v Waratahs at Sky Stadium, Wellington. Photo: Elias Rodriguez / Photosport

The Hurricanes are set to take on the Waratahs at Wellington's Sky Stadium.

The Canes are looking bounce back from their first loss of the season against a Tahs team that has been disappointing this season against everyone not called the Crusaders.

Clark Laidlaw suddenly finds himself down to his fourth and fifth choice hookers, so Kianu Kereru-Symes and Raymond Tuputupu will need to make sure their lineout throwing and scrummaging is on point. Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
