Miracle in Albany: Moana pull off massive win over Hurricanes

Follow all the action from North Harbour Stadium, Albany.

Moana Pasifika Miracle Faillagi scores his third try during the Super Rugby Pacific match, Moana Pasifika v Hurricanes, North Harbour Stadium, Auckland. Photo: Michael Thomas/ActionPress

Ardie Savea faces off against his old team for the first time, and will lead Moana Pasifika as they serach for their first win of the season. The Canes come in after a tough loss to the Blues last weekend, but welcome back All Black Ruben Love in at fullback. Kick-off is at 7:05pm NZT.
