About 50 firefighters battling blaze in Northland

Several fire trucks and three helicopters are also at the scene of the scrub fire in Ngunguru.

Photo: Supplied/Ann Austin

Fire crews are battling a scrub fire in Northland which had earlier been threatening several homes in a coastal settlement.

Fire and Emergency were first alerted to the blaze in Ngunguru at about 3.30pm on Monday.

Several fire trucks are at the scene as well as three helicopters and about 50 firefighters.

Photo: Supplied/Ann Austin

FENZ said crews are working on containing the fire and protecting properties which were under threat.

It said the fire is not currently endangering any homes.
