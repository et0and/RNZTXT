Netball live: Steel v Pulse

Follow the latest ANZ Premiership action from Invercargill.

Georgia and Kate Heffernan are in action for the Steel tonight. Photo: Alan Lee / www.photosport.nz

The Central Pulse could go top of the table if they beat the Southern Steel in their ANZ Premiership clash in Invercargill tonight.

The teams are playing for the Robyn Broughton Legacy Trophy for the first time, honouring a woman who coached both teams during a decorated domestic career.
