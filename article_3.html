Christmas weekend traffic: One dead in Bay of Plenty

One person has died on the roads today, as travellers clog highways in the run-up to Christmas Day.

Photo: RNZ / Richard Tindiller

One person has died on the roads today, as travellers clog highways in the run-up to Christmas Day.

There have been three major crashes in the past few hours.

In the latest, a person has been killed in an accident around 1.30pm on State Highway 32, on Whakamaru Road in the Bay of Plenty.

Earlier, a car and motorbike collided on State Highway 25 on the Coromandel Peninsula between Hikuai and Whangamata. That road could be closed all day.

And people are injured after a vehicle crashed on State Highway 1, the Waikato Expressway this morning.

Delays are expected on those routes.

Updates on any road closures are available on the NZTA/Waka Kotahi Journey Planner website.
